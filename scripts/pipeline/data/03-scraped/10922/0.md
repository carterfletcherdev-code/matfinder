 # Children's Program #### Build a Solid Foundation of Success for YOUR Child! If you would like to:
 - Increase your child’s self confidence and self discipline - Strengthen their concentration and focus - Improve your child’s level of fitness and health - Cultivate greater respect for themselves and others - Develop academic and athletic performance - Learn valuable self defense skills

 Children (Ages 7-12 years old) receive age-appropriate physical training and self-defense instruction. This program emphasizes respect, discipline, fitness, self-control and concentration. Our foundation begins with routines rooted in respect and grows into a sharpened focus on their skills and their instructor - all while having fun and earning belt levels. As a part of their work in the dojo(training center), children are awarded recognition and public praise for their accomplishments.
