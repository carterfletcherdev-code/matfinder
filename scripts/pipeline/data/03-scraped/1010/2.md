# Come Get Down In The Garden Welcome to Peace Garden Grappling Club. Located in South Fargo, PGGC was founded in 2022 with one goal in mind: bring high level submission grappling to the area without the formalities. We focus on realistic techniques based in American Jiu- Jitsu, Wrestling, and Judo. We offer top tier facilities and an environment that makes you want to train. Schedule a Free Trial

 ## Why The Garden? High Level Instruction
 Head Coach Baily Badillo sets the standard at PGGC. He works closely with our coaching staff to make sure what’s being taught is current, effective, and actually works in live training. We don’t chase trends—But we don’t teach outdates techniques either. Everything on the mat has a purpose
 Culture
 At The Garden we train hard, consistently, and with safety in mind especially for kids and beginners, but we don’t confuse “safe” with “soft”. Everyone is pushed at the right pace and respect is non-negotiable
 Community
 What really separates PGGC is the people. This isn’t a gym where everyone disappears as soon as class ends. Members train together, support each other and spend time together outside of the gym. Whether that’s helping a teammate prep for competition, watching fights on a Saturday night or just getting something to eat. If you walked through the door, chances are we already have more in common than you think.
