 ### Honored by the community. In 2024, we were honored with the Official Community's Choice Award for Eugene. This recognition means a lot to us, not for the title but because it came from our students, families, and neighbors. We're proud to serve this community and grateful to be part of your journey.

 - We Defy Foundation Superhero Jiu Jitsu Academy is a proud affiliate of the We Defy Foundation, a nonprofit organization that helps combat veterans overcome military-connected disabilities through Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu and community. As a We Defy affiliate academy, we provide a supportive environment where veterans can train, heal, and grow on and off the mats. Through discipline, connection, and shared purpose, we honor those who have served by giving them a place to belong and a path to recovery through Jiu-Jitsu.

 - C4C Police Jiu-Jitsu Superhero Jiu Jitsu Academy is an affiliate of C4C PJJ (C4C Police Jiu-Jitsu), a training program that focuses on defensive tactics, control techniques, de-escalation, and use-of-force strategies for law enforcement and public safety professionals. Through this partnership, we host and support certified C4C PJJ curriculum and events, delivering real-world grappling skills centered on safety, control, and accountability. Our goal is to empower officers and practitioners with practical tactics they can trust under pressure.
