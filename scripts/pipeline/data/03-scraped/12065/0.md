## How we train ### Train creatively. Roll with purpose. At Superhero Jiu Jitsu Academy, Jiu-Jitsu is more than a martial art. It is a mindset built on adaptability, creativity, and practical application. Under the guidance of Mike DeWitt, a dedicated black belt in the 10th Planet system, you will learn No-Gi Jiu-Jitsu in a way that helps you think on the mats while building confidence, control, and problem-solving skills with every roll. Our approach helps you develop your own style within a proven framework so you are prepared for competition and self-defense in a fun, challenging environment.
 Whether you are new to Jiu-Jitsu or ready to level up your game, training with Mike and the team at Superhero Jiu Jitsu Academy will help you unlock your potential. Each class is structured so you leave better than you came, supported by a community that pushes you to improve while keeping training engaging. Discover why this approach produces creative, adaptable grapplers who are ready for the challenges they face on and off the mats.
