 ### Weather Policy
 Keeping Safety First

 The safety of our students and staff is always our top priority. During inclement weather, our administrative team closely monitors local conditions and official alerts. In the event of any schedule changes, we will notify you promptly via text, email, and social media.
 Below is our general weather policy. Please note: conditions can change rapidly, and final decisions may vary based on real-time circumstances.

 ##### Snow and Ice Classes will typically continue as scheduled if main roads are clear and safe . However, if we experience freezing rain or overnight snow, we will take the following steps:

 - A team member will assess local road conditions and/or follow recommendations from city officials. - Early morning classes (5 AM / 6 AM) may be canceled. - A decision about mid-day classes will be made and announced by 9 AM. - A decision about evening classes will be made and announced by 2 PM.

 We reserve the right to update or adjust the schedule as weather conditions evolve.

 ##### Severe Weather: We remain open during thunderstorms unless one of the following occurs:

 - Flash flooding affects major roads leading to our facility (rare). - A tornado watch is issued during class hours that could impact safe travel. - A tornado warning (meaning a tornado or rotation has been spotted) is in effect during class times or near times when students and staff would be commuting.

 We appreciate your understanding and flexibility as we prioritize safety and strive to communicate updates as quickly as possible.
