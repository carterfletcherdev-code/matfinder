 ## For All Ages

 There’s no age limit to start practicing Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu. In Brazil, this discipline is taught from a very young age and can be practiced well into later life.
 That’s why it’s called Arte Suave – the Gentle Art.
 At the Exodus Brazillian jiu jitsu Academy, we offer classes for children to introduce them to Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu and support them on their journey toward becoming a future black belt.
