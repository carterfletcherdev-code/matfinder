 2️⃣ Quality Immersion

 We offer monthly workshops and seminar upgrades, often with guest instructors from different styles, giving students exposure to diverse techniques and perspectives. Past and current workshops include
 - Karate (Japanese/ Okinawan) - Chinese (Tai chi, Bagua, XingYi, Qi Gong) - Fitness (Stretch/ Tone, Yoga, Get Fit) - Women’s Self-Defense - Okinawan Weapons - Competition Strategies - Yoga - Cardio Kickboxing
 These sessions, along with free public opportunities like open mat twice a month and women’s self-defense classes, allow students, parents and family members to explore a variety of interests while engaging in a shared, family-friendly training environment.

 3️⃣ Top-Notch Facility
 Over 1,300 sq. ft. of clean mat space
 Our facility is safe for every class, with mats and equipment free of cracks or gaps, and cleaned daily as well as after each use. Beyond maintaining a clean environment, we actively instill good hygiene and safety practices in every session, teaching students to wipe down equipment, sanitize hands, and respect shared spaces. This not only ensures a healthy training environment but also reinforces habits of personal responsibility and care that are essential both on and off the mat.

 4️⃣ Experienced Instructors

 All primary instructors are in-house and trained under our program, bringing diverse skill sets to support students of all ages and levels. We encourage students to actively seek knowledge wherever it can be found—through practice, observation, and learning from different perspectives. To broaden their experience, we also recruit special guest instructors from time to time, providing students with exposure to unique teaching styles, advanced techniques, and specialized training opportunities that complement their regular instruction.

 5️⃣ Research & Resources

 Students are encouraged to explore and learn beyond the dojo using our extensive online library and home video training . Exposure to martial arts techniques inspires and motivates students by showing them new skills and training methods.

 Practicing at home and using online resources like YouTube sparks creativity, inspires motivation, and encourages consistent improvement, fueling both their passion and progress in martial arts.

 6️⃣ Proven Quality

 Performing at the top levels of karate since 1995, our students have also made their mark in grappling competitions, consistently producing exceptional martial artists. You can check out our Wall of Fame to see their accomplishments, and over the years we’ve received countless letters from former students who have successfully defended themselves in real-life situations. These achievements highlight not only the quality and effectiveness of our training but also the lasting impact martial arts can have on people’s lives.

 7️⃣ Deep Lineage

 Our system embraces Bruce Lee's Jeet Kune Do-inspired philosophy : take what is useful, discard what isn’t, tailored to each individual’s body type. Our Okinawan hard-style roots trace back to the 1200s , with torchbearers who were karate legends and pioneers . Our catch wrestling lineage comes from masters like Karl Gotch and Billy Robinson , providing world-class instruction in every aspect of training.

 9️⃣ Fitness & Self-Defense

 Martial arts at ShinPu-Ren is for everyone . We offer cardio kickboxing, yoga, women’s self-defense programs , and other fitness classes alongside traditional and modern martial arts. Self-defense is the backbone of martial arts , and we make it accessible to anyone looking to improve fitness, confidence, and safety.
