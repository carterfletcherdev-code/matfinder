 ## Grappling with Monroe Hall BJJ Our Professional | BJJ Gi | BJJ No-Gi | Wrestling | Sambo Staff
 Brazilian jiu jitsu , known as the "Gentle Art," focuses on ground fighting, leverage, and submission holds. It builds core strength, flexibility, and cardiovascular endurance. BJJ is practiced in two formats: Gi, using a traditional uniform for grips and chokes, and No-Gi, emphasizing speed and athleticism. Training cultivates patience, confidence, and mental resilience.
 Wrestling emphasizes takedowns, control, and positioning through clinch work and ground control. This ancient combat sport focuses on explosive power, balance, and technical precision. Wrestling training develops exceptional strength, agility, and mental toughness while teaching athletes to control opponents through leverage. It builds discipline, resilience, and competitive spirit.
 Sambo , a Russian martial art meaning "self-defense without weapons," combines judo and wrestling with unique submission techniques. It emphasizes both practical self-defense and competitive sport. Sambo training develops strength, flexibility, and tactical awareness, teaching practitioners to control opponents through throws, pins, and submissions.

 ## Flowing with Baltimore Aikido Academy Our Professional Aikido Staff
 Aikido , known as the "Way of Harmony," is a Japanese martial art that focuses on redirecting an opponent's energy rather than meeting force with force. It emphasizes flowing movements, joint locks, and throws to neutralize attacks while protecting both the practitioner and the attacker. Aikido training develops physical coordination, mental discipline, and spiritual awareness through its unique blend of martial technique and philosophical principles.

 #### Background & Journey Born and raised on the east side of Baltimore City, Maryland, Jesse spent his early years living in a high crime area and was frequently a target of bullying. Encouraged by one of his teachers to take up wrestling as means of self-defense, Jesse joined the wrestling team at Eastern Technical High School as a freshman, and continued to wrestle for the school's varsity team.
 Inspired by watching the famous UFC fight between Matt Hughes and Carlos Newton, Jesse enrolled in Combat Sports Center of Maryland to study Muay Thai. After his first class with Coach Greg Dorsey, Jesse was addicted and participated in any and all classes that were accessible to him.
 Jesse signed up for his first amateur MMA fight after only two months of training. Leveraging his wrestling base, he took down his opponent two minutes into the first round. Subsequently, his competitive spirit drove him to continue to hone his skills, eventually winning a WKA Novice MMA tournament in Hampton, VA.

 #### Elite Training Experience Jesse has trained at other prominent gyms with World-Class coaches including Henry Smith (Rude Boy Muay Thai). Through Henry, he was invited to go to the BMF Ranch in Albuquerque, NM and trained with Donald "Cowboy" Cerrone in preparation for a UFC fight against Miles Jury.
 His time spent at the ranch confirmed his ability to train and compete with some of the world's best fighters. Jesse trained and sparred with several MMA stars including Cowboy himself, Paul Felder, Jon Bones Jones, and Pat "Bam Bam" Healy.

 #### Coaching Achievements 6 World Champions
 10 National Champions
 17 East Coast Champions

 After his own personal success and achievements, Jesse realized his calling as a coach. He returned to Baltimore, with a vision of helping others achieve their goals and grow as individuals through Muay Thai. His passion and commitment to help others led him to establishing American Muay Thai in 2015.

 #### Training Philosophy "If you combine boxing technique with the head movement and footwork of Muay Thai, you can be very effective at striking." American Muay Thai is a style that combines the best elements of boxing, Muay Thai, and Dutch-Style Kickboxing with a focus on clean technique, power, and efficiency. The gym focuses on empowering individuals to develop skills from the ground up, catering to everyone from Professional Fighters to children in a welcoming and fun environment with major attention to safety, health, technique, and having fun.
