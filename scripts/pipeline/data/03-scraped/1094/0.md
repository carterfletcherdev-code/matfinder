### Welcome to Grand Valley Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu ## We Train to Build Strength, Confidence, and Purpose Grand Valley BJJ is a place where confidence is built, skill is earned, and people of all ages and backgrounds come to grow. For over a decade, our academy has been a home for beginners taking their first step onto the mats and experienced athletes pushing themselves at the highest levels. We offer Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu and Muay Thai in an environment that values discipline, respect, and strong community. Our coaching team includes accomplished competitors, world champions, and seasoned instructors who are committed to developing every student. Throughout the year, our students also train with visiting BJJ World Champions and UFC fighters, gaining exposure to a wide range of styles and perspectives. At Grand Valley BJJ, you don’t just train — you become part of something strong, supportive, and built to last.

 ### Grand Valley BJJ Manifesto ## A Statement from the Head Coach Grand Valley BJJ was built on the belief that Jiu-Jitsu is more than techniques and belts—it is a system for building capable humans. The same principles that create success on the mat create success in business, family, and life.
 This manifesto is not marketing language. It is our operating system. It defines how we train, how we treat each other, and how we respond under pressure. If you train here, you are expected to pursue mastery, take responsibility, and play the long game.
 This room is for people who value discipline over excuses, pressure over panic, and growth over comfort.
 — Head Coach, Grand Valley BJJ
 1. Own the Position – We take responsibility for our results. Outcomes are created by positions we accept or allow. If something isn’t working, we improve the position. Ownership is power.
 2. Systems Beat Moments – We value consistency over hype. Success is built through daily reps, fundamentals, and systems that hold under pressure.
 3. Pressure Over Time – We apply patient, intelligent pressure. We advance position by position until resistance breaks. Calm pressure wins.
 4. Focus Creates Leverage – We train with intent. We avoid distraction and build depth. Focus multiplies effort.
 5. Technique Before Strength – We prioritize leverage, timing, and efficiency over ego and brute force. Skill scales. Technique lasts.
 6. Adapt or Get Passed – We stay curious and evolving. Stagnation is the only failure.
 7. Longevity Is the Goal – We protect our bodies, teammates, and culture. We train hard today so we can still train years from now.
 Our Commitment:
 We train with humility. We respect the room. We raise the standard.
 Grand Valley BJJ is more than a gym. It’s a proving ground. It’s a long game.
 Train with purpose. Lead by example.

 ### From day one, Grand Valley Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu has been embraced by the community.
 After moving from Texas, our founder, Kevin Lewis, began teaching at a local gym. He quickly made an impact and, before long, his students encouraged him to open a school of his own. Kevin took their advice, opening Grand Valley BJJ in September of 2013. But what started out as a side hustle soon became a full-time gig. Five years later, Grand Valley BJJ had grown immensely and we decided it was time our space did too. After expanding into another unit and doubling our mat space, Grand Valley is now home to about 300 students!
 We have loved sharing our passion for the martial arts throughout this past decade, and can’t wait to continue changing lives – one technique at a time.
