### No Restrictive Contracts
 We are so confident in our program that unlike many schools, we will not tie you to our gym with long-term contracts, . No long term contracts means you can pause, cancel, or upgrade anytime needed with no penalties.

 ### No Uniforms
 While we offer team rashguards & apparel, we never require it to be purchased. At 10th Planet Burbank you are welcome to wear your own training gear, any brand- any style. This means lower start up costs for students along with wearing what is comfortable for you.

 ### No Pressure
 The only pressure we encourage is in your grappling and striking. Picking a gym should be a great experience so We offer no pressure trial lessons. No long sales pitch or holding you hostage to sign up.
