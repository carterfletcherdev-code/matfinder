 ## Tulsa BJJ . ### for everyone

 Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu (BJJ) is a grappling-based martial art and combat sport focused on ground fighting and submissions, developed from Japanese Jiu-Jitsu and Judo.

 Jiu jitsu emphasizes ground techniques that allow a smaller, weaker person to control and defeat a larger, stronger opponent, while Judo focuses on takedowns.

 ## About us. ### Brazilian Jiu Jitsu for Sport and Self Defense

 Tulsa Combat Sports was founded in 2023 and is a comnglomerate of the Tulsa Judo Club and Balanced Bodies Jiu Jitsu, creating an all-inclusive training center that includes Judo, Jiu Jitsu, Muay Thai, Yoga, and Massage Therapy.

 ## Learn BJJ. ### Family friendly atmosphere

 As an affiliate of Triton Fight Center, Tulsa Combat Sports helps set the standard for Brazilian Jiu Jitsu instruction in Tulsa, Oklahoma.

 CLASSES

 ## Classes. ### What we teach

 ###

 ### Gi Fundamentals

 With technical detail and an emphasis on positional stability and control, these classes are a great place to start your journey for sport or self defense.

 ###

 ### No-Gi Fundamentals

 A great place to take your first class. The techniques provide students the skills needed to defend themselves with a focus on escaping poor positions.

 ###

 ### Judo – Tulsa Judo Club

 Judo directly complements jiu jitsu by improving takedown skills in a competitive evnronment so you can learn to get your opponent to the ground.

 ###

 ### Competition Class

 Competition classes build on the techniques taught in the fundamentals classes and help students build skills to prepare for tournaments.

 ###

 ### Drills for Skills Class

 A class focused on getting additional repetitions of techniques taught each week, with increasing resistance and movement for realistic training.

 ###

 ### Open Mat – Judo / BJJ

 Whether it's time to test your skills through repeated rounds against fully resisting opponents or you want to sharpen your skills, open mat is the place to be.
