# Classes Whether you’re stepping on the mats for the first time or leveling up your game, we’ve got a class for you.
 Book a free class View schedule

 ## Fundamentals
 Our Fundamentals class teaches essential techniques, positions, and concepts with an emphasis on leverage, control, and solid fundamentals. Recommended for beginners with no prior experience.

 ## Intermediate & Advanced
 For students with a solid foundation who are ready to level up. This class focuses on advanced techniques, dynamic positional sparring, and connected sequences with an emphasis on timing, strategy, and adaptation in live situations.

 ## Kids: Juniors Ages 8–12
 Designed for kids ages 8–12, combining fun and discipline while teaching foundational technique and effective self-defense. Through structured drills and games, kids build confidence, focus, and teamwork, while having a great time on the mats.

 ## Kids: Little Champs Ages 4–7
 Specially designed for children ages 4–7. This fun, engaging class introduces the basics of Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu through playful drills and positive reinforcement. Kids develop coordination, confidence, and respect in a supportive environment.
