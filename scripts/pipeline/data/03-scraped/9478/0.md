 ## Why Choose M3 Martial Arts?

 It started with a unique idea and we developed a program that sees the student as the hero of the story, not the style of martial arts we teach. Parents have come to us with an internal, external, and philosophical problem (s) their child was experiencing. An external problem of being bullied, an internal problem of lack of confidence to speak up to a bully, or the philosophical problem of “can I really defend myself?”
 Students and parents where looking for guides with the competency, authority, and empathy to help their children become their own hero. They wanted someone who truly understands, cares about, and has a genuine sincerity in their heart to understand these problems.
