 ### Open Mat at Calvert MMA Academy® Open Mat is a time for active members of Calvert MMA Academy® and Lineage BJJ® to train freely outside of structured classes. During Open Mat, students can drill techniques, roll with teammates, work on specific positions, or simply get extra mat time in a relaxed but respectful training environment.
 This session is open to all active members , regardless of rank or experience level. It’s an opportunity to improve your skills, ask questions, experiment with techniques, and build camaraderie with teammates.
 ### Guest Policy We welcome visiting grapplers and friends from other academies. However, all guests must be approved in advance before attending.
 If you would like to visit Open Mat:
 • You must contact us ahead of time through our website contact form or by phone
 • You must train at another academy or be invited by a current member
 • Walk-ins are not permitted
 This policy helps us maintain a safe, clean, and respectful training environment for our members.
 ### What to Expect • Open rolling and positional sparring
 • Time to drill techniques with teammates
 • Gi or No-Gi depending on the session
 • A relaxed but respectful atmosphere focused on learning and improvement
 Open Mat is a great way to get additional training time, sharpen your skills, and connect with the Team Lineage community.
 ### Who Can Attend • Active Calvert MMA / Lineage BJJ members
 • Approved visiting grapplers from other academies
 ### Interested in Visiting? If you are a visiting student from another academy and would like to join us, please contact us in advance so we can coordinate your visit.

 Adult Beginner Fundamentals (Ages 14+)
 This one-hour class is designed for adults and teens (ages 14+) who are new to Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu or looking to strengthen their foundational skills.  A one-hour weekly class designed exclusively for active first responders , providing practical self-defense techniques and scenario-based training.  ⚠️ This training does not supersede agency policies or state law. Techniques shared are meant to supplement professional training, not replace official departmental procedures.

 Instructor-Led Open Mat / Vale Tudo Integration (Ages 14+)
 Schedule: Every other Friday
 Time: 6:00 PM
 Location: Calvert MMA Academy® – 25 Dalrymple Road, Sunderland, MD
 This instructor-led session is designed to help students develop a more complete understanding of grappling, striking, transitions, and overall fight awareness in a controlled training environment.
 Depending on class size, space, and training goals, students may begin from the feet and work through scenarios that integrate striking with grappling exchanges , helping them better understand distance, pressure, control, and decision-making under more realistic conditions.
 This is not a brawl, and it is not a “tough guy” class.
 The purpose of this session is technical development, strategy, composure, and controlled application —not trying to hurt your training partner. Students are expected to train with discipline, respect, and control at all times.
 Because of the nature of the class, following the rules is mandatory. Anyone who cannot train safely, respectfully, and within the structure of the session will be removed from the class and may lose access permanently.
 This class is a valuable opportunity for students who want to build better timing, stronger awareness, and a more complete understanding of how striking and grappling connect in live situations.
