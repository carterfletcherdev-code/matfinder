BRAZILIAN JIU-JITSU

 - KIDS PROGRAMS Our kids classes are broken up into age 2 groups: 4-7-year-olds and 8-15-year-olds. Our classes instill children with core values and help them acquire new skills, teaching them how to defend themselves and avoid conflict, build confidence and discipline, all while having fun! - FUNDAMENTALS The fundamentals class was designed by JG Academy and based on a 32 class curriculum. If you are a beginner with little or no experience, this is the class for you! You will learn the basic movements, positions and concepts that are essential for Jiu-Jitsu. - ADVANCED This Advanced Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu program is our main group class. It is designed for students who have more experience and a firm grasp of basic Jiu-Jitsu concepts. This advanced program includes drilling and live sparring (rolling) with an emphasis on technique. - NO-GI No-gi class is designed for advanced students and consists of Jiu-Jitsu techniques and concepts adapted to make up for the lack of Gi to grab. Students must wear shorts and rashguard uniforms to attend.
