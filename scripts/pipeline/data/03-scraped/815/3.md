 ## Have Questions? We've got the answers right here!

 How Do I Join and Get Started?
 Joining SAS Team Jiu Jitsu is simple! Just contact us via phone, email, or our online form to schedule your first class. We offer a free trial session for new students so you can get a feel for our academy and training environment. Once you decide to continue, we’ll help you find the right membership plan that works best for you.

 When Should I Expect To See Results?
 Results vary from person to person, but many students begin to see improvements in their skills, fitness, and confidence after just a few weeks of consistent training. For more noticeable progress, including increased endurance and technical ability, most students see significant results within 3 to 6 months. Remember, consistency is key!

 Do You Provide Guidance on Nutrition & Recovery?
 Yes, we offer general advice on nutrition and recovery as part of our training program. Our instructors can provide tips to help you fuel your body for optimal performance and recover effectively to prevent injury. For more personalized nutrition plans, we recommend working with a certified nutritionist, and we can guide you toward resources if needed.

 Do I Need to Already Be Fit To Join?
 No, you don’t need to be in top shape to begin training at SAS Team Jiu Jitsu. BJJ is a great way to get fit, and we welcome beginners of all fitness levels. Our classes are designed to meet you where you are, and you’ll gradually build strength, flexibility, and stamina as you train.

 What Makes SAS Jiu Jitsu - Thorndale Different From The Other Gyms Near Me?
 SAS Team Jiu Jitsu – Thorndale stands out because of our focus on technique, community, and personal growth. We offer a welcoming environment where students of all levels can thrive, whether they’re training for self-defense, competition, or fitness. Our instructors are passionate about providing high-quality, personalized instruction, and we pride ourselves on creating a tight-knit, supportive family atmosphere.

 How Many Days Per Week Should I Attend?
 The number of days you train depends on your goals and schedule. Most students attend classes 2-3 times per week to see steady progress. However, we offer a flexible class schedule, so you can train more or less often as needed. Training consistently will help you build both skill and fitness faster!

 How Does The Program Work?
 Our program is designed to teach Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu in a progressive, step-by-step manner. We break down complex techniques into easy-to-learn parts, ensuring that you can build a solid foundation. Each class typically includes warm-ups, technique drills, live sparring (rolling), and cool-downs. We focus on both self-defense and sport Jiu-Jitsu, and we offer classes for all skill levels, from beginners to advanced practitioners.

 What Should I Expect In A Class?
 In each class, you can expect a combination of physical conditioning, technique instruction, and sparring (if you’re ready for it). Our instructors will guide you through specific techniques, breaking them down in a way that’s easy to understand. Beginners may focus more on drilling techniques, while more experienced students may engage in live rolling to test and refine their skills. You’ll also have the opportunity to ask questions and get feedback from the instructors throughout the class.
