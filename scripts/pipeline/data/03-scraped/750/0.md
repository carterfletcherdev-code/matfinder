 #### Class Level Explanations

 - All Levels* These classes do not have a separation by skill level. All are welcome to join! - Fundamentals* The classes are going to focus on fundamental level material. - Fund-Adv* The material we will cover ranges from fundamentals to advanced and everything in between. We welcome beginners to take the class, but please be aware that some of the material might be a little harder to grasp than in our Fundamentals classes. - Intermediate Members must be at an intermediate level or higher to join. Typically, 6 or more months of training. See a coach if you're unsure. - Advanced These classes are reserved for more experienced athletes and require the coach's permission to join. - Kids Classes The classes are for kids ages 5 - 12 only. - Teen Classes These classes are for young athletes ages 12-19 only. At this age, most of our teens are also welcome to join in on regular adult classes as well, but these classes offer them an opportunity to train in a teen-only environment 3 days a week.
