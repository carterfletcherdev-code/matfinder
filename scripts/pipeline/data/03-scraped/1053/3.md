 ## Beginner-Friendly Brazilian Jiu Jitsu Training

 Starting something new can feel intimidating, but our adult BJJ program is built specifically to help beginners succeed.
 Our instructors break techniques down step-by-step so students learn proper positioning, leverage, and control from day one. Classes emphasize safety, respect, and steady improvement.
 You will learn how to:
 • Escape bad positions
 • Control opponents using leverage instead of strength
 • Apply submissions safely
 • Build endurance and confidence on the mat
 No prior martial arts experience is required. Many of our members started with zero grappling experience .
