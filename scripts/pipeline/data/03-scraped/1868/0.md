## Kids Class Coventry Brazilian Jiu Jitsu Kids Program focuses on teaching kids ages 6 – 12 the fundamentals of Jiu Jitsu. Our bully-proof intensive class teaches discipline, leadership, respect, and confidence through jiu jitsu. We believe all children should be taught how to defend themselves and we look forward to speaking with you on how we can teach and mentor your children.

 ##### Insights - Gain Discipline & Focus - Gain Respect & Confidence - Learn Self-defense - Are challenged Physically and Mentally - Enjoy a Fun and Friendly Atmosphere
