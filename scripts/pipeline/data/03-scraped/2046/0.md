 ## About Us
 Fortis MMA was created to provide a first class environment for the instruction of mixed martial arts. We offer a complete line of instruction in the following: Gi Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, No-Gi, Kickboxing, Fitness, Wrestling, Mixed Martial Arts. We also specialize in private training for individual instruction or group training. Each member of our staff has been selected meticulously for their experience in martial arts. We have multiple UFC veterans and world champion coaches who have competed in the highest level in the world. They are here to provide you world class instruction in a first rate facility where safety and a comfortable learning environment are our top priority.
