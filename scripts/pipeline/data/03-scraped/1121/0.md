 About Us
 ## Liberty Jiu Jitsu opened its doors in 2021 with a mission to serve families in the Grovetown community.We offer programs for every stage, including Preschool Jiu Jitsu, Kids Jiu Jitsu (ages 5-7 and 8-12), and an Adults program where teens are welcome to train. In addition to Jiu Jitsu, we offer Muay Thai four days a week, and a free weekly Women’s Kickboxing class. Our goal is creating a space where everyone can grow, build confidence, and feel at home on the mats. ## At Liberty, we believe martial arts is about more than learning techniques. It’s about building character, resilience, and lifelong friendships in a supportive environment. Whether you’re stepping onto the mats for the first time or continuing your journey, our classes are designed to help every student progress with confidence.

 Programs at Every Stage
 ## Preschool Jiu Jitsu: Build coordination, focus, and confidence through structured play. ## Kids Jiu Jitsu (5 -7 and 8-12): Develop discipline, respect, and real self-defense skills. ## Teens & Adults: Train Jiu Jitsu, Muay Thai, and Kickboxing in a supportive, challenging environment. ## Free Women’s Kickboxing: A welcoming space to build strength and confidence.
