Our preschool and kindergarten students (ages 3-6) have unlimited energy and little fear. Everything is new, exciting, and challenging to them.
 Our Tiger Program offers a strong foundation in basic life skills such as:
 - Respect - Courtesy - Self-discipline - Sportsmanship - Following Directions - Improving Motor Skills

 Instructors use the safest, most fun instructional methods appropriate for this age group. This program works in conjunction with the Juniors program, thereby emphasizing the same important monthly themes and value systems.
