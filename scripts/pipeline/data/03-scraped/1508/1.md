Our Programs

 - Adult Jiu Jitsu Whether you're looking to get in shape, learn self-defense, or test yourself in competition, our adult BJJ classes offer high-level technique in a safe, and challenging enviornment. Gi and No Gi classes available. - Big Kids Jiu Jitsu For ages 8 & up, our big kids program teaches real Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu in a way that keeps things fun, structured, and challenging. Kids build confidence, focus, and athleticism while learning how to defend themselves — all while being part of a supportive team. Perfect for beginners and returning students alike. - Little Kids Jiu Jitsu For ages 5-7, we keep it playful, but our youngest students still learn the foundations of BJJ through games, drills, and movement. These classes help kids develop coordination, discipline, and body awareness while having a blast on the mats.
