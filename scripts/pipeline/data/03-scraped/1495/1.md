# Welcome to Rally Point BJJ

 At Rally Point BJJ, we have 3 core values guiding the way we operate:
 Integrity- We believe in doing the right thing, even when no one is looking.
 Community- We believe in taking care of each other--whether it is making the neighborhood a better place or assisting our training partners. No one makes progress completely on their own in this sport. It take a sense of community and partnership to grow.
 Respect- Respect is of utmost importance at our school. People from all different backgrounds are welcomed and greeted on our mats. We promote a culture of safety, inclusion and autonomy. When a student "taps out," they can be confident that their training partner will stop applying the submission.
 We're excited to see our members grow and make progress- on the mats and in life.
 We are a proud We Defy Foundation facility! This means we are certified to provide Brazilian Jiu Jitsu instruction to veteran athletes who have received scholarships through the We Defy Foundation. We Defy Foundation is a 501(c)(3) Non-profit organization that provides combat veterans coping with military connected disabilities a long term means to overcome their challenges through Brazilian Jiu Jitsu and fitness training. For more information on how to donate or apply for a scholarship, please visit https://wedefyfoundation.org
