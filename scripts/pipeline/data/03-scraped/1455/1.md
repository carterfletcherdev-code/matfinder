### Kids Jiu Jitsu

 Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu (BJJ) isn't just for adults—it’s amazing for kids too! This awesome martial art is all about ground grappling and taps into natural human instincts. When it comes to self-defense, BJJ teaches kids how to stand up to bigger opponents with confidence. But that’s just the beginning! BJJ helps kids build self-esteem, stay disciplined, channel their energy, and learn respect—all while being part of a super supportive community filled with future friends and mentors. We offer both Gi and No-Gi classes for ages 6-15. Let’s get rolling!

 #### Teaches Hard Work

 Don’t get us wrong, BJJ is a blast…but it’s also hard work! There’s no better feeling than setting a goal and finally crushing it!  #### Teaches Self-Defense

 As a parent, keeping your child safe is your number one priority. While we hope our kids never need to use these skills in everyday life, every good parent still wants their children to be prepared. That’s why our martial arts training focuses on equipping your kids with the self-defense techniques they need for any situation—from dealing with a neighborhood bully to facing a stranger on the street. But self-defense is just part of the story. In our classes, your child will also learn respect and self-discipline, understanding how to diffuse situations without violence whenever possible—and knowing when using force is necessary to stay safe.

 #### Building A Community

 At Studio X from the moment your child steps into our school, they’ll be welcomed into a supportive, inclusive community of instructors and fellow students who are all rooting for their success. You might be surprised to see just how strong the friendships in our BJJ classes are, especially since students regularly spar with each other! Jiu-Jitsu teaches kids both the excitement of winning and the humility of losing, and we make sure that respect for one another is a core part of every student’s journey—through all the ups and downs of training.

 ## FAQs

 - What should my child wear to class? For their fist class preferably wear bottoms that are free of pockets, belt loops, zippers, drawstrings or loose fabric. Wear a top that won’t fall over their face if they turn upside down. For the Gi class, we will loan them a Gi for the first class. - What can I expect in a kids’ Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu class? Our classes include warm-ups, technique instruction and technique drills with a partner. If there’s time at the end of class, kids participate in a fun game, that’s actually designed to teach them movement and coordination. - Any pre-class hygiene tips? Kids should be clean as free of smell as they can. Nails should be clipped for hands and feet. Gi and No-Gi gear should be washed and cleaned for each class. - I don’t want my child getting into fights. Won’t Jiu Jitsu make them want to fight? No. Jiu Jitsu has its roots in self-defense, but we teach children that violence is not the answer to their problems. - Is Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu safe for kids? Yes! When taught by qualified instructors, like those at Studio X, BJJ is a safe for kids. Our instructors prioritize safety, and students learn age-appropriate techniques, aligned with a standardized, age-appropriate curriculum. - Will my child get hit or hurt? What do you do to help prevent injuries? While minor injuries like bruises and strains are possible, our safety measures, supervision and responsible instruction help minimize risks. Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu doesn’t involve striking, so there’s no punching or kicking. - How long are kids’ classes? How often does my child need to attend? Our classes are between 45 minutes to an hour, depending upon age. We offer classes five days a week to accommodate parents’/kids’ busy schedules. Your child can attend one class each week or all five — it’s your discretion - Do I need to be present during my child’s class? While it’s not required, we’d love for you to come watch your child’s class! We have plenty of seating where you can see them in action. And if you need to take care of work during class, we’ve got you covered with spaces for charging your phone or working on your laptop. Feel free to make yourself at home!
