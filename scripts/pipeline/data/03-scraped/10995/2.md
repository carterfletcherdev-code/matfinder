## Child | Youth | Teen Programs

 At Bazzani Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, the mats are a place to learn self-defense and a space to grow in confidence, discipline, and respect. Our youth programs give kids the chance to explore Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu in a safe and supportive setting that keeps learning fun.
 Students practice through games, drills, and techniques that match their age and skill level. They begin to understand how to control their bodies, focus their minds, and work with their teammates. Every child is welcomed, whether it’s their very first class or they have years of experience.
 The goal is to help young athletes build strength that goes beyond training. With guidance from skilled instructors and encouragement from teammates, kids discover resilience, develop humility, and carry their confidence into daily life.

 # FAQ's # |
 # FAQ's |

 - What ages do you accept? We welcome children from 4 to 17 in our children, youth, teen, program. We group students by age and skill level so every child learns at a comfortable pace. - Is this safe for kids? Yes — our curriculum is built to emphasize control, respect, and movement before introducing submissions. Safety is our priority in every class. - When will my child start sparring or rolling? Younger age groups begin with drills, positional work, and games. As they mature, sparring is gradually introduced under close supervision. - Does my child need a gi or prior experience? No prior experience is needed. For the first few classes, athletic wear without zippers or buttons is fine. Eventually, your child will need a clean gi and belt to train. - How will my child benefit beyond self-defense? Children build confidence, discipline, teamwork, and resilience. Many parents see improvements in focus, respect, and responsibility in everyday life.
