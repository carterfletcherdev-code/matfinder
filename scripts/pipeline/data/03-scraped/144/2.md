 ## Frequently Asked Questions Please contact us if you cannot find an answer to your question.

 Is jiu jitsu right for me? Hell yea! BJJ is all about technique and little to do with size. Come spend a few days with us on the mats and we can help you keep the bullies at bay.

 Is Brazilian jiu jitsu what Joe Rogan does? Yes! And training with us will also make you a better podcaster.

 Do I have to be a bad ass or a guy to train BJJ? Not at all. We have people from all walks of life. Guys, girls, short people, giants, jocks, nerds...

 What is jiu jitsu? Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu (BJJ) is a grappling based martial art that focuses on ground fighting techniques and submission holds involving joint-locks and chokeholds. BJJ uses the principals of leverage, angles, pressure and timing, as well as knowledge of the human anatomy, in order to achieve a non-violent submission of one’s opponent. Jiu-jitsu is often referred to as a game of “human chess” because it exercises both the body and mind and you are constantly adapting your strategy to the movements of your opponent.

 Studies have shown that the majority of street fights end up on the ground which is why at RiceBros our goal is for you to feel confident and capable while fighting from the ground. Black belt instructor, Nicholas Rice has a knack for taking a complicated technique and breaking it down to a level that a beginner can understand and implement. We at RiceBros know that what sets Nicholas apart is that he teaches the little “nuggets” that makes the technique work!
