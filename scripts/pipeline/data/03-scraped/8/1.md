 # Getting Started How do I get started?
 You can simply pick a class and show up, or you can book through our website, any classes labeled "fundamental or All Levels" would be an appropriate class to attend. Our classes are designed to meet you where you are at, you don't need experience or knowledge of martial arts to join our classes.

 Our one week $7 trial period gives students an opportunity to try out our Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, Wrestling, MMA and Kickboxing classes before becoming a paying member. You may do as many classes as you like during your trial week. Those who decide to become a member are set up on automatic monthly payments.
 Please show up about 10 minutes early on your first day of training in order to sign the waiver form, pay your trial fee, get a tour of the gym and to get fitted for a loaner gi (uniform). We do ask that once you become a member, you purchase your own gi/kickboxing gear from any number of online websites, we also offer a wholesale gear bundle at sign on.
 If you have any questions please call us or send us a text at 308-627-0643 or email at kearneycombatsports@gmail.com.
