# Classes

 ## It's Time to Start Your Journey

 Discover strength, skill, and confidence as you embark on a transformative journey through martial arts. Whether you’re looking to master self-defense, improve your fitness, or build discipline, our classes are tailored to help you achieve your goals every step of the way.

 ##### Jiu Jitsu Master the art of leverage and technique in this grappling-focused martial art for all levels.

 ##### Muay Thai Dive into the art of kickboxing with powerful strikes and techniques using punches, kicks, elbows, and knees.

 ##### Wrestling Build strength, speed, and control with this dynamic grappling discipline.

 ##### Kids' Programs Empower children with discipline, confidence, and foundational martial arts skills in a fun and safe environment.

 ## Why Monarch?

 We are a martial arts academy dedicated to provided world-class training in a safe and supportive environment. We offer expert instruction for both kids and adults. Our mission is simple: to deliver the best martial arts experience possible while empowering our students to achieve their goals and become the best version of themselves. Whether you’re a beginner or an experienced fighter, we are committed to your growth, discipline, and overall success.
