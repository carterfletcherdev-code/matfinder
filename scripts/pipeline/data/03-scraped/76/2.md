 ## Common Questions

 Is this program suitable for beginners?
 Yes, this program is perfect for beginners!
 Our Kids Martial Arts Program is designed to accommodate all skill levels, including those who are new to martial arts. Our instructors focus on creating a supportive and engaging environment where beginners can learn foundational techniques at their own pace while building confidence and fitness.

 What should my child wear to class?
 New students can wear comfortable athletic clothing. Once enrolled, we recommend purchasing a uniform appropriate to the martial art style being practiced, which can be arranged through our academy

 How often are classes held, and what is the class duration?
 Classes are held multiple times a week, with each session lasting approximately 45 minutes to an hour. We offer flexible scheduling to accommodate your family’s needs.

 What age groups do you accept in your Kids Martial Arts Program?
 Our program is designed for children aged 4 to 12, with classes tailored to different age groups to ensure appropriate instruction and engagement.
