 ## What is Filipino Martial Arts?

 Filipino Martial Arts (FMA) is an all-encompassing martial art that uses striking and grappling techniques but is primarily a weapons base martial art. Filipino Martial Arts teaches one how to effectively use blunt and bladed weapons. This allows the student to be able to defend him or herself against an attacker using a weapon even if the student is unarmed. Filipino Martial Arts is both mentally and physically challenging and develops speed and fast reaction time.

 ### Why Train in Filipino Martial Arts?

 - Learn practical self-defense skills against weapon-based threats. - Develop speed, agility, and fast reaction times. - Build confidence through mastering advanced weapon techniques. - Improve mental focus and physical endurance. - Train in a martial art with deep cultural and historical roots.

 ### A Unique Weapon-Based Martial Art

 Filipino Martial Arts provides a distinct approach to self-defense by emphasizing weapon training alongside striking and grappling. The skills you develop in FMA prepare you for real-world situations while enhancing your overall physical and mental abilities.
