 ## CHECK OUT OUR BJJ CLASSES!

 ADULT BRAZILIAN JIU JITSU

 Our Adult Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu program offers a path for every individual seeking to enhance their fitness, self-defense skills, and overall well-being. Whether you're a beginner stepping onto the mats for the first time or a seasoned practitioner looking to refine your techniques, we have a program tailored to your needs. Choose from our two distinct tiers:

 - Fundamentals Program: With absolutely no experience required, this is the perfect place to start for those new to BJJ or seeking to solidify their foundational skills. Our Fundamentals Classes provide a comprehensive introduction to the basic techniques and principles of Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, Judo and Wrestling, emphasizing safety & proper execution.

 - Mastery Program: Designed for intermediate / advanced practitioners who want to take their Jiu-Jitsu journey to the next level. Our Mastery Classes delve deeper into the technique chains, concepts and strategies of grappling (with a special emphasis on Takedowns), pushing your limits and unlocking your full potential!

 In addition to our group classes, we also offer Private Lessons for those seeking personalized attention and accelerated progress. Our experienced instructors can work with you 1-on-1 to address specific goals, shorten your learning-curve of the basic techniques, or personalize a game plan tailored to your strengths & weaknesses

 KIDS BRAZILIAN JIU JITSU

 Our Kids Jiu-Jitsu Program is thoughtfully designed to foster your child's growth and development through the martial arts. Wrestling basics are heavily incorporated into our BJJ curriculum to develop well-rounded young athletes, and we offer these classes in two age-appropriate tiers:

 - Ninjas Program (Ages 5-8): Our Ninjas Program introduces young children to the fundamentals of grappling in a fun and safe environment. This youngest age-group learns primarily through engaging drills & matches that build their coordination, focus, and self-confidence.

 - Juniors Program (Ages 9-12): Our Juniors Program builds upon foundational skills, teaching children more advanced techniques, strategies, and the importance of discipline and respect.

 In addition to these Ninjas & Junior Beginner Classes, we also offer a very competitive Advanced Kids Program . These classes focus on more detailed techniques, drills, and high-volume Live Training to push the more experienced young competitors to the next level.

 ## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
 - What Is Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu (BJJ)? Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu (BJJ) is a martial art and combat sport that focuses primarily on grappling and ground fighting. It emphasizes technique and leverage over size and strength, making it effective for people of all shapes and sizes. BJJ training develops physical fitness, flexibility, and strength while also improving mental focus, discipline, and problem-solving abilities - Do I Need Any Experience To Get Started? Absolutely not. Most folks coming in for the first time have *never* trained before, and our academy welcomes beginners of all ages and fitness levels!We offer classes designed specifically for those new to BJJ, focusing on basic techniques and building a strong foundation. Our experienced instructors will guide you through every step of your journey! - What Should I Wear To My First Class? If you’re starting your Intro Trial, your Gi (uniform) will be waiting for you to change into! Just bring a water bottle and flip-flop/slides(Ladies - be sure to bring a sports bra and compression shorts to wear under the uniform) For Kickboxing Classes, just wear normal gym clothes, such as workout shorts w/ no pockets or yoga pants, and a t-shirt. Loaner gloves & shin-guards will be provided for your first two classes! - Is BJJ Safe For Children? Yes, BJJ is safe for children when taught by qualified instructors. Our Kids Programs focus on developing fundamental skills, building confidence, and fostering a positive learning environment. Safety is our top priority, and we implement age-appropriate techniques and supervision to ensure a secure training experience - What is your Drop-In Policy? We love having visitors, and never charge "mat fees" for cross-training. Visitors may reach us via cell/email about dropping in! - What Are The Uniform Requirements? Triangle Academy Uniform Requirements White, Blue or Black Gi only (IBJJF-compliant colors)No Shirt/Rash Guard under Gi (Sports Bras required for female athletes)Mouthguards required for Live Training
