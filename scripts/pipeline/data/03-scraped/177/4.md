# BUILDING THE NEW GENERATION OF JIU JITSU BLACK BELTS We Offer Elite Level Training in Brazilian Jiu Jitsu, Judo and Self-Defense
 ## Welcome To Masters Martial Arts! Masters Martial Arts School was created to build the new generation of black belts, where students will be able to learn the arts of Brazilian Jiu Jitsu, Judo and self-defense. Our mission is to continue to develop high-level practitioners, competitors and fighters as well as harness the power of martial arts to support and strengthen our local communities in Kailua, Hawai’i and internationally. We will strive to uplift youth by instilling self-discipline, confidence and teamwork, resilience and the ability to defend themselves. We are also passionate about empowering women through self-defense, helping them to build their sense of security, stability, and confidence in a supportive and respectful environment.
