### 69ef0cdc1969d #### Royal Kids 2(8-13 year olds)
 This class continues with the development of self-discipline and self-confidence. Children will review the fundamentals of jiu jitsu while also being introduced to more advanced techniques. There will be a bigger focus on the finer details of jiu jitsu concepts and movements. Through self-respect, respect for instructors, and respect for their peers they have become aware of their responsibilities as a student and training partner. Therefore, sparring will take place in this class.

 Goals:
 - Self-discipline - Self-confidence - Self-respect - Respect for others - Sparring - Improved coordination & fluid movment
 Program includes:
 - Fundamentals of BJJ - Introduction of Advanced BJJ - Sparring - BJJ games
