# 8 WEEK WOMEN’S JIU JITSU PROGRAM MONDAYS: 5:00PM - 6:00PM
 APRIL 6TH - MAY 25TH
 $100 for the entire program.
 Current members are free.
 Prorated pricing if you join after the program has started.

 ### What It’s About This 8-week program is for women looking for a comfortable and welcoming place to start their jiu-jitsu journey. Led by experienced female instructors, students will learn the foundational concepts of no-gi jiu-jitsu in a supportive, beginner-friendly environment. Whether your goal is self-defense, fitness, or simply trying something new, this is a great place to start.

 ### Details & Pricing AGES 15 AND UP
 This course will begin Monday, April 6th and end Monday, May 25th. Classes are every Monday at 5pm.
 No experience is required and you do not have to be athletic or in shape to join us. For brand new students who do not own a rashguard, you can wear a tshirt and leggings. Please make sure there is no metal on your clothing (snaps, zippers) and please remove all jewelry before getting on the mats. Bring a water bottle and slides/sandals as you will be removing your shoes before getting on the mat.
 The price of the entire program is $100, but students are welcome to join at any point during the 8 weeks and your registration fee will be prorated based on how many classes are left.
 A 2nd member on the same account is 50% off, and a 3rd member is 75% off.
