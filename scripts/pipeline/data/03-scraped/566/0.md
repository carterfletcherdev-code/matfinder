At John Machado Jiu-Jitsu Allen we teach the art of Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu and practical self-defense techniques while helping you achieve your personal goals. Much of what is learned in our academy translates to everyday life and our students often seeing positive changes in their lives on and off of the mats.
 We welcome all experience levels (from absolute beginners to high level practitioners) and we have classes for all ages (teens, adults, and seniors). Our programs offer a simple, yet fast paced approach to learning Jiu-Jitsu, while getting in shape and having fun.
 Our academy is family oriented and our students work together to achieve their goals.
