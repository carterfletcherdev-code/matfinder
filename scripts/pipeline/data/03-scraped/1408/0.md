# Welcome to Montrose Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu Whether you're taking your first steps onto the mats or looking for a new home to continue your journey, Montrose BJJ offers what you're seeking. For those just beginning, we understand the mix of excitement and uncertainty. That feeling of potential waiting to be unlocked. Our fundamentals program builds a solid foundation where questions are welcomed and progress is celebrated, no matter how small.
 For experienced practitioners, we provide the technical depth, training partners, and challenging environment needed to refine your game. Our community includes competitors and dedicated students who push each other to new heights while maintaining the supportive atmosphere that makes jiu-jitsu special.
 What unites us all—from white to black belt—is the pursuit of something deeper than just technique. It's the daily practice of facing challenges head-on. The focus and resilience that transfers from the mats to every aspect of life. The community that becomes family.
 At Montrose BJJ, we offer gi and no-gi jiu-jitsu, wrestling, and Muay Thai for all ages and skill levels. Whatever your goals—self-defense, competition, fitness, or personal growth—you'll find the coaching and community to help you achieve them.
 Your journey continues or begins here. Come join us on the mats.

 Finding Montrose BJJ
 Our gym is located at 1445 Hawk Pkwy, Montrose, CO 81401, we share a building with Western Slope Appliance, but have completely separate entrances. Here's how to find us:
 From S Townsend Ave (the main road):
 - Turn onto Hawk Parkway from S Townsend Ave - Look for the Western Slope Appliance building – we share this location - Western Slope Appliance faces east toward S Townsend Ave (the main road) - Our Montrose BJJ entrance is on the opposite (west) side of the building - Drive around to the back (west side) of the building to find our dedicated entrance - Look for Montrose BJJ signage to guide you to the right door
 Parking: Plenty of free parking is available in our shared lot. Park near the back/side entrance for easiest access to our gym. Please do not block the ramp to the right of our building entrance- thank you in advance!
 We look forward to seeing you on the mats!
