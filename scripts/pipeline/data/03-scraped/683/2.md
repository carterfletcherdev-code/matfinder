 #### Double Five Mid Cities ## Our History
 Double Five Mid-Cities was founded by Westin Vance and the Collins Brothers—Dylan and Tanner Collins—with a clear mission: to create a gym where athletes of all ages and backgrounds could train with intensity, purpose, and mutual respect. What began in Bedford, Texas, as a small but passionate training space has evolved into the premier martial arts academy in the area.
 Led by IBJJF top-10 black belt competitor Westin Vance and the Collins Brothers—decorated brown belts in Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu and high-level wrestling standouts with deep roots in competitive grappling and coaching—Double Five is built on a foundation of technical excellence, grit, and community. The team later expanded to include UFC veteran Victor Altamirano, bringing world-class MMA experience into the fold, and Tanner’s wife, Ashley Collins—a high-level female black belt who leads our women’s BJJ program with expertise, leadership, and a passion for empowering others.
 Together, we’ve cultivated a multidisciplinary environment offering elite Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, competitive wrestling, dynamic boxing, and authentic Muay Thai. Our programs include private training, youth development, and specialized seminars in self-defense and first-responder tactics.
 From the start, we’ve held to our core values: train hard, train smart, and uplift the room with positive energy. Through expert coaching, a tight-knit culture, and a commitment to growth, Double Five Mid-Cities has become the go-to gym for those who want to get stronger, sharper, and more confident—on the mat and in life.
