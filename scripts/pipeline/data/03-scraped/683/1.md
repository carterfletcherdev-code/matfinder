 ## Kids Jiu-Jitsu
 Our Kids Jiu-Jitsu Program is designed to help children build confidence, discipline, and coordination in a fun and engaging environment. Through structured lessons, interactive drills, and exciting games, kids learn the fundamentals of Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, including self-defense techniques, positional control, and problem-solving under pressure. Our experienced coaches focus on teaching respect, perseverance, and teamwork, ensuring that every child develops both physical skills and valuable life lessons. Whether your child is looking to gain confidence, improve athleticism, or simply have fun while learning a practical martial art, our program provides a safe, supportive, and energetic atmosphere for all skill levels.

 ## Adult Jiu-Jitsu GI
 Our Adult Gi Jiu-Jitsu Program is designed for all levels, focusing on technical precision, strategy, and adaptability. With an emphasis on control, leverage, and submissions, this program helps students develop a complete game from top, bottom, and standing positions. Whether you’re looking to improve fitness, learn self-defense, or compete, our structured training ensures steady progress in a supportive and challenging environment. Classes blend fundamentals, advanced techniques, and live training, giving you the tools to build confidence, sharpen your skills, and excel in the art of Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu.
 Come train with us and experience the power of the Gi—your first week is FREE!

 Required gear: Jiu Jitsu Gi and rash guard.

 ## Adult Jiu-Jitsu No-Gi
 Our Adult No-Gi Jiu-Jitsu Program is built for all skill levels, whether you’re looking to get in shape, learn self-defense, or compete at a high level. With a focus on developing a complete grappling game, this program ensures you become dangerous from every position—standing, on top, and off your back. You’ll learn how to wrestle effectively, control opponents from dominant positions, and escape or attack from bottom, giving you the skills to dictate the fight wherever it goes. Training is tailored to each individual, ensuring that beginners build confidence and fundamentals while more experienced students push their limits with high-intensity rounds. Classes emphasize practical techniques, smart strategy, and progressive training, helping you develop strength, skill, and adaptability at your own pace. Whether you’re stepping onto the mats for the first time or leveling up your game, you’ll train in a fun, challenging, and supportive environment.
 Come train with us and experience the power of No-Gi Jiu-Jitsu—your first week is FREE!

 ## Wrestling
 The Double Five Mid-Cities Wrestling Program, led by the Collins Brothers (Dylan and Tanner Collins), offers elite wrestling instruction for all ages, starting at 5 years old. Our youth programs (ages 5-12 and 13-18) focus on Folkstyle and Freestyle/Greco-Roman wrestling, preparing athletes to excel in USA Wrestling and UIL competitions. For adults (18+), our training is designed to sharpen wrestling for BJJ and MMA, emphasizing takedowns, control, and defensive skills tailored for submission grappling and cage fighting. With a strong focus on fundamentals, we ensure wrestlers build a solid foundation, technical precision, and a competitive mindset. Whether you’re stepping on the mat for the first time or looking to dominate in competition, this program will take your wrestling to the next level.
 Join us for a FREE week trial today!
 Required gear: Rash guard and shorts (no pockets).
