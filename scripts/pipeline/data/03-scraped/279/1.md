 ## Jiu Jitsu at Vitruvian #### What is Jiu Jitsu? Jiu Jitsu, often referred to as the "gentle art," is a martial art that focuses on leverage, joint locks, and submission holds. Unlike striking-based disciplines, Jiu Jitsu emphasizes controlling your opponent and using their energy against them. It's a highly effective form of self-defense and is a key component in modern mixed martial arts (MMA).

 #### Benefits of Jiu Jitsu #### Benefits of Jiu Jitsu
 Training in Jiu Jitsu offers numerous physical and mental benefits. You'll develop strength, flexibility, and endurance while learning to stay calm and focused under pressure. Jiu Jitsu also teaches problem-solving skills and enhances your ability to think strategically—valuable traits both on and off the mat.
