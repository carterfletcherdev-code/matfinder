 # SINCE 2015 ### Spectrum Jiu Jitsu is dedicated to physical empowerment and helping individuals find their potential through the sport of Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu. Spectrum was founded by teaching professionals with a passion for helping people of all walks of life reach their goals.

 ## WHAT MAKES SPECTRUM DIFFERENT?

 PHILOSOPHY

 At Spectrum Jiu Jitsu we provide instruction for all walks of life: from competitors to fitness or self-defense minded individuals, to people just looking for a fun new hobby. At Spectrum, it is our mission to help you reach your goals, wherever they may fall on the spectrum.

 COMPETITION

 Spectrum Jiu Jitsu was founded by the Miller brothers and specializes in teaching the competitive sport of Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu. Spectrum is the most decorated BJJ school in the area and consistently produces regional and national champions of all ages.

 PEDAGOGY

 At Spectrum Jiu Jitsu we implement the latest concepts, pedagogy, and teaching methodology so you can learn BJJ in the most efficient way possible using the ecological approach. Our aim is to train you to improve your mind and body through the art of Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu.
