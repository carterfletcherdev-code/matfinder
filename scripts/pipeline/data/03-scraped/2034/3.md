 ## COMMON QUESTIONS & ANSWERS

 ### 1. What is the best way to get started? The best way to get started is to walk through the front door! Please know that we all started as beginners and everyone is so welcoming. Walk through that door and everything else will fall into place.

 ### 2. Do I need previous training? Nope! We have beginners who have never lifted weights or been involved in any martial arts all the way to elite level athletes. The cool thing about Spartan Nation is everyone cheers each other on. The hardest part for most people is walking through the door. After that, it’s just an awesome adventure of self-improvement.

 ### 3. What kind of gear will I need? For your first time, all you need is comfortable workout clothes and a water bottle. For equipment, you can borrow some of our loaner gear if you do not have your own. For Muay Thai we use boxing gloves and shin guards. MMA we use hybrid mma gloves. Mitt Boxing and Cardio Kickboxing use Boxing gloves. Submission Wrestling and all fitness classes have no additional equipment.

 ### 4. Do you have members who compete? We do have members who compete locally, nationally, and internationally. We also have members who do not compete. We love all of our members and totally understand everyone has their own goals and reason for joining the Spartan Nation. Come. Find your Greatness.

 ### 5. What are your class times, and do I need to book in advance? Our Class times are listed in our class schedule section here on the website. No reservations are required for class at this time. Just show up!

 ### 6. Do you offer private training or one-on-one coaching? Our trainers are world class and we do offer private training. If you are interested in getting customized work for your goals, schedule a private training session with a Coach.

 ### 7. Are your membership options affordable and flexible? Yes! We want everyone to be able to train and experience the benefits of Spartan Nation. We are very proud of the class variety we offer in a small gym and want to bring the benefits to as many as possible. Come down and let’s get you started.

 ### 8. Do you offer kids' classes or programs suitable for families? At 4:30 PM Monday through Thursday, we offer side by side kids and adults classes. We designed our program this way so families can come and workout together.

 ### 9. Why join Spartan Nation? We offer a wide variety of classes led by expert level instructors. If you show up, you will improve. (And you’ll have an amazing, fun journey too.)

 ### 10. What is the main goal of Spartan Nation? Sparta Nation exists to emotionally balance, physically transform, and mentally elevate. Come. Find your Greatness.
