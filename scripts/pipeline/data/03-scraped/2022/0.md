 #### Welcome To ### Logic Jiu-Jitsu Club
 Welcome to Logic Jiu-Jitsu Club in Tacoma, Washington! We are a 4,500 square-foot family-oriented Jiu-Jitsu academy for everyone – from those who want a laid-back experience to ultra competitors. Our mission has been and always will be to be the best Jiu-Jitsu academy in Washington. This goes for both the training experience and our competitive team! We want students to enjoy martial arts – whether they are competing or are looking to lose weight, gain confidence, courage, or gain a sense of belonging. We strive to maintain traditional Jiu-Jitsu customs while developing new and advanced techniques to push the next generation.

 ### What Sets Logic Jiu-Jitsu Club Apart
 We’re more than just a place to train—we’re a supportive, high-energy community built on strong values, expert instruction, and a clean, welcoming environment where every student can thrive.

 ##### Community Anyone who joins our Jiu-Jitsu academy is welcomed with open arms. You can train alongside like-minded people who are looking to make themselves better.

 ##### Facility Our 4,500 square-foot facility is tailored to help all our students grow. You can come in and feel comfortable in a training environment kept meticulously clean.

 ##### Relaxed We don’t want anyone starting Jiu-Jitsu classes to be tense. Our environment is laid-back and allows you to work hard while not feeling stressed out.

 ##### Energy We are always keeping the atmosphere energetic and positive. While training smart and hard, we play up-beat music to boost your morale and get you ready to work.

 ##### Competition Our competition team is the best around. We have built the top team in Washington through our strong technical training.

 ##### Instructors Our coaches bring years of Jiu-Jitsu experience providing clear, personalized guidance to help every student grow at their own pace.
