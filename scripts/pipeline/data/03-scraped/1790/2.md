# Atos HQ Open Mat March 27, 2018

 Open mat is the perfect opportunity to sharpen your Jiu Jitu by drilling, and problem solving with your teammates. Most academies offer an open mat and we are not exception. Our open mat is every Saturday from 11am-1pm. There’s no structured class so you are free to come and go as you please during open mat, which creates a chill, fun environment. A lot of people come from other gyms as well, giving everyone the opportunity to cross train and get some rolls with new people. It’s also a great way to meet other people in the gym that you may not usually have a conversation with because you’re focused on training. It’s hard sometimes to really get to know people just by going to class. Open mat is a great way to over come this, and really start getting to know everyone in the academy.
 If you are interested in learning more about our program please give us a call at 858-292-5040 or email us at staff@atosjiujitsuhq.com and get started on your FREE one week trial today.
