### WHAT IS BRAZILIAN JIU-JITSU

 Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu is one of the most valuable and thriving martial arts for self-defense, grappling competitions, physical health, and mental resilience. It is an efficient ground combat method focusing on grappling techniques, including take-downs, joint locks, and many other submissions.
 Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu is more than just a martial art. It can support you in managing stress while cultivating patience, problem-solving, discipline, humility, confidence, and mental toughness.
 BJJ will help you stretch your limitations and get out of your comfort zone while leaving a smile on your face.
