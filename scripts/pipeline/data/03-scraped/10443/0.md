 ## GRACIE JIU-JITSU
 Jiu-Jitsu: the gentle art, often called “The Art of Human Chess," is arguably the most effective system of self-defense ever devised. Unlike many martial arts that rely on brute strength, striking, or rigid forms—Jiu-Jitsu focuses on leverage, timing, and technique to overcome larger opponents. It is a "living" art; every session is a puzzle where you learn to navigate physical problems with a calm, analytical mind. This unique blend of high-intensity physical engagement and deep mental problem-solving creates an experience that is as much about intellectual growth as it is about physical prowess...
