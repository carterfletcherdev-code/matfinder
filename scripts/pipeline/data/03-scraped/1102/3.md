 ## Frequently Asked Questions Please contact us if you cannot find an answer to your question.

 Am I too old to start BJJ? No, BJJ is practiced by people of all ages.

 Do I need to be in shape to start BJJ? No, BJJ is actually a great way to get in shape.

 I have trained in another martial art. Will it transfer to BJJ? Maybe. Arts such as Judo, Sambo, and Wrestling can usually transition fairly easy to BJJ. Other traditional stand up arts such as Karate, Taekwondo, and Kung Fu do not. But don't be discouraged, BJJ will help round out your skill set if you do or have practiced a tradition stand up martial art.
