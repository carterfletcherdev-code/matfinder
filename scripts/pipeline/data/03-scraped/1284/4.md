### We are Elite, a community of dedicated Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu practitioners based in Metro Detroit.

 ## Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu classes in Livonia, Michigan.

 Conveniently located in Livonia, Michigan and serving the communities of Plymouth, Canton, Northville, Novi, Westland, Garden City, and everywhere else in metro Detroit, Elite Jiu-Jitsu offers high-quality instruction in Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu. Our instructors are dedicated practitioners and their philosophy is to always remain a student of the martial arts . We serve our students with their growth and proficiency as the prime objective.
 We are grateful that you have taken the time to visit our website. Please have a look around. If you have an interest in checking out our school, please feel free to give us a call or send us an e-mail. We look forward to seeing you at Elite!
