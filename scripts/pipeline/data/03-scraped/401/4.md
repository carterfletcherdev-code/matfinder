# JG ACADEMY TRACY, CA

 At JG Jiu Jitsu and Fitness our aim is to bring high quality, accessible and affordable Brazilian Jiu Jitsu to the Tracy area community. Our unique approach to combining traditional and innovative Jiu Jitsu methods and techniques is what separates us from every other academy in the Central Valley.

 Our instructors have a wide range of expertise, and are here to train you through every step of the learning process. Not only are our instructors world champion grapplers but also members of the community and local business owners. Whether you’re joining us as a beginner or a professional, there’s always more room to perfect your form. Browse our site to learn more.
 Above all else our main goal is to provide a fun and safe environment for all people to come and enjoy. We always strive to deliver a positive and family orientated atmosphere.
