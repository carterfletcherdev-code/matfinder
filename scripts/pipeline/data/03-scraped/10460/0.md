 Dear Friends,SOMA Academy is an incredible place filled with great people who love training and improving together.If you're curious about jiu-jitsu, the best first step is to schedule a quick visit using the calendar. The visit is free and only takes about 10 minutes.During your visit you’ll:• Watch part of a class• Meet the team and see the academy• Learn how our beginner program works• Ask any questions you may haveThere’s no training during this visit, so it’s a relaxed and easy way to see what we do and decide if it feels like a good fit for you.We look forward to meeting you.The SOMA Team |
