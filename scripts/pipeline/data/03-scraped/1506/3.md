 Journey BJJ's Brandon Gagnon becomes 2019 IBJJF Master World's Champion 10/3/2019 1 Comment Journey Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu's Brandon Gagnon competed in the Blue Belt Master 1 Medium-Heayweight Division with 46 other competitors from around the world and came out on top finishing in first place in a 47 man bracket at the 2019 IBJJF World Master Jiu-Jitsu Championship. Brandon won all 5 matches via submission (4 triangle chokes and 1 loop choke) on his way to his 2019 World Championship title.
