 # Meet Your Instructor ## Head Professor - Cleidson Ramos
 Cleidson Ramos is a 2nd Degree Black Belt from Sao Paulo, Brazil. Cleidson started Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu at 14 years old. In 2012, Professor Cleidson opened his first BJJ academy in Brazil. In 2015, Professor Cleidson was promoted to Black Belt. Professor moved to Texas in 2017 to teach BJJ for about 6 months before moving to California.
 Awards and Titles:
 - 8x BJJ State Champion - Brazilian national champion - Pan American Champion (CBJJE) - 2x South American champion (CBJJE) - 3 Gold Medalist - International Teams Championships (WLPJJ) - Argentina Open Champion - Silver medalist - South American (IBJJF) - Silver medalist - North Carolina Open (IBJJF) - Silver medalist - Los Angeles Open (IBJJF) - Golden medalist - San Diego Open (IBJJF) - Golden medalist Nashville Open (IBJJF) - Golden medalist - Chicago Open (IBJJF) - Silver medalist - American National - Golden medalist - San Jose Open (IBJJF)
