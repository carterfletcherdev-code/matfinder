 # Upcoming Events # Mahamed Aly Seminar Friday, April 10th | 6:30 - 8:30pm
 We are excited to announce our upcoming Seminar with Professor Mahamad Aly on Friday, April 10th! Professor Aly is an accomplished Black Belt and 4x IBJJF World Champion. He is the co-owner of One Way Martial Arts in Alexandria, Virginia.
 Come and share the mats with us and learn from this incredible athlete and Professor!
 This event is open to Roma JJA & Kolosa JJA students in the Adults and Kids Advanced Programs. Please stop by the front desk, or click the button below to send us an email and reserve your spot!
