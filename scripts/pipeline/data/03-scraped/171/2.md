 # Kids Advanced

 Gis may not have any other school or affiliate patches. Gis are available to rent or for purchase at the academy.

 ## Kids Advanced Open to colored belts ages 7-13 (with instructor’s permission)
 Our Kids Advanced Class is ideal for the motivated student who wants to focus on competitive Jiu Jitsu. Professor Kawika leads these young athletes to have a healthy attitude, set goals, overcome adversity, and take pride in their accomplishments. Students continue to be challenged as they master detailed techniques; building strength, self confidence, and determination. They develop a strong mindset as they learn about competitive jiu jitsu, point systems and strategies. This team travels off-island, representing Roma Jiu Jitsu Academy at tournaments across the State and on the mainland.
 Class Length | 1 Hour
