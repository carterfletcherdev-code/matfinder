 ## The History of Taekwondo

 Taekwondo has a long history in Korea. Soo Bak is a combat technique from Asia that began as a Tae Kyon martial art and eventually became known as Soo Bak. Koguryo soldiers used these techniques and subsequently taught them to the legendary Hwarang of Silla.

 Korea's early history includes walled city-states and rural communities which developed into confederated kingdoms - each loosely ruled by a central entity. These earlier civilizations were often influenced by and engaged with the Chinese. Finally, after many years, a confederation of three powerful kingdoms emerged and created a time of cultural, political, and military advancements. This era is historically significant because it was when Taekwondo began, and much of the martial tradition we see today came into being.

 ## The Many Benefits Of Martial Arts Training

 Martial arts classes promote a healthy, active lifestyle. Regular physical activity improves mood and reduces stress.

 Over time, practicing martial arts improves your physical fitness, self-esteem, and confidence.

 Intense martial arts workouts may help you lose weight. One hour of martial arts can burn more than 600 calories!

 In addition, martial arts classes and training are ideal for building functional strength and lean muscle mass through whole-body movements.

 Students learn fundamental skills helpful for multiple aspects of life, including academic pursuits, athletic endeavors, and creating higher self-awareness.

 Martial arts training improves attention. As a result, students reap the benefits of better concentration to excel academically.

 Martial arts teaches much more than self-defense. It creates positive connections with instructors, elders, and family members.

 Martial arts students aren't content with making excuses. Instead, they learn by repetition and having a good time!
