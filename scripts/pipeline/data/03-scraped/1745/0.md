 ## PROGRAMS AND FACILITIES TO FIT YOUR FAMILY'S NEEDS

 ## Welcome to Primate Jiu Jitsu Primate Jiu Jitsu is a family-focused martial arts academy offering high-level training for kids, teens, and adults —from complete beginners to experienced athletes. Our goal is simple: help students build confidence, skill, and toughness in a positive, supportive environment.
 ### Programs for Every Age - Ages 4–6 (Play-Jitsu)Our youngest program uses games, movement, and structured fun to introduce Jiu Jitsu fundamentals. Classes are designed to build coordination, balance, listening skills, and confidence—without kids feeling like they’re in a “serious” class. - Ages 7–12Kids develop real grappling skills, self-defense awareness, and discipline while learning how to train with control and respect. This program helps channel energy in a constructive way and builds confidence that carries into everyday life. - Teens & AdultsBeginner-friendly fundamentals through advanced training, with a curriculum designed to work for hobbyists, competitors, and parents training alongside their kids.
 No experience required—just a willingness to learn and show up.
 ### A Complete Grappling Curriculum Our curriculum blends the most effective techniques and strategies from Brazilian Jiu Jitsu, Wrestling, and Judo . Students learn how to control opponents in every phase of grappling—standing, takedowns, scrambles, top control, bottom positions, and submissions—so they’re competent and dangerous in all situations.
 ### Safe, Clean, Spacious Training Environment Primate Jiu Jitsu features 2,500 square feet of sprung mat space , designed to reduce impact and keep training safer for kids and adults alike. The academy is clean, organized, and intentionally designed for focused training—not overcrowded chaos.
 Additional amenities include:
 - A dedicated exercise area - Two on-site showers - A strong emphasis on cleanliness and hygiene
 ### Flexible Adult Training Options We offer adult Jiu Jitsu classes three times per day to fit busy schedules:
 - 6:00 AM - 12:00 PM - 5:15 PM & 6:15 PM
 Our 5:15 PM fundamentals class allows parents to train Jiu Jitsu while their kids are on the mat next door , creating a shared experience without sacrificing family time.
 ### Muay Thai Training In addition to grappling, we offer Muay Thai classes five times per week . These sessions build striking fundamentals, conditioning, and mental toughness while complementing our grappling program.
 ### A Culture Built on Growth Our instructors are experienced, passionate, and committed to helping every student improve—regardless of age, fitness level, or background. You’ll train in a welcoming, no-ego environment where safety, learning, and long-term progress come first.
 Whether you’re enrolling your child, training for yourself, or doing both—Primate Jiu Jitsu is built for families who want real skills and real growth.
