## Meet Your Professor
 Hello, I am Professor Gabriel Rueda of InMotion Grappling. I have studied and trained in the arts for 14 years. The last 7 years I have learned through the teachings of Sensei Erik Paulson, following the CSW curriculum in Catch Wrestling, STX Kickboxing and Brazilian Jiu Jitsu. Once a week I attend training with my Sensei to ensure I stay educated so I can continue to share/teach my students with the best and latest techniques.
 I have previous experience in coaching kids and adults. I have attended and continue to attend 3-4 day training sessions through the Coach Development Program at CSW, as well as the five day CSW World conference camp.
 I believe our children are the future and pathway to success.
