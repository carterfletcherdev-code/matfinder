 # Call or walk in to schedule your free trial! Open six days a week, National City Jiu Jitsu Club offers the most comprehensive martial arts training program in the area with morning and evening classes available as well as bookings for seminars, workshops and private training. We offer a full schedule of classes for kids and adults in Jiu Jitsu, Muay Thai, Boxing, MMA, Self Defense and Defensive Tactics(LE/Military). We have the most flexible membership packages available with no annual contracts and we offer membership packages at reduced rates for our military/veterans and first responders/nurses. We have world renowned coaches with multiple championships and a fast growing student body that ranges from beginners to professional athletes. Come train with us!
