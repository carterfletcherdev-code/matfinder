# Kids Classes at 10th Planet Allentown Kids classes are a great way for your child to build confidence, learn discipline, and have fun while learning practical forms of self defense. Children age 7 and up can join our kids classes and will train with other children until they are old enough to start in our adult programs.
 # Benefits of Martial Arts for Children There are many benefits of martial arts for children. At Finishers we try to encompass all of them. You can expect your child to build confidence and friendships that will go beyond the mat. They will also get great exercise while learning tested forms of self defense in a safe and nurturing environment.
 Are Martial Arts right for my child?
 YES!
 Everyone is right for martial arts. It doesn’t matter how big or small, shy or bold your child is. Anyone can participate and become successful in martial arts.
