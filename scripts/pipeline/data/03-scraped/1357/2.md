# Welcome to Midtown Grappling Academy Our world-class Brazilian Jiu Jitsu program is located in the heart of Midtown in Memphis, TN. The team is led by Alexandre Meadows, a black belt in the art under Marcelo Garcia.

 Our Programs

 - Adult Gi Our adult gi BJJ classes offer comprehensive instruction in Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu fundamentals and advanced techniques, combining movement, specific drilling, and live sparring. Whether you're a complete beginner or experienced grappler, you'll develop practical self-defense skills, improve your fitness, and join a supportive community dedicated to continuous learning. - Adult No Gi Our adult no-gi classes focus on fast-paced grappling without the traditional gi, emphasizing positional control, wrestling takedowns, and dynamic submission chains. Perfect for MMA enthusiasts or those seeking a different challenge, these classes build explosive cardio, adaptable technique, and a modern approach to submission grappling. - Kids BJJ Our kids BJJ classes teach practical self-defense and grappling techniques in a fun, engaging environment that builds confidence, discipline, and respect. Through age-appropriate instruction, interactive games, and supportive coaching, children develop physical fitness, problem-solving skills, and lasting friendships on the mat.

 ## About the Gym

 - What is MGA? Located in the historic Midtown neighborhood of Memphis, MGA boasts one of the largest mat spaces with the largest volume of jiu jitsu competitors. MGA also pays homage to the Professor Alex’s jiu jitsu lineage through Professor Marcelo Garcia. - Why this Gym? We live by the credo that “Iron sharpens iron”, and stepping into MGA, you will notice high level talent teaching and training here daily. We prioritize excellent technique, high level conditioning, and a competitive mentality for both those who complete around the world, and those looking for a healthy and fulfilling way to stay in shape.
