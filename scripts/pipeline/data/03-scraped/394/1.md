 ### ACADEMY ETIQUETTE

 Hygiene : Wear fresh, clean uniforms for each class. Wear a rash guard under gi uniform. Maintain personal hygiene, including trim nails and use of deodorant. No bare feet in the gym.
 Punctuality : Arrive on time, but better late than never. If late, wait at the edge of the mat until acknowledged by the instructor to join.
 Respect : Bow when entering and leaving the mat. Bow and shake the hand of each person on the mat who was there before you.
 Mat Behavior : Do not be a disruption during instruction. Absolutely no shoes on the mat.
 Training Partners : Shake hands before and after working with a partner. Practice courtesy and respect. Communicate injuries and intensity level expectations.
 Safety : Practice techniques with control, physically and emotionally.
 Cleaning : Practice ownership of the gym and help clean the mats after class. Clean up after yourself after bathroom use and pick up after yourself.
 Attitude : Maintain a positive, humble and respectful attitude. Be open to learning and constructive criticism. Support and encourage your teammates.
