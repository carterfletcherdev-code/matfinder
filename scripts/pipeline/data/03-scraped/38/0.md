# FREE TRIAL

 Here at Myers Kenpo Jiu-Jitsu Academy we enjoy building relationships with our clients, starting things off with 2 private lessons. We will give you / your child a free lesson and details about memberships and tuition.
 In our first introductory class the student will be taught some basic moves, along with basic etiquette. The student will be expected to return to their second lesson being well practiced with the previously learned material. They will be evaluated on the basic moves, as well as respect, and understanding of self-discipline.
 Share your goals and interests with us, it is our mission to help students empower themselves.
 Contact us today to start your FREE class session. If you are interested in the Lafayette location, please book a session Monday - Thursday and arrive at the Lafayette location. If you are interested in the West Lafayette location, please book you lesson on a Friday and arrive at the West Lafayette Location.
 You will receive a return email containing a link, so you can conveniently book your lesson directly into the Dojo's schedule.
