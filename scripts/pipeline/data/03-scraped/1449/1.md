# Elevate striking system

 Elevate Striking Systems is our stand-up combat program in Grand Junction, Colorado, designed to build real-world striking skills through Muay Thai and Kickboxing fundamentals, taught in a safe, structured, and supportive environment.
 Whether you’re brand new to striking or an experienced martial artist looking to sharpen your stand-up, this program meets you where you are.
