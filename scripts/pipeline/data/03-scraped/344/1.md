 ## About Kids Martial Arts
 Our Kids Martial Arts program is for 4-year-olds and up and is based on mixed martial arts, or MMA, which was made popular in the UFC and is becoming the fastest-growing martial art for self-defense and helping kids become bully proof.
 It consists of elements from kickboxing, muay thai, Brazilian jiu-jitsu, judo, and high school-style wrestling to make it the most effective self-defense system! We know your time matters, so we don't waste class time doing fancy forms and breaking boards. We are a hands-on style of self-defense.
 You child will be learning practical skills on day one!

 ### 4-7 YEAR OLD LITTLE NINJAS Our Little Ninjas Martial Arts program is for boys and girls ages 4-7, and teaches the fundamentals of self defense with techniques from Muay Thai, wrestling and Brazilian Jiu-jitsu.
 For this age group, we maintain a higher instructor to student ratio as these kids require a little more personal attention.
 Combined with smaller class sizes, game based learning helps students stay focused, develop discipline, and prepare them for more advanced training as they grow.

 Little Ninja students spend class time developing strength, conditioning, and agility through fun, age appropriate drills, while learning basic self defense techniques and introductory sparring.
 Students will also work in pairs and small groups to build focus, teamwork and social skills.

 ### 8+ YEAR OLD YOUTH Our Youth Martial Arts program is for kids ages 8+ and is a self defense focused program rooted in mixed martial arts, incorporating Brazilian Jiu-jitsu, wrestling and Muay Thai.
 At this age, discipline and focus are highly emphasized, as students are expected to practice techniques with increasing independence and responsibility.
 Compared with our Little Ninjas program, Youth students receive more detailed instruction and advanced technique.

 Students will also concentrate on advanced Muay Thai and Brazilian Jiu-jitsu sparring to help master technique, grow their confidence and learn control.
 Along with improved strength, conditioning and agility, students develop confidence and life skills that extend beyond the mat and carry over into school, sports and daily life.

 ### KID'S COMPETITION ### ABOUT NORTH ORANGE COUNTY MARTIAL ARTS "From our humble beginnings of teaching a few friends in a garage to having worked with thousands of students over the years, we continue our mission of serving the people of North Orange County by helping them live better lives through self discipline, physical fitness and self defense"

 Although great for self defense, mixed martial arts is also excellent for competition.
 If your child is looking to become more than just bully-proof, competition training is an excellent way to increase their skill level and provide another path to test themselves and help achieve mastery.
 The NOCMA Fight Team allows students to compete in Brazilian Jiu-jitsu and Youth MMA tournaments all throughout Southern California.

 Our students participate in many of the most popular events such as NAGA, IFS, Subfighter, UFSL and NABJJF.
 Many of our Youth students have gone on to become great high school wrestlers and even amateur MMA fighters.

 More than just extra training, the NOCMA Fight Team is a close knit community of students, parents, and coaches who value discipline, accountability, and mutual support - on and off the mat!
