 ### ABOUT BRAZILIAN JIU-JITSU Brazilian jiu-jitsu, or BJJ, is the martial art made famous by Royce Gracie in the first UFCs.
 He proved that a smaller, weaker person could not only defend himself against a larger, stronger attacker but also defeat him in battle!
 Bjj is know as the "Gentlemen's Martial Art" because its focus is on learning how to defend yourself with a series of techniques and not just brute strength.

 Since technique, not just size and strength, is what makes jiu-jitsu so effective, it's the perfect martial art for men and women of all ages.
 Over time, you will gain confidence not just from the ability to defend yourself, but from the increase in your overall fitness.
 Due to the nature of training Brazilian Jiu-jitsu, you will develop improved strength, endurance and flexibility!

 Brazilian Jiu-jitsu is also great for the community that is created.
 Many adults enjoy the training since becoming proficient requires many training partners.
 You will meet new people and even form friendships with others who practice the art.
