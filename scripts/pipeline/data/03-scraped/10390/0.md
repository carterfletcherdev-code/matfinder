 ## Our Legacy For over three and a half decades, Tommy Cho Martial Arts has been a cornerstone of martial arts excellence in our community. Founded in 1988, our school has helped thousands of students discover their potential through the disciplines of Taekwondo and Gracie Jiu-Jitsu.
 Our philosophy combines traditional martial arts values with modern training methods, creating well-rounded practitioners who embody the principles of respect, discipline, and continuous self-improvement.

 ## Our Programs ### Taekwondo Our Taekwondo program emphasizes dynamic striking, powerful kicks, and practical self-defense.
 Students develop flexibility, strength, and mental focus while progressing through our time-tested curriculum.
 Additionally, students have the opportunity to learn traditional weapons training including the bo staff, nunchaku, sword, and Filipino fighting sticks.
 - Traditional forms (poomsae) - Dynamic striking techniques - Competition training - Self-defense applications

 ### Gracie Jiu-Jitsu Our Gracie Jiu-Jitsu program focuses on practical self-defense and ground fighting techniques that work for practitioners of all sizes and strength levels.
 Students learn the art of leverage and technique, developing the ability to control and submit opponents through proper positioning and submissions.
 The curriculum emphasizes real-world self-defense scenarios while also preparing students for sport competition and continuous personal development.
 - Ground fighting fundamentals - Self-defense scenarios - Sport jiu-jitsu techniques - Progressive curriculum

 ## Expert Instructors ### Master Tommy Cho 3rd Degree Black Belt in Gracie Jiu-Jitsu | 7th Dan Black Belt in Ho Shin Do Karate | 5th Dan Black Belt in Taekwondo | 4th Dan Black Belt in Choi Kwon Do
 With over 35 years of teaching experience and 44 years of martial arts training, Master Cho has dedicated his life to sharing the art of martial arts. His journey began with traditional martial arts, achieving the rank of 7th Dan in Ho Shin Do Karate, 5th Dan in Taekwondo, and 4th Dan in Choi Kwon Do. His competitive achievements include winning the prestigious Battle of Atlanta tournament, a major sparring competition in the martial arts community.
 In 1998, Master Cho expanded his martial arts expertise by beginning his training in Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu. His dedication and skill led him to earn his 2nd degree black belt, making him a respected practitioner in both traditional and modern martial arts disciplines.
 Master Cho's teaching style uniquely combines technical excellence with spiritual wisdom. He believes in helping students develop not only their physical skills but also their character and personal growth.
 His deep understanding of martial arts principles and commitment to personal development has inspired generations of students to reach their full potential, both on and off the mat.

 ### Master Billy Cahill 4th Dan Black Belt in Taekwondo | Purple Belt in Gracie Jiu-Jitsu
 Beginning his martial arts journey 14 years ago, Billy quickly discovered a deep passion for the art that would transform his life. After just a few years of training, he began teaching and has now been sharing his knowledge with students for 12 years.
 A dedicated competitor with extensive experience across multiple disciplines, Billy has competed in numerous tournaments across Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, Judo, Taekwondo, Karate, and Muay Thai, as well as multiple cage fights in both MMA and Muay Thai. His competitive achievements include championship belts at both NAGA and Newbreed tournaments, winning gold at both the purple and blue belt levels.
 As an instructor, Billy brings his wealth of competitive experience and teaching expertise to every class, helping students develop their skills while fostering a love for martial arts that mirrors his own journey.
