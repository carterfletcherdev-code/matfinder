 ## Frequently Asked Questions Please reach us at julia@sirenjiujitsu.com if you cannot find an answer to your question.

 What should I wear to my first class? We recommend wearing clothes you would wear to any workout in. We will provide you with a gi to use for your first week. We do not recommend tank tops for NoGi classes.
 Please have nails trimmed short for you and your training partner's safety.
 No jewelry or piercings is advised.
 We also ask that you do not wear makeup as this can transfer on to others uniforms.

 What fitness level is required? We welcome any fitness level! Jiu Jitsu is a very unique skill and workout, you will build endurance, strength and flexibility as you train!

 When can I start? Adults can join our jiu jitsu program any time! You don't need to wait for a specific start date.

 How do I sign up for the free trial program? Sign up is easy! Email Coach Julia, julia@sirenjiujitsu.com , or just stop by during one of our classes.
 Remember, the first 4 classes are free.
