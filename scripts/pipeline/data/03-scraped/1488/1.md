 ## Frequently Asked Questions Please reach us at julia@sirenjiujitsu.com if you cannot find an answer to your question.

 What should my child wear to jiu jitsu class? Your daughter should wear comfortable athletic clothes under a Gi that allow for easy movement during jiu jitsu training. We recommend fitted athletic wear like leggings or shorts with a t-shirt that won't get in the way during martial arts practice. Please have a gi by the second class.

 What ages can participate in the girls' jiu jitsu program? Our girls' martial arts classes are designed for ages 6-12, but we welcome girls who are turning 6 soon or are about to age out of the program. If your daughter is close to either age boundary, contact Coach Julia at julia@sirenjiujitsu.com to discuss how we can accommodate her.

 When can she start? Girls can join our jiu jitsu program any time during the program - you don't need to wait for a specific start date. Our Monday and Thursday 6 PM classes are designed so new students can integrate seamlessly regardless of when they begin. Coaches will ensure your daughter feels welcome and catches up quickly with age-appropriate jiu jitsu instruction.

 How do I sign up for the girls' jiu jitsu trial program? Sign up is easy! Email kidsclasses@sirenjiujitsu.com , follow this link: https://kick.site/nerucgg5 , or just stop by during one of our classes.
 Remember, the first class is free.
