## BEGINNERS CLASS
 ## BUILD A STRONG FOUNDATION
 PROGRAM DESCRIPTION:
 The Basics Program was created to ensure that all newer students will begin their jiu-jitsu journey with a strong foundation. The curriculum focuses on introductory techniques from every position and core concepts in jiu-jitsu practice that will aid in the student’s development and progress.
 ​
 BELT RANK:
 - White, Blue, and Purple Belts ONLY
 ​
 PROGRAM GOALS:
 - Introduce students to all positions and situations - Build coordination and learn jiu-jitsu movements - Understand core concepts and ideas - Inspire students to live the jiu-jitsu lifestyle
 ​
 CURRICULUM INCLUDES:
 - Beginner jiu-jitsu techniques - Self-defense techniques - Overview of all foundation positions and situations - Mobility and agility drills
