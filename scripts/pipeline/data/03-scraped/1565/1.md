# Coach Damon Jackson
 Damon Jackson is a professional MMA fighter currently competing at the highest level in the UFC. His initial background started with wrestling, starting at a young age he wrestled all the way through college. He wrestled at Missouri Valley College and received All American stats in 2012, taking 5th at the NAIA National Tournament. Damon is a certified USA Wrestling Bronze level coach, he was the head coach at Ozark Missouri taking 12 wrestlers to state. He fought amateur MMA through college and turned pro shortly after graduation. Damon is one of Fortis MMA’s top professional MMA fighters. He is currently in the top 20 best featherweight fighters in the world. He has 10 fights in the UFC and has held impressive win streaks coming against the top featherweights in the division. He has received multiple performance bonuses for some of his highlight reel finishes. Damon currently holds a record of 22-5 with 19 finishes! He is an outstanding fighter and coach who has experience at the highest level of combat sports.
