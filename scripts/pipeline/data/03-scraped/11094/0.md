 ## About Us #### World Class Instruction All of our children's classes are taught by our head instructor. Sensei David has over 46 years martial arts experience. A dedicated
 professional he was inducted into the United States Martial Arts Hall of Fame in 2021 as Master Martial Arts Instructor of the Year! He is currently ranked number 1 in the World by the North American Sport Karate Association for 2025.

 He is not only a top karate instructor but a dedicated father as well. His goal in every class is to set each child up for success, not only in the dojo but in life.

 #### Next Step Life Skills Development Our life skills and character development program is comprised of a
 series of rotating monthly themes. Themes include such topics as Focus, Self Discipline, Confidence, Kindness, and Team Work. Each student is required to complete an assignment to earn their NEXTSTEP stripe.

 This program is designed to help children stay focused on goals and develop a true feeling of confidence and accomplishment. But more importantly opens up fun and interesting conversations at home.
 And it is a powerful tool for parents to learn about their child's
 thoughts and creates great bonding opportunities!

 #### Martial Arts Curriculum Your child will learn a blend of traditional Okinawan Karate and modern martial arts systems including techniques from Boxing, Kick Boxing, Krav Maga, Tae Kwon Do, Judo, Wrestling, MMA, and Jui Jitsu.

 In addition, we specialize in youth sport karate instruction. Our sport karate curriculum focuses on competition forms, weapons and sparring. Traditional sports are not for every child, but unlike traditional school sports no child ever sits the bench in martial arts!

 When paired with our life skills development program our goal is to create resilient, capable, confident young people who can travel through life with grace and poise.

 ## Frequently Asked Questions Please contact us if you cannot find an answer to your question.

 How can martial arts improve my child's grades in school? Martial arts training teaches focus, self discipline, perseverance, and respect. The NextStep life skills development program teaches goal setting strategies and reinforces positive social habits while rewarding consistent achievement. The combination of these skills and practices come together to aid any student on the path to academic success and success in life.

 My child is very timid and shy, how can martial arts training help? At KickStart every child breaks a board to earn their white belt and gain admission to the school. It is a new experience, exciting, challenging, and sometimes a little scary. We talk about courage, removing doubt, and smashing through any obstacle that would stop us. Obstacles like fear and doubt. For the children on the shy side, they learn something about themselves many for the first time that day. By the time they earn a JR Black Belt they will have had thousands of similar wins and the culmination creates true confidence.

 Can I watch my child's class? YES! You will notice on your very first visit to the dojo our ample seating areas. We encourage all parents to watch each class. Children do better with support from their parents in all things, martial arts is no exception. Our program is entirely built and centered around family and a culture of self improvement. Plus you wont want to miss the excitement of class! Each class you will watch your child grow and overcome challenges for the first time!
