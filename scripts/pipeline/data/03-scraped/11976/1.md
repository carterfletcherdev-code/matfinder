 ## COMMON QUESTIONS & ANSWERS

 ### 1. Who is Impact Martial Arts Academy for? Impact Martial Arts Academy is perfect for those seeking elite-level training to build unbreakable mental and physical toughness under expert coaches who demand accountability. Whether you’re a beginner stepping into the ring, a parent building discipline in your youth, or a pro sharpening championship skills, our hardcore community pushes everyone to dominate.

 ### 2. Do I need prior fitness or fight experience? No, you don’t need prior fitness or fight experience to train at Impact Martial Arts Academy. Our coaches tailor every class for all levels-from total beginners building foundational skills to seasoned fighters sharpening pro-level techniques. We emphasize proper form, progressive challenges, and relentless accountability so everyone starts strong and grows tougher together.

 ### 3. What types of training does Impact Martial Arts Academy offer? Muay Thai, MMA, Kickboxing, fitness & Nutrition

 ### 4. Are classes coached or self-guided? Each class is coach-guided with technique corrections and personalized scaling to ensure beginners build a strong foundation while advanced fighters push toward competition readiness. This coached approach drives accountability and rapid progress in our high-intensity classes.

 ### 5. What does a typical training session look like? We follow a structured, high-intensity flow designed for all levels. It starts with a dynamic warm-up to prep your body and mind, followed by detailed technique instruction on Muay Thai or MMA skills like strikes, clinch work, or grappling. You’ll then drill those moves with partners, hit pads for power and precision- all under expert coaching.

 ### 6. Can Impact Martial Arts Academy help with weight loss? Not only can we assist with weight loss, but do it in an incredibly fun manner – keeping you engaged and excited.

 ### 7. What are your opening hours? 9 AM to 8 PM most weekdays.

 ### 8. Is there parking available? YES, in front of the location, directly across the street and on the east side of the building.
