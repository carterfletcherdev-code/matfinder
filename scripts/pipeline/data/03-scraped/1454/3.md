## Kids Summer Program

 For the months of June, July & August, Kids Jiu Jitsu & Wrestling classes are free for new students. Classes are available 7 days a week. There is an attendance policy of 3 classes a week to qualify for the program. Spaces are limited. Please email info@workshop-nyc.com to reserve your child’s enrollment.
