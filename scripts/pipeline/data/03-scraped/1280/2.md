 # Frequently Asked Questions
 - Do I need any experience to start?No experience is needed! Our beginner-friendly classes are designed to help you feel comfortable and confident from day one. - What should I wear to my first class?If you don’t have a gi yet, wear athletic clothing without zippers or buttons. We can also lend you a gi for your first class. - Is the first class really free?Yes! We offer a free trial class so you can experience the academy and our community with no pressure. - What ages do you accept for kids’ classes?We typically start kids at age 4 and group them by age and skill level to ensure safe and effective training. - Do you offer classes for women only?We offer co-ed classes and special workshops that create a welcoming space for women of all level. - How do I sign up?Just click on the “Try a Free Class” button, fill out a short form, and we’ll confirm your spot. - What if I’m not in shape?No problem—our classes are designed to help you get in shape while learning Jiu-Jitsu at your own pace. - Do you offer competition training?Yes, we have specific training sessions for those preparing to compete, led by experienced coaches. - How often should I train?We recommend 2–3 times per week for steady progress, but it depends on your goals and schedule. - Is there a membership commitment?We offer different membership options, including month-to-month and discounted plans with longer commitments.
