 ## Event and Class FAQs

 Find answers to common questions about our weekly events and classes below.

 ##### What should I wear to the classes?

 We recommend wearing comfortable athletic clothing. For judo and jiu-jitsu, a gi is preferred, but not required for beginners.

 ##### Do I need prior experience to join?

 No prior experience is necessary. Our classes are designed to accommodate all skill levels, from beginners to advanced practitioners.

 ##### How can I register for a class?

 You can register online through our website or contact us directly to secure your spot in the class.

 ##### Are there age restrictions for participants? Our classes are open to participants aged 5 and up. We have specific sessions tailored for different age groups.

 ##### What is the class schedule? Classes are held weekly. Please check our online schedule for specific days and times.

 ##### What safety measures are in place? We prioritize safety with trained instructors and proper equipment. All participants are briefed on safety protocols before each session.

 ##### Can I observe a class before joining? Yes, we welcome prospective students to observe a class. Please contact us to arrange a visit.

 ##### What is the cost of the classes? Our pricing varies based on the class type and duration. Please visit the Join Us page or contact us for detailed information.

 ##### Do you offer family discounts? Yes, we offer discounts for families enrolling multiple members. Contact us for more details on our family plans.
