 Taking Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu training has several advantages outside of learning self-defense techniques. Benefits abound at Hamilton’s BJJ:
 Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu is a demanding exercise improving strength, flexibility, and cardiovascular condition. BJJ’s dynamic motions offer a complete-body exercise that helps practitioners remain in shape and condition.
 BJJ teaches mental discipline by demanding strategic thinking and problem-solving. Anticipating and opposing an opponent’s movements helps one develop a strategic attitude and sharpens cognitive abilities.
 Mastery of Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu moves one toward self-confidence. Students get a sense of success as they advance through the ranks and reach personal benchmarks that results in more confidence.
 Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu classes offer a good way for release of tension. The physical exercise releases endorphins, which promote a more calm and good attitude of mind.
