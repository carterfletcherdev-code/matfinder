# JIU JITSU

 JIU JITSU is...

 Brazilian jiu-jitsu (BJJ) is a grappling-based martial art whose central theme is the skill of controlling and restraining a resisting opponent. Its practical approach to handling common attacker situations has led BJJ to become known as the most effective self-defense system in the world.
 Grappling and submission techniques are practiced daily to learn how to control an aggressive adversary. Proper technique will overcome size and strength; these techniques are practiced against knowledgeable and resisting opponents in our safe and controlled atmosphere.
