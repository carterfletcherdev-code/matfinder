 ## your kids will love training!

 - They will learn through objective-based lessons and practice. - We incorporate techniques in all positional sparring. - We help create mental and physical connections during live sparring. - They learn how to manage emotions and difficult encounters. - We offer games, incorporating the lesson, to build muscle memory. - We encourage good physical training, fitness, and endurance! - We are strong believers that kids should have fun training!

 LITTLE PANDAS
 Ages 4-6
 A fun and engaging class where kids get to play while learning fundamental Jiu Jitsu and grappling movements, in addition to strengthening and developing motor and cognitive skills.

 KIDS CLASS
 Ages 7-12
 Kids learn fundamental and advanced technique for self-defense and sport Jiu Jitsu and submission grappling. In this class kids are paired up at the end for live practice and sparring.

 TEENS CLASS
 Ages 13-15
 A class dedicated to focus on the teen age group and help with fine-tuning technique and learning more advanced movements. In this class the teens challenged physically and mentally.
