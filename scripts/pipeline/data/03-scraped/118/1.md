# Schedule an INtroduction

 ### begin your jIu-Jitsu journey We’d love to show you around the gym and answer any questions you have.
 Please fill out the form below and we will contact you to set up an introduction before your first class. The introduction period is a free week trial where you can attend classes in our academy.
 Once scheduled, come 15 minutes before your appropriate class. We have the Gi (uniform) available to loan for your free week trial. Please wear a shirt and some sort of bottoms for underneath the Gi. We also recommend bringing water and sandals/shoes for use off the mat. Please also review our Dojo Etiquette .
