 As kids grow they develop core physical and social skills that will lead them towards a positive adulthood. Whether your child is an introvert or a social butterfly, Gracie Barra’s kid’s brazilian jiu jitsu in North Austin sets kids up for success.

 Kids need strong leaders, individual goals, and feelings of empowerment. With Gracie Barra Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, our Future Champions program offers kids a motivating environment that inspires them to be the best that they can be. Our professional instructors guide students through important lessons around concentration, teamwork, and competition while providing kids with the structure and encouragement they need to achieve their goals. Kids and teens develop both physically and mentally through Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu and grow even stronger with their practice.

 The GB Kids Brazilian Jiu Jitsu in North Austin Program is divided into four categories to accommodate students of different ages and levels:

 - Future Champions - Little Champions (ages 5 to 8) - Juniors (ages 9 to 12) - Teens (ages 13 to 16)

 ## How This Program Will Help Your Child/Teen
 - 1Builds Confidence and Discipline - Through structured training and achievable goals, kids gain self-assurance while learning the value of hard work and respect. - 2Improves Focus and Self-Control - Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu teaches children how to concentrate, stay calm under pressure, and channel their energy productively. - 3Encourages Teamwork and Friendship - In a positive, community-based environment, kids learn cooperation, sportsmanship, and how to support one another both on and off the mat.
