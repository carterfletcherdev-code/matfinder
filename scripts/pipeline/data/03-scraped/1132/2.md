 # About Krav Maga

 Krav Maga (“krahv magah”) is the official hand-to-hand combat system of the Israeli Defense Forces. It’s also the preferred system for U.S. Military and law enforcement personnel. Krav Maga is not a traditional martial art. No katas. No rituals. Based on simple principles and instinctive movements, this reality-based system is designed to teach real self-defense in the shortest possible time. Learn to defend against common chokes, grabs, and bear hugs, as well as weapons such as guns, knives, and sticks. Just as important, we teach people to function during the stress and shock of a sudden, violent encounter. Tested on the battlefield and in the streets, Krav Maga’s practical approach to self-defense will make you safer and more secure.

 Krav Maga is also the required training for scores of federal, state, and local law enforcement agencies and one of the most efficient self-defense systems for real-life situations, emphasizing practical defenses against real attacks.

 Krav Maga is known for its practical approach to self-defense, and our kids' curriculum focuses on prevention first. Children learn situational awareness, boundary setting, and how to recognize unsafe situations before they escalate. Training emphasizes avoiding danger whenever possible and responding calmly and intelligently when avoidance isn’t an option.

 Classes are structured to help kids understand how to stay composed under pressure. They learn simple, effective movements designed specifically for children, always with safety as the top priority. There is no unnecessary aggression or intimidation. Instead, kids are taught responsibility, respect, and self-control alongside physical techniques.

 ## FAQs
 What is Krav Maga at Soulcraft Martial Arts in Hamden, CT all about?

 What is Soulcraft Krav Maga?

 Krav Maga is a generic Hebrew word meaning “contact fighting.“ The word Maga means “contact“ and the word Krav means “battle“ or “fight.“ Krav Maga is in an advanced Israeli Self Defense System designed to give real solutions to any violent situation one may encounter. The effectiveness of this system is in part due to its simplicity and reliance on your already existing instincts.

 I am totally out of shape. Can I handle training at your Hamden CT Studio?

 Yes! Training Krav Maga at Soulcraft is physically challenging, however, every exercise, drill, or technique is designed to help you improve – no matter what your current fitness level, we will be there to support you every step of the way.

 Is Krav Maga a good idea for a woman?

 Yes, Yes and Yes! Krav Maga by definition gives a smaller/weaker person real life street advantage against a larger/stronger assailant. Women are naturally targeted for various assaults, from domestic violence to robberies to attacks of a sexual nature, date-rapes and stalkers. Unlike other martial arts which rely on a person’s strength and size for advantage Krav Maga will give you the edge you need to never be a part of the statistics.

 I already have a gym membership – is Krav Maga at Soulcraft as good for fitness as it is for self-defense?

 Yes! Practicing Krav Maga at our Hamden, CT studio regularly engages your entire body and provides an intense strength and cardiovascular workout. It’s really all you need and it’s way more fun than a treadmill!!
