 ## FAQs
 What is Muay Thai at Soulcraft Martial Arts in Hamden, CT all about?

 Am I’m too old or out of shape to learn at Soulcraft Muay Thai?

 No way! At our Hamden, CT studio Brad Wolfson and Master Marcio Stambowsky work with students ranging in age from 4 to 64; and we teach people with varying levels of physical fitness. All of our exercises, drills, and sparring can be tailored to fit your individual fitness goals and everyone in New Haven County can get in great shape while having fun and making new friends!

 I’ve never practiced a martial art before, is Soulcraft BJJ the right environment for me to learn?

 Yes! Our Hamden, CT classes are packed with students from a wide array of martial arts backgrounds. Some hold high rank in other martial arts, while for others, Muay Thai is the first art they have studied. Soulcraft is a welcoming family environment. Regardless of your martial arts background, you are welcome to train, learn and grow with us… so come on in!

 What are your training facilities like?

 Muay Thai classes are conveniently located at 1125 Dixwell Avenue in Hamden, CT. As one of New Haven County’s premier Muay Thai and Brazilian Jiu Jitsu academies, we are easily accessible to the Merritt Parkway, Whitney Ave, Dixwell Ave and I- 91.

 We feature the guidance of an experienced Muay Thai and MMA coach and Soulcraft offers a spacious mat area with plenty of extra room, access to showers and changing rooms and a lounge area for relaxing before or after class. Everything needed to learn some great techniques and get in the best shape of your life is right here!

 Q: Is Muay Thai at Soulcraft suitable for women?

 Absolutely! Muay Thai is all about the use of technique. Therefore ,it is perfectly suited for females—a demographic we specialize in teaching.
