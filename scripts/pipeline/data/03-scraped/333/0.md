### Free Class On Sundays Join Professor Tish and the team on Sunday mornings at 8 AM. This class is always free, and open to the public. We always have loaner uniforms if needed.
 ### Instruction Classes are taught by Tisha “Tish” Adams and private lessons are available upon request. Tish has over a decade of experience teaching and competing in Brazilian Jiu Jitsu. She was recently awarded her black belt by Carlson Gracie Jnr. and our head coach, Troy Acker. We are lucky and blessed to have her knowledge and expertise to teach anyone looking for a women’s jiu jitsu classes. Oftentimes, we are discovered by women who happen to literally be searching for “women’s jiu jitsu near me”. Since we are centrally located in Orange County, California, we are quite close to major cities. Each class is a great cardio workout featuring brazilian jiu jitsu women’s self defense. You are welcome to try a free class today!
