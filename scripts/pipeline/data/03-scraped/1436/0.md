 ## Kickboxing

 ### All Ages This discipline is built around the theory of striking, movement, and timing. It focuses on understanding distance, angles, and the relationship between offense and defense. Practitioners explore how different techniques connect, when they are applied, and why they are effective in various scenarios. With an emphasis on strategy, awareness, and decision-making, it offers an engaging framework for those interested in the principles behind striking and self-defense.

 Learn More

 ## Jiu-Jitsu

 ### All Ages This discipline focuses on the theory of control, leverage, and positioning. It emphasizes understanding timing, angles, and how movements connect within a larger system. Practitioners develop awareness and problem-solving skills through structured techniques, creating a clear and beginner-friendly path to technical mastery.
