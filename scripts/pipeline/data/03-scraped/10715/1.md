# We are a Brazilian Jiu Jitsu school located in Gray, Maine

 Carll Clan BJJ is a family-oriented Brazilian Jiu Jitsu school. Our mission is to teach self-defense through the art of Jiu Jitsu. We believe no one should be a victim in life. Carll Clan BJJ focuses on teaching Jiu Jitsu to children and adults in a friendly, professional environment. Brazilian Jiu Jitsu is a great tool that builds self-confidence and increases fitness in anyone, regardless of your age, size, strength or experience.
