From beginners learning the fundamentals to experienced practitioners preparing for competition , our school provides world-class instruction focused on discipline, confidence, and lifelong growth .

 #### Meet The Team At ### Claudio França BJJ San Jose
 ##### Erik Benaquisto ###### Owner and Professor
 Erik Benaquisto , a native of Santa Cruz, began his Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu journey in 1999 under the mentorship of Master Claudio França , one of the most respected figures in the sport. In 2009, Erik expanded his passion for teaching by partnering with Claudio to grow the Claudio França Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu academy in San Jose , now one of the most established BJJ schools in the Bay Area .

 Known for his aggressive pressure game, Erik’s style of Brazilian Jiu Jitsu is evident in his utilization of the lapel for chokes and opponent control. His relentless, no-quit mentality has led to the achievement of multiple gold medals in the competitive Black Belt division.

 Beyond BJJ, Erik’s experience in Kickboxing and MMA showcases his well-rounded martial arts expertise. For him, Jiu-Jitsu is more than a sport—it’s a lifestyle that builds strength, resilience, and balance. Guided by his philosophy, “Prepare for war, fight for peace,” Erik invites students to train with purpose, challenge themselves, and discover personal growth through the art of Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu in San Jose.
