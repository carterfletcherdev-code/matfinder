# Adult Classes

 ## Our Classes ### Fundamentals Our Fundamentals classes are designed for beginners and those looking to reinforce their core skills. These 1-hour sessions focus on essential techniques, defensive strategies, and positional control to build a solid foundation in Jiu Jitsu.

 ### All Levels Our All Levels classes are open to everyone, from white belts to black belts. These 1.5-hour sessions cover a wide range of techniques, from basic to advanced, and focus on drills, live sparring, and practical applications. Instructors adjust the intensity to suit all experience levels.

 ### Competition Designed for those looking to compete, our Competition classes run 1.5 hours and focus on high-intensity drills, advanced techniques, and competition-specific strategies. Participants will work on takedowns, guard passing, submissions, and situational sparring to prepare for the demands of competition.
