# Kid Classes

 ## Teens, Kids, and Tots Classes ### High-Level Instruction At 10th Planet Palmdale, we believe every student deserves the highest quality instruction regardless of age. That’s why the same experienced coaches who lead our adult classes also work hands-on with our kids. This ensures that our youth program benefits from the same technical depth, attention to detail, and training philosophy that makes our adult program so effective. All youth classes run for 1 hour, providing the perfect balance of focus and engagement.

 ### Teens Class (Ages 11–14) Our Teens Class is built to challenge and develop students ages 11–14 with a mix of technical instruction, live training, and age-appropriate competition prep. Classes run for 1 hour and are structured to build confidence, discipline, and athleticism in a fun and supportive environment. Pricing typically ranges from $125–$175/month, with discounts available for families, siblings, and long-term signups. Contact us to find the plan that works best for your teen!

 ### Kids Classes (Ages 7+) Our kids program ranges between $125–$175/month with no long-term commitment - perfect for busy families looking for flexibility. We also offer discounted rates for families, multi-child households, and long-term commitments. Reach out to learn more about what option works best for you! Classes are 1 hour in length, structured to keep students active and engaged.

 ### Rolling Tots (Ages 4–6) Monthly: $99.00 An affordable option for parents who want their children to benefit from early martial arts training. Includes unlimited access to all classes for ages 4–6. Each session is 45 minutes long and designed to introduce young students to the fundamentals in a fun, supportive environment.
