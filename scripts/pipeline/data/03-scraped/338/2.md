 ## Welcome to 10th Planet Palmdale ### About 10th Planet Jiu Jitsu 10th Planet Jiu Jitsu, founded by Eddie Bravo, is a revolutionary approach to no-gi submission grappling. Unlike traditional Brazilian Jiu Jitsu, 10th Planet focuses on unorthodox techniques and adaptability. At 10th Planet Palmdale, we embrace the philosophy that Jiu Jitsu is constantly evolving, providing innovative techniques alongside fundamental skills to create a comprehensive and effective training experience.

 ### Our Philosophy At the core of 10th Planet Jiu Jitsu is a belief in evolution and growth. We prioritize flexibility, creativity, and adaptability in grappling techniques, ensuring that our practitioners stay ahead of the curve. Our instructors are dedicated to helping you develop a personalized approach to Jiu Jitsu that suits your strengths and goals.
