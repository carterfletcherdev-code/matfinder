## Programs

 #### Open Mat Open Mat provides our academy students with dedicated time to train freely and practice Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu techniques learned in class. These sessions allow students to drill, review techniques, and gain additional mat time in a relaxed training environment at our Carmel, Indiana academy.
