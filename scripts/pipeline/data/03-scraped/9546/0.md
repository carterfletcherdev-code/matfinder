 ## Kids Jiu-Jitsu (Levels 1–3) Growing Together
 Our Kids Jiu-Jitsu program is divided into three levels to match each child’s age and development.
 Students progress through:
 - Level 1 (Ages 4–6) - Level 2 (Ages 7–9) - Level 3 (Ages 10–13)
 Placement is based on age, size, coordination, skill level, and fight IQ to ensure the right balance of safety and challenge.
 We focus on confidence, discipline, and real-world self-defense, with an emphasis on awareness, control, and anti-bullying—helping kids grow on and off the mat.
