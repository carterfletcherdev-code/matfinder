 ### Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu

 ### TRY IT FREE

 Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu (BJJ) is a grappling-based martial art that specializes in ground fighting, submissions, and positional control. Rather than relying on brute strength, BJJ emphasizes leverage, timing, and precise technique to neutralize and overcome larger or stronger opponents. Practitioners learn how to transition through positions, escape from disadvantageous situations, and gradually work toward a dominant position. The ultimate objective is to force the opponent to submit through carefully applied joint locks or chokeholds, demonstrating the art’s effectiveness in both sport competition and real-world self-defense.
