 Deep Blue Brazilian Jiu Jitsu Classes
 At Deep Blue, we believe Brazilian Jiu Jitsu is for everyone. In an effort to meet everyone's needs, we offer different classes for different skill levels, with long term plans for more. Below is a description of each class and who the intended student audience is for. "KJJ" are our Kids Jiu Jitsu classes. Our kids programs get more advanced with age, but each class is still structured to welcome everyone to the mats. Our adult BJJ class names are modeled after an academic curriculum. New students can start in BJJ 101, and have a clear path forward as they become more comfortable in their training. Our women's only class is listed as "WJJ". A simple guide to our class audience:
 100 level: Beginner level classes open to all. There will be no live sparring in these classes.
 200 level: Our core training curriculum, open to all adult students. There will be live sparring in these classes.
 300 level: These will be our competition focused classes, and will be for 1 striped white belts and above only .
 400 level: Coming soon . These will be our advanced classes, and will be for blue belts and above only. Please speak to the coaches before you begin attending the 400 level classes.
 We offer a free trial week in our memberships options so that you can come discover the art risk free. Please feel free to reach out with any questions.
 KJJ: 4-5yrs
 Our KJJ:4-5yrs program is designed to make your children love the mats. Your kids will learn all the fundamental positions and movements of Brazilian Jiu Jitsu, while having lots of fun. We disguise our lesson plans in fun games and activities, so your kids will intuitively learn the skills required to be successful Jiu Jitsu artists!
 KJJ: 6-8yrs

 Our KJJ:6-8yrs program introduces your child to submissions, live sparring, and a more developed understanding of each Jiu Jitsu position. This is the age kids begin developing an interest in competing, so we start to ramp up the competition focus. We offer two sessions, each with classes twice per week. Please review the schedule for details.
 KJJ: 9-13yrs
 Our KJJ: 9-13yrs program is a comprehensive Jiu Jitsu curriculum where we leave it all on the mats. This is where our foundational movements and positions are transformed into a precise martial art. We still manage to have a lot of fun! We offer two sessions, each with classes twice per week. Please review the schedule for details. We also have a competition class for our 9-13yr age group.
 BJJ 101
 BJJ 101 is a 14 week curriculum designed to introduce new students to all the major positions and techniques of Brazilian Jiu Jitsu. You can start anytime during these 14 weeks, and go through the program as many times as you want! BJJ 101 also has an online classroom, where all the techniques and concepts taught have a corresponding video and description so that you can see the structure of the whole curriculum and come back to lessons for easy reference. BJJ 101 is open to all adult students.
 BJJ 125
 BJJ 125 is a fundamentals workshops class, rotating through the key topics, 2-3 weeks at a time, that new students have trouble with. This class will go into more detail than our BJJ 101 class, and introduce you to a higher level of Jiu Jitsu understanding. Online classroom in development!
 BJJ 200
 BJJ 200 is our BJJ for self defense class. Focusing on BJJ fundamentals, self defense scenarios and accompanies with a yoga cool down.
 BJJ 201
 BJJ 201 is the core curriculum for our adult program. We use a position based approach, starting with the most basic attacks and movements of a position, and develop a strong game as we move into more advanced techniques and transitions. This class is in the gi, and is open to all adult students.
 WJJ 201
 WJJ 201 is our women's only Brazilian Jiu Jitsu class. This class is one hour and will model our BJJ 201 class, but will follow a separate curriculum track. Note: this class is not scheduled until further notice.
 BJJ 225
 BJJ 225 is an intermediate workshops class. In this class we take an in-depth look at systems and concepts to help take your game to the next level. This class in in the gi.
 BJJ 250
 BJJ 250 is our no-gi class. We follow a separate curriculum track as BJJ 201, and focus on more no-gi appropriate techniques and strategies. We recommend newer students focus on gi training, but BJJ 250 is open to all adult students.
 BJJ 290
 BJJ 290 is our Dynamic Games class. This class is modeled after the ecological approach to training, where situational games are designed to develop your skillset in a dynamic environment. This is a no-gi class.
 BJJ 301
 Our competition class is currently only scheduled leading up to competition days. This is a high-paced class focused on situational rounds and training at competition intensity. This class is in the gi.

Through private lessons, you can build a strategic game, problem solve challenges to break through plateaus, be introduced to whole new sets of techniques, and get the detailed feedback you need to push yourself to the next level. Private booking is available on our scheduling page - select the drop down menu at the top right of the schedule, and then select BJJ privates.
