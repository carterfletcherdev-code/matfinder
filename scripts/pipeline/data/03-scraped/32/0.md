## With Locations in Winona and La Crosse ## Welcome to Flow Jiu-Jitsu! Contact us today to redeem your free week of classes! Welcome to Winona, MN and La Crosse, WI’s best Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu school. Our members range from casual hobbyists all the way to world champion competitors. Regardless of your level of grappling experience, what your goals are, or how old you are, we have a class that is right for you . Between our two locations , we offer Jiu-Jitsu seven days a week, and offer morning and night classes on our schedule in order to best suit your needs.
