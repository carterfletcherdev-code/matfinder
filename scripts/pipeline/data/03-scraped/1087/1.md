 ## Welcome to Soca BJJ Glen Cove

 Soca Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu is proud to open its doors in the beautiful North Shore city of Glen Cove, Long Island. The Soca BJJ Academy was founded by multiple time world champion and BJJ Legend Alexandre Soca Freitas, producing a fierce team of high level Jiu-Jitsu competitors in kids, juveniles, adults and masters divisions.
 In Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, we pride ourselves on its accessibility to all who are interested in trying this gentle art. Here at Soca Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu Glen Cove, we are bringing the core fundamental curriculum taught by Alexandre Soca Freitas, ideal for those who are new to this martial art.
 We welcome everyone to try BJJ by offering our free introductory classes. Schedule your free intro today!
