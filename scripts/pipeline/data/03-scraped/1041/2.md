 ## What To Wear
 ### Gi Class You can attend your first class in comfortable athletic clothes, like a t-shirt and shorts or leggings. If you already have a gi, you are welcome to wear it.
 If you decide to sign up to continue training, we require you to wear our team gi. It helps create consistency, teaches respect for the environment, and helps everyone feel like part of the group. We will help you get the right gear when the time comes.

 ### No-Gi Class For your first class, you can wear a fitted athletic shirt or rash guard and shorts or leggings without pockets.
 If you decide to sign up and continue training, we require you to wear our team rash guard and shorts. This helps create consistency and keeps the mats clean and organized.
 We will guide you through the gear process when the time is right.

 ## Frequently Asked Questions Still have questions? These are some of the most common things our women students have asked before stepping onto the mat for the first time.

 ### Do I need to be in shape to start?
 No, you don’t need to get fit before trying class. This program is beginner-friendly and helps you build strength, confidence, and skill over time, starting from wherever you are.

 ### Who will I be training with?
 This is a women-only class at our Fort Myers location. You’ll be training with other women in a supportive and respectful setting, led by instructors who focus on clear guidance and a comfortable pace.

 ### Can I bring my child with me while I train?
 Yes, we have quite a few students that are parents, and we designed our facility with that in mind! There’s a space where kids can hang out while you train. There are also benches near the mat and a waiting area with couches and a TV in the front lobby, so you can stay focused knowing your child is nearby.
