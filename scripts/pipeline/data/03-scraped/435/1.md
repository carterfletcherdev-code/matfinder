 FAQ ## Questions from West Covina Residents
 ### How far is 10th Planet Covina from West Covina? About 2 miles. If you're near the Westfield West Covina Mall, it's a straight shot north on Citrus Ave — roughly 5 minutes by car. From the South Hills area, you can take Azusa Ave to Badillo St and be at the gym in under 10 minutes.

 ### Do I need martial arts experience to start? Not at all. Most of our members started as complete beginners. Our beginner classes are designed to teach fundamentals from the ground up, and experienced training partners will work with you at your pace. We have morning and evening beginner sessions so you can pick the time that works best.

 ### What ages do your kids programs cover? We run three age groups: 3–5, 6–9, and 10–15. Kids Jiu Jitsu and kids kickboxing run Monday through Friday starting at 4 PM, and Saturday mornings feature wrestling. Many parents from West Covina drop their kids off after school and either watch or take an adult class in the other room.

 ### Is there parking at the gym? Yes. There's free parking directly in front of the building at 1050 N Citrus Ave. You won't have to walk far or deal with meters.

 ### What's the difference between 10th Planet Jiu Jitsu and regular BJJ? 10th Planet is 100% no-gi — no traditional kimono (gi). The system was founded by Eddie Bravo and focuses on a unique set of positions and submissions designed specifically for no-gi grappling. It's effective for self-defense and MMA, and it's easier to get started because you don't need to buy a gi — just shorts and a t-shirt.
