 FAQ ## Questions from Baldwin Park Residents
 ### How far is 10th Planet Covina from Baldwin Park? About 4 miles — typically 8 to 10 minutes by car depending on where you're starting from. From central Baldwin Park, the fastest routes are south on Puente Ave to Badillo St then west to Citrus Ave, or a short hop west on the I-10 to the Citrus Ave exit. Both land you at 1050 N Citrus Ave without any major traffic trouble.

 ### Do I need any martial arts experience to start? None at all. The majority of our members walked in for the first time with zero background. Beginner classes are structured to teach fundamentals from the ground up, and you'll be paired with experienced training partners who will work with you at your pace — not throw you in the deep end. We run beginner-friendly sessions in both morning and evening time slots.

 ### My kids go to Baldwin Park High School — is there a good time slot for them? Yes. Kids and teen classes run Monday through Friday starting at 4 PM, which lines up well with the end of the school day at Baldwin Park High, Sierra Vista, North Park, and the middle schools. Saturday morning has wrestling as well. The 10–15 age group trains alongside competitive-minded teens, so high schoolers fit right in without being bored by a class that's too basic.

 ### Is there parking at the gym? Yes, free parking is available directly in front of the building at 1050 N Citrus Ave. No meters, no parking structures — just pull in and walk a few steps to the door.

 ### What makes 10th Planet Jiu Jitsu different from other martial arts in the area? 10th Planet is 100% no-gi — you train in athletic clothes, no traditional kimono required. The system was founded by Eddie Bravo and built around a distinct set of positions and submissions optimized for no-gi grappling. It's widely used in MMA and self-defense contexts, and it's genuinely different from what most area gyms teach. Beyond the system itself, we also run Fuzion Muay Thai for striking, so you can develop a complete skill set under one roof.
