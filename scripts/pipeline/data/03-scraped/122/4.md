# Welcome to Unicorn Jiu Jitsu At Unicorn Jiu Jitsu, we are building courage in the world by creating a safe place for people to fight to become the best version of themselves. We are a small Brazilian Jiu Jitsu, and Kickboxing academy in Portland, Oregon. Unicorn Jiu Jitsu is the only jiu jitsu academy in Oregon with a female BJJ Third Degree Black Belt as the head coach. We are a family owned small business, owned by Hillary and Brian VanOrnum and Andrew Wright (Hillary and Andrew are brother and sister).
 Unicorn Jiu Jitsu is affiliated with One Jiu Jitsu USA
