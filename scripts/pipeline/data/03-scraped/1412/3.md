Striving to be Moore Oklahoma's premier Jiu Jitsu Academy!

 Blackout Jiu Jitsu is home to a safe, clean, and fun training environment where great students and expert coaches meet.

 ## Brazillian Jiu Jitsu Here at Blackout Jiu Jitsu we thrive in the Gi. Adult classes are offered 4 days a week and cover everything from competition and advanced skill sets to students just starting their martial arts journey. Our classes are structured to ensure the best possible gym environment for students of all skill levels and backgrounds.

 ## No Gi Jiu Jitsu Our No-Gi classes offer our students a chance to cross train and expand their grappling knowledge outside of the Gi. We appreciate both Gi and No-Gi as we find they greatly compliment the other. Blackout's No-Gi curriculum is wrestling based, focusing more on positions and movements you will find yourself in without the Gi.

 ## Youth Jiu Jitsu Blackout's youth classes are a great way to introduce martial arts to your little one in a safe, fun, and engaging way within a family-friendly community. We hold classes 4 days a week to ensure consistent training, and to accommodate for those busy family schedules we all have. Whether this is your child's first time in martial arts or they are a decorated competitor, we have something for you, and your family.
