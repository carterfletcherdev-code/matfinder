 ## Welcome to Tiger Schulmann’s, Colmar, PA

 Led by owner and head instructor Sensei Michael Marchand, Tiger Schulmann’s Colmar offers a safe and supportive environment for kids and teens to build fitness and confidence while providing challenging ways for adults to discover the mind-body benefits of kickboxing.

 Conveniently located off the Bethlehem Pike, we’ve helped thousands of people of all ages find friendship, confidence, fun, and full-body fitness through the study of martial arts. The Tiger Schulmann difference is our certified instructors, each trained over years of apprenticeship. “We have the most passionate and knowledgeable instructors anywhere on the planet,” says Marchand. “Their love for martial arts and their passion for helping members achieve their goals is unmatched. We truly are one big family.”

 ## Meet Our Head Instructor

 ## Sensei Michael Marchand

 Michael Marchand began his martial arts journey at age 22 in search of positive change in his life. He found a role model in his Sensei and quickly decided to follow in his footsteps. Today, he brings over two decades of teaching expertise to his role as Owner and Head Instructor of Tiger Schulmann’s Colmar.

 All these years later, Marchand says “helping people achieve their goals and live their best life literally keeps me up at night.” A coach and mentor to kids of all ages, he says, “the younger the student, the more there is to teach them about life. Their outlook on life is so fresh and pure.” Teens, he feels, especially benefit from martial arts training. “I love having the chance to help a teenager avoid the mistakes I made,” he explains. “It’s really rewarding to help them change into the young adult they always wanted to be.”

 Whether members come for kids karate or adult kickboxing, the Tiger Schulmann’s difference is its team of certified instructors. “We have the most passionate and knowledgeable instructors anywhere on the planet,” says Marchand. “Many have been training here since they were 5 years old. Now adults, they have a unique way of connecting with members. The love they have for martial arts and their students is unmatched. We’re truly one big family.”

 ## Frequently Asked Questions

 WHAT TYPE OF CLASSES DO YOU OFFER?
 We offer beginner level classes for all age groups as well as intermediate and advanced level classes in kickboxing and jiu-jitsu.

 HOW DO I PREPARE FOR MY FIRST CLASS?
 No preparation is needed. Just wear comfortable workout clothes and show up, our instructors will handle the rest!

 DO YOU OFFER CLASSES FOR BEGINNERS?
 Yes, we offer beginner classes for adults and kids. No experience required.

 HOW MANY TIMES PER WEEK DO I ATTEND?
 We recommend a minimum of two classes per week. Many adults like to attend three or more classes per week.

 WHAT TIME ARE CLASSES?
 We offer a variety of class times to meet individual needs. Ask about our schedule for more detail.

 HOW IS THE TIGER SCHULMANN'S WORKOUT DIFFERENT THAN GOING TO A GYM?
 Most people can’t stick with the gym. 95% quit after just one month. It’s boring and there are not enough reasons to keep you consistent. With Tiger Schulmann’s, you get our 3 dimensional workout which includes total body results + learning the life skill of self-defense and on top of that, our classes are intriguing and fun! Results, fun and self-defense make it easy to stay consistent and consistency is the key to success!

 AT WHAT AGE DO CHILDREN START CLASSES?
 Our Karate Cubs pre-school program begins at 3yr. Our children’s martial arts program begins at 5 yr. old.

 WHAT DO I WEAR FOR MY FIRST CLASS?
 For your private beginner session class, we recommend sweats and a t-shirt.

 HOW LONG ARE CLASSES?
 Adult classes are 45 minutes to one hour. Children, Kids and Cubs classes are 45 minutes.

 DO YOU OFFER DISCOUNTS?
 Yes, we offer a 20% discount for each additional family member. In addition, we offer active law enforcement a 25% discount.

 HOW IS TIGER SCHULMANN'S MARTIAL ARTS DIFFERENT ?
 Tiger Schulmann’s Martial Arts differs in many ways. From the dedication and expertise of our head instructors who have a minimum of 10 years experience and are the owners of their schools, to the unique and unparalleled curriculum that we teach, to the professional and beautiful facilities we provide. Nothing else is Tiger Schulmanns.

 HOW IS TIGER SCHULMANN'S KICKBOXING DIFFERENT FROM CARDIO KICKBOXING AT A GYM?
 Tiger Schulmann’s head instructors are highly trained professionals with a minimum of 10 years of experience in the martial arts, teaching authentic and effective Muay Thai Kickboxing techniques that will really work if you ever need them. Many cardio kickboxing instructors have little to no actual martial arts experience and are merely aerobic instructors.

 HOW MANY CALORIES CAN I BURN IN AN HOUR?
 While we estimate you will burn about 800 – 1000 calories in a class, many of our students have measured over 1200 calories burned in just one class using their own fitness bands.

 WHAT DO I NEED TO KNOW BEFORE I START CLASSES?
 You don’t need any previous experience. Just show up in some comfortable workout clothes and our professional staff and instructors will make sure you have a great workout and learn a skill that will change your life!

 IS SPARRING OR CONTACT REQUIRED?
 No, sparring and contact are not required. We offer separate classes for those who want to participate in sparring.

 DO I HAVE TO COMPETE?
 No, competition is not required. However, for those students interested, we do offer the opportunity to compete in different types of events, and even have a professional fight team that successfully competes at the highest levels of martial arts competition.

 DO YOU OFFER PRIVATE LESSONS?
 Yes, most of our schools offer private lessons. You can contact the individual location for more information.
