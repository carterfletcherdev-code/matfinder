 ## Krav Maga Core Values

 ###### Small Class Size

 At Erie Krav Maga, we keep our classes small so every student gets the attention they deserve. This approach allows instructors to focus on technique, provide real-time feedback, and ensure you’re training safely while making progress toward your goals.

 ###### World-Class Instructors

 Our instructors bring years of experience and passion to every class. At Erie Krav Maga, you’ll learn from world-class coaches who are dedicated to helping you build confidence, master practical self-defense, and perform at your best.

 ###### Self Defense

 Self-defense is at the core of our training. Erie Krav Maga teaches practical techniques to protect yourself against real-world threats while building strength, endurance, and confidence that extend far beyond the mat.

 ###### New Friends & Mentors

 When you join Erie Krav Maga, you’re not just signing up for a class—you’re joining a community. Students build lasting friendships, find mentors, and train alongside supportive peers who motivate each other to succeed.
