 Fitness | Self-Defense | Competition Training ## Odin's Halls Brazilian Jiu Jitsu And Fitness Odin’s Halls was founded in October of 2018 by Steve Ficca, then a brown belt hungry to bring something new to the Las Vegas Jiu Jitsu scene. From day one, the Wolfpack made an impact—earning recognition as one of the fastest-rising gyms in town. Within a short time, Steve achieved his black belt, and the team went on to win an unprecedented 29 team championships across both kids and adult programs in just a few short years.

 Seven years later, Odin’s Halls stands at the top of the Las Vegas competition scene, holding the highest win/loss ratio of any gym in the valley by an unprecedented 22% margin. The Wolfpack roster now includes 15 national and international champions across kids, teens, and adults—proof that excellence here runs deep across every level.
 In September of 2025, Odin’s Halls expands to central Las Vegas, bringing the same consistency, community, and cutting-edge Jiu-Jitsu that defined its success on the north side of town. The new Paradise location will be led by head coach Brigham Dorrity, Odin’s Halls’ very first homegrown brown belt.
