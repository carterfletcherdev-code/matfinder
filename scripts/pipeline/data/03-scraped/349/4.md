## Welcome to Alliance Jiu Jitsu Inland Empire Looking for the best Brazilian Jiu Jitsu academy in Redlands and the Inland Empire? At Alliance Jiu Jitsu Inland Empire , we combine world-class instruction, a supportive family atmosphere, and proven training programs for kids, teens, and adults of all skill levels. Whether you’re brand new to martial arts, a parent looking for a positive activity for your child, or an athlete preparing for competition, our academy has a program designed for you.
 What sets us apart is our community. From our Baby Eagles program for ages 3–5 to our adult fundamentals, advanced, and women’s classes , every student receives expert coaching in a welcoming environment built on respect, discipline, and teamwork. Our instructors are highly trained and passionate about helping you achieve your goals—on and off the mats.
 As part of the Alliance Jiu Jitsu network , one of the most successful teams in the world, our students benefit from a curriculum trusted by champions and everyday practitioners alike. You’ll gain real self-defense skills, improve fitness, build confidence, and join a supportive community that feels like family.
