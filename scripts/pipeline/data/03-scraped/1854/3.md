 # What to expect from our Adult Martial Arts Program
 If you're new to jiu-jitsu, or haven’t been on the mat in a while, the first thing you’ll feel is the soreness—muscles you didn’t even know existed will make themselves known. But if you stick with it, that initial soreness will give way to real transformation. You’ll notice your body becoming leaner and fitter; your mood will improve, irritability will fade, and restful sleep will become the norm. Over time, the self-defense skills you learn in jiu-jitsu, judo, and BJJ classes will feel built into you, with many moves becoming automatic, woven into your subconscious mind.
 As the weeks turn into months, training at Winter Haven Martial Arts will shift from a challenging new hobby to an essential part of your life. If you make it through that first year, you’ve probably hit a turning point—jiu-jitsu won’t just be something you do; it’ll be something you can’t imagine living without. Martial arts at Winter Haven becomes a lifelong pursuit, a "good addiction" that keeps you coming back for the growth, camaraderie, and the endless depth of the art itself.
