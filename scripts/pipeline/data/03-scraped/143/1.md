## Class Offerings

 Lotus Academy offers an array of different class options for the convenience of students of all age groups.

 #### Gi Classes

 The Gi is slower and requires more patience. Gi training instills the importance of fine technique—especially grip fighting. All students that compete find the transition to No Gi competition straight forward, and Pete’s students have a record of winning tournaments.

 #### No Gi Classes

 Lotus Academy offers two No Gi classes every week. When combined with Gi training, students will find their No Gi improving rapidly—becoming proficient in the art.

 #### Fundamentals/Flow Classes

 Fundamentals classes are focused on helping students build a solid foundation that they will carry through the rest of their Jiu Jitsu journey.

 ### KIDS PROGRAM Lotus Academy offers a kids program for helping children and adolescents learn the basics of Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu in a fun and stimulating environment. In the kids classes, students learn discipline, hone their Brazilian Jiu Jitsu skills, and build self-confidence.
