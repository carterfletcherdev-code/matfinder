At Eclipse Blackbelt Academy, we offer a female only environment to enable the study of kick boxing, modern free-style karate & self defence. The classes cater for all levels of fitness & ability; beginners are welcome at any time.
 Classes are structured offering one class per week predominately instructional , learning techniques & self defence whilst the second class offers a fun & exciting workout to develop fitness levels with padwork & circuit training. Cardio-vascular fitness, strength, balance, co-ordination & flexibility will improve giving you confidence & energy to do more!
 We hope to see you soon to enable us to demonstrate our expertise. If you have any questions regarding our Women Only classes, please contact Kerry Cope.
 Class Times: Tuesdays @ 6:15 – Wednesdays @ 7:00 – Thursday Mornings (term Time) @ 9:30
