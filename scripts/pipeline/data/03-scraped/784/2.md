 #### New - Women’s Only Self-Defense & Jiu-Jitsu Classes - Every Saturday 9:00am ### Empower. Protect. Thrive.

 At Ghost Squad Castle Rock, our Women’s Only Self-Defense and Jiu-Jitsu program is designed to help women of all ages and fitness levels build confidence, strength, and real-world self-defense skills in a positive, supportive environment.
 Led by experienced instructors and coaches who understand the unique challenges women face, this class goes beyond fitness—it’s about empowerment, awareness, and community.

 ### What You’ll Learn
 - Practical Self-Defense Skills: Learn how to defend yourself effectively against larger or stronger opponents using leverage, technique, and strategy—not brute strength. - Situational Awareness: Develop the mindset and instincts to recognize and avoid danger before it happens. - Ground Defense & Escapes: Gain control and learn how to escape from common holds or attacks. - Confidence Through Skill: As you progress, you’ll discover an inner strength that transforms how you carry yourself—both on and off the mat.
