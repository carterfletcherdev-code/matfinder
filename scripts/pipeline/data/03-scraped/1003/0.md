 ## Frequently Asked Questions Performance Jiu-Jitsu is proud to offer our classes to kids, teens, and adults. Below are some of the most frequently asked questions we receive.
 ### Do you have early morning BJJ classes? Yes! We regularly have early morning adult BJJ classes that start at 6:30 am. This can help you start your day right with movement and training before heading off to work. We also offer Jiu-Jitsu classes during your lunch break and early on weekends.
 ### Are your classes for advanced BJJ students? We have a BJJ class for you, regardless of your belt color. Here at Performance Jiu-Jitsu, we have beginner, all-level, and advanced BJJ classes. It’s great for white belts to train with blue, brown, or black belts, but it’s also good for similar belts to train with each other to test advanced skills.
 ### Do you have open mat times? We sure do! This is a great time to test skills you’ve learned in class. Open Mat for BJJ is an unstructured time to train or practice with other students. You can come to work on a specific skill, free-roll to find openings you’d otherwise miss during a structured class, or simply hang out with your BJJ friends.
 ### Do you offer Gracie Jiu-Jitsu classes? At Performance Jiu-Jitsu, we offer Gracie and Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu classes. Gracie Jiu-Jitsu focuses more on self-defense, while Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu focuses more on the sport of Jiu-Jitsu and preparing for competitions. We believe that being taught more aspects of Jiu-Jitsu is essential.
 ### Are there kids’ BJJ classes? Our Kids’ BJJ program is offered for children ages 5-13. We separate the kids into two different age group classes to focus on different self-defense techniques and to allow your children to practice with like-sized partners. At our BJJ gym, we have classes during the week and on weekends for your kids to train.
 ### Do you offer adult BJJ classes? Yes, we have adult BJJ classes. We have many different adult Jiu-Jitsu classes offered throughout the week, so we are sure to have a class that fits your schedule. At Performance Jiu-Jitsu, our black belt instructors offer classes such as all-level Gi, white belt fundamentals, self-defense, and more.
