For your first workout at 9Round Kansas City, MO - Washington St., we recommend arriving 10-15 minutes before your appointment time to fill out a wavier and tour the studio before you start your workout.  Because our workouts combine high-intensity interval training (HIIT), kickboxing, strength training, and cardio, they are designed to maximize calorie burn while building strength.

 ## How often should I work out at 9Round to see results? We recommend working out at 9Round Kansas City, MO - Washington St. 3-5 days a week for the best results.
 Many of our members find it easy to work out multiple times per week thanks to our efficient 30-minute workouts with no class times.

 ## Is kickboxing good for weight loss? Yes. Kickboxing workouts combine high-intensity cardio, strength training, and core exercises that can help burn calories and build lean muscle.
 Many members use 9Round workouts as part of a fitness routine designed to support healthy weight loss.
