 #### Learn More ### About Arctic Training Center

 #### About Us We make sure every day that our students are encouraged to be their best. Our team uplifts everyone who walks through the doors past fears and to discover their comfort level with us. We focus on a strong mindset as well as effective, efficient Jiu Jitsu for self defense and especially competition. And here – we practice what we preach. Many of our instructors, including co-owner Dania Seaver, the first Palestinian woman to earn a black belt, still compete on the mat. This has made us the best competition Jiu Jitsu gym in the area. But we are more than just a competition gym. Culture matters just as much. Every year we lead 10-15 social gatherings and outings to build camaraderie and togetherness. All we do is to support everyone who wants to take martial arts lessons. Whatever you need, we are here for you!
 #### History When we opened our Palos Heights martial arts gym at the end of 2020, we knew we wanted to give back to the community. Everything we do is for the community. First, our name. Instead of being a “school” or “academy”, we are a training center – a place for people to belong and get better. This includes us hosting our women’s only classes in a separate building, keeping everyone safe and comfortable. We have even added mat spaces in both buildings as well as more classes and programs, allowing for personal training, fitness, and competition. All of this work has been led by our owners, Alex and Diana Seaver. They are highly accomplished martial artists, each winning high-level competitions and Diana being the first Palestinian Female Black Belt. Even with all they have done, they continue to put their heart into making Arctic Training Center a place for everyone to train and make their life better.

 ### The Arctic Training Center Advantage
 Arctic Training Center is the most unique Jiu Jitsu gym in Palos Heights – and probably anywhere. With high-level competition, strong community bonds, and a second specialized location, we are here to make your martial arts training experience the best it can be!

 ##### Women’s Only Classes and Building Not only do we have our own women’s martial arts classes – we have our own women’s martial arts building! You can come in and train and feel comfortable in one of our 4-5 martial arts classes a day! We are a block away at 12332 South Harlem, Palos Heights, IL .

 ##### Competition Jiu Jitsu Success Arctic Training Center has seen students – those even in kids Jiu Jitsu – start with us and become dominant competitors. This carries onto our instructors, who still compete at the highest levels.

 ##### Personable Instructors Joining a martial arts gym can be intimidating to some. Our coaches treat every student with respect and are always listening to what they need to make their martial arts lessons as beneficial as possible.

 ##### Personal Training and Nutrition We make sure you not only improve your physical fitness through martial arts training, but also away from the mat. You can join us and receive personal training as well as nutritional advice.

 ##### Social Activities and Gatherings Strong bonds are a huge component of our Palos Height Jiu Jitsu community. That’s why we run 10-15 events every year, from Laser Tag to social gatherings to become closer on and off the mat.

 ##### Mindset Focus We don’t just teach you self-defense martial arts and on-the-mat skills. Our focus is to improve your mindset and build up your mental strength.

 ### Serving Communities Across the Palos Area We proudly welcome students from throughout the southwest suburban communities surrounding Palos Heights to train at Arctic Training Center. Families and individuals from Palos Park, Worth, Crestwood, and Alsip choose our academy for structured, professional Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu instruction that builds confidence, discipline, and real-world self-defense skills. Whether you’re brand new to training or continuing your journey, our programs serve students of all ages and experience levels while keeping high-quality instruction close to home.
