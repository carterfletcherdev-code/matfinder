 ## Frequently Asked Questions About Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu

 ### Do I need experience to start Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu?

 #### 1

 No experience is required to start Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu at Fight Club Fitness. Our Birmingham BJJ classes are beginner-friendly and focus on fundamentals, safety, and controlled training so new students can learn at a comfortable pace.

 ### Is Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu good for self-defense?

 #### 2

 Yes. Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu is one of the most effective martial arts for real-world self-defense, especially in close-range situations. Our BJJ training in Birmingham emphasizes control, positioning, and awareness rather than strength alone. Coach Tony is also a retired Birmingham Police Officer!

 ### Do I need a gi to train Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu?

 #### 3

 A gi is recommended for Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu training, but new students are welcome to start without one. If you’re unsure what gear you need, our Birmingham BJJ instructors will help guide you before your first class.

 ## Is there sparring in beginner BJJ classes?

 #### 4

 Beginner Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu classes may include optional, light positional training. Safety is always the priority, and students are never required to spar until they feel ready and comfortable.
