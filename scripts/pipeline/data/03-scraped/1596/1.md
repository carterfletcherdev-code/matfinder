# Welcome to Six Blades Jiu-Jitsu San Antonio North West

 ## Jiu- Jitsu for Everyone 1) Demonstration by Instructor 2) Observation by student 3) Imitation 4) Practical Application 5) Questions/Adjustments 6 ) back to number 1 and Repeat! With this process, the entire curriculum of RGJJ in whatever Mode can be absorbed into muscle memory by all students regardless of ability or lack of. Our goal is to train all of our students with the ability to defend themselves safely in whatever environment with Confidence, Calm, and a Level Response. When we compete, we represent RGJJ with Honest Competitive Spirit, Fair Play, and Honor.
