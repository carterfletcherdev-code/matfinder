More Than Classes: Private Lessons & Events at Sheepdog

 BLOGS | January 4, 2026

 At Sheepdog Jiu-Jitsu, we believe training should meet people where they are—whether that’s on the mat during a regular class or through a customized private experience. In addition to our group programs, we proudly offer private lessons, corporate events, and birthday parties in Tulsa , each designed with the same commitment to safety, structure, and practical training.
 These private event offerings allow individuals, families, and organizations to experience Sheepdog Jiu-Jitsu in a more personalized and flexible way.
 ## Private Jiu-Jitsu Lessons: Focused Coaching, Faster Progress Our Private Jiu-Jitsu Lessons provide one-on-one or small-group instruction for students who want individualized coaching and accelerated progress. Private lessons are ideal for a wide range of people, including beginners who want extra attention, experienced students refining technique, competitors preparing for events, youth or teens who benefit from focused instruction, and adults seeking practical self-defense skills.
 Each private session is tailored to the student’s goals and experience level. Lessons can focus on real-world self-defense , sport Jiu-Jitsu , Gi or No-Gi training , or specific areas where a student wants improvement. Many students use private lessons alongside group classes to reinforce skills, break through plateaus, and gain deeper understanding more efficiently.
 Private lessons are available to both members and non-members , with flexible scheduling options to fit busy lifestyles.
 ## Corporate Events: Professional Team Building With Purpose For organizations looking for meaningful corporate team-building activities in Tulsa , Sheepdog Jiu-Jitsu offers custom corporate and group events that go beyond traditional outings. These events combine structured physical training with leadership development, communication, and situational awareness—all in a professional, approachable environment.
 Corporate events can include:
 - Team-building experiences that promote trust and collaboration - Employee self-defense and situational awareness training - Confidence, leadership, and stress-management development
 Every event is fully customizable, including length, group size, scheduling, intensity, and overall focus. No martial arts experience is required, and all activities are designed to be safe, controlled, and suitable for mixed fitness levels and office environments.
 Events can be hosted at our gym or on-site at a company location when appropriate. First responders and related organizations receive discounted pricing.
 ## Birthday Parties: Fun, Active Celebrations Kids Love If you’re searching for a unique and engaging option for birthday parties in Tulsa , Sheepdog Jiu-Jitsu offers fun, age-appropriate celebrations that keep kids active and smiling. Birthday parties include martial arts–based games, light self-defense instruction, and plenty of movement—always supervised by experienced instructors.
 Activities vary by age group to ensure kids are challenged while staying safe and having fun. Parents can choose to provide food and cake themselves, or have pizza, cake, and refreshments arranged upon request.
 Our birthday parties offer a structured, screen-free alternative to traditional party venues and are a great way for kids to burn energy, build confidence, and celebrate with friends.
 ## One Philosophy, Many Ways to Train Whether through private lessons, corporate events, or birthday parties, every Sheepdog Jiu-Jitsu offering is guided by the same philosophy: practical, effective training in a safe, structured environment . We focus on real-world application, intelligent decision-making, and building confidence that carries beyond the mat.
 If you’re interested in learning more or booking a private event, we invite you to reach out and explore how Sheepdog Jiu-Jitsu can create a customized experience for you, your team, or your family.
