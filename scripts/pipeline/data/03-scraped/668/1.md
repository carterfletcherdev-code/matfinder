## Virtue BJJ # Adult Brazilian Jiu Jitsu At Virtue BJJ, our all levels program is designed to advance both your conceptual and physical proficiency in Brazilian Jiu Jitsu. We have taken the complex system of BJJ and broken it down into fun and easy to learn lessons. We focus on the finer details in the techniques and also introduce you to the world of Jiu Jitsu as a sport. We start class with a warm-up and stretching then move on to our techniques of the day to learn. We will introduce situational training and controlled sparing to finish out the class.
