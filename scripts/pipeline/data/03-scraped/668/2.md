## Virtue BJJ # Kids Brazilian Jiu Jitsu Virtue BJJ offers martial arts classes for kids ages 3 and up. Our students learn important life skills such as discipline, resilience, respect for themselves and others, teamwork, and confidence in our Kids BJJ program. They will also reap the physical benefits like strength, endurance, coordination, body awareness, and countless other attributes that come with learning Brazilian Jiu Jitsu. BJJ is the most practical martial area for self-defense, which makes it ideal for children to learn. With no striking involved, our kids can train and compete safely with a much lower risk of head injuries than any other contact sport.
