### Virtue BJJ Texas ## About Us At Virtue Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu in Belton, TX, we offer high-level instruction in BJJ for men, women, and kids of all ages. We have a variety of classes that teach all of these skills in a very welcoming and family-focused environment. We also have one of the most active competition teams in central Texas and we compete regularly in Jiu-Jitsu tournaments, both locally and nationally. So don’t wait, come check us out to learn discipline, gain self-confidence, get in shape, and have some fun with the best Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu program in the area. We have classes 6 days per week! Train for more than competition, train for life!
 Proudly serving Temple, Belton, Salado, Killeen, Harker Heights, and surrounding areas.

 #### Virtue BJJ Texas ### Teens/Adults Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu At Virtue Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, our all levels program is designed to advance both your conceptual and physical proficiency in BJJ. We have taken the complex system and broken it down into fun and easy to learn lessons.
 Adult Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu

 #### ### Little Ninjas (ages 3+) This fun, high-energy class helps kids build confidence, coordination, and discipline through games and drills. Students learn basic BJJ positions, teamwork, and respect in a playful, structured setting with patient, supportive instructors.

 #### ### All Level Kids Classes (ages 6+) Our All Levels Kids Class welcomes beginners and experienced students, teaching fundamental BJJ techniques while building confidence, discipline, and respect. In a safe, fun environment, kids progress at their own pace, develop teamwork skills, and grow on and off the mats.

 #### ### Virtue Wrestling Club Our all-levels Wrestling Program teaches the fundamentals of folkstyle wrestling to boost BJJ skills and prepare kids/teens for school wrestling. Free with Unlimited membership, with special club rates and school student discounts available.

 #### ### Open Mat Open mat is a relaxed, no-ego time to drill, roll, and connect with teammates. All levels welcome—experiment, ask questions, and get extra mat time outside class. No formal instruction, just good rounds, good vibes, and fun. Visitors from other academies are welcome!

 #### ### Judo Our Judo class focuses on throws, takedowns, and grip fighting, teaching timing, leverage, and balance over strength. Open to beginners and experienced students, it’s perfect for sharpening stand-up skills for BJJ or competition. Soft subfloor and crash pad ensure safe, comfortable training.
