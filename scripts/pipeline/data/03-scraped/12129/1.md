# Why you should be at open mats…
 Jul 13 Written By Craig Hanna

 You went to class all week, learned a bunch of techniques, and watched countless YouTube videos with the latest moves. Now it’s time to unleash your fury at open mat!!! Or maybe just work on some stuff.
 Seriously though, open mats are less structured than a class and are there for you and your teammates to work on moves, positions, or rolls at your own pace. Whether you’re experimenting with new moves, having trouble dialing something in, or just need some tough rounds… open mats provide a perfect opportunity for you to learn and improve. Plus, with a large mix of belt levels and gi vs. no-gi, you’ll gain invaluable experience from several perspectives.
 We offer open mats for Sessions members and invites only on Friday’s at 12:15 and Saturday at 11:15. Hope to see you there!
