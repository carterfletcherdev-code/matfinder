 # Programs Offered at Mountain Division American Jiu-Jitsu

 ### 1 Kids American Jiu-Jitsu

 - Introduces children to American Jiu-Jitsu in a safe and fun way. - Builds confidence, discipline, respect, and character. - Includes age-based classes such as 8–13 years.

 ### 2 Adult American Jiu-Jitsu (Gi)

 - Structured for all skill levels, from beginner to advanced. - Focus on fundamentals: takedowns, sweeps, submissions, and core positions while training in the gi.

 ### 3 Adult American Jiu-Jitsu (No-Gi)

 - Training without the gi, focusing on grips, movement, and techniques for no-gi grappling. - Practical for both self-defense and real-world application.

 ### 4 Open Mat Training

 - Flexible practice time for drilling, rolling, or sparring with teammates. - Encourages experimenting with techniques in a less formal structure.

 ### 5 Private Lessons

 - One-on-one or small group sessions with a coach. - Personalized to refine techniques, improve strategy, or reach specific goals.

 ### 6 Seminars

 - Specialty instruction sessions, often featuring detailed techniques or guest instructors. - Allows for deeper exploration of American Jiu-Jitsu concepts.

 ### 7 Self-Defense & Kickboxing

 - Classes that combine striking, situational awareness, and self-defense strategies. - Complements American Jiu-Jitsu by teaching stand-up and striking skills.
