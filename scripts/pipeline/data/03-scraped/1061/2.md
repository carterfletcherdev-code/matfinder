 Fundamentals Class - Beginner to Intermediate
 Our Fundamentals Class is built for students new to Jiu Jitsu and grappling through early intermediate levels .
 It delivers a structured approach to the core movements, positions, and techniques that every practitioner relies on.
 Each class combines technical instruction and purposeful conditioning , helping students improve:
 Strength • Endurance • Mobility • Coordination • Body Awareness
 As you progress, you’ll develop a deeper understanding of:
 • Foundational Jiu Jitsu and grappling concepts
 • Practical self-defense applications
 • Efficient movement and control in key positions
 This class is ideal for anyone looking to build or sharpen their basics with a clear, supportive path to consistent improvement and long-term growth .

 Intermediate to Advanced Class
 Open to all students, this class focuses on expanding your game through progressive technique development, strategic concepts, and live training .
 Each session begins with a systematic breakdown of two high-level techniques or sequences , followed by 30 minutes of guided sparring (rolling) to apply what you’ve learned in real time.
 Expect to develop:
 • Deeper technical precision
 • Timing and adaptability in live situations
 • Advanced problem-solving and situational awareness
 • Confidence under pressure
