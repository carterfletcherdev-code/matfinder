# Kids Classes

 ## Creating Confident Kids Jiu Jitsu is touted as a successful “anti-bullying” program as it provides kids the ability to protect themselves against bullies without punching or kicking. Giving kids techniques to defend themselves with deflection moves and submission holds, jiu jitsu provides your child with life long skills that build confidence to take on challenging circumstances.

 ### Gi vs. No Gi

 The Gi is the traditional “kimono” consisting of pants, jacket and belt. All kids classes will consist of Gi sessions with the exception of No Gi on Wednesdays. The Gi can be purchased from anywhere and can be in any color!

 ### Competition Opportunities

 Manitowoc Jiu Jitsu Academy (MJA) offers many opportunities for kids to display their skills in a competitive setting. MJA is a top seeded team across the many different jiu jitsu gyms that are represented at these competitions and the kids bring home medals to show for their dedication and hard work.
