 ### Jiu Jitsu Benefits

 Learn Practical Self Defense
 Jiu Jitsu teaches effective techniques for defending yourself, especially for a smaller person against a larger attacker. The techniques are applicable for real-life situations.

 Get a Great Workout
 A 60-90 minute Jiu Jitsu workout will leave you drenched in sweat. It’s a full body workout that uses muscles you never knew you had. Rolling builds your cardio. A class can burn 400-1000 calories.

 Learn New Skills
 Keep your brain sharp by constantly learning new techniques and strategies. Jiu Jitsu is always evolving, providing continual learning for students of all levels.

 Meet New People
 The Jiu Jitsu community is very friendly. Training partners help each other improve in a spirit of camaraderie. Lifelong friendships are built on the mats.

 More Fun Than the Gym
 Jiu Jitsu training is dynamic and communal, unlike the repetitive and isolating gym workout. There’s a great sense of community and engagement.

 Test Your Comfort Zone
 Stepping outside your comfort zone leads to tremendous personal growth. Jiu Jitsu will challenge you physically and mentally, building confidence and pride.

 Relieve Stress
 Jiu Jitsu helps relieve stress through physical exertion, human connection, and by forcing you to focus on the present moment.

 Build Humility
 You will be humbled as a beginner, but coaches and training partners will guide you with empathy. This teaches kindness and patience.

 Gain Confidence
 Testing yourself, persisting through challenges, and making connections builds unshakable self-confidence over time.
