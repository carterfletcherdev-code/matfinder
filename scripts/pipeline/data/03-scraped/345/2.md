 - Core Focus: Basic movements, coordination, and skill development. - Builds: Physical strength, confidence, and a sense of achievement. - Activities: Basic movement drills, grappling games and supervised light sparring for safe, joyful exploration.

 - Core Focus: Basic principles, techniques, movement patterns, and strategies with age-appropriate rigor. - Builds: Coordination, strength, confidence, and grappling skills. - Activities: Movement and technical drills, grappling games, and supervised sparring for fun, self-defense, and competition.

 ### Fundamentals Master the building blocks of Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu with a streamlined curriculum crafted by experts. Efficient, effective training that gets you rolling from day one — suitable for teens and adults of all levels.
 - Core Focus: The effective application of grappling skills, principles, and techniques. - Training Style: General and technical warmups, classic drilling, live/situational reps. - Progression: Culminates in live sparring to solidify skills.
