 ## Frequently Asked Questions Get answers to common questions about training at 10th Planet Mission Viejo

 ### 1 Do you offer free trial classes? We do not offer free trial classes. Instead, we use a Class Pass system. You can purchase a Day Pass ($35) or a Week Pass ($100) to try our training. If you decide to join, the value of your pass can be credited toward the membership of your choice.

 ### 2 How much is the Beginners Course? Our 6-week Beginners Course is $599. This includes all classes during the program period.

 ### 3 What should I wear to class? No Gi/Wrestling:
 Shorts (no zipper/pockets) and compression top/rashguard. Wrestling shoes optional for Wrestling class

 Gi Jiu Jitsu:
 Gi (jacket and pants) with a belt.

 Muay Thai:
 Gloves, shin guards, hand wraps, mouthpiece, and cup. We recommend elbow and knee pads later for sparring.

 MMA:
 All Jiu-Jitsu and Muay Thai gear, plus MMA gloves.

 Mandatory Hygiene Rules:
 - No bare feet while off the mat - No shoes while on the mat (if wearing wrestling shoes for class then must bring shoe covers to step off of training area) - Clean set of clothes for each class taken in a day - No leaving in sweaty training gear. MUST have dry top/bottom to change into for after class or no training will be permitted for the day - No training with any skin irregularities. We expect our students to check their skin daily and inspect for any signs of skin infections. Ask your instructor what warning signs may appear like if you are ever unsure - Finger/toe nails must be trimmed and filed before every class - Shower immediately after class

 ### 4 Do I need to be in shape to start? No prior fitness level is required. Our Beginners Course is designed for people of all fitness levels and will help you build conditioning gradually.

 ### 5 What Should I expect? Jiu Jitsu is very rewarding! You should expect to get tired, sweaty, all while having a great time and learning to defend yourself.
