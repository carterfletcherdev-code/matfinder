 Grappling Foundation
 ## Wrestling Wrestling is the foundation of all grappling martial arts. At Rising Tide MoCo, our wrestling program focuses on takedowns, control, and top pressure — skills that translate directly to Jiu-Jitsu, MMA, and real-world self-defense.
 Our classes are designed for all experience levels. Whether you've never wrestled before or you competed in high school or college, you'll find structured instruction that sharpens your skills and builds explosive athleticism.
 Wrestling classes emphasize functional movement, takedown chains, scrambles, and mat returns. You'll develop the strength, conditioning, and grit that only wrestling can build.
 ### What You'll Learn - Single-leg and double-leg takedown technique - Clinch work and underhook positioning - Scramble recovery and mat return drills - Explosive conditioning and functional strength - Wrestling for BJJ and MMA applications
