 Common Questions
 ## Ventura BJJ FAQ
 ### How far is Espiritu Jiu-Jitsu from Ventura? Espiritu Jiu-Jitsu is located in Oxnard, approximately 10 miles south of downtown Ventura. The drive takes about 15 minutes via US-101 South to Victoria Avenue.

 ### Do you offer early morning BJJ classes for Ventura commuters? Yes. We offer 6:00 AM classes Monday, Tuesday, Thursday, and Friday. Many of our students from Ventura train before work and are on the road by 7:15 AM.

 ### What BJJ programs are available near Ventura? Espiritu Jiu-Jitsu offers programs for Adults (17+), Teens (11-16), Kids (7-10), and Little Bears (4-6). All programs include Gi, No-Gi, and sparring sessions taught by black belt instructors in the Renzo Gracie lineage.

 ### Is there a free trial class for Ventura residents? Absolutely. Your first class is always free with no obligation. Fill out our trial form or call (805) 203-0847 to schedule. We provide loaner gis for your first visit.

 ### Why train at Espiritu instead of a gym in Ventura? Espiritu Jiu-Jitsu is a dedicated BJJ academy — not a mixed martial arts gym. Our head instructor, Professor Jeremy Espiritu, is a black belt under Shawn Williams in the Renzo Gracie lineage. The focused curriculum, small class sizes, and authentic lineage set us apart.
