 ## Grandmaster Helio's original self defense system

 Royce Gracie Jiu-Jitsu Academy of Fayetteville offers a comprehensive, multi-level Gracie Jiu-Jitsu curriculum developed by Royce Gracie, adhering closely to his teachings for all experience levels.
 Our program includes hundreds of techniques that could be crucial in real-world situations, covering self-defense, grappling, weapon defense, law enforcement/military applications, and a curriculum for kids.
 Originally, Grandmaster Helio’s self-defense system was only available through private lessons. Royce Gracie created our program to allow students to learn his father’s techniques in a group setting.

 # Frequently Asked Questions

 #### What is Royce Gracie Jiu-Jitsu (RGJJ)? - Royce Gracie Jiu-Jitsu (RGJJ) is a martial art that focuses on grappling and ground fighting techniques. It emphasizes the use of leverage, technique, and strategy to control and submit opponents, making it effective for self-defense and competition.

 #### Do I need prior experience to join? - No prior experience is required! Our classes cater to all skill levels, from complete beginners to advanced practitioners. Our instructors will guide you at your own pace.

 #### What should I wear to my first class? - For your first class, wear comfortable athletic clothing such as a rash guard, t-shirt, and shorts without zippers. If you have a gi (kimono), bring it along. We also have rental or loaner gis available for beginners.

 #### Do I need a gi (kimono) to train? - We offer both gi and no-gi classes. While a gi is required for traditional classes, no-gi training allows for rash guards and grappling shorts. Beginners can start without a gi and purchase one later.

 #### How often should I train? - Training frequency depends on your goals and schedule. Beginners may start with 2-3 classes per week, while advanced students and competitors often train daily. Consistency is key!

 #### Is Jiu Jitsu safe? - Yes! Jiu Jitsu is designed to be a controlled and safe martial art. Our instructors emphasize proper technique, controlled sparring, and injury prevention. However, as with any sport, minor injuries may occur.

 #### Will I have to spar (roll) in my first class? - No, beginners are not required to spar in their first class. You will first learn fundamental techniques before gradually participating in live training (rolling) when you feel comfortable.

 #### What age groups do you offer classes for? - We offer classes for children, teens, and adults. Our kids’ programs start as young as 5 years old, and we have adult classes for all age groups.

 #### How long does it take to get a black belt in RGJJ? - BJJ belts are earned through consistent training, skill development, and dedication. On average, it takes 8-12 years to achieve a black belt, though the timeline varies for each individual.

 #### Will my child become a bully? We find that our students become more confident to stand up to bullies and have more humility and kindness to others through their martial arts lessons.

 #### Can I train RGJJ for self-defense? - Absolutely! RGJJ is one of the most effective martial arts for real-world self-defense. Our curriculum includes practical self-defense techniques that are useful in real-life situations.
