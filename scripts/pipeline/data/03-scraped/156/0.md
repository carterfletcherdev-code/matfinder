 ## WHAT WE OFFER

 #### ADULT CLASSES

 Our adult classes are beginner to advanced friendly. Our black belt instructors teach effective BJJ techniques while focusing on building a strong foundation of tried and true basics. They will help you develop your BJJ skills based on your individual needs.

 #### Kids CLASSES

 Kids BJJ classes focus on learning the basics, utilizing BJJ for self defense, building confidence, and having a fun time. Kids who train become stronger and more disciplined, and learn tools that they can use in all aspects of life.

 #### Private SESSIONS

 Private sessions are available for those looking for BJJ training tailored specifically for their needs. One-on-one lessons are a great option for those looking to take their training to the next level, or those who don’t love the group training environment.
