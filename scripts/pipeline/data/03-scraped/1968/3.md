 What types of martial arts do you specialize in?

 We focus on Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu for adults, teens, kids, and women—offering mindful, practical self-defense and personal growth.

 How do I get started with a class?

 Fill out our online form to schedule your introductory session, and our team will help guide you through every step.

 Are there separate classes for children?

 Yes, our Kids Jiu-Jitsu program is designed for children and youth, blending discipline, fun, and growth in a mindful setting.

 Can I drop in for just one class?

 Yes, we allow drop-ins.

 Is Ground Dwellers The Woodlands beginner-friendly?

 Absolutely. Our programs welcome beginners and experienced practitioners alike, with expert coaching in a supportive, calming environment.

 Do you offer women-only Jiu-Jitsu classes?

 Yes! Our women’s program is specifically structured to provide a safe and empowering training space for women of all ages.

 What should I bring to my first class?

 Wear comfortable athletic clothing or a gi if you have one. Our team will provide further guidance after your introduction.

 Is martial arts suitable for those over 50?

 Definitely. Our mindful approach and supportive coaches ensure students of all ages benefit from Jiu-Jitsu training.

 What areas do you serve?

 We serve The Woodlands, Conroe, Spring, Tomball, and Magnolia—welcoming members from surrounding areas to join our academy.
