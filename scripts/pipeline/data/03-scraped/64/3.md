## Carlson Gracie Green Valley # Homeschool Jiu-Jitsu Looking for the perfect mix of fitness, discipline, and fun for your homeschooler? Our Homeschool Jiu-Jitsu Program at Carlson Gracie Green Valley is designed with families like yours in mind!

 ### Homeschool Jiu-Jitsu
 #### Why Choose Our Program Our Homeschool Jiu-Jitsu Program offers daytime classes designed to fit homeschool schedules, making it easy for families to participate. We provide a safe and encouraging environment for students of all ages and experience levels to grow and learn. Kids build confidence, respect, and resilience while learning real self-defense through Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu. Along the way, they make new friends and form meaningful community connections.

 #### What Parents Love Character development is at the heart of every class, helping students grow both on and off the mats. Our small-group environment ensures personalized attention so every child feels supported and encouraged. Classes also provide a healthy outlet for energy, which helps students improve focus and perform better in their academics.
