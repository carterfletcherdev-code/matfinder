 # Brazilian Jiu Jitsu - Delray Beach Florida

 ## Where we keep fighting

 After 11 years serving in the Marines and Army, I didn’t have much patience for how local BJJ gyms were run. Disrespectful management, hidden fees, zero brotherhood, and cult-like atmospheres. So I started Patriot BJJ, the gym I wish existed when I started training.
 At Patriot BJJ in Delray Beach, we do things differently with down-to-earth management. No unnecessary rituals, cult mentality, or power trips. We offer the most affordable BJJ training in the area with transparent pricing and no nickel-and-diming. But most importantly, we focus on what actually works: real BJJ technique, practical skills, and training that builds genuine confidence, mental toughness, and brotherhood.
 We welcome adults as well as kids. Whether you’re a complete beginner, experienced practitioner, you’ll find a community that respects your time, your money, and your goals. Veterans and military receive special benefits including $25 off monthly tuition. And qualifying veterans have access to free training scholarships through our WeDefy affiliate program.  ## The Men Behind "Patriot"

 ## Down to earth management that respects you

 Owner

 Thales Rodrigues

 Thales Rodrigues is the owner and founder of Patriot BJJ. A BJJ blue belt, Thales served as an Infantryman in the US Marines and Army from 2014 to 2025, deploying multiple times around the world.
 He started Patriot BJJ to build a strong community for veterans and active duty service members; and because he didn’t agree with the way many other gyms were run.
 Patriot BJJ is veteran-owned, built on real discipline, respect, and brotherhood.

 Coach

 Rodrigo Aliperti

 Meet Professor Rodrigo Aliperti. Head Instructor at Patriot BJJ. A Black Belt under Nova União, Black Belt in Judo, and Coral Belt in Karate, he brings over 20 years of teaching experience to the mats.
 Professor Rodrigo has dedicated his life to changing lives through martial arts; not just teaching techniques, but building confidence, discipline, and character in every student.
