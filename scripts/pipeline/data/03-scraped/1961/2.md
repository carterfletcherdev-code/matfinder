 # Welcome to Behring Jiu Jitsu & Shinobi MMA Northern NY's premier martial arts studio!

 ## No-Gi Grappling This class focuses on submission grappling without the gi, blending techniques from Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, wrestling, and judo. Students work on takedowns, positional control, and submissions in a structured, high-intensity environment designed for both competition preparation and skill development.

 Learn More

 ## Brazilian Jiu Jitsu Brazilian Jiu Jitsu (BJJ) is a martial art and combat sport that emphasizes ground fighting and submission holds to control and defeat an opponent. You'll learn to control and submit opponents in a friendly and challenging environment.
 Learn More

 ## Mixed Martial Arts This class combines striking and grappling in a full mixed martial arts format. Each session is split into striking and grappling portions, allowing students to develop skills in stand-up techniques, takedowns, and ground control. Designed for both beginners and experienced athletes, the class emphasizes practical application, conditioning, and overall fight strategy.
 Learn More

 ## Kid's Brazilian Jiu Jitsu Brazilian Jiu Jitsu Kids teaches children self-defense, discipline, and physical fitness through ground-fighting techniques and submission holds. Kids will build their confidence and develop a solid foundation in BJJ, regardless of their skill level.
 Learn More

 ## Judo Judo is a Japanese martial art where throws, holds, and grappling techniques are used to subdue an opponent. You'll gain physical strength, flexibility, mental discipline, and increased self-confidence from practicing judo.
 Learn More
