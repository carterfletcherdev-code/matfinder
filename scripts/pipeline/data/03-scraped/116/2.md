# BRAZILIAN JIU-JITSU

 ## THE GENTLE ART

 Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu (BJJ) is a martial art and combat sport that emphasizes ground fighting and submission holds. Known as the "gentle art," it allows a smaller, weaker person to successfully defend against and overcome a larger, stronger assailant through the use of leverage, proper technique, and strategy.
 BJJ teaches that control over an opponent can be achieved through skilled positioning and the application of joint locks and chokeholds. It encourages practitioners to approach challenges with calmness, efficiency, and a focus on continuous learning.
 The practice of BJJ is not just physical; it also fosters mental growth, building confidence, resilience, and a profound understanding of human movement and interaction.
