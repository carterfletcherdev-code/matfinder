 #### Central WASHINGTON’S PREMIER MARTIAL ARTS ACADEMY

 Axiom Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu is proud to be affiliated with Professor Marcelo Alonso and part of the Carlson Gracie Family.
 In 2008, Professor Jim McWiggins began teaching Brazilian Jiu Jitsu to Matt Fluegge and a small group of friends in Wenatchee. Two years later, Professor Jay Bretz moved to Leavenworth and founded Axiom Jiu Jitsu with Professor Jim McWiggins thanks to the help of Matt Fluegge. Since then, Axiom has grown into the premier martial arts academy in Central Washington thanks to the hard work and dedication of Jim, Matt, and Jay.
