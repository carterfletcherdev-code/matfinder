 ## FAQ

 Your Questions Answered

 ### Why Train Brazilian Jiu Jitsu? Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu (BJJ) teaches leverage and technique over size and strength , making it an effective martial art for self-defense. Most real-world fights go to the ground—BJJ prepares you to stay in control. Whether you’re training for fitness, competition, or confidence, Jiu Jitsu builds mental sharpness, cardio, and resilience .

 ### What will my first class look like? After signing up, our team will schedule your 30-minute orientation before your first Fundamentals class. You’ll learn the basics—BJJ history, bowing onto the mat, and key positions—before joining the class for warm-ups and drilling with a partner. No experience? No problem. Our coaches will guide you every step of the way!

 ### Is jiu jitsu safe? Yes! Your safety is our priority . Our structured curriculum ensures controlled training, especially for beginners. With expert instructors and a strong culture of respect, you’ll learn Jiu-Jitsu in a safe, supportive environment while improving your fitness and confidence.

 ### Do I have to enter into competitions? Not at all! Competing is encouraged, but never required at Easton —your training is at your own pace. Some students train purely for self-defense, fitness, or personal growth, while others enjoy testing their skills in competition. Whatever your goals, we’re here to support you.
 For those interested in competing, we encourage students to check out the Easton Open , our in-house Jiu-Jitsu tournament. This event provides a great opportunity—especially for beginners—to experience competition in a fun, supportive environment alongside teammates before stepping into larger tournaments.
 Train for yourself. Train for competition. Train for life. No matter your path, Easton is here for you.

 ### What makes Easton's jiu jitsu different? Easton Training Center has been Colorado’s Jiu-Jitsu pioneer since 1998 . Founded by Professor Amal Easton, a Renzo Gracie black belt , our academy has produced over 100 black belts and trains competitors at all levels, from local tournaments to professional MMA fighters.

 ### What's special about the Easton community? Jiu-Jitsu is more than just training— it’s the people . At Easton, you’ll join a team that supports, pushes, and helps you grow. Whether you’re here for self-defense, fitness, or competition, you’ll find lifelong friendships on and off the mats.

 ### Where can I train Brazilian Jiu Jitsu near me? Easton Training Center has nine academies across Colorado , offering world-class Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu for all levels. Whether you’re in Denver, Boulder, Centennial , or beyond, you’ll find expert coaching, structured programs, and a welcoming community. Try a free class today!

 ### Does Easton offer jiu jitsu for kids? Yes! Our Kids Brazilian Jiu Jitsu program is available at all Easton locations across Colorado . Designed for ages 4-13 , our program helps kids build confidence, discipline, and self-defense skills in a fun, supportive environment.

 ### What should I wear to my first jiu jitsu class? For your first class, just bring comfortable athletic clothing —we’ll provide a Gi for you to borrow. Avoid clothing with zippers, and bring a water bottle. Once you sign up, you’ll get your own Gi to train in. Come ready to learn, train, and have fun!
