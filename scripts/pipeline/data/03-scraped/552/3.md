 ## Learn More About Us:

 #### About Us: At Clever Combat Jiu-Jitsu & MMA, we take pride in cultivating an inclusive and comfortable culture. We embrace each student’s unique journey, providing adaptive support to help everyone achieve their personal goals. Whether you’re a family seeking a fun activity or a competitor striving for excellence, you’ll find a welcoming environment here.
 Our focus remains on student improvement, with multiple instructors available to offer guidance during training sessions. Additionally, our flexible Troubleshooting class allows students to work on specific needs across BJJ, Wrestling, Kickboxing/Muay Thai, or MMA. This commitment to personalized training, combined with our intricate techniques and kinesthetic approach, has propelled us to become one of the strongest competitive teams in the area. Experience a supportive and calming environment at Clever!
 #### Our History: Clever Combat Jiu-Jitsu & MMA stands apart from any other martial arts school. Founded in 2017 by William Schwartz and his four friends, the academy was built on a foundation of shared passion and experience. Having trained Jiu-Jitsu together for around 20 years, these co-instructors understand the value of community in martial arts.
 Throughout his life, William has witnessed how some schools and instructors cultivate a toxic culture. This experience inspired him and his friends to create Clever, a space that actively promotes positivity and a healthy community, ensuring that martial arts remain pure. Together, they strive to guide their students on a similar path, fostering friendships that allow individuals to train side-by-side on their long martial arts journey.
