# Martial Arts for Kids

 ## Building Stronger Minds & Bodies Since 1985

 When children grow up in a structured, positive environment focused on core values like respect, self-discipline, and health, they’re more likely to carry those values with them into adulthood. That's why many kids begin training at White Dragon as young as 4 years of age. And, whether it’s improving self-confidence, developing self-defense skills that end bullying, or instilling better attitudes toward health and fitness, White Dragon Martial Arts has the training program that’s right for your child!

 ## What's Our Secret to Success Teaching Kids?

 It’s our highly trained instructors who make learning fun and exciting! By pairing each child with his or her own personal instructor, we make sure that each student gets the expert attention needed for success. Our one-on-one approach also ensures that kids gain the kind of martial arts knowledge and skill that would normally take years to develop from a group class only approach.
