 Will My Child Become A Bully?
 We find that our students become more confident in standing up to bullies and show more humility and kindness towards others through their martial arts lessons.

 We encourage parents to watch their children in their kids martial arts lessons. We love parents getting involved! And so do their kids. Even better, join some of our martial arts classes for adults. Your kids will love that you have something in common. It will build a strong family bond!
 Additionally, if you wanted some ‘me-time’? Simply drop them off, and go grab a coffee. They will greet you with a huge smile when you return, because they had SO much fun!

 Do I Need To Be Fit For This?
 No, not at all! We find that people start at varying fitness levels. But, you will find that you do get fit by doing our North Richland Hills martial arts classes. Isn’t that the point, though? A fun workout that gets results!

 How Long Until I Get My Black Belt?
 Truthfully, the journey to a black belt is an individual one. Everyone can progress at a different pace. There is no judgment on students for how long it takes. What we can tell you is that it is SO WORTHWHILE!
 The self-defense skills you will master can truly be life-saving!
 However, the other benefits of striving for a black belt, like perseverance, never giving up, commitment, and self-discipline, lead to lifelong success.
 You will find that your instructors at TKD Armor Academy North Richland Hills are dedicated to helping you on your journey to black belt or whatever your personal goal may be.

 Make sure you wear comfortable clothes that you can do sports in! Not really suitable to practice kicks in a skirt.
 Be sure to bring a water bottle to your martial arts classes. You will find TKD Armor Academy in North Richland Hills has martial arts classes for kids and adults that are a great workout and lots of fun!  Not at all. In fact, many students starting in our adult martial arts classes would describe themselves like that before they started. So you will fit right in!
 Give yourself some time, and you will be astounded at how much you improve! You’ll find that our very experienced martial arts instructors are trained to keep their teaching simple. So even the most uncoordinated person will develop ‘ninja-like’ skills! Come see for yourself!
