# Classes & Schedule

 #### 10th Planet Jiu Jitsu Elevate your Jiu Jitsu skills regardless of your experience level. Immerse yourself in expert-led classes, dynamic training sessions, and a supportive community as you elevate your skills and confidence on the mat.
 Our All Levels classes provide a balanced training focus where you can refine grappling skills, expand your repertoire, and improve your all-around Jiu Jitsu ability.
 All Levels classes are open to both beginners and more experienced practitioners looking to learn and sharpen technique.
 At 10th Planet Jiu Jitsu Phoenix we offer a variety of different packages to meet all of your training needs. From 1 year contracted memberships to month to month options, we have you covered!
 ​

 #### Kids #### (ages 7-12) Our 10th Planet Little Lobos Kids Class is a dynamic and engaging program tailored to children, introducing them to the art of 10th Planet Jiu-Jitsu. Through crafted drills and exercises, children not only learn the basics of Jiu Jitsu, but also essential social skills. Our coach is highly trained and skilled in working with children, fostering a love for movement and physical activity.
 Building Confidence and Discipline:
 One of the key focuses of the Little Lobos Kids class is instilling confidence and increasing self-esteem in your child, while learning the basis of self defense from a wrestling and jiu jitsu standpoint. Our Coach emphasizes the importance of good habits, respect, teamwork, and discipline. Through a positive and an encouraging atmosphere kids can thrive and enjoy the learning journey at their own peace.
 Family-Friendly Community:
 Beyond the Mats, the Little Lobos Kids Class encourages family involvement and creates a supportive community for both children and parents. Gym events, competitions, and social activities provide opportunities for families to connect, creating a supportive network that extends beyond the class and enhances the overall experience for both kids and parents.
 ​

 #### Fundamentals The 10th Planet warm-ups are a series of common BJJ exchanges that go beyond repetitive drills from static or limited positions. These warm-ups are an excellent way for beginners to grasp the concepts of jiu jitsu and apply them in a sequence of movements.
 Emphasizing transitions, attacking combinations, and counters, these exercises involve resistance and realistic movements from both training partners. This approach contrasts with traditional BJJ drills, where one person acts as a passive grappling dummy, ensuring a more engaging and practical training experience.

 #### Jiu Jitsu 101 #### Teens/Adults Whether you're new to jiu jitsu or have been training for years, our Fundamentals class is designed to help you build a solid foundation or fill in the gaps of your grappling skills.
 Jiu Jitsu Fundamentals is the cornerstone of all 10th Planet Jiu Jitsu training. New students will learn the essential building blocks of jiu jitsu and grappling, while more advanced practitioners can refine their skills and maintain a strong foundation by working alongside newer students.
 No prior experience is necessary to join the Fundamentals class. Regardless of your fitness level, grappling offers an intense, physically demanding workout that challenges both mind and body.
