 ## About Ares East Mesa At Ares East Mesa Brazilian Jiu Jitsu, we offer a complete training experience for students of all ages and skill levels through our Gi, No-Gi, Kids, and Women's programs. Our curriculum is built on the foundation of traditional Brazilian Jiu Jitsu, emphasizing technique, control, and strategy.
 Training in both Gi and No-Gi enhances versatility while kids and women's programs keep the community inclusive, technical, and confidence-building.
 Expect a supportive environment, detailed coaching, and structured classes that help every belt level progress.
 The cost of enrollment varies based on the individual goals and needs of each student. Factors such as training frequency, program selection, and personal objectives all influence the overall investment. To determine the most suitable program and associated costs, it is recommended to contact Professor Alex Martinez directly. He can provide personalized guidance to help you choose the best path for you or your child's martial arts journey.
