## Kids Jiu Jitsu

 Welcome to our Kids Brazilian Jiu Jitsu class—where little warriors build big skills! This dynamic program is designed for children ages 4+ and focuses on developing self-confidence, discipline, and respect through the exciting world of martial arts. Whether your child is shy, energetic, or somewhere in between, Jiu Jitsu helps them grow into confident, capable individuals—all while having a blast.
 ### What to Expect: - Fundamental Techniques: Kids learn takedowns, escapes, and submissions in a safe, age-appropriate way. - Fitness & Coordination: Classes include warm-ups, agility drills, and movement games to boost strength and motor skills. - Bullying Prevention: We teach kids how to stay calm, set boundaries, and defend themselves responsibly. - Positive Environment: Our instructors foster teamwork, respect, and perseverance—on and off the mat. - Fun & Engagement: Every class is packed with energy, encouragement, and just the right amount of silliness to keep kids excited to come back!
