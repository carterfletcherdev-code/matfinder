 FAQs

 ##### How do I get started? The first step is to visit the school and observe a class. At that time we can answer any questions you may have, give you a tour of the facilities, and go over our program options. Schedule your visit today to find out more about what we have to offer.

 ##### How much does it cost? We offer several different program options to meet your individual needs. When you visit the school to observe a class, we will be more than happy to go over each of our memberships to find the one that works best for you.

 ##### I don’t have any prior experience in martial arts or Taekwon-do, do you take beginners? We welcome beginners, as well as people with previous experience in Karate, Judo, Kung Fu, and other martial arts. All new students are given private introductory lessons so they can learn at their own pace in a one-to-one setting. Once the introductory lessons are finished, students can attend any of the regular classes on the schedule.
