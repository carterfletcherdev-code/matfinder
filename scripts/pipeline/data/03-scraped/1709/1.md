 # ADULT Brazilian JIU-JITSU

 # Our Adult Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu Program is designed to meet you where you are—whether you’re brand new to the mats or looking to sharpen advanced skills. Classes blend fundamentals, self-defense, and technical training in a structured yet supportive setting. # With expert instruction and a step-by-step approach, students gain confidence, improve fitness, and develop practical skills they can use on and off the mats.  ## Fundamentals Class

 In our fundamentals Brazilian jiu-jitsu class you’ll learn how to handle yourself on the mats. You will learn how to maintain guard, control position, and submit your opponent. Most importantly you will improve your fitness and boost your confidence.

 ## Class Structure

 - Warm up (running, ab workout, stretch) 15min - Technique Instruction 5-10min - Drilling Technique - Sparring/Rolling

 ## No-Gi Training

 During no-gi training you’ll learn specific training for doing Brazilian jiu-jitsu without a gi. No-gi training has become very popular, especially in the world of competitive Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu. In our no-gi classes you will learn how to compete with the best. Get in touch and join us for no-gi training.

 ## Class Structure

 - Warm up (running, ab workout, stretch) 15min - Technique Instruction 5-10min - Drilling Technique - Sparring/Rolling

 ## Takedown Training

 While Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu is focused on the ground game, takedowns are still important. Grappling cannot take place with both competitors standing after all. Learning proper takedown technique can give you a real advantage in competition. So join us for our takedown class, where you’ll learn judo and wrestling takedowns to round out your grappling game.

 ## Class Structure

 - Warm up (running, ab workout, stretch) 15min - Technique Instruction 5-10min - Drilling Technique - Sparring/Rolling
