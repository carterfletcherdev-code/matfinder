# Webster Martial Arts of Madison #### Where Tradition Meets Evolution A modern blend of Tang Soo Do, Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, and movement arts — for all ages and skill levels.

 Led by third-generation martial artist Grayson Webster, this flagship program at One Tribe blends the power and tradition of Tang Soo Do with the fluidity of Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu and modern movement practices like yoga, hand balancing, and animal locomotion. Students explore a full-spectrum martial arts journey that includes traditional Japanese and Filipino weapons training, dynamic striking, real-world self-defense, and the art of board breaking.

 It’s more than self-defense. It’s a physical practice that builds strength, flexibility, and mental focus — while instilling the values that shape lifelong character.

 ## Our Roots, Our Legacy #### A Family Tradition Webster Martial Arts is more than a program — it's a lineage. Grayson Webster is a third-generation martial artist, raised on the mats and mentored by his father, Jamie. The values passed down through generations are the backbone of every class we teach: respect, perseverance, and purpose.

 #### Trained by the Best Grayson holds a 3rd Dan Black Belt in Tang Soo Do and a Blue Belt in Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu under Daniel O’Brien. His continued training with One Tribe instructors reflects the same dedication he instills in every student.
