He began his training in 2004 and has competed across the U.S., Canada, and Brazil — including the Rio Open. A lifelong martial artist and family man, John is passionate about building both fighters and a strong community. He leads the Adult BJJ and Homeschool programs.

 #### Adam Mendez – Teen/Kids BJJ Program Director Adam started his BJJ journey in 2016 at START BJJ, under the instruction of Professor Rafael "Gordinho" Correa de Lima. In 2021 he began training under the instruction of John Bruss at Rocket City Revolution BJJ. He was promoted to brown belt in July 2024. He enjoys instructing, learning new techniques, and meeting new people.
 While he's not training or instructing BJJ, he enjoys spending time with this family and 2 dogs hiking new trails. He is a proclaimed DIY'er and can't wait to start or complete a new project.
