## FAQ

 ##### 🥋 General Gym Information

 What is Like Minded Jiu Jitsu all about?
 We’re about training Jiu Jitsu for the love of it. Simple as that. This is a community for folks who show up to put in the work -no egos, no drama. We focus on creating an environment we’d want to train in ourselves.

 Who teaches at Like Minded Jiu Jitsu?
 The gym is led by highly experienced instructors with decades on the mat. You’ll be learning from folks who’ve competed, coached, and trained at all levels – but we let the mats do the talking. No big headshots or self-promotion here.

 What type of training environment can I expect?
 Welcoming, supportive, and tough in the right way. We’re here to build skill and community – not chase belts or sales pitches. You’ll be surrounded by like-minded people who love Jiu Jitsu.

 What does “Like Minded” mean in the name?
 It means you gotta want to be here. You gotta want to train. It’s about building a room full of people who share that mindset – committed, respectful, hungry to learn.

 Is the gym suitable for beginners?
 Absolutely. We welcome beginners of all ages – just come ready to learn..

 Do you offer both Gi and No-Gi classes?
 Yep. We train both Gi and No-Gi. And we encourage our students to do both; it’ll make your Jiu Jitsu better, plain and simple.

 ##### 💰 Pricing and Membership

 How much does it cost to train at Like Minded Jiu Jitsu?
 $110 per month. No hidden fees. No nonsense. Just show up and train.

 Do you offer family rates or discounts?
 We do offer family rates – but every situation is different. Just call us and we’ll talk it through.

 Are there any hidden fees or additional costs?
 Nope. What you see is what you pay. The only other thing you’ll need is a gi – and we can help you with that.

 Do you offer private lessons?
 Not something we advertise. If you’re a student here and we think it makes sense, we’ll talk. But our focus is on building skill in class, with the team.

 Is there a contract or long-term commitment required?
 No long-term contracts. Pay monthly, train as much as you want.

 ##### 👦 Youth Program

 Do you have classes for kids?
 Yes – and it’s a big focus for us. We love working with the youth and helping them grow through Jiu Jitsu.

 What ages do you accept in the kids program?
 Generally ages 5 and up. If you have questions about your child, just reach out.

 What does the kids program include?
 Jiu Jitsu fundamentals, discipline, confidence, and plenty of fun. We take pride in developing both skill and character in our youth program.

 Do parents need to stay and watch during class?
 You’re welcome to stay and watch – we have a viewing area. Totally up to you.

 ##### 🥋 Uniform & Gear

 Do I need to buy a gi or any equipment?
 For Gi classes, yes – you’ll need a gi. For No-Gi classes, rashguard and shorts are fine. No fancy gear required.

 Can I purchase a gi through the gym?
 Yes – we have team gis available at a very fair price. We’re not here to mark it up on you.

 Is there a dress code for class?
 For Gi class team gi preferred. For No-Gi rashguard and shorts (no metal or zippers). Clean gear is always expected.

 ##### 📅 Schedule & Attendance

 What is the current class schedule?
 You can view the full schedule on our website. We offer classes throughout the week for both kids and adults.

 How often can I train each week?
 As often as you want. Your membership gives you unlimited access to classes.

 Can I attend both Gi and No-Gi classes?
 Yes and we highly encourage it.

 Do you offer open mat sessions?
 Yes. We host open mat which is a great time to roll, drill, and hang out with the team.

 ##### 🏆 Competitions & Seminars

 Does Like Minded Jiu Jitsu participate in competitions?
 Many of our students compete, and we support that. But competition is optional; you set your own path here.

 Do you host seminars or events?
 Occasionally but our focus is on regular training. Follow our Instagram for updates on seminars when we do host them.

 Are seminars open to the public?
 It depends on the event. Some are team-only, some may be open. We’ll always post details on Instagram.

 ##### 📍 Location & Contact

 Where is Like Minded Jiu Jitsu located?
 We’re located in Phoenix, AZ. You can find our full address and directions on the Contact page

 How do I contact the gym?
 Call or text us; we love when people text. You can also reach us through the contact form on the site.

 Can I text the gym instead of calling?
 100%. Most folks text us first. It’s easy and quick.

 ##### 🔎 Other Common Questions

 Do you offer free trial classes?
 Yep. Come try a class & see if the vibe is right for you.

 Can I visit the gym before signing up?
 We kindly ask that all visitors schedule ahead – we’re not currently set up to accommodate drop-ins or open mat visitors.
 However, if you’re interested in becoming part of our community, we’d love to invite you to try a free introductory class or learn more about membership options.

 What makes Like Minded Jiu Jitsu different from other local gyms?
 Our environment. It’s about the people on the mat, not the marketing. We keep the focus on training, growth, and community.

 What should I expect at my first class?
 Come in with an open mind. You’ll meet a friendly crew, get a feel for our training, and leave knowing whether this is the right spot for you.
