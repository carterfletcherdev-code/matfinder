 # Oak Park Martial Arts | River Forest Karate, Self Defense, Tae Kwon Do

 We are the Original Home for True Martial Arts in Oak Park, IL, building a legacy of martial arts and self-defense excellence since 1994 (30+ years!). Led by Sr. GrandMaster Yu, a 9th-Dan, licensed 1st Class Certified, internationally recognized Sr. GrandMaster , we proudly uphold the ancient, over thousands of years Martial Arts Teachings, including Tae Kwon Do, Aikido and Hapkido, Kudo, Gumdo, and Judo. We educate and create future leaders with a well-rounded awareness and knowledge of societal challenges, guiding them to distinguish their Way.
 Master S.H. Yu Martial Arts believes that the purpose of the Martial Arts Dojhang is to promote discipline and the Way which includes the Way of Life, Justice, Peace, and Prosperity. The Martial Arts Dojhang is NOT a playground nor is it a birthday party venue, it is a sacred space for human development and fostering a disciplined purpose, good health, strength, and community through practice.
 We believe the Martial Artist is “The One who follows and creates the Way using his/her own mind, body, and spirit.” Our teachers and instructors have a mission: they are responsible to develop each student’s highest quality of life . Our purpose is to “help the student discover the foundation of their life and build their interior kingdom.” This is true Martial Art.

 ## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) ### What martial arts styles do you teach?
 We offer a comprehensive curriculum led by Sr. GrandMaster Yu, including Tae Kwon Do, Hapkido, Aikido, Judo, and Kumdo (the art of the sword). Our 12-week foundational program is designed to introduce students to these diverse disciplines in a structured way.

 ### Do I need prior experience to join?
 Not at all! We welcome students of all levels, from absolute beginners to advanced practitioners. Our Children Level 1 and Adult/Teen classes are specifically designed to meet you where you are, focusing on the “Way” of discipline and personal growth regardless of your starting point.

 ### What is the best age for a child to start martial arts?
 We find that children as young as elementary age benefit immensely from the structure of the Dojhang. Our Children’s programs focus on building self-confidence, coordination, and respect, helping them create a strong “interior kingdom” during their developmental years.

 ### Can I observe a class before signing up?
 Yes! We encourage interested students and parents to contact us to schedule an on-site visit or a free class observation. Seeing the discipline and harmony of our Dojhang firsthand is the best way to understand our unique approach to martial arts.

 ### How often should I or my child attend classes?
 While progress depends on the individual, most students see consistent growth by training 2 to 4 times per week. You can view our current class schedule to find times that fit your family’s routine.
