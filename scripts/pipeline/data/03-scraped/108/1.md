 What is it
 ## What Is Kids BJJ? Kids Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu at Bittencourt BJJ is a structured martial arts program for children ages 5 and up. Classes focus on real self-defense, physical coordination, and the discipline that comes from consistent training.
 BJJ teaches children how to handle and resolve physical confrontations without relying on striking. It is one of the safest and most effective martial arts for kids, and the lessons learned on the mat carry directly into school, sports, and everyday life. Learn more at the International Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu Federation (IBJJF) .

 Common questions
 ## Kids BJJ FAQ What age can my child start? We accept children from age 5. If your child is younger and you are curious about starting early, feel free to contact us and we can talk through the options.

 Is BJJ safe for kids? Yes. Kids BJJ is one of the safest martial arts for children. There is no striking involved. Classes emphasize controlled technique, body awareness, and respect between training partners. Instructors maintain a close eye on every student at all times.

 Does my child need any experience? No experience is needed at all. The first class is free and your child will be placed in the right group from day one. Beginners are welcomed and guided from the very start.

 What should my child wear to the first class? Athletic shorts and a t-shirt are fine for the free trial. Once your child joins, they will need a Gi (kimono). We can point you in the right direction for that after the first class.

 Can parents watch the classes? Yes, parents are welcome to watch. Many families find it motivating for their kids to have someone cheering them on, especially in the early weeks of training.
