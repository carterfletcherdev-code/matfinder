 ## Frequently Asked Questions
 See some of our most common questions and answers below, or call us at 469-261-5351

 - What age groups do you offer classes for? Sloth Academy offers classes for children ages 5–12 in the Little Sloths BJJ program, as well as adult classes for individuals of all experience levels. - Is Brazilian Jiu Jitsu safe for kids? Yes, Brazilian Jiu Jitsu is a safe and controlled martial art that focuses on technique rather than striking. Our instructors are experienced in teaching kids and prioritize safety and proper training methods. - How do I know if my child will enjoy martial arts? We recommend scheduling a free trial class so your child can experience the fun and engaging environment firsthand. Most kids love the challenge and camaraderie of training with peers. - Do you offer self-defense training? Yes, all of our programs include self-defense techniques that are practical and easy to learn. Whether you're a child or an adult, you'll develop skills to protect yourself in real-world scenarios. - What should my child wear to their first class? Kids can wear comfortable, athletic clothing for their first class. Once they enroll, they’ll need a properly fitted gi (uniform), which we can help you acquire. - How often should my child attend classes? We recommend children attend classes 2–3 times per week to ensure consistent learning and progress. However, the schedule is flexible to accommodate your family’s needs.
