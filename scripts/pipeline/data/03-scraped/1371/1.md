## Kids

 Are mixed martial arts suitable for children?
 At Arena Combat Sports in Little Havana, we encourage a positive & safe atmosphere. We instill attributes such as confidence, self esteem, hard work, leadership, and physical conditioning. All are vital qualities that will carry over into life's daily challenges. Mixed martial arts for children provides an outlet for excess energy. Martial arts for children teaches them how to avoid a potentially dangerous situation without having to resort to violence (through body language, confidence, and communication). In a worst case scenario, your child will have the tools to keep them safer from physical harm.
