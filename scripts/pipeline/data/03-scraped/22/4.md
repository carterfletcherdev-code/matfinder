 FAQS

 #### Do I need to be in shape to start Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu? ####

 No. Most beginners start Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu with little or no fitness background. Our coaches help you build strength, endurance, and mobility step by step. If you’re looking for a martial arts school in Kingston where beginners feel comfortable learning at their own pace, our classes are designed for exactly that.

 #### At what age can kids start martial arts classes?

 Kids can usually begin martial arts around age 4. Our Juniors program focuses on coordination, listening skills, and confidence while introducing basic jiu-jitsu martial arts in Kingston in a safe and structured way.

 #### Is Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu safe for beginners and children? ####

 Yes. Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu focuses on control, leverage, and technique rather than striking. Classes are supervised by experienced instructors who emphasize safety, proper training habits, and respect for training partners. Our goal is to provide a safe environment for anyone training at our Premier Martial Arts Facility in Kingston.

 #### What should I wear to my first BJJ or yoga class? #### ####

 Wear comfortable athletic clothing that you can move in easily. For BJJ, many beginners start with a t-shirt and workout shorts. For yoga, typical athletic wear works well. If you’re visiting our Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu academy near Saugerties, just bring water and arrive ready to learn.

 #### Can beginners start Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu with no experience?

 Yes. Most students start with zero martial arts experience. Our beginner-friendly classes teach the fundamentals step by step, making it easy to start learning jiu-jitsu martial arts in Kingston without feeling overwhelmed.

 #### Is Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu good for self-defense?

 Yes. Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu teaches you how to control distance, escape dangerous situations, and defend yourself using technique rather than strength. Many people train BJJ because it is one of the most practical martial arts for real-world self-defense.
