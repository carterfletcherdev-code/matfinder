 ###### Beginner Jiu-jitsu
 Essence is where your Jiu-Jitsu journey begins.
 This foundational program introduces the core principles of Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu in a clear, structured, and empowering environment. Designed for those new to the art or returning after time away, Essence builds confidence, movement awareness, and technical fluency through essential techniques and positional understanding.
 Beyond the mat, students often discover increased focus, discipline, and overall well-being. At Trein, Jiu-Jitsu becomes more than self-defense, it’s a path to personal transformation.

 ###### Intermediate Jiu-jitsu
 Evolution is where your game starts to take shape.
 Tailored for blue belts and beyond, this program bridges the gap between fundamental and advanced Jiu-Jitsu. Evolution refines your technique while expanding your tactical awareness, helping you build strong habits, chain attacks, and develop fluid movement.
 Every session deepens your understanding of the art, enhancing timing, precision, and flow so you can move with confidence and purpose on the mat.

 ###### Advanced Jiu-jitsu
 Elevate is where skill becomes mastery.
 Crafted for experienced practitioners and competitors, Elevate challenges you with high-level technique, strategy, and situational drilling. This class integrates wrestling, judo, sambo, and advanced Jiu-Jitsu systems in a curriculum built to sharpen your instincts and expand your game.
 Train alongside elite partners and world-class instructors as you refine speed, reaction, and seamless transitions, unlocking the next level of your performance.

 ###### Beginner No Gi
 A powerful start, without the gi.
 Beginner No-Gi lays the foundation for grappling in a faster, grip-free environment. This program teaches core principles such as posture, positional control, escapes, and fundamental submissions, all adapted for No-Gi movement and intensity.
 Whether you're brand new to Jiu-Jitsu or transitioning from gi-based training, this class builds functional skill and body awareness in a supportive, high-energy setting. It’s the ideal starting point for exploring the art through a fresh lens.

 ###### Advanced No Gi
 Where intensity meets innovation.
 Built for advanced practitioners, this program explores the cutting edge of modern grappling. Advanced No-Gi blends wrestling-based entries, positional strategy, and specialized submission systems (including leg locks and transition-heavy flows) into a high-paced, competition-calibrated environment.
 Train with intent, elevate your precision, and develop the tactical mindset required for elite-level No-Gi performance.

 ###### Private Trainning: Learn Faster
 Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, like any sport or skill, rewards those who work hard and train smart. Group classes offer the best environment to learn, as training with a variety of partners challenges you in different ways, accelerating your progress. However, private lessons are an essential complement for any serious practitioner.
 A focused 30- or 60-minute session with one of our instructors provides personalized guidance that can significantly improve your technique and game flow.
 We highly recommend that every student take at least one private lesson per month to review techniques and address specific challenges in their Jiu-Jitsu journey.
 Benefits of Joining Our Private Training Program:
 - Gain a deeper understanding of targeted areas in your BJJ game - Set clear, achievable goals and work consistently toward them - Receive individual attention tailored to your needs - Access a customized curriculum designed just for you - Enjoy focused sparring sessions with your instructor - Break through training plateaus - Accelerate your learning curve - Catch up on techniques or concepts from missed classes
