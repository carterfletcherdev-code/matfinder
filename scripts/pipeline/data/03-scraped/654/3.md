 ## Frequently Asked Questions

 Most frequent questions and answers about our martial arts programs

 Will My Child Become A Bully?
 We find that our students become more confident to stand up to bullies and have more humility and kindness to others through their martial arts lessons.

 Are Parents Allowed To Watch?
 We encourage parents to watch their children in their kids martial arts lessons. We love parents getting involved! And so do their kids. Even better, join into some of our martial arts classes for adults. Your kids will love that you have something in common. It will build a strong family bond!
 Additionally, if you wanted some ‘me-time’? Simply drop them off, and go grab a coffee. They will greet you with a huge smile when you return, because they had SO much fun!

 Do I Need To Be Fit For This?
 No, not at all! We find that people start at varying fitness levels. But, you will find that you do get fit by doing our Austin, Cedar Park & Baton Rouge martial arts classes. Isn’t that the point though? A fun workout that gets results!

 How long is the trial class?
 Our trial classes typically last between 20 minutes for the 3 to 5 year olds and 50 minutes for all other classes.

 How Long Until I Get My Black Belt?
 Truthfully, the journey to black belt is an individual one. Everyone can progress at a different pace. There is no judgement on students for how long it does take. What we can tell you, is that it is SO WORTHWHILE!
 The self-defense skills you will master can truly be life-saving!
 However, the other benefits of striving for black belt, like perseverance, never giving up, commitment and self-discipline lead to life-long success.
 You will find that your instructors at AMP BJJ Austin, Cedar Park & Baton Rouge are dedicated to helping you on your journey to black belt or whatever your personal goal may be.

 Is Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu safe?
 At AMP BJJ, safety is our top priority. BJJ is generally safe when practiced with proper supervision and respect for training partners. Our instructors emphasize safety and will guide you on how to train safely.

 What if I have a pre-existing injury or health condition?
 Please inform our instructor about any injuries or health conditions before the class. We can modify techniques or provide alternative exercises to accommodate your needs.

 How do I protect myself from injuries?
 Follow our instructor’s guidance, tap out early and often, and communicate with your training partners. Use proper protective gear if needed.

 Can I bring a friend to the trial class?
 Absolutely! We encourage you to bring a friend to try the class together.

 What age can children start training BJJ?
 We have classes for children starting as young as 3 or 4 years old, depending on the child’s readiness and our program policies.

 How Do I Claim Your Limited Time Offer?
 We do only have a certain amount of space in our martial arts training area. Take advantage of our limited time offer…The fastest way is to fill in the form.
 Additionally, you can always call us, we would love to chat with you about what we do, and if our Austin, Cedar Park & Baton Rouge martial arts classes would be a great fit for you and your family!

 What should I wear to the trial class?
 At AMP BJJ , we recommend wearing comfortable athletic clothing for your trial class. We will provide a loaner gi for the class.
 Be sure to bring a water bottle to your Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu classes. You will find AMP BJJ in Austin, Cedar Park & Baton Rouge has martial arts classes for kids and adults that are a great workout and lots of fun! Most students are grinning from ear to ear by the end of the class. We are sure you will be too!

 I'm Not Very Sporty or Coordinated, Will That Be A Problem?
 Not at all. In fact, many students starting in our adult martial arts classes would describe themselves like that before they started. So you will fit right in!
 Give yourself some time, and you will be astounded at how much you improve! You’ll find that our very experienced martial arts instructors are trained to keep their teaching simple. So even the most uncoordinated person will develop ‘ninja-like’ skills! Come see for yourself!

 What should I expect during the trial class?
 At AMP BJJ, we utilize the Ecological Dynamics approach, which focuses on realistic scenarios and decision-making skills rather than repetitive drilling. This means you’ll learn through engaging, game-like activities that simulate real-life situations, helping you develop practical and adaptable skills. Our instructors will guide you through each step, ensuring you understand the techniques and feel comfortable.

 Will there be someone to guide me through the class?
 Yes, our instructors and more experienced students will be there to help you throughout the class, ensuring you understand the techniques and feel comfortable.

 How much does membership cost after the trial class?
 Membership costs can vary. After your trial class, we’ll provide you with details on pricing, membership plans, and any promotions available.

 Are there any hidden fees?
 We believe in transparency. We’ll inform you about any additional costs, such as gi purchases, belt testing fees, or registration fees, so you understand the full cost of membership.

 Can families train together?
 Yes, we offer family classes and discounts for multiple family members training together. Ask us about our family membership options.
