 # FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

 #### What age groups can join your martial arts classes?

 We offer classes for kids, teens, and adults of all skill levels. Our programs are designed to suit beginners as well as experienced practitioners.

 #### Do I need prior experience to start training?

 No, beginners are welcome! Our instructors will guide you step by step, ensuring you learn the basics before advancing to higher levels.

 #### What should I wear for my first class?

 Comfortable workout clothes are fine for beginners. Once you enroll, we’ll guide you on the proper uniform and gear needed for training.

 #### Is martial arts training safe?

 Yes! Safety is our priority. Our instructors emphasize proper technique and controlled practice to minimize the risk of injuries.

 #### How often should I train to see progress?

 Training at least 2-3 times per week is recommended for steady improvement. However, progress depends on consistency, effort, and personal goals.

 #### Was Professor Jesse in the Marine Corps?

 He is a 16-year United States Marine Corps veteran with four combat deployments (OIF I, II, III, and OEF), and he brings that same leadership, structure, and intensity into everything he teaches.
