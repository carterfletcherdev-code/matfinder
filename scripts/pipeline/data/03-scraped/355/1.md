 #### Welcome TRUJITSU Unified is a union of Academies that share the same values. We believe in Community, Camaraderie, and making Jiu Jitsu available to everybody. We are everything from professional athletes, to parents, to career professionals, to college students. But what we all have in common is an interest in making the most out of Jiu Jitsu for everyone, and helping each other with whatever their goals may be.
 Some academies focus on competition, and others focus on having fun. TRUJITSU Unified academies focus on accomplishing both.
