# Let’s get social

 ## Unum kids
 - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -

 Our goal is to help children on and off the mats-Focus & Self-regulate. In a regular kids class, children learn takedowns, falling control, passing and defending their opponent and at an older age, submissions. Our classes mainly focus on grappling and takes punching and kicking out. During class, children will learn a variety of Jiu jitsu techniques. When they are comfortable, we encourage them to implement the techniques in a controlled sparring session against an opponent. Our goal is to instill control, confidence, strength, passion and respect. Not bullies. Whether the goal is for recreation, competition, or just self-improvement, we look forward to helping.
 Kids class is open to 4-16yrs.
