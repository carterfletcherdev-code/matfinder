 ### How old do you have to be to start training?
 All our classes are for adults 18 and over. We do not offer any youth classes but there are great ones in the area.

 ### Is it really for all the classes?
 Yes, they really are a month for unlimited training. We believer there’s already enough to worry about , training shouldn’t be one of them. Martial arts should make your life better, not harder. This is our way of giving back to the community and keeping the doors open for anyone who wants to train.

 ### I am a beginner, can I still train?
 Yes, we welcome beginners. We highly encourage taking either intro to Muay Thai or intro to Jiu Jitsu (No Gi) first.

 ### Do I need any gear to get started?
 We have gear you can use while you try the trial class or you can purchase the gear from us. Below is what you will need for each class:
 - No gi Jiu Jitsu- Basic gym attire, or rash guard. - MMA- Basic gym attire, or rash guard. 8 oz gloves, leather shinguards. Optional items mouth guard, hand wraps, and cup. - Muay Thai- Basic gym attire, leather shinguards, 16oz boxing gloves. Optional items mouth guard, hand wraps, and cup.
