 # Downriver's #1 Award Winning JIU-JITSU ACADEMY!

 Metro Jiu-Jitsu is a university endorsed martial arts school located in Southgarte, Michigan that offers classes for students of all ages and levels. With over 30 years of experience and a team of skilled instructors, you can learn a new discipline while improving fitness, learning self-defense, and other important traits. Our 7,000 square foot facility is even equipped with a snack bar, enhancing our students' overall training experience and welcoming culture.

 # Meet the Master Instructors Behind Metro Jiu-Jitsu

 Obtaining a high rank in Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu does not automatically make you a good coach. This is why all of our coaching staff go through extensive training on the fundamentals of teaching.
