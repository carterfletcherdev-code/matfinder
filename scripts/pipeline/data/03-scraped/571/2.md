Skip to content ## OUR PROGRAMS

 ### Adult Jiu-Jitsu ALL LEVELS – Our adult classes offer high-level instruction, a supportive team environment, and training that challenges every grappler to grow and improve at every level.

 ### Advanced Jiu-Jitsu Our Advanced Jiu-Jitsu class is designed for experienced practitioners ready to take their technique, strategy, and timing to the next level. This class focuses on refining fundamentals, exploring advanced positions, and developing competition-level transitions and submissions. Students can expect a challenging pace and detailed instruction to sharpen their game and push their limits.

 ### Women's Only Gi Train in a supportive, women-led environment that emphasizes empowerment, technique, and community. Perfect for beginners and experienced women looking to sharpen their gi jiu-jitsu.

 ### Open Mat Open Mat is your time to roll, drill, and explore techniques at your own pace. Sharpen your skills or just connect with teammates.. everyone is welcome. No formal instruction, just good training.

 ### No Gi Jiu-Jitsu Adults No-gi jiu-jitsu offers a faster-paced, grip-free grappling experience focused on control, pressure, and transitions. Great for cross-training or adding variety to your ground game.

 ### ADULT BEGINNER JIU-JITSU Our Adult Beginner Jiu-Jitsu class is the perfect starting point for anyone looking to learn the fundamentals of Jiu-Jitsu in a supportive, no-pressure environment. You’ll build confidence, improve fitness, and develop practical self-defense skills while learning basic techniques, positions, and movements at a pace that’s right for you. Classes focus on technique, safety, and fun, making it easy for adults of all ages and fitness levels to jump in and start their Jiu-Jitsu journey.

 ### Little Ninjas Ages 3-5 Our Little Ninjas program introduces kids to martial arts through fun, movement-based learning. We focus on coordination, listening, and confidence. All in a positive and playful environment.

 ### Youth Jiu-Jitsu Ages 6-14 Kids gain more than just self-defense here. Our youth classes build discipline, respect, focus, and resilience – while keeping training fun, challenging, and engaging for every skill level.

 ### Striking/MMA Our Striking/MMA class combines the best techniques from striking and grappling arts to create a well-rounded skillset for both competition and self-defense. We focus on building strength, improving conditioning, and developing real-world skills in a safe, supportive environment.
