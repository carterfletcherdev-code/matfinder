 ## Professor Kaliffa Oliveira
 Born in Brazil and currently the head coach of Gracie Barra Greystone, Professor Kaliffa has held a judo black belt since August 2001 and a Brazilian jiu-jitsu black belt under Carlos Gracie Jr. since May 2013. Throughout his martial arts career, he has earned numerous national titles both in Brazil and the United States, and he is a two-time world judo champion

 ## Head Instructor José Leonardo Ferreira Júnior
 A black belt since November 2020 under Professor Kaliffa Oliveira, he is a decorated Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu competitor with titles that include NoGi World Champion, Panamerican Champion, American National Champion, Brazilian National Champion, and Abu Dhabi World Pro Champion. He is also a multiple-time Open Class champion at IBJJF events.

 ## Assistant Coach Brendan Chisolm
 Brendan Chisolm is a Jiu-Jitsu Purple Belt at Gracie Barra Greystone in Birmingham, AL. Known for his technical knowledge and patient teaching style, Brendan leads both Gi and No-Gi classes for students of all ages and experience levels.
