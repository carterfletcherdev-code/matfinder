 ## West Bank Social Club Welcome to the Westbank Social Club, or as we like to say the WBSC.

 We are the home of a random assortment of people from all walks of life. It would appear from the outside that we couldn't possibly have anything in common, but once you walk through the door you know instantly we are FAMILY.

 We are the home of NolaBJJ WB, and offer Jiu-Jitsu, Krav Maga, boxing, kickboxing and many more personalized fitness programs.

 Our goal is to bring fun and excitement to people's lives.
