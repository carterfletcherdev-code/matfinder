 Meet Our Awesome Team

 ## Sandro Silva - OwnerProfessor Sandro Silva is a fourth-degree black belt from Brazil with over 33 years of martial arts experience. Trained under Professor Iliarde Santos in the lineage of Ricardo de la Riva and Carlson Gracie, he has competed in more than 150 fights across Boxing, Capoeira, Muay Thai, Judo, BJJ, MMA, and Vale Tudo. ## With 18 years of teaching experience in several countries, Sandro brings a unique and engaging teaching style shaped by his background as an athlete, instructor, father, and war veteran. He is dedicated to helping students build confidence, discipline, and resilience through the power of martial arts.

 ## Suzanne Brito - Owner ## Suzanne is a psychologist with 10 years of experience and a dedicated Jiu-Jitsu practitioner of over six years. She supports our team with effective teaching strategies focused on each student’s individual needs—building confidence, discipline, and resilience. Active in our Kids Program, she helps create a safe and positive environment where children can learn and grow. With her background in psychology, she promotes emotional well-being and believes that learning and growth never stop.

 ## Professor Douglas Souza # Douglas Souza is a Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu black belt (1st degree) and Judo black belt from Brazil, with a strong background in competition and leadership. An active competitor with a long and successful history across multiple tournaments, he brings real, high-level experience to his teaching. # A former Brazilian Army officer, Douglas combines discipline, structure, and technical precision to create an effective and motivating training environment. He has extensive experience coaching both adults and children, helping students build confidence, skill, and resilience through Jiu-Jitsu.

 ## Michael "Bam" Bambarger - Jiu Jitsu Coach ## He is a career military officer and lifelong student of discipline, leadership, and service. With over 12 years of training in Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, he brings a fundamentals-first approach rooted in consistency, humility, and continuous improvement. ## Holding a Master’s degree in Education, Bam combines technical knowledge with a structured and thoughtful teaching style. His background in military leadership and education shapes his approach—building strong foundations, developing problem-solving under pressure, and helping students grow with confidence.

 ## Edward Echohawk- Jiu Jitsu Coach ## A career Army Oﬃcer, Edward has served all across Europe and the Middle East with two tours in Iraq. He found Brazilian Jiu Jitsu while training with Army Special Operations. He has trained soldiers to defend themselves in armed and unarmed combat for nearly two decades. He continues training both BJJ and Muay Thai. He began training with Professor Sandro in 2023 and received his purple belt in 2024. A life long learner, Edward sees every day on the mat as an opportunity to learn, teach, and improve.

 ## Joshua Roginsky - MuayThai Coach ## With over 8 years of Muay Thai and striking experience, teaching across all age levels, Josh brings both technical precision and a deep passion for the art to every class.Josh views Muay Thai as a beautiful, strategic art form and a fantastic basis for striking in MMA. His goal is to help students not only execute techniques, but truly understand their purpose and strategy so they can adapt, remember, and make them their own.

 Frequently Asked Question

 #### What age groups can join your martial arts classes?

 We offer classes for kids from 5y and up, and adults of all skill levels. Our programs are designed to suit beginners as well as experienced practitioners.

 #### Do I need prior experience to start training?

 No, beginners are welcome! Our instructors will guide you step by step, ensuring you learn the basics before advancing to higher levels.

 #### What should I wear for my first class?

 Just come in comfortable workout clothes — we’ll provide a clean loaner gi for your first lesson. Can’t wait to have you on the mats! 👊😊

 #### Is martial arts training safe?

 Absolutely — your safety is our top priority. We always have experienced black belts on the mats to guide the training, ensure proper technique, and keep every class controlled and safe.
