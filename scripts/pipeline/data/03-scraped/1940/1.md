 # Welcome

Classes are led by head coach John Goforth , with coaches Mike Smith , James Bowers , Jake Stepisnik , Daniel Krug , Ali Hosseinian , Eric Eide , and our Youth Collective .
 New students are always welcome, feel free to drop in during scheduled classes , or contact us with any questions.
