 # What We're About We're a Jiu-Jitsu club focused on mental health, fitness, self-defence and competition.
 ## # # Who Can Get Involved? W e cater to all ages and skill levels, led by Black Belt Trevor Sweeney. At SBJJ our mission is to provide you or your child with high-level jiu jitsu instruction. We want to do so in an environment that’s welcoming for everyone. Whether you just want to get in shape, want to learn self-defence, or want to compete, you have a place. Our members come from all walks of life, from ages of 4 to 50. There's no prior experience or fitness level required to start!
 ## # How Do I Start? You can message us under the 'Contact' page above, on Facebook or Instagram! We'll let you know about any openings and give you more information about how you can start!
 ## # Our Kids' Program Our Kids Program caters to kids from 4 to 13. Led by our instructor Brendan Cloney, our kids program will develop numerous qualities in your child: Their fitness, ability to defend themselves, their confidence, balance and coordination and much more. Jiu Jitsu is a fantastic alternative to the standard team based sports offered in schools. Your child will learn in a fun, safe and professional environment.
 # Our Teens' Program Our Teens program caters from 12 - 17 and is led by coaches James Walsh and John Keeling. Our Teens class acts as a feeder program for our adult classes and are run more similarly to that. We focus on integration and instilling confidence in these teens as they make the transition into young adulthood. Here they learn to be highly capable grapplers, and more importantly well-rounded, confident young people. This program covers both gi and no-gi grappling.
 All of our instructors are Garda vetted and undertake Tusla training on child-safeguarding and welfare.
