We are delighted to add a new class to the the timetable . Every Wednesday, from 7.30-9.00pm you can benefit from a free open mat. Work on your game, drill, spar or improve your conditioning with your team mates.
 The class is free for members, and £5 for non-members. All welcome.
 Note: please queue outside the Loft and allow the kids, teens and parents to leave the room.
