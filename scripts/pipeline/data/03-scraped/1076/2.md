 ## The Gracie Jiu-Jitsu Lineage Behind Megaton Gallup Jiu-Jitsu

 Royler Gracie is a legendary figure in Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, celebrated worldwide for his competitive achievements and for shaping the techniques and principles that define the art today. It was through his influence that Megaton Dias brought Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu to the United States , introducing the practice and philosophy that became the foundation of Megaton Gallup Jiu-Jitsu.
