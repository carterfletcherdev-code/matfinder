# Private BJJ InstructionSan Francisco In addition to our regularly scheduled kids and adult classes, Romulo Melo BJJ also offers private and semi-private instruction to meet your scheduling needs.

 Private classes are perfect for those with hectic schedules who still want to keep up with their training. There are many other reasons to choose private instruction, too. Perhaps you would just like to train as a family or would like some extra self-defense tips. Or maybe you want to brush up on your techniques if you are planning a trip abroad or desire to progress faster. Private instruction makes a great gift for anyone you know, whether they are already enrolled in our academy or they have always wanted to try out martial arts.

 Private classes are also an excellent move for those planning to compete, whether it is strictly in Jiu-Jitsu or professional MMA fights. You will be provided with training tailored specifically to your needs, goals, and abilities in order to give you the edge using advanced techniques.
