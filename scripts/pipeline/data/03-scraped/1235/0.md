## "The more you learn, the less you use strength, the more you use technique."– Rickson Gracie (Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu Master)

 ### Jiu-Jitsu Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu (BJJ) is a martial art and combat sport that focuses on ground fighting and grappling techniques. It emphasizes the use of leverage, technique, and body positioning to control an opponent and defend against attacks. BJJ is known for its effectiveness in self-defense, allowing a smaller person to defend themselves against a larger, stronger opponent by using submission holds such as joint locks and chokes. It’s a practical art often referred to as "the gentle art," since it focuses more on technique and strategy rather than brute force.

This class introduces students to more advanced techniques and concepts while encouraging discipline, focus, and respect. Students will continue to develop their physical and mental strength, learning how to apply techniques in a controlled and safe manner. Kids Jiu-Jitsu is designed to prepare students for the transition to our Teens BJJ program, where they will further refine their skills and increase their understanding of Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu.

 ## TEENS JIU-JITSU Our teen Jiu-Jitsu class focuses on teaching essential grappling techniques and submissions in a supportive, challenging environment. Teens will work on building strength, flexibility, and problem-solving skills while learning effective self-defense. This class also emphasizes respect, discipline, and teamwork, helping teens develop both physically and mentally.

Whether you’re a beginner or have experience, this class offers a great balance of fitness, skill development, and mental challenge. It’s ideal for those seeking both self-defense skills and a full-body workout, all in a supportive environment.
