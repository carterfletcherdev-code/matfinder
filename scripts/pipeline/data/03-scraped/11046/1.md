## Be a Karate Kid!6 - 10 year olds!

 Master Kil's martial arts classes for kids provide benefits immediately, with results that last a lifetime.
 At Master Kil's Taekwondo, your child will learn:
 - Dicipline. - Build up self confidence. - Better focus and concentration skills. - The importance of courtesy and respect. - Self-control and cooperation. - Self -defense while building strong flexible bodies. - How to set and achieve goals. - Assertiveness not aggressiveness.

 Your child will love the excitement of learning new Tae Kwon Do kicks, forms, and self-defense techniques and practicing with their new friends. Parents tell us that increased self-confidence and improved coordination are just two of the primary benefits they’ve seen as a result of participating in Master Kils program.

 Tae Kwon Do also helps teach your child valuable character traits such as courtesy, respect, cooperation, commitment, and perseverance—characteristics that will also help your child excel at school, team sports, and goal setting.
