 ## Welcome to the Oregon Jiu Jitsu Lab, where we don't just teach jiu-jitsu – we transform novices into competent grapplers, regardless of age or prior experience.

 At Oregon Jiu Jitsu Lab, we're passionate about Brazilian jiu-jitsu - a martial art focused on ground fighting and submissions. We believe in learning, having fun, and improving daily. With classes 7 days a week, both kids and adults can find the perfect time. If you or your child aim to excel in jiu-jitsu, you're in the right place!

 # Why Train With Us? ### Proven Excellence Our program takes you step-by-step, from a beginner to a skilled jiu jitsu practitioner. We're set up to make sure you progress smoothly and confidently.

 ### Effective Techniques We focus on practical training. Every move you learn is tested and proven. Our emphasis on ecological teaching ensures you're always learning effectively.

 ### Beyond Just Training We mix sports psychology with hands-on training. This combo boosts both your mind and skills, helping you learn faster and better.

 ### Welcoming Community Join a place where everyone is valued and every achievement celebrated. We're more than a gym; we're a community supporting each other.
