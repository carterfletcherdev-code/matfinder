# Austin Judo Club ## Judo the way it was meant to be taught: Safe Simple Effective.
 ## Visit Our Class Try a class for a small $15 mat fee. Contact Us for more information.

 ## About Austin Judo Club Our group is taught by Rick Cockerham and Henry Bruns. Experienced teachers who bring decades of expertise to our training program. Additionally, Zdenek Matl, Sensei 9th Dan in Judo and 9th Dan in Ju-Jitsu, serves as an advisor and occasional teacher. With a lifetime of experience in classical Judo, Mr. Matl offers insights and techniques focused on safety, simplicity, low power, and effectiveness—true to the spirit of Judo.
 Why Train with Us?
 - Expert Coaching: Rick Cockerham and Henry Bruns bring decades of combined experience, offering personalized guidance for every student. - Competition Ready: For those aiming to compete, we provide advanced training to prepare you for tournaments at any level. - Mastery Focus: Our goal is long-term physical excellence—develop skills that stay with you for life. - Detailed Instruction: Every change of balance and movement is broken down so you understand why it works, giving you a deep understanding.
 Classes & Schedule

 We do not offer children's classes. Adults only.

 - Monday & Wednesday 6:30 PM – 7:30 PM: Teaching class for all levels (Open to drop-ins). This class focuses on slow, precise execution of techniques, ensuring that every movement is performed correctly and effectively. - Monday & Wednesday 7:30 PM – 8:30 PM: Practice & Cardio. In this session, we play dynamic, engaging games designed to teach specific aspects of the fight while improving physical mastery. Each game is structured to challenge your coordination, balance, and reaction time. It's a high-energy cardio workout that reinforces what you learned in the first hour—turning skill-building into a fun and competitive experience.
