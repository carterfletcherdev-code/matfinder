## Membership Information
 We invite those interested in our programs to enjoy a complimentary week of training to experience what we offer. Membership sign-ups are handled at the front desk of Gym Love. Our membership rates are as follows:
 - Adult Membership: $120 per month - Youth Membership (Ages 9-14): $120 per month - Children's Membership (Ages 5-8): $85 per month - Family Membership: Households with three or more members enrolled will receive a rate of $85 per person.
 For children under 5 years old, enrollment is considered on a case-by-case basis. Please contact us with any questions or for further information.

 Why We Don’t Have a Required Uniform Policy at South Tahoe Jiu Jitsu

 At South Tahoe Jiu Jitsu, we’re committed to making Jiu Jitsu accessible, affordable, and welcoming to everyone. We believe that requiring students to buy specific gear on top of paying a monthly membership fee places an unnecessary financial burden on them.
 Jiu Jitsu training apparel can be costly, and requiring additional purchases just to train is a business practice we question. Here, you’re free to wear what fits your budget, including any gear you already own—regardless of school patches or branding. We value individuality and self-expression, and we want each student to feel comfortable being themselves on the mats.
 We’re all about growth and learning together, without the pressure of extra costs. Come as you are, and let’s focus on what really matters: training hard, supporting each other, and improving together.
