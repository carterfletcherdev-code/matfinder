 FAQ

 What is Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu?
 Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu is a grappling-based martial art. In the U.S, it’s well known for improving physical fitness, increasing self-confidence, and teaching practical self-defense skills.

 Is Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu suitable for beginners?
 Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu is beginner-friendly and appropriate for people of all ages and fitness levels.

 Can Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu help with self-defense?
 Definitely. Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu focuses on effective self-defense techniques that work for people of different sizes and strength levels.

 What can I expect in my first Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu class?
 Your first class will typically include a warm-up, followed by instruction on fundamental movements, techniques, and positions.

 Does Jiu-Jitsu help with weight loss?
 Yes, it is a high-energy, full-body workout that burns a lot of calories while helping you get in shape and stay active.
