 ## About Us Washington Han Mu Do opened its doors in 2010 with a vision of bringing this comprehensive martial arts system to the Maple Valley area. Guided through a series of intentionally designed kicks, strikes, sweeps, throws, and other self-defense focused skills; Washington HMD strives to insight awareness, balance, persistence, and respect among all of its students. We run classes for children and adults of all ages and skill levels. Whether you have never heard of Han Mu Do, or have been practicing it for years, you are welcomed and encouraged to join our family! The curriculum is designed in such a way that each person can progress at their own pace. Life is not a competition, and neither is the practice of Han Mu Do.

 Han Mu Do was developed in 1989 by Dr. He-Young Kimm. Combining over 40 years of personal knowledge and experience with other forms of Korean martial arts, Dr. Kimm sought to integrate the different techniques and philosophies he had spent his life studying. He worked to create a single martial arts system that focused on achieving a holistic balance in life, as well as within ourselves. It is a series of movements aimed at targeting physical strength, flexibility, respect, confidence, resilience, and situational awareness. Built around both empty hand and weapons techniques, students learn to harness their innate power. They discover that focusing this power on accomplishing goals, continued growth, and life-long learning, is a powerful skill that will allow them to craft a life of their choosing.

 #### Our Philosophy We believe that a consistent practice of the techniques and philosophies set forth by the World Han Mu Do Association, leads to a lifelong commitment toward personal growth and discovery. We work to create an environment of support that motivates all students to maintain high personal standards through strengthened persistence and resilience. Standing on a foundation of physical fitness and skilled self defense, students are empowered to have full control over self (mind, body, & spirit), enabling them to live a happy, fulfilled life. We place a strong emphasis on discipline and respect for kids, stress relief and fitness for adults, and a fun place for the entire family to train and grow together.

 #### Modern Studio Right in the heart of Maple Valley, we are a well equipped studio with all of the materials and state-of-the-art equipment required for the study of Han Mu Do. With a varied group of instructors, our group training approach, and a commitment to always improving our practice and business, Washington HMD provides a place filled with passion, purpose, and a genuine sense of community.

 ## Founder of Han Mu Do #### Dr. He-Young Kimm He-Young Kimm was born in Su Won County near Seoul, Korea in 1940 to a family who traditionally emphasized the pen and the sword. Beginning the practice of martial arts at the age of 7, Dr. Kimm studied a long list of different styles and techniques. (Yudo, Taekwondo, Tang Soo Do, Kong Soo Do, Hapkido, Won Kwang Wha, Kim Jung Soo, and Kuk Sool to name a few.)

 Honoring his upbringing, Dr. Kimm received a Bachelors of Science in Marine Science, a Masters and PhD. in History, and a teaching certificate in Hapkido. He went on to publish several books on martial arts and philosophy, and in 1987 introduced Han Mu Do. Originally titled "Yuj Kwon Sul" and "Han Mu Yuh Kwon Sul", Han Mu Do (meaning "way of the Korean martial arts") is a structured accumulation of over 40 years of knowledge and experience.

 Dr. Kimm holds a 9th dan in Hapkido, and advanced ranks in several of the other disciplines that he has studied over the years. He is the President of Dae Han Kido Hwe Operations in America; Director of the International Commerce Committee; and President & Founder of the World Han Mu Do Association.
