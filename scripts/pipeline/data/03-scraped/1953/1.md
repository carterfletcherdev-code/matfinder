# Adult Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu Classes in Manhattan Unity Jiu Jitsu offers you top-level instruction and training. We teach techniques that have been proven to work, passing on what we’ve learned as well as our own style that we’ve developed over many years of training.
 Our free Introductory Class ensures that everyone enters a regular class comfortably and with a basic understanding of Jiu Jitsu. The class consists of one 45-minute lesson required for anyone new to Jiu Jitsu.
 Our Women’s Program is integrative, meaning we have taken measures to ensure that gender isn’t an issue for getting the best training possible. We want women in all our classes, and in any given class you will have the option of training with another female, should you want to.
