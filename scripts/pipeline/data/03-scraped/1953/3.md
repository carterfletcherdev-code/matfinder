Founded in 2015, we’ve grown from a small Midtown East space into one of the most prestigious BJJ academies in the world — producing dozens of World Champions at every belt level, including multiple Black Belt World Champions.
 We stay true to what got us here: hard work, good people, and a fun family environment where everyone feels welcome — from complete beginners to elite competitors.
 Train with us seven days a week. We offer gi and no-gi BJJ, wrestling, white belt fundamentals, pro competition training, self-defense, and private coaching — all at our West 14th Street location.
