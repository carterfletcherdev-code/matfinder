 # Brazilian Jiu Jitsu at Nova Jiu Jitsu

 At Nova , Jiu Jitsu is more than just a sport, self-defense system or fitness program. It’s a lifestyle that improves the whole person with physical, mental and transcendent benefits.

 ## Who We ARE

 NOVA Jiu Jitsu was founded in 2009, making it one of the most senior and well established gyms in Rochester, New York . Under the leadership of 3rd Degree Blackbelt Jason Plaisted, NOVA has an elite team of coaches, bringing more than 50 years of combined experience to the mats.
 The environment at NOVA Jiu Jitsu is unlike any other. Our community strives to provide a safe and supportive environment for all types of athletes.

 ## Our Jiu Jitsu Classes

 ## GI Class

 The Gi is the standard BJJ uniform. It’s made of a thick, durable material designed to withstand being grabbed and pulled. It provides students an opportunity to practice the many grips, chokes and points of control employed in BJJ without ruining your normal clothes.

 ## No-GI Class

 No-Gi classes offer a similar but faster-paced experience. Attended in grappling shorts (athletic shorts without pockets) and a rashguard. No-GI is a great way to fine tune your skills and focus your control on faster opponents.

 ## Kids Class

 Open to children, ages 4-12. These classes provide kids with a safe, constructive and fun atmosphere to learn sportsmanship, build confidence and gain a different perspective on fitness.
