# Classes

 Classes

 WHAT WE DO:
 At Baltimore BJJ we learn by doing.
 With constraint led games and deliberate practice, our classes focus on providing opportunities to develop core skills and movements in real time.
 There is no limit to who can learn this way. We’ve found that both new and established member skill acquisition and confidence have dramatically improved since adopting this school of teaching.
 FEEL FREE TO CONTACT US WITH ANY QUESTIONS!
