 #### History Jiu-Jitsu Unlimited has been a source of Sioux City Jiu-Jitsu training since 2022. From day 1, the focus has been on helping students use Jiu-Jitsu to propel their development on and off the mat.
 Taylor Pace, the new owner of our gym, began his Jiu-Jitsu journey here. Beforehand, Taylor had practiced in other martial arts. But something was different with Jiu-Jitsu. Once he found it, he was hooked. Taylor officially took over in 2026, but had begun implementing his No-Gi, Constraint-Led Approach prior to that.
 Taylor never imagined his career would lead to being a full-time Jiu-Jitsu gym owner – but now he couldn’t imagine it being anything else. He is fully dedicated to providing the Sioux City community with the best experience possible to improve their life on and off the mats!

 #### Explore Our Jiu-Jitsu Programs ### Programs
 Our Jiu-Jitsu programs are designed for everyone to understand. Throughout the use of the Constraint-Led Approach (CLA), students are placed in situations where they have to problem-solve. We believe this is the best way for students to grow their game. Check out each program to find the right fit for you and your kids!

 #### Little Lions – Kids Jiu-Jitsu (ages 4–6) We make sure to build the foundational skills in Little Lions. Students work on coordination while learning plenty of life skills. You’ll watch your children improve their respect, discipline, and other valuable traits. The foundational skills alongside a structured environment will translate to them building high-level grapplers. Sometimes their skills look better than the adults!

 #### Lion Cubs – Kids Jiu-Jitsu (ages 7–9) The Lion Cubs program kicks up the focus on technique. Class gains some intensity as we begin to focus more on grappling and sparring. This is also the age group where we first begin discussing competition and if it’s right for your child.

 #### Young Lions (10 – 13) We consider the Young Lions program the bridge between kids and adult Jiu-Jitsu. Students are allowed a healthy transition that allows them to take their game to the next level among similarly aged peers.

 #### Adult Jiu-Jitsu (ages 16+) Adult classes are a competitive environment where everyone makes each other better. You will watch your game grow while also possibly improving your physical and mental health. It’s a great atmosphere to work hard and become your best self!
