## ABOUT THE GYM
 Raise Your Standards & Get Fit Today

 Quest Fitness Center has been changing lives all over Murray Kentucky since 1982, by Grand Master Dinh. We believe that fitness is not a hobby, but a way of life. We created our gym in hopes of it being a second home to all of our customers. Whether you exercise everyday or you want to Raise Your Standards our gym is your go-to place. From a fully equipped gym, Tae Kwon Do classes, Cardio Kickboxing classes, Boot Camps, Mixed Martial Arts classes, and more Quest Fitness Center can help shape the new you. We want to be your next workout partner. Call us today!
