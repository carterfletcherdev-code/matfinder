 ABOUT THIS TOURNAMENT
 This tournament is targeted at beginner and first-time competitors, ages 4-13. This is tournament is round-robin format, with three-minute rounds. Each competitor is guaranteed at least two matches.
 This tournament is named after Tim Burrill, founder of Tim Burrill Brazilian Jiu itsu (BJJ) in Rhode Island. Tim is a well-respected black belt and known as one of Carlos Machado's early black belts from Texas, who later moved to Rhode Island. He's spent many years sharing his extensive knowledge and philosophy through teaching and interviews and considered a significant figure in spreading BJJ.
