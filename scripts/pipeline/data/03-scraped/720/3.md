#### Welcome to

 # Fight Sports clubBoynton Beach, FL

 Contact Us

 Fight Sports Club Boynton Beach is more than just a gym—it’s the meeting point between experience, excellence, and evolution.
 Founded by two icons of the fight world—Marcio Silva, 3X World Champion in Gi, Nogi and elite BJJ Instructor, and Luiz “Buscapé” Firmino, veteran of PRIDE, DREAM, and WSOF, —this gym brings together decades of battle-tested skill and a shared passion for building fighters from the ground up.
 Marcio Silva began his martial arts journey at age 5 with Judo. By 16, he was a professional cyclist. At 25, he was traveling the world as a sponsored paintball athlete. In the early ’90s, he found his calling: Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu.
 He immersed himself in the roots and modern evolution of BJJ, training with global icons like Roberto “Cyborg” Abreu, leading him to open his own Fight Sports affiliate.
 Luiz “Buscapé” Firmino brings the warrior mindset of a pro fighter with over 30 professional MMA bouts, facing names like Justin Gaethje and Kawajiri. His experience adds elite-level strategy, discipline, and intensity to every class.
 Together, they built Fight Sports Club Boynton Beach to be a gym where tradition meets performance, and every student—from kids to black belts—becomes part of something greater.
