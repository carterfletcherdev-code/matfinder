 ## Frequently Asked Questions

 #### Do I need any prior experience to join classes?

 No experience is needed! Atlas BJJ welcomes beginners of all ages to train — whether you’ve never stepped on the mats before or already have some background in martial arts. Classes are designed to help students improve at their own pace.

 #### What should I expect on my first visit?

 You can start with a FREE introductory trial class to see how you like training. Arrive a few minutes early, wear comfortable workout clothes, and you’ll be guided by friendly instructors. If you decide to join afterward, the team will help you get started with the full program.

 #### What age groups and programs do you offer?

 Atlas BJJ offers training for kids, adults, and women’s self-defense programs. Their classes are structured for all ages and skill levels, helping students build confidence, discipline, strength, and self-defense skills.

 #### What are the benefits of training Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu?

 Training at Atlas BJJ isn’t just about techniques — students also gain physical strength, improved confidence, better discipline, practical self-defense skills, and a supportive community environment.
