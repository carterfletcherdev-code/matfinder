 # Our Mission

 #### Discover the champion within and embrace your inner crazy. SOTG Lifestyle is a Bay Area based Mixed Martial Arts program located at Omni Movement in Hercules, CA. While it is tied to fitness and MMA, it is deeply connected to the mission of self love and self belief. SOTG is a lifestyle, rooted in biblical principles that help nurture and strengthen both the body and mind.
 About SOTG Lifestyle

 #### “Jiu-Jitsu can change your life, if you let it.” — Coach Derrick Easterling
 We want to make you strong. Mentally, physically and emotionally. We want to give you the mindset, self-discipline and resilience to achieve your goals. In life as well as in Jiu-Jitsu. Together we’ll harness the power of Jiu-Jitsu.
