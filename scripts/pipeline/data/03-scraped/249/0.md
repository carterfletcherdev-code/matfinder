 ## There’s a Program for everyone!

 ##### PROGRAMS FOR MEN, WOMEN, AND CHILDREN OF ALL LEVELS

 ### Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu

 At Sensei Studio, we are a proud member of the Caio Terra Association. All classes follow the curriculum set forth by 12-time world champion, Professor Caio Terra, and our schools run in accordance with the CTA standards!

 ### Kids Programs

 At the Sensei Studios we believe that it’s more than helping your child succeed on the mat, it’s about helping your child succeed in life. Jiu-Jitsu teaches children discipline, respect and martial values that will be with your child for a lifetime.

 ### Fitness | STRIKING PROGRAMS

 In addition to our Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, we also offer classes in Strength and Conditioning as well as kickboxing. Whether you prefer to keep it standing or take it to the ground, there's something for everyone at Sensei Studios!
