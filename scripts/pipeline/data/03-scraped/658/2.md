### Jiu jitsu with a mission.

 Guardian Baltimore, a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization, is committed to providing youth jiu jitsu scholarships to underserved children and high quality jiu jitsu to our adult members.
 We are dedicated to offering year-round stability and consistency through our programs, focusing not just on their physical transformation, but also on fostering positive friendships, mentorship opportunities, healthy meal programs and a secure environment during challenging times.

 Donations make our youth scholarship possible.
