 #### Adopt a Cop BJJ

 We are a proud affiliate of Adopt A Cop BJJ! This nonprofit program sponsors local police officers so they are able to train Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu for free until they reach blue belt level. At this point they will have an immense advantage in controlling a combative situation over someone who has no knowledge or experience in grappling regardless of size or strength. We are honored to be a part of this program and help our police officers learn jiu-jitsu and support the safety of our community.
 Please contact us if you are interested in receiving a sponsorship.
