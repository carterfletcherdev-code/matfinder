 ### What to Expect - Jiu-jitsu is a close contact sport. Hygiene and safety are crucial - No shoes on the mat, no bare feet off the mat - Please keep you and your gear clean and free of foul odors - Nails should be trimmed and neat - Wear comfortable, athletic clothing (t-shirt, leggings/shorts) free of zippers or metal
