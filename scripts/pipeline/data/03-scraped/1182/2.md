# Tiny Tots Jiu Jitsu (3-5) ### Tiny Tots Jiu-Jitsu for Kids Ages 3–5 Our Tiny Tots Jiu-Jitsu program for kids ages 3–5 introduces young children to Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu in a fun, safe, and age-appropriate environment . This early childhood martial arts program is designed to help toddlers and preschoolers build confidence, coordination, and discipline through movement-based learning.
 Classes focus on basic Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu fundamentals , including balance, rolling, body awareness, and following instructions. All techniques are taught through games and structured activities , with no striking , making this one of the safest martial arts programs for young children.
 Your child will benefit from:
 - Improved motor skills and coordination - Increased confidence and self-esteem - Better listening and focus - Learning respect and teamwork - A positive introduction to kids martial arts
 Our Tiny Tots BJJ classes are short, engaging, and designed specifically for preschool-aged children . Instructors are experienced in both Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu and child development , ensuring a safe and encouraging learning environment.
