# Kids Jiu Jitsu (6-12) ### Kids Jiu-Jitsu for Kids Ages 6–12
 Our Kids Jiu-Jitsu program for ages 6–12 teaches children the fundamentals of Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu in a safe, structured, and positive learning environment . This program is designed to help kids build confidence, discipline, focus, and self-defense skills while staying active and having fun. Classes emphasize grappling, control, balance, and technique , with instruction tailored to each age group and experience level. Students learn how to defend themselves without striking , making Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu one of the most effective and safest martial arts for children.
 Your child will learn:
 - Core Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu techniques and positional control - Self-defense skills appropriate for real-world situations - Improved focus, discipline, and listening skills - Confidence, resilience, and problem-solving - Respect, teamwork, and sportsmanship
 Our Kids BJJ classes combine technical instruction, drilling, and live positional training in a controlled setting. Instructors emphasize safety, character development, and personal growth , not just competition.
 Whether you are searching for kids Jiu-Jitsu near me , Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu classes for children , or a confidence-building martial arts program , our Kids Jiu-Jitsu program provides a strong foundation for both self-defense and lifelong fitness .
