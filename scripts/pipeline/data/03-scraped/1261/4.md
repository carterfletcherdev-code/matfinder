 Wrestling
 At Vici Wrestling Club in League City, TX, our youth wrestling program is designed to build discipline, confidence, strength, and athletic foundations for kids ages 3–18. We offer age-specific classes including tots (3-4), barbarian (5-6), kids (7-11) Silver (Middle School and beginner High School), and Gold (advanced competitors). In every class we aim to give every child the opportunity to learn the fundamentals of wrestling in a safe, high-energy environment. Our experienced coaches—led by national champions and world-level wrestlers—focus on proper technique, conditioning, and competition preparation to help athletes succeed on and off the mat. Whether your child is new to the sport or aiming for elite competition, our program provides the best youth wrestling training in the Houston metro area, helping athletes develop the courage, work ethic, and skills to excel.
