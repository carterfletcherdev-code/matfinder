 ## Kids Summer Camp

 Our summer sessions are the perfect place for your child to start their journey to becoming Bullyproof. We build confidence, resilience, and real-world self-defense skills through a dynamic blend of Jiu-Jitsu, Judo, and Wrestling. While our curriculum ensures kids build valuable life skills, our primary goal is for every child to have fun and truly enjoy their summer.
 Led by Sensei Mike Murrell, the program uses a high-energy, positive-reinforcement model that transforms complex grappling concepts into engaging games. This is a safe, supportive environment where kids make lasting summer memories while learning to protect themselves.
