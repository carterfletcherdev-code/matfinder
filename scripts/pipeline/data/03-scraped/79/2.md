# Three Reasons Why Martial Arts Is the Perfect Summer Activity For Kids In Reno
 Apr 17 Written By Scott Fritzinger

 When school’s out and the summer energy kicks in, parents everywhere are on the lookout for activities that keep their kids active, engaged, and growing. Enter Guerrilla Jiu Jitsu —an exciting and dynamic option that combines fun, fitness, and valuable life lessons.
 Here are three powerful reasons why Guerrilla Jiu Jitsu is the ultimate summer activity for kids:
 ### 1. It Builds Confidence and Discipline Guerrilla Jiu Jitsu isn’t just about kicks and punches. It's about learning focus, respect, and inner strength. As kids progress and earn new belts or skills, they gain a huge sense of accomplishment. That boost in confidence can carry over into every part of their life—from social settings to school challenges.
 ### 2. It Keeps Them Active and Healthy Summer is the perfect time to get kids off the couch and moving. Our classes are high-energy and engaging, offering a full-body workout that improves coordination, strength, and flexibility. Unlike some team sports, martial arts also allows each child to progress at their own pace—no pressure, just progress.
 ### 3. It’s a Fun and Safe Way to Learn Self-Defense One of the greatest benefits of martial arts is teaching kids how to protect themselves—not just physically, but emotionally and mentally too. Our curriculum is taught in a way that keeps kids excited while empowering them with real-world skills in self-awareness and conflict resolution.
 Whether your child is shy, super energetic, or somewhere in between, Guerrilla Jiu Jitsu offers a summer experience that’s fun, enriching, and unforgettable. It’s more than just an activity—it’s a journey of growth and confidence that lasts long after summer ends.
