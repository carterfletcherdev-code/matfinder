 ## About Our School

 The Guerrilla Jiu Jitsu Motto: Train With Everyone.

 Whether you are an absolute beginner who is terrified of stepping on the mat the first time or a seasoned competitor, our black belt instruction and decades of experience will give you the skills you need to succeed not just on the mat but off as well. For over 20 years, we have been providing a safe, comfortable, and welcoming environment for everyone who walks through our doors with expert black belt instruction.
 We know you need to go to work and your child needs to go to school the next day. Getting hurt is a obviously a concern. What makes Guerrilla different is how we teach and how we train.
 If you’ve tried jiu jitsu before and didn’t like that experience, call us , text us , or better yet swing by and try a class for free to see the difference.
