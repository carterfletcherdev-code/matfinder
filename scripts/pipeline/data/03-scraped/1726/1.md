# DELTA TRAINING CENTER #PROGRESSOVERALL #ALWAYSFORWARD

 Delta Training Center was created to provide a world class training environment for grappling and fitness hobbyists, competitors, and everything in between.
 We strive to provide a holistic learning and training center for every student, at any level of experience or goal set.
 This space was created to empower the student to push forward in relentless pursuit of becoming the best version of themselves. The first step to that journey starts with opening the door.

 # WHAT WE OFFER The facility provides instruction, training, and equipment to support goals of members from hobbyists to elite competitors.

 ## BRAZILIAN JIU JITSU
 A grappling art used in MMA and known for effective real-world self defense. We offer classes for adults and children, in both gi and nogi (without the gi) styles.

 ## WEIGHT AREA Weight area open for training from 4am-11pm daily for adult and teenagers with parent supervision. Includes squat rack, free weights, dumbells, kettle bells, assault bike, rowing machine, treadmill
