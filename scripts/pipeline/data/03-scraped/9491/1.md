 MMA Striking class, where athletes of all levels can enhance their stand-up game and develop the skills needed for mixed martial arts. This class focuses on integrating various striking techniques, footwork, and defense strategies for a well-rounded approach to MMA.
 Each session starts with a dynamic warm-up that includes agility drills and mobility exercises to get you ready for high-intensity training. You'll then progress to technique work, where you'll practice striking fundamentals like punches, kicks, elbows, and knees, along with combinations that transition seamlessly for MMA. The instructors break down each technique, paying close attention to proper form and movement, while offering advanced tips for experienced fighters.
 You'll also engage in partner drills and controlled sparring rounds to apply what you've learned in real-time scenarios, developing your timing, accuracy, and situational awareness. The class emphasizes adapting your striking to the unique demands of MMA, including managing distance, clinch work, and striking from unconventional angles.
