 ### Benefits of Jiu-Jitsu for Children - Bully Proof, Self-Defense and Street Awareness - Authentic Gracie Jiu-Jitsu - Instilling Confidence, Respect, and Discipline - Development of Social Skills - Development of muscle, motor skills and overall coordination - Physical Fitness

 #### Teaching Kids Self Defense is Our Specialty ##### Self Defense At its' core, Jiu-Jitsu teaches kids how to defend themselves against attackers and when to find help.

 ##### Anti- Bullying We give your child the tools needed to overcome physical, verbal, and psychological abuse. Instilling in them the confidence necessary to deal with uncomfortable situations.

 ##### Teamwork & Responsibility We teach our students to deal with bullies without turning them into one. We emphasize discipline, responsibility, and respect!
