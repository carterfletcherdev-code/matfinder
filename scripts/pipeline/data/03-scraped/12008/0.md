 ## Check in guide

 ### Zen Planner Reservations and check ins can only be done 30 minutes prior to start time.
 #### Step 1 Download the Zen Planner Member App for iPhone or Android and log in. See the front desk if you don't know your login.

 #### Step 2 Select the calendar tab and reserve the class you want to attend. Make sure to confirm your choice in the pop-up

 #### Step 3 When you are ready to start class, click check in and confirm your choice.

 #### Step 4 If you see a green check mark next to the class, you're checked in and ready to go!

 ### Front Desk #### Step 1 Select the class you wish to attend on the left side of the screen

 #### Step 2 Search your name until you see your full name appear below. Then tap the circle above your name.

 #### Step 3 You have successfully checked in if you see a green check mark next to your name and your profile appears in the Checked In tab.
