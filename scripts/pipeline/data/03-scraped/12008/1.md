## Overview #### Ages 3+Must be potty trainedNo degree offered This is not your ordinary martial arts class! Our enthusiastic instructors use martial arts as a tool to teach your child valuable life lessons. Warriors learn respect, confidence, focus, self-control, and much more in a fun environment, fueled by an age appropriate mix of taekwondo, kickboxing, and streetwise self-defense. The skills and drills used in class are taught in a way students can understand and use at both home and school!
