# Programs ##### Next Level Martial Arts & Fitness provides many programs to help each student find their best self, providing physical and mental health benefits. Our martial arts training programs in Tigard include Brazilian Jiu Jitsu, split between kids classes (5–16) and adult classes (16+), Thai Boxing (12+), and Strength and Conditioning (16+ unless otherwise approved). Each class incorporates lessons to take in and out of the academy to life itself!

 #### Next Level Martial Arts & Fitness ### Programs Overview

 #### Kids Brazilian Jiu Jitsu ##### Our Kids Brazilian Jiu Jitsu program is designed to build confidence, discipline, and physical fitness in a fun and engaging way. Through age-appropriate techniques and drills, children learn self-defense skills, teamwork, and respect. With experienced instructors fostering a positive environment, kids gain the tools to grow on and off the mat.

 #### Adult Brazilian Jiu Jitsu ##### Our Adults Brazilian Jiu Jitsu program offers a comprehensive approach to self-defense, fitness, and personal growth. Whether you’re new to martial arts or an experienced practitioner, our classes provide a supportive space to refine technique, build resilience, and achieve your goals. Every session emphasizes skill development and mental strength, empowering you for life both on and off the mat.

 Learn More

 #### Thai Boxing (Ages 12+) ##### Our Thai Boxing program combines traditional techniques with modern training methods to create a high-energy, full-body workout. You’ll learn powerful striking skills, improve coordination, and enhance endurance while training in a supportive and challenging environment. Perfect for all skill levels, this program is as effective for self-defense as it is for achieving peak fitness.

 #### Strength and Conditioning ##### Strength and Conditioning at Next Level Martial Arts & Fitness is designed to improve your athletic performance and overall health. Through dynamic workouts tailored to your level, you’ll develop functional strength, endurance, and flexibility. This program complements martial arts training perfectly, helping you build the physical foundation to excel in all aspects of life.
