# Kids Brazilian Jiu Jitsu ##### Building Confidence Through Movement Our Kids Brazilian Jiu Jitsu classes in Tigard offer an exciting blend of physical activity, discipline, and life lessons. Designed to build confidence and resilience, young students learn foundational techniques in a safe and engaging environment. With our skilled instructors and age-appropriate training, kids not only develop martial arts skills but also cultivate respect, focus, and teamwork.

 #### Brazilian Jiu Jitsu isn’t just for adults. ### Our Kids Brazilian Jiu Jitsu Program Overview
 #### Builds Confidence and Character ##### Kids who train in Brazilian Jiu Jitsu learn essential life skills that extend far beyond the mats. Through consistent practice, they develop a sense of perseverance, patience, and humility that helps them tackle challenges head-on. They build respect for themselves and others, learn the value of hard work, and experience the rewarding feeling of accomplishing their goals. These lessons in discipline, dignity, and self-awareness prepare them to face obstacles both in training and in everyday life, shaping them into confident, resilient, and compassionate individuals.

 #### It’s Fun and Engaging ##### At Next Level Martial Arts & Fitness, we create an atmosphere where kids can enjoy themselves while learning invaluable skills. Our instructors are passionate about sharing their love of Jiu Jitsu with the next generation, combining structured lessons with playful activities. Classes include teamwork-driven games, exercises that boost strength and flexibility, and creative drills that channel kids’ boundless energy into focused learning. It’s the perfect balance of fun and discipline, ensuring kids stay motivated and excited about their training journey.

 #### Promotes Inclusivity and Growth ##### In our kids’ programs, everyone trains together, creating a sense of unity and equality. Kids have the opportunity to partner with peers of varying sizes, skill levels, and strengths, which accelerates their learning and adaptability. Training with diverse partners teaches them to approach challenges with an open mind and a willingness to grow. This inclusive environment fosters a family-like bond among students, where everyone supports and pushes one another to become their best. Through this shared experience, young practitioners gain not just skills, but lifelong friendships and values.
