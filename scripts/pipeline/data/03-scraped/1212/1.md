## Mini Jits Kids #### For ages 3-5 A fun high-energy class designed to introduce your preschooler to the jiu-jitsu movement and develop class social skills.

 ## Jits Kids #### Minimum age 5 Jits Kids will focus on the fundamentals of Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu in a fun and active class setting.

 ## Adult BJJ #### Minimum age 14 Fundamentals of BJJ based on leverage and proper body mechanics.

 ## Competition Team #### All ages We compete at all local tournaments such as Naga, Fuji, Newbreed, Grappling Games, etc. We also have competitors that prefer bigger competitions such as IBJJF, Summit Grappling, Submission Hunter Pro, etc. Whatever level competitor you chose to be, we will train according.
