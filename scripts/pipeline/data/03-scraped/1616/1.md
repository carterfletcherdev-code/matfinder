# Jiu-Jitsu # BQUICK Jiu-Jitsu Under the BQUICK lineage, Thunderkick focuses on the core techniques that every jiu jitsu practitioner should know. With this class, members will be able to improve their overall jiu jitsu knowledge, improve their movement and coordination, and receive an introduction to more advanced jiu jitsu techniques.
 # No-Gi No-Gi is grappling without the traditional uniform, instead you wear shorts and a rash guard. In No-Gi you cannot grab your opponent’s clothing. Members will have the opportunity to learn the same modern jiu jitsu being taught in Gi classes, but learn how to adapt them to No-Gi situations.
