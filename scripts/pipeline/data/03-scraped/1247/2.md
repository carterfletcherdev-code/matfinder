 # The Fundamentals of Jiu-Jitsu:

 ## Your Guide to starting Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu

 Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu (BJJ) is a martial art that emphasizes technique over brute strength, making it applicable for everyone. Whether you’re a complete beginner or have less than 6 months of training, let our fundamentals of Jiu-Jitsu guide your progression. At Mavericks BJJ , we believe that a strong foundation in the core movements, principles, and techniques is the key to becoming proficient in Jiu-Jitsu.

 ## Why Are the Fundamentals of Jiu-Jitsu So Important?

 Mastering the basics of Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu lays the groundwork for more advanced techniques. The fundamentals of Jiu-Jitsu are not just a set of techniques; they are the underlying concepts that guide their application. By building a solid understanding of these fundamentals, such as escapes, guard passing, and submissions, practitioners learn to apply them more effectively in real scenarios, adapting to different situations with greater skill and understanding.

 At Mavericks BJJ , we offer an Introduction to the Fundamentals of Jiu-Jitsu course designed to build a strong foundation in essential techniques.

 ## What Will You Learn in the Fundamentals of Jiu-Jitsu Course?

 Our Introduction to the Fundamentals of Jiu-Jitsu course covers essential techniques in a progressive, engaging, and practical way. The curriculum is divided into three main sections:

 ### Building the Foundation - Learn core movements such as shrimping, breakfalls, and standing up in base.
 - Master foundational techniques like the guard sit-up sweep, closed guard back take, and more.

 ### Developing Escapes & Passing the Guard - Escape from challenging positions like side mount, back control, and mount.
 - Guard passing techniques, including the Captain Morgan and knee cut pass.

 ### Putting it All Together - Combine techniques like the triangle armbar combo, scissor sweep to mount, and collar chokes.
