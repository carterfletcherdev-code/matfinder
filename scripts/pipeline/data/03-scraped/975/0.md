## CLASSES AT MID CITY MMA
 JIU JITSU
 Jiu Jitsu is a predominantly ground-based martial art which uses leverage, angles, pressure and timing, as well as knowledge of the human anatomy, in order to control and submit one’s opponent. Jiu Jitsu focuses on control positions, pins, and the application of chokes and joint-manipulations, all while utilizing grips on the gi (or kimono).

 NOGI SUBMISSION GRAPPLING
 Nogi Submission Grappling is like Brazilian Jiu Jitsu but without the traditional uniform (gi/kimono and belt). Instead, shorts/spats and rash guards are worn. In nogi, you cannot grab your opponent's clothing.
