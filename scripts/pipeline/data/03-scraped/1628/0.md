# Classes

 ## Serg Brazilian Jiu Jitsu and Fitness offers classes for beginners and experienced students. Our Fundamental classes have students that may be attending their first class learning alongside students with years of experience in a positive environment. Attention is placed on learning basic technique and self defense for new students and refinement for experienced students. No experience needed to attend and even advanced practitioners can progress. Brazilian Jiu Jitsu is an excellent way to lose weight and stay in shape while learning the most effective form of self defense.
