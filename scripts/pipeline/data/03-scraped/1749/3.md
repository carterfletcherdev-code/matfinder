 ### All about

 # carsons

 ### Mission Carson Fight & Fitness will bring a world class training facility for mixed martial arts and fitness to the Tulsa metropolitan area. This will be accomplished by creating a community where every individual can be equipped, encouraged, and motivated to maximize their potential.

 #### Jonny Carson Professor Carson has spent over 20 years training with some of the top martial arts practitioners in the world and brings a blend of traditional Brazilian Jiu Jitsu and American Jiu Jitsu. He also brings a unique style of Muay Thai that cannot be found anywhere else in the 918 or in the surrounding areas. This style was honed from years of training under Muay Thai legend Jongsanan Fairtex aka “The Woodenman”.

 - Whatever your goal is, we are here to help

 #### All ages, all levels

 Here at Carson Fight Fitness, we want to help people at all stages of their fitness journey. Whether you are brand new or a black belt, we are here to help you get better each time you come in to our classes.

 #### approach We also recognize that that you will spend more time living off the mats than training on the mats which makes us passionate about training the whole person. Success in life can be found when there is alignment, or syzygy, in the whole person. Whether you are a beginner, an intermediate practitioner, advanced, or competitive athlete, there is something for you at Carson Fight & Fitness.
