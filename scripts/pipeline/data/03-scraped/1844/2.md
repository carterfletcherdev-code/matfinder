# Free Trial Classes

 What’s a trial class?
 It’s important to make sure that this is a good fit for you BEFORE you sign up. We want you to feel comfortable here, with yourself, and your potential peers. We encourage all new students, experienced and unexperienced, to come in for a week of trial classes before you sign up.
 There’s no commitment, and it’s free!

 How do the trial classes work?
 It’s easy! Just stop in at your convenience, or drop us a DM on social media to chat or ask any questions. They’re totally free with no commitment, so stop in, try a few, and make sure we are a good fit for you!
