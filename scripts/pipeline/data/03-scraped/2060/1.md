 # Our Programs World-Class Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu for Every Age and Skill Level
 Brazilian Top Team North Dallas offers specialized BJJ programs designed for children, teens, and adults. Each program is carefully structured to develop technical skill, mental discipline, and physical fitness in a safe, supportive environment led by world-class instructors.

 Ages 4-7
 ## Little Axes Our Little Axes program introduces young children to the fundamentals of Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu in a fun, engaging, and safe environment. Through age-appropriate games, drills, and activities, children develop coordination, confidence, and discipline while learning valuable self-defense skills.
 The Little Axes curriculum is specifically designed for children aged 4-7, focusing on gross motor skill development, basic BJJ movements, and social-emotional growth. Classes emphasize respect, listening, following instructions, and teamwork—skills that benefit children both on and off the mat.
 ### Program Features - 45-minute classes, 2x per week - Perfect duration for young attention spans - Character development curriculum - Focus on respect, discipline, and perseverance - Anti-bullying & self-defense techniques - Age-appropriate personal safety skills - Coordination & balance training - Foundation for athletic development - Safe, supervised environment - Small class sizes with experienced instructors - Belt progression system - Milestone recognition and goal setting
 Parents love the Little Axes program because they see immediate improvements in their child's confidence, focus, and physical fitness. Many children who start in Little Axes continue their BJJ journey through our Strong Axes program and beyond.
 Start Your Child's Journey

 Ages 8-15
 ## Strong Axes The Strong Axes program provides structured, comprehensive Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu training for pre-teens and teenagers. Students develop real grappling ability, mental toughness, and competitive spirit through rigorous technical instruction in both Gi and No-Gi disciplines.
 Strong Axes students train in a more advanced curriculum that introduces complex techniques, live sparring, and competition preparation. This program builds physical strength, cardiovascular fitness, problem-solving skills, and resilience. Many Strong Axes students compete in local and national tournaments, earning medals and building confidence through competition.
 ### Program Features - Gi & No-Gi training - Complete grappling skillset development - Competition opportunities - Local and national tournament preparation - Structured belt progression - Clear path from white to green/brown belt - Live sparring sessions - Controlled practice with peers at similar skill levels - Leadership development - Senior students mentor newer teammates - Physical conditioning - Strength, flexibility, and cardiovascular fitness
 The Strong Axes program is ideal for youth who want to take their training seriously, whether their goal is self-defense, competition success, or simply developing a lifelong skill. Our instructors provide individual attention to help each student reach their potential while fostering a team-oriented, respectful training culture.

 Ages 16+
 ## Adult Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu Our Adult BJJ program welcomes students of all experience levels, from complete beginners to advanced practitioners. Whether your goal is self-defense, fitness, competition, or personal growth, Brazilian Top Team North Dallas provides world-class instruction in both Gi and No-Gi Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu.
 Adults train alongside teammates of similar skill levels in dedicated Beginners classes and All Levels classes. Our curriculum covers fundamental positions, submissions, escapes, takedowns, and strategy. Advanced students have access to competition training, open mat sessions, and specialized technique seminars led by Professor Leonardo Machado and guest instructors.
 ### Program Features - Beginners & All Levels classes - Structured progression from day one - Self-defense & competition tracks - Choose your focus or train both - Gi and No-Gi training - Develop well-rounded grappling skills - Open mat & sparring sessions - Practice techniques in live rolling - World-class instruction - Learn from Professor Leonardo Machado and experienced coaches - Supportive team culture - Train with friendly, respectful training partners
 Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu offers adults a complete workout that challenges both body and mind. Beyond physical fitness and self-defense skills, students develop problem-solving ability, stress management, and lifelong friendships. Our academy welcomes professionals, parents, and athletes who want to add BJJ to their lifestyle.
 Most adults start with 2-3 classes per week and gradually increase as their schedule allows. Unlimited membership options provide maximum flexibility for dedicated students. Join us for a free trial class and experience the BTT difference.
