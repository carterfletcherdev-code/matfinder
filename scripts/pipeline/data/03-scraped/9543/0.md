 ## QUESTIONS TEMPE PARENTS ASK What's the best age to start kids jiu-jitsu?
 Our kids program is designed for children who are ready for structured group activities. Every child develops differently — reach out with your child's age and we'll let you know if they're a good fit for the current class.

 Is jiu-jitsu safe for kids?
 When taught properly, BJJ is one of the safest martial arts for kids. There's no striking — only controlled grappling and positional work. Our coaches are trained to maintain a safe, supervised environment, and kids learn to tap immediately when they need to stop.

 How is BJJ different from the karate studios in Tempe?
 Karate focuses on striking and forms. BJJ focuses on grappling, control, and submissions. In practical self-defense terms, BJJ teaches your child to neutralize a bully without throwing a punch — which is why many Tempe parents are making the switch.

 Absolutely. We have a viewing area where parents can watch every class. Many Tempe parents tell us watching their child's progress is the best part of their week.
