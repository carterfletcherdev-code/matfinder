 # Frequently Asked Questions

 Find answers to your questions right here.

 - Q: Do I need any experience to start? I've never trained before. A: None at all. Most of our members started exactly where you are — no background, no idea what to expect. Our beginner classes are specifically designed to teach you the fundamentals from scratch in a welcoming, no-ego environment. You'll never be thrown on the mat and left to figure it out. Our coaches meet you where you are and build from there. - What should I wear to my first class? A: For your first visit, athletic shorts and a t-shirt are completely fine — no gear needed. If you decide to stick around, we'll help you get set up with the right equipment (gi or no-gi attire depending on the class). Just bring water, a positive attitude, and show up a few minutes early so we can get you checked in and introduced to the team. - Q: Is jiu jitsu safe? I don't want to get hurt. A: Safety is our first priority on the mat. Jiu jitsu is one of the safest martial arts because the focus is on technique and control — not striking. We train at a pace that's appropriate for your level, and our coaches emphasize proper movement and tap-out culture from day one. Beginners train with beginners, and everyone here looks out for each other. - Q: How do I know which class is right for me? A: We offer classes for all levels — from complete beginners to advanced competitors. If you're brand new, start with our Fundamentals classes. Not sure? Come in for a free trial class and our coaches will point you in the right direction after seeing where you're at.
