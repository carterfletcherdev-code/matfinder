# Kids programs #### Unlimited classes! At Fight City SLC, our mission is to help kids actually improve. We really enjoy seeing kids learn skill, build confidence, respect, and become stronger. To improve, kids need consistency. Thats why we provide 2 afternoon hours of kid classes Monday-Thursday, and then one hour on Fridays. That way, they can come when it best fits your schedule. To come in the afternoon, kids must be 8-13yrs.

 #### Homeschool Hours! With the growing homeschool community we are excited to offer special hours for homeschool kids.
 Mondays 12-1pm, and Wednesdays 12-1pm.
 We accept kids 5yrs+ for our homechool class hours.
 Mondays we focus on Jiu-Jitsu, and Wednesdays we focus on striking and kicking.
 For the afternoon kids classes, kids must be 8yrs or older.

 #### What you need! Kids will need their own water bottle.
 Kids wanting to participate in boxing will need boxing gloves.
 Kids wanting to participate in jiu jitsu will need a gi/jiu jitsu uniform.
 All equipment will be for sale at the gym.
