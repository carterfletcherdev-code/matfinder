 ## Our Story
 At White Label, we understand the importance of feeling safe and confident in the face of potential danger. Our business was inspired by a personal experience involving a violent incident, which led to the creation of SHESAFE Training, a women's only program offered four days a week. We specialize in self-defense training, martial arts, personal training, nutrition coaching, and health and wellness promotion services, with a focus on supporting women. Our experienced trainers are passionate about helping our clients develop the proper techniques to exceed their potential, both physically and mentally.
