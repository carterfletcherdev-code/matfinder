 ### Jiu Jitsu “Jiu Jitsu is the art and science of control that leads to submission.” It applies leverage and momentum to focus the greatest amount of force that a practitioner can generate against the weakest point of an adversary’s body. This can allow a weaker or smaller jiu jitsu practitioner to defeat a larger opponent. Jiu Jitsu is a martial art of the mind as much as it is the body. It is the science of know the correct technique and when to apply it, and the art of creatively flowing from each technique to the next.
 What you will need for this class:
 A jiu jitsu gi and a belt. If you do not have one, they can purchased at the school. Loaner gis and belts are available for free trial students. A rash guard or t-shirt is recommended for underneath the gi top, but is not required.
