# Nitsuj martial arts LIVE interactive virtual classes #### Join our Virtual martial arts classes in our Virtual Studio Nitsuj Martial Arts virtual classes are live and interactive group classes in the comfort of your home. Our fun safe and positive 30 minute weekly classes are available on any device via zoom app.

 Our fun and goal oriented virtual classes include:

 - A safe and positive outlet to connect with friends - Exercises and activities to release excess energy and stress - Character development through the Sensei High Five ™️ System (Peacefulness, Discipline, Respect, Bravery, and Honesty) - Virtual ceremonies where the children will progress up the ranks and earn their belts and trophies.

 Tuition is $59 a month. You can cancel anytime, there are no registration fees, test fees, or any other hidden fees. Once registered, your tuition will be charged monthly to the card on file.

 The first class is FREE. If you decide to sign up for monthly classes, a uniform (pants and Gi top) and white belt will be mailed to you for free!

 If you have any questions please text or call 860-299-6097, or email justin@nitsujmartialarts.com
 *Virtual classes are for ages 3 and up.
