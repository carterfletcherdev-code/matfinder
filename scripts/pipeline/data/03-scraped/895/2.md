### Grove City Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu Academy offers the highest level and most experienced instruction in the Grove City, southwest Columbus Ohio area in Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, Muay Thai and Kids martial arts.

 ### Classes We Offer

 - Brazilian Jiu Jitsu Done in a “Gi” (uniform). BJJ is a self-defense martial and combat sport based on grappling, ground fighting and submission holds. BJJ approaches self-defense by emphasizing taking an opponent to the ground, gaining a dominant position, and using a number of techniques to force them into submission. - NoGi Jiu Jitsu Similar to BJJ but is a faster pace, has more wrestling and also many more leg locks. This class does not wear the Gi but wears shorts and a rashguard. - Muay Thai A kickboxing style from Thailand using punches, kicks, knees, elbows and clinch work. Heavily utilized in MMA, Muay Thai is known for its power, and ferocity. - MMA We have adults who sometimes want to step in to the cage to test their skills after training for awhile. GCBJJ has produced many champions in MMA - Youth Brazilian Jiu Jitsu Same as the adult program but from ages 5-13. Kids will be broken down in to age groups and learn BJJ at their pace. - Youth Muay Thai An introduction to Muay Thai and cardio workout for kids. They will learn Muay Thai basics.
