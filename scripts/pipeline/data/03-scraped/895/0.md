# Schedule

 We understand that everyone leads a different life, and everyone has a different goal or reason for being in our gym. At Grove City Brazilian Jiu Jitsu, we work hard to offer various classes and times, to better accommodate the schedules and goals of all of our members.

 See the schedule below, each class and time remains the same week after week. Please feel free to reach out to any of the coaches if you have questions, and we will do our best to answer them for you.

 MONDAY/WEDNESDAY
 10:00-11:30am: Adult No Gi
 4-4:50pm: Kids BJJ
 5-6pm: Members Only Open Mat
 6-6:45pm: Adult BJJ Instruction
 6:45-7:30pm: Members Only Open Mat
 7:30-8:30PM: Adult Muay Thai

 SUNDAY
 10-11:00am: BJJ Fundamentals

 TUESDAY/THURSDAY
 10:00-11:30am: Adult BJJ
 5-5:45pm: Kids Muay Thai
 6-6:50pm: Kids BJJ
 7:10-8:30PM: Adult No Gi (Front Room)
 7:10-8:30PM: MMA (Back Room)

 FRIDAY
 6-7:00PM: Boxing

 SATURDAY
 9-9:50am: Kids No Gi
 10-11:15am- Adult BJJ
 11am-12:00pm: The Legion Project (first responders, Active Military/Vets, free of charge)

 Note: New students are not permitted to start their free week on Saturday, as this class format varies from the normal classes. Both adults and kids are permitted to attend a month of normal classes before attending Saturdays.
