 #### About Our School We strive to provide the highest quality BJJ training at The Jiu-Jitsu Institute. Our staff brings in heaps of experience and knowledge. Our training is well thought out – always evolving and improving. Students are kept engaged with interactive activities that are fun and don’t focus on one thing for too long. We also make sure to be friendly and inviting to everyone, encouraging anti-bullying and staying patient with students at their own pace. Multiple coaches stay on the mat at a time for more personalized training as we push consistency for true growth. We encourage students to be their best, even possibly become future instructors themselves! Whatever your goals are, The Jiu-Jitsu Institute is here for you!

 #### History The Jiu-Jitsu Institute has brought the Wilmington community the best Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu experience we can since May 2018. Founder and owner Gil Alcocer opened our doors to bring his passion for Jiu-Jitsu and its benefits to others. Gil’s own BJJ experience has shown him that you can learn any lesson through BJJ and correlates directly to life. After beginning martial arts at 7 years old and serving in the Air Force, Gil began Jiu-Jitsu at 23. He trained under the renowned Mendes brothers, who built up his Jiu-Jitsu skills and knowledge. He’s used all his experience to make The Jiu-Jitsu Institute a special place, with hopes to grow even more to best serve the community.

 ### More Than Martial Arts – A Place to Grow
 At The Jiu-Jitsu Institute, we go beyond technique. Our academy is built on core values that create the best environment for growth—cleanliness, camaraderie, engagement, high-level instruction, and patience. From a spotless facility to a welcoming community, every detail is designed to help you train safely, stay motivated, and progress at your own pace.

 ##### Clean Cleanliness is one of the most important things we strive toward at The Jiu-Jitsu Institute. Everything is kept as high quality as possible for students to train in a healthy, germ-free environment.

 ##### Camaraderie You can join us and get more than just great BJJ training. We have built a culture that sees families and community members come together and grow while developing strong bonds.

 ##### Interactive Our classes make sure to keep students engaged at all times. Everything is planned to keep students on their toes with hands-on activities.

 ##### High-Level Technique Our technique is amongst the best. Our experienced staff prides themselves on providing training at the highest level possible.

 ##### Patience We don’t expect students to become the best fighters in the world overnight. Students are taught to grow at their pace as instructors help each student reach their own goals.

 ##### Safety The well-being of our students is a top priority. From proper warm-ups to supervised sparring, we focus on keeping Jiu-Jitsu safe and enjoyable for everyone.
