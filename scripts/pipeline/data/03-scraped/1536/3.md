 # Common Questions & Misconceptions

 ## Will I have to Spar? Our kickboxing and jiu-jitsu classes are geared towards a variety of fitness and experience levels. We never make our students spar or do anything they aren’t comfortable with.

 ## Do I Have Time To Train? We offer classes as early as 6am and as late as 8pm. Our classes last one hour and many people come in during their lunch break or before work if night time doesnt work.

 ## Do Women Train Here? Absolutely! We offer a safe, fun and friendly environment for all ages, fitness/experience levels and genders. You will always be greeted with a smile and we all work as a team to make sure every member benefits from their experience.

 ## Do I Have To Be In Shape? There is no better way to get in shape. Our coaches will help you improve based off your personal goals and fitness level. The best thing you can do is start.

 ## Do I Need Prior Experience? No experience needed. Our students range from beginners to seasoned black belts. Everyone here is happy to help new students learn the art we love.

 ## Am I Too Old To Start? You are NEVER too old to start. Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu focuses on skilled and controlled movements not strength or explosiveness. Many practitioners continue to train into old age as this art often times becomes a lifestyle.
