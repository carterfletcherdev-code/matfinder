### Welcome to Roughstock JiuJitsu

 Roughstock was founded on the principle of providing students with the opportunity to train jujitsu at a high level. We emphasize not only mastering the martial art itself but also understanding the reasoning behind our techniques, all within a fun and supportive environment. Here, you’ll find a place where you can feel comfortable being yourself, engage in open communication with both your coaches and fellow students, and thrive without judgment.

 Strength in Mind and Body

 At Roughstock Jiu Jitsu, we believe that jiujitsu is more than just a physical self-defense art form. It's a way of life that requires respect, determination, and a winning mindset both on and off the mat. Our experienced instructors are dedicated to helping you unleash your full potential, both in our Jiujitsu School and in life.

 ## Programs & Schedule

 Kids Gi & No-Gi
 Gi JiuJitsu involves wearing a traditional martial arts uniform called a Gi , which consists of a jacket, pants, and a belt that indicates rank.
 NoGi JiuJitsu is practiced without the Gi. Kids wear athletic clothing such as rash guards, shorts, and spats.

 Adult Gi & No-Gi
 Gi JiuJitsu is the traditional form of Jiu-Jitsu, practiced while wearing the Gi uniform, consisting of a jacket, pants, and belt.
 NoGi JiuJitsu is the modern and faster-paced counterpart to Gi BJJ, practiced in athletic attire such as rash guards, shorts, and spats.

 Open Mat
 An Open Mat in Jiu-Jitsu is an informal training session where practitioners of all levels gather to roll (our version of sparring), practice techniques, and exchange knowledge in a less structured setting than a traditional class. It’s open to anyone from the gym, and visitors from other academies are welcome. We encourage cross-training.
