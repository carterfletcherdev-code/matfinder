 ## Rafael Babilônia Cristovão
 Rafael Babilônia Cristovão is a Unity Jiu Jitsu Black Belt under Professor Murilo Santana and a dedicated and experienced jiu jitsu competitor and instructor. Born and raised in Brazil, Rafael first became interested in jiu jitsu at 9 years old to learn the art of self defense and to protect his family. At 19, he left his hometown for São Paulo to practice jiu jitsu full time. While in São Paulo, Rafael received his brown belt from multiple time world champion Leandro Lo.

 In 2016, he moved to NYC to train under Professor Murilo Santana at Unity Jiu Jitsu. Four years later, Rafael received his Black Belt from Murilo. During his time at Unity, Murilo’s instruction was so influential Rafael shifted his jiu jitsu goals from competitor to full time jiu jitsu professor.

 ## Frequently Asked Questions
 FAQ

 ### Is Brazilian Jiu Jitsu suitable for beginners?

 Yes, Brazilian Jiu Jitsu is suitable for beginners of all ages and fitness levels.

 - - - -

 ### Do I need to be in shape to start training Brazilian Jiu Jitsu?

 No, Brazilian Jiu Jitsu is for everyone, regardless of current fitness level.

 - - - -

 ### I'm worried about getting injured. Is Brazilian Jiu Jitsu safe?

 Brazilian Jiu Jitsu emphasizes technique and control, reducing the risk of injury.

 - - - -

 ### Can Brazilian Jiu Jitsu help with self-defense?

 Absolutely. Brazilian Jiu Jitsu teaches effective self-defense techniques for individuals of all sizes and strengths.

 - - - -

 ### What can I expect in my first Brazilian Jiu Jitsu class?

 In your first class, you’ll start with a warm-up followed by instruction on basic techniques and positions.

 - - - -

 ### I'm not interested in competing. Can I still train Brazilian Jiu Jitsu?

 Yes, many practitioners train for self-improvement, fitness, and camaraderie without competing.

 - - - -

 ### What if I'm a parent considering enrolling my child in Brazilian Jiu Jitsu?

 Brazilian Jiu Jitsu can benefit children by promoting discipline, confidence, and physical fitness. Our academy offers a safe environment with experienced instructors adept at engaging children effectively.
