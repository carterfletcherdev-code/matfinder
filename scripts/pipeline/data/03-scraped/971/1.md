# Adult Classes

 ## Adult Classes Our Adult program is open to anyone 13 years and older, with training tailored to each student’s size and ability to ensure both safety and progress. The curriculum at Kuma covers everything from the fundamentals of Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu to advanced strategy and technique, making it the perfect fit for complete beginners as well as experienced practitioners.
 Classes include technical instruction, live drilling, and controlled sparring, giving students the chance to sharpen their skills, build fitness, and develop real-world confidence. Beyond the mats, the program strengthens discipline, focus, and problem-solving—qualities that carry over into everyday life.
 What makes Kuma special is our community-first atmosphere. Training here isn’t just about learning Jiu-Jitsu; it’s about becoming part of a team that pushes each other to improve, celebrates progress, and builds lasting friendships. Whether your goal is fitness, self-defense, or competition, Kuma provides the structure, challenge, and support to help you succeed.
