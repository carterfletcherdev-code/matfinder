 ### Self Defense Brazilian Jiu-jitsu is the most effective and in demand martial art system in the world today.
 It is the foundation of most of the world’s military and Law Enforcement agencies combative systems, and it has also become the go-to style of self-defense for men, women and children of all ages around the world.

 ### Competition Brazilian Jiu-jitsu offers a safe and prolific competitive outlet for adults and children worldwide.
 Whether you’re new to competitive sports or a former high school or college athlete looking to get back into the game, Sport Jiu-Jitsu’s local, national, and international events provide the perfect opportunity to reignite your passion for a competitive lifestyle.

 ### Lifestyle For many practitioners, the attraction to Brazilian Jiu-jitsu is the ‘Jiu-jitsu lifestyle’ .
 A way of living life that supports a positive fitness-oriented lifestyle, and healthy choices in all areas of your life while you pursue your fullest potential.
