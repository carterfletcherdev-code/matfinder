# Traditionalism, Professionalism, and Brotherhood.

 Fairbanks World Tang Soo Do Karate Academy is a certified studio within the World Tang Soo Do Association. As a member of Fairbanks World Tang Soo Do and the WTSDA, you will be a part of a family that is 180,000 members strong and growing. Also, with hundreds of schools in over 40 countries, there is a good chance that wherever you travel or relocate, there will be a certified studio nearby that recognizes your rank and will welcome you as a member of the Tang Soo Do family.
 Association membership includes rank and promotion certification, newsletters, and invitations to sanctioned events such as tournaments, clinics, and camps. For more information about the World Tang Soo Do Association please visit: wtsda.com
