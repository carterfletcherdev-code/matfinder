 ## About Cavalo BJJ
 At Cavalo BJJ, we believe that Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu (BJJ) is a sport for everyone. BJJ is a ground-based martial art focused on using leverage and positions to apply submission holds against the opponent or subdue him with superior positioning. The techniques used in BJJ allow all ages and body shapes to excel in martial arts.

 Cavalo BJJ has been locally owned and operated in Columbia, MO since 2010 and we are dedicated to helping students of all ages and skill levels along their jiu jitsu journey. Our smaller class sizes ensure personalized attention and coaching. At Cavalo BJJ, members not only become training partners but also friends. We foster a family-friendly environment where it's common for multiple family members to train here.
