 ## What Will My Child’s First Class Look Like?

 Signing up is easy! Once registered, our team will schedule a 30-minute orientation before their first class. We’ll lend them a Gi and introduce them to their coach to make sure they feel comfortable before stepping onto the mats.

 Class starts with fun warm-ups and movement drills, followed by practicing basic techniques with a partner. Kids will drill positions in a controlled, safe setting, and finish with interactive games that reinforce Jiu-Jitsu movements.

 ## FAQ

 Your Questions Answered

 ### Is Jiu Jitsu Safe for Kids? Yes! Jiu-Jitsu is designed to be a controlled , technique-based martial art. At Easton, we ensure a safe training environment with expert instructors, structured classes, and age-appropriate instruction. Kids progress at their own pace and always train under supervision.

 ### Does my child have to compete? Not at all! Competition is optional at Easton. While we encourage kids to challenge themselves, Jiu-Jitsu is about learning and growing at their own pace. For those interested, our Easton Open Tournament provides a great first competition experience in a friendly, supportive environment.

 ### What makes Easton's Kids Jiu-Jitsu program different? At Easton, we’ve been teaching Jiu-Jitsu since 1998 and have built one of the largest and most welcoming martial arts communities in the country. Our program emphasizes F.R.E.D. (Focus, Respect, Energy, and Discipline) to ensure kids develop skills on and off the mats .

 ### What should my child wear to their first Jiu-Jitsu class? We’ll provide a loaner Gi (uniform) for your child’s first class! Just have them wear comfortable athletic clothing (like a t-shirt and shorts under the Gi). After enrolling, you can purchase a Gi at the academy.

 ### Does Easton offer Family Jiu-Jitsu classes? Yes! Select locations host Family Jiu-Jitsu classes where parents and kids can train together. We also offer special family-friendly events throughout the year. Check our schedule page for upcoming sessions!

 ### Are there punches and kicks in Jiu-Jitsu? No, Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu is a grappling-based martial art that focuses on control, leverage, and technique to neutralize opponents. Unlike striking arts like Kickboxing or Karate, Jiu-Jitsu teaches takedowns, positional control, and submissions—without punches or kicks.
