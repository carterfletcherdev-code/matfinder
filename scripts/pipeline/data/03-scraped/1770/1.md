## Brazilian jiu jitsu

 #### The Adult Brazilian Jiu Jitsu program welcomes students of any skill level. The classes provide participants with the motivation, exercise, and techniques to meet each of their fitness goals.

 We provide a welcoming and encouraging atmosphere for all those interested in developing their bodies and self-defense skills. Participants are taught various grappling and submission techniques mixed with striking. These will allow students to quickly gain advantage over opponents and neutralize them. By leveraging their body weight against an opponent and utilizing proper techniques students can be taught how to overcome larger and more powerful opponents.
 Head instructor Nestor Bayot will provide individual instruction and continually challenge the students and personally be there to support and help meet each student's goals. Nestor along with the class will encourage and motivate you to maintain your new fitness routine and love for Brazilian Jiu Jitsu.
