 # LET’S get to work.

 Gambit Submission Fighting is a combat sports training facility out of New Paltz, NY, serving Ulster, Dutchess, Orange, Putnam, and Westchester counties, including New Paltz, Poughkeepsie, Kingston, Beacon, Wappingers Falls, and the entire Hudson Valley.
 Our core focus is on Brazilian Jiu Jitsu (BJJ) and Mixed Martial Arts (MMA), and we also offer classes focusing on wrestling, judo, Muay Thai/Kickboxing, or boxing, as well as classes designed for women or children.
