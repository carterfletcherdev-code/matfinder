### Our Programs Carpio Jiu Jitsu offers programs designed for all ages and levels.

 #### Kids/Youth Program Our kids program focuses on introducing the child to the art and sport of jiu jitsu. Students will develop motor and learning skills that will help them succeed on and off the mats. Jiu jitsu is a positive outlet for children and youth to express themselves through physical activity.

 #### All Levels Program Our basics program will provide a general understanding of the different areas of jiu jitsu and all that it entails. Students will learn different goals that are set forth for them in every aspect of jiu jitsu. The foundation of excellence is built on fundamentals!

 #### Advanced Program Our advanced program is created to evolve the student’s learning process. Instruction will focus on multiple techniques and variations that follow when you train with more experienced opponents. The student will develop a clear path to implement in their jiu jitsu.
