 # Getting Started

 ### Your first visit to a boxing gym may feel intimidating, however at Gorilla Combat, we strive to provide a safe and non-aggressive atmosphere for everyone to learn and train!

 What to Bring | First Class | How to Wrap Your Hands

 ### Recommended Classes to Start With

 ### What to Bring

 While all of our classes are foc used on the fundamentals, and you may start with any class, here are the classes we recommend starting with:
 Muay-Thai : Monday & Wednesdays Level 1-2 @5:30pm
 Muay-Thai : Muay Thai: Saturday at 11am, All levels
 Boxing : Tuesday & Thursday at 5:30pm & Saturdays 10am
 Brazilian Jiu-jitsu : Tues (no-gi) & Thur (gi) 6:45pm

 All you need to bring is comfortable workout attire and a desire to learn!
 Please arrive 10-15 minutes before your first class and we’ll get you set up!
 We will lend you a pair of gloves for your first couple visits, however we encourage you to purchase your own after attending a few classes. Professional hand wraps, ankle wraps, boxing gloves and other equipment may be purchased on-site or through our online store.
