** Now offering kids classes 6 days per week! **

 The Northwest Jiu-Jitsu Academy is one of the oldest and largest Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu schools in the Seattle area, located in Seattle, Washington near Green Lake.
 Owner and chief instructor Brian Johnson is 3-time world champion and 5th-degree black belt, awarded to him by the legendary Rigan Machado and endorsed by the Machado Brothers.
 Northwest Jiu-Jitsu Academy offers kid , teen, and adult Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu classes, in addition to women's classes, circuit training, and competition training.
