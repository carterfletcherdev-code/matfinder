 Questions & Answers

 ## Frequently Asked Questions We would love to answer your questions, some of them are here so that you may quickly go through them. If you have any other queries contact us .

 What classes do you offer?
 We offer a variety of classes for all fitness levels, including:
 - Brazilian Jiu Jitsu (BJJ) – Beginner, Intermediate, and Advanced levels - Kickboxing – Focus on striking, conditioning, and self-defense - Fitness Classes – High-intensity training, conditioning, and strength-building sessions

 Do I need any prior experience to join?
 Not at all! We welcome beginners. Our classes are designed to cater to all skill levels, from those who have never stepped on the mats to seasoned fighters. Our instructors will guide you through the fundamentals and help you improve at your own pace.

 What should I wear to class?
 For BJJ , you’ll need a gi (kimono) or rash guard and shorts for no-gi classes. For Kickboxing , we recommend wearing comfortable workout clothes and boxing gloves. No zippers, jewelry or shoes are permitted during training. If you’re new, we can help you get set up with any gear you might need.

 Do you offer classes for kids?
 Yes! We have specialized classes for children, teaching them Jiu Jitsu, kickboxing, and general fitness. These classes focus on building confidence, discipline, and respect in a fun and safe environment.

 What are your membership options?
 We offer flexible membership options, including:
 - Monthly memberships with no long-term commitment - Family discounts for multiple family members - Drop-in rates for occasional attendees

 Do you provide private lessons?
 Yes, we offer private lessons for Jiu Jitsu, Kickboxing, and general fitness. These sessions are tailored to your individual needs and goals. Please contact us to arrange a private lesson.

 Is there parking available?
 There is public parking behind the dominos on main street and on Washington Street. You are allowed to park at the post office when they are closed (after 5pm)
