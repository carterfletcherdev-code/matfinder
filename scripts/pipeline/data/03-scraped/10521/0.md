 ### Welcome to the website of Moo Sul Kwan, a Tae Kwon Do, Hapkido, and Judo Martial Arts school with locations in Missouri and Florida.
 # Welcome to Moo Sul Kwan The Moo Sul Kwan Martial Arts Institute has been the leader in martial arts, self-defense and fitness education since 1969, with specialized programs designed for every age group in Tae Kwon Do, Hapkido and Judo.
 Founded by the late Great Grandmaster Lee H. Park at Southeast Missouri State University in Cape Girardeau, Missouri, today the Moo Sul Kwan Martial Arts Institutes have spread to cities all across the nation and are overseen by the American Martial Arts Sports and Education Association with Grandmaster Charles Hildebrand as current president.
