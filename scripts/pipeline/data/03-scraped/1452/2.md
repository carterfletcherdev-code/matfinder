 OUR STORY
 ## THE JIU LIVRE LEGACY ### Professor Jarbem Pacheco Welcome to Jiu Livre Grappling Office! We take immense pride in presenting our exceptional instructor, Jarbem Pacheco. With an impressive resume, Jarbem is a black belt in Brazilian Jiu Jitsu under the renowned Paulo Miyao, and a black belt in Luta Livre under Nicolas Renier.
 His accomplishments speak volumes: Pan American Champion in Gi and No Gi, Masters World's Medalist in weight and Open Class, and multiple-time IBJJF Open Champion. Jarbem's expertise extends to the prestigious UFC, where he has served as a seasoned corner.
 With over two decades of grappling experience, Jarbem brings unparalleled knowledge and skill to our academy. Join us to learn from the best!
