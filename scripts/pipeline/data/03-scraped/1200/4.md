 ## Frequently Asked Questions

 Do I need any experience to start?
 No. Our fundamentals program is made for complete beginners. You will learn everything from the ground up.

 What should I wear to class?
 Wear comfortable athletic clothing. For your free intro class, we will provide a clean loaner uniform if needed.

 Is Jiu-Jitsu safe for beginners?
 Yes. Safety is our top priority. We teach proper techniques and ensure that all training is done with control and respect.

 Can my child train too?
 Absolutely. Our kids program teaches discipline, confidence, and teamwork in a fun and positive environment.

 Do you offer No-Gi classes?
 Yes, we have numerous weekly No-Gi classes that focus on grip control, speed, and modern grappling applications.

 How often should I train?
 Two to three classes per week is a great start for beginners. Consistency is more important than intensity in the beginning.
