 ## What We Teach

 Our Youth BJJ Program is designed for children ages 5 to 13. Classes are grouped by age and experience so that every student feels supported and challenged.
 Each class includes:

 - Warm-ups that build coordination, balance, and agility - Drills that teach the fundamentals of Jiu-Jitsu - Safe, playful training games that build reaction and awareness - Partner work that teaches cooperation and communication - Lessons about respect, responsibility, and sportsmanship

 Children learn that effort and focus lead to real progress. Over time, they gain confidence not just in class but also at school and home.

 ## Frequently Asked Questions

 Do kids need any experience to start?
 No. Our kids classes are perfect for beginners. We teach every student from the ground up.

 Is Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu safe for children?
 Yes. Safety and respect are our top priorities. All training is supervised closely and taught at a controlled pace.

 What should my child wear?
 Comfortable athletic clothing is fine for the first class. We provide a loaner uniform if needed.

 How often should my child train?
 Two to three times per week is ideal. Consistency helps kids build skills and confidence faster.

 Will my child learn self-defense?
 Yes. Jiu-Jitsu teaches leverage and control, giving smaller individuals the tools to protect themselves effectively.

 Do you offer competition opportunities?
 Yes, for those who are interested. Competition is completely optional and never required.
