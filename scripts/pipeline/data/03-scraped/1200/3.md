 ## What You Will Learn

 The Fundamentals program focuses on clear, essential skills that every new student should understand.
 Each class includes:

 - A warmup that builds mobility, balance, and Jiu-Jitsu specific movements - Step-by-step instruction on basic positions such as guard, mount, side control, and back control - Beginner-friendly techniques including escapes, sweeps, transitions, and simple submissions - Partner drills that develop timing, coordination, and confidence - Controlled positional training that allows you to practice safely with resistance

 We typically focus on one theme for several weeks so that everyone has enough time to understand, practice, and retain the material. This creates steady progress without feeling rushed or overwhelmed.

 ### Frequently Asked Questions

 Do I need to be in good shape to start?
 No. Jiu-Jitsu improves your fitness over time. Many students start with little or no athletic background.

 What should I wear?
 Wear comfortable athletic clothing for your first class. We provide a loaner uniform if needed.

 Is the class safe for beginners?
 Yes. This class is specifically designed for first time students. All instruction is controlled and supervised.

 Will I have to spar on my first day?
 Not unless you feel ready. Beginners focus on technique and controlled positional training. Full sparring is optional until you feel comfortable.

 How fast will I improve?
 Progress depends on consistency. Most students notice better coordination and confidence within the first few weeks.

 Can I train if I have past injuries?
 In many cases yes. Let the instructor know before class so techniques can be adjusted for your comfort and safety.

 How often should I attend?
 Two to three classes per week is ideal for steady improvement, but even once a week builds progress over time.
