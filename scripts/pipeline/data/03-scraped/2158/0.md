 ## BRAZILIAN JIU JITSU

 At Aspire Combat Sports Academy Liverpool we offer a complete, structured system of Brazillian Jiu-Jitsu (BJJ) delivered by fully qualified coaches and instructors in a positive and safe environment.
 As one of the fastest-growing sports in the UK, Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu or BJJ is regarded as one of the most effective martial arts in the world. Made famous by the Gracie family, Brazillian Jiu-Jitsu is known as the ‘Gentle Art’, where proper technique and precision is valued over brute strength, aggression and size advantage. This makes BJJ a great choice no matter your height, weight, sex or age. So if you’re looking to learn how to defend yourself, get fit or even just learn something new, Aspire Combat Sports Academy is can provide an amazing introduction to BJJ in Liverpool.
