Takedowns for Jiu Jitsu

 BLOGS AND NEWS | August 26, 2025

 Takedowns are an essential part of Brazilian Jiu Jitsu.
 Good takedowns will give you an advantage in the match, allow you to stay ahead of the game.
 When you are in the later stages of a match and need to get that extra point off your opponent, a takedown can be game-changing.
 More than the ability to take the opponent down, it gives you the benefit of moving to a dominant position and finishing with a submission.
