Life is full of unpredictable people. Some cross the line, and when they do, you will be glad you know how to defend yourself.
 Start with Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu . Sure, there are Boxing , Krav Maga and MMA . They are all great and they will all teach you how to get fit and tough. But they all rely a LOT on physicality, age and size . The stronger, younger and bigger you are, the more they work in your favor.
 BJJ is different. It is built on technique, timing and control, not brute force .
 You already have all you need to make it work. Tall or short, fast or slow, whether you are 8 or 58, it does not matter. BJJ is a martial art that truly adapts to you and teaches you how to use what you’ve got. Your “weaknesses” can become your greatest source of strength. You will get in better shape in the process, and you will also learn to be proud of who you are. You start feeling comfortable in your own skin.
