# Programs
 Jiu-Jitsu is a hot topic in the world of martial arts. If you’ve ever practiced it, you probably understand the hype! Jiu-Jitsu provides athletes with benefits that go far beyond the gym, such as increased physical fitness, weight loss, higher energy, and better productivity. But there’s one life skill that really sets Jiu-Jitsu apart from other forms of martial arts – it's self defense training. Self-defense is a skill that every person should have under their belt and, lucky for you, Jiu-Jitsu is one of the best types of self-defense that money can buy. Read about all of the awesome programs we offer!

 ## Kids Martial Arts
 When it comes to self-defense, Jiu-Jitsu empowers a smaller individual to fight against a larger opponent. But the advantages don’t stop there. Jiu-Jitsu helps kids gain confidence, learn discipline, focus their energy, foster respect for others, and be a part of a one-of-a-kind community full of future friends.

 ## Adult Martial Arts
 Made for the real world, Jiu-Jitsu is a form of self-defense that every person should master. Jiu-Jitsu focuses on ground-level altercations – utilizing holds, throws, blows, and a variety of submission techniques.
