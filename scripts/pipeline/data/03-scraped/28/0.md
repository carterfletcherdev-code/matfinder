 # About Garra Kokomo
 Garra Jiu-Jitsu & MMA Academy is a premier martial arts gym offering gi & no gi BJJ, wrestling, kickboxing, youth classes and mma. We have two large mat spaces, a full cage, weights, women’s and men’s locker rooms. Garra is a place for everyone to accomplish their goals - professional and amateur fighting, physical fitness , camaraderie, competitive BJJ, protection for yourself and family.
