 ## 🧠 What Is Brazilian Jiu Jitsu?
 Brazilian Jiu Jitsu (BJJ) is a grappling-based martial art that focuses on:

 - controlling opponents through technique and leverage - submissions such as chokes and joint locks - ground-based self-defense scenarios

 Unlike striking-based martial arts, BJJ teaches you how to stay calm under pressure and control larger, stronger opponents using skill instead of strength.

 This is one reason it has become one of the fastest-growing martial arts in the United States.

 ## 👊 What to Expect in Your First Class
 Many beginners searching “jiu jitsu near me” are nervous about starting. That’s normal.

 A typical beginner class includes:

 - a warm-up focused on movement and coordination - step-by-step technique instruction - partner drilling (controlled practice) - optional light sparring for more advanced students

 You do NOT need:

 - prior experience - to be in shape - special athletic ability

 Most students start completely new and improve quickly within just a few weeks.
