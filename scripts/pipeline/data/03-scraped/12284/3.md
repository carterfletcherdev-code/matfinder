 ## Seo’s Martial Arts Academy Makes History at 2025 Kukkiwon Hanmadang! ARVADA, CO – July 31, 2025
 Seo’s Martial Arts Academy proudly traveled to Anaheim, California , to compete in the 2 025 World Taekwondo Kukkiwon Hanmadang , held from July 17–19 . This event marked only the second time in Kukkiwon history that the prestigious Hanmadang tournament was hosted in the United States.
 Under the direct coaching of Grandmaster J. Seo , a dedicated team of 23 competitors represented the academy. The team ranged in age from 10 to 56 years old and in rank from Brown Belt to 4th Degree Black Belt , showcasing the depth and diversity of Seo’s martial arts community.
 The team competed in multiple categories with outstanding results :
 - Individual Power Breaking: 23 medals - Individual Creative Forms: 23 medals - Demonstration Teams: All three teams, each composed of four competitors, medaled in their divisions
 The highlight of the event was the Black Belt All-Ages Form Team - which claimed the championship title with a high-flying, acrobatic performance that combined synchronized techniques with power and precision.
 At the conclusion of the tournament, Seo’s Martial Arts Academy was r ecognized with a Kukkiwon record— the team with the most wins in the history of the World Hanmadang competition . This historic achievement was made possible by the unwavering leadership of Grandmaster Seo and the tireless support of the academy’s Master Instructors and community.
