 ## What Sets Cho’s Martial Arts in Menomonee Falls Apart?
 Click each point below to read more

 At Cho’s Martial Arts, students experience a unique approach to Taekwondo rooted in the authentic Jidokwan tradition-offered exclusively in Menomonee Falls. The focus goes far beyond traditional forms, emphasizing practical, real-world self-defense skills. Training covers all vital targets-head, legs, and body-and incorporates a full arsenal of techniques, including elbows, knees, punches, and kicks. This ensures students are fully equipped to protect themselves in any situation.
 Unlike many schools that limit head contact or spend most of their time on forms (kata, poomsae), Cho’s prioritizes realistic sparring and situational defense. Students regularly practice defending all areas of the body under pressure, so they’re less likely to freeze or panic if ever struck in the face-an essential skill for real-world encounters.

 • Comprehensive, Realistic Self-Defense Training

 Classes at Cho’s are led by a team of highly experienced instructors, including former champions and seasoned black belts with decades of teaching expertise. Instruction is always hands-on and personal-students receive direct guidance and mentorship from the school’s most accomplished teachers.

 • Expert, Hands-On Instruction

 Cho’s Martial Arts is built on a foundation of respect, discipline, and personal development. The environment is both supportive and challenging, fostering confidence and character in every student. Parents and students frequently praise the positive, inclusive atmosphere where everyone is encouraged to reach their full potential.

 • A Culture of Respect and Growth
