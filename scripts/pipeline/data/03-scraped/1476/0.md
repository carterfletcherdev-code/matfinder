 ### etiquette Jiu Jitsu is a very close contact martial art. These rules are in place to provide the best possible training experience for everyone.
 - Arrive to class clean and odor-free. Shower before class if needed. - Always wear your sandals off the mat. - Always wear a freshly washed Gi or No-Gi uniform. - Bare feet only on the mat. No wrestling shoes. - Finger and toe nails must be neatly trimmed. - Do not train if you have a rash, skin infection, fungus or are sick! - Respect the instructor(s) authority and trust their guidance in regards to safety.
