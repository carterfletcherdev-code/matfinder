 ## What to Expect from Beginner Karate Classes When you join our beginner karate classes, you’ll start with the fundamentals:
 - Stances: Building balance and body control - Strikes: Learning basic punches, kicks, and elbow strikes - Blocks: How to defend yourself safely - Footwork: Moving with purpose and precision - Forms (Kata): Step-by-step patterns that teach coordination and focus
 Each class is structured to help beginners gain confidence quickly — no prior experience required.
