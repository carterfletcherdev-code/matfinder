# Kids Jiu-Jitsu The Gracie Technics kids program teaches concepts and ideas of Jiu-Jitsu in a fun and enjoyable environment. We are dedicated to providing the highest level of instruction in a manner that is easy for your child to understand.
 The class will focus on fundamental techniques that will improve coordination, flexibility and motor skills. Our instructors will encourage your child to build confidence and knowledge with unique challenges both physically and mentally.
