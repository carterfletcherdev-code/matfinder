# Fundamental Jiu-Jitsu The Fundamental class is tailored for the beginning Jiu-Jitsu practitioner. We will focus on the essential Jiu-Jitsu techniques and concepts that will build a solid self-defense and Jiu-Jitsu foundation.
 ## White to Blue Curriclulum This class is focused on the Pedro Sauer Jiu-Jitsu White to Blue Belt Curriculum. The Gracie Technics Fundamental Class is built specifically for new practitioners. The Fundamentals program will take you to the next level in a systematic and proven curriculum. We will outline exactly what is needed to progress from White to Blue Belt in our academy.
 Although this class may be geared for beginners, students with higher rank also attend these classes to review the Fundamental curriculum. This allows students to improve on basic body mechanics and movement.
 **Uniform (Gi) is required**
