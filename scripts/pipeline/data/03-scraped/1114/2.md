# Senzo Training and Tactics Group – Program Overview Senzo Training & Tactics Group provides practical, high-stress defensive tactics and threat-response training grounded in real-world experience. Led by retired Sergeant and 4th-degree BJJ black belt Nate Burnette, our courses prepare law enforcement, security teams, organizations, corporate groups, and civilians to respond decisively to violence and high-risk situations.
 Training includes threat assessment, active-shooter response and defense, scenario-based decision-making, and foundational medical care for critical incidents. All instruction is fully scalable and customizable to any skill level or organizational requirement.
 Participants develop confidence, awareness, and proven skills they can immediately apply — whether on the street, in the workplace, or at home.

 ##### Bio – Nate Burnette
 Nate Burnette is a retired Sergeant from the Sacramento County Sheriff’s Office with over 20 years of experience in law enforcement, including SWAT operations, major crimes, fugitive apprehension, and high-risk tactical deployments. He is a decorated officer, awarded three Silver Stars and a Bronze Star for bravery and leadership in the line of duty.
 Recognized as a use-of-force expert and certified Force Science analyst, Nate has developed and delivered thousands of hours of training in firearms, defensive tactics, tactical decision-making, de-escalation, and threat assessment. His real-world, scenario-driven instruction has been provided to all levels of expertise across local, state, and federal agencies.
 In addition to his law enforcement background, Nate is a 4th-degree black belt in Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu with over 25 years of training experience. He developed and oversaw the defensive tactics program for his agency, integrating control-based techniques and martial arts fundamentals into practical, high-pressure environments.
 Nate is the founder of Senzo Training & Tactics Group, providing advanced instruction for law enforcement, school safety teams, church security ministries, and corporate protection staff. He is also the owner and head instructor of Senzo Jiu-Jitsu Academy in Greenville, South Carolina.
 His mission is to prepare individuals and organizations with the skills, mindset, and tactical readiness to respond effectively in today’s complex security landscape-while upholding the highest standards of safety, integrity, and
 professionalism.
