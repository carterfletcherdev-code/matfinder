# Kids Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu Our program goes far beyond just teaching techniques—it’s a life-shaping experience. In every class, students train in a positive, high-energy environment where they are encouraged to grow in confidence, respect, and perseverance. The lessons they learn on the mats carry into everyday life, helping them at home, in school, and in other activities.

 #### Kids BJJ ### Ages 6–10
 ##### Child Development This program is focused on supporting healthy development. Through structured training, kids improve focus, concentration, and discipline while staying active and building lifelong healthy habits. Jiu-Jitsu is an incredible way to help children prepare for future challenges both physically and mentally.

 ##### High-Energy Task-Based Games We know kids learn best when they’re having fun. That’s why our classes include engaging, task-based games that reinforce BJJ fundamentals while inspiring teamwork, friendly competition, and camaraderie. By blending structured learning with play, students work hard without even realizing it—and love every minute of it.

 ##### Technical Improvement At this stage, kids are still mastering the basics of Jiu-Jitsu while developing mentally, physically, and emotionally. Our program focuses on building a strong foundation in Gi Jiu-Jitsu, giving students the tools to begin their journey toward self-defense and, if they choose, competitive BJJ.

 #### Kids BJJ ### Ages 10–15
 ##### Larger Concepts As students mature, we introduce them to broader concepts in Jiu-Jitsu. Training at this stage includes both Gi and No-Gi instruction, giving students a deeper understanding of the art while allowing them to explore their full potential.

 ##### Competitive Strategies For those interested in testing themselves in competition, this is where the journey begins. We teach the key differences between competitive and self-defense Jiu-Jitsu, including point systems, tournament strategies, and advanced techniques. Our goal is to help students step into competition with confidence, ready to face any challenge.

 ##### Gi and No-Gi Training This program also serves as the first introduction to No-Gi Jiu-Jitsu at Senzo. Students learn grappling skills that complement and enhance their Gi training, creating a well-rounded skill set that applies across all aspects of Jiu-Jitsu.
