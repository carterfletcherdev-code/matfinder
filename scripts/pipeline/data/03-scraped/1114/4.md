 #### Our School At Senzo Jiu-Jitsu Academy, we believe Jiu-Jitsu is for everyone. Our mission is to serve each student on their journey — whether that means improving fitness, mastering techniques, or testing themselves in competition. With a carefully designed curriculum, we provide clear structure and proven results for anyone willing to put in the effort on the mats.
 But Senzo Jiu-Jitsu is more than just training. We’ve built a supportive, tight-knit community where students encourage one another and grow together. The name “Senzo” means ancestry, and we embrace that spirit by creating a culture that honors respect, connection, and long-term growth. From regular classes to special events, we foster bonds that extend well beyond the academy.
 Whether you’re from Greenville, Traveler’s Rest, Taylors, Easley, Five Forks, Simpsonville, or anywhere in the Upstate, you’ll find a welcoming home at Senzo Jiu-Jitsu Academy — a place to challenge yourself, build confidence, and become part of something lasting.

 #### History Since opening in September 2024, Senzo Jiu-Jitsu Academy has quickly grown into a home for Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu in Greenville and the surrounding communities of Traveler’s Rest, Taylors, Easley, Five Forks, and Simpsonville. From day one, our vision has been simple — to create a welcoming environment where anyone can step onto the mats and feel supported in their Jiu-Jitsu journey.
 That commitment has fueled rapid growth. In less than a year, we’ve expanded our kids’ program twice and added both Gi and No-Gi classes for children and adults. These milestones reflect not only our passion for Jiu-Jitsu but also the trust our students and families place in us.
 The name Senzo means “ancestry,” and it captures the heart of our philosophy. We honor the Japanese roots of Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu while carrying forward the lineage of our owner and head instructor, Professor Nate Burnette, a fourth-degree black belt.
 Before founding Senzo, Professor Nate served more than 20 years in law enforcement and spent over a decade as the head instructor of Synergy Jiu-Jitsu in California. Throughout his career, he trained thousands of students and officers in Jiu-Jitsu, defensive tactics, and firearms. That blend of martial arts mastery and real-world experience now shapes Senzo Jiu-Jitsu into more than a school — it’s a place where students of all ages and backgrounds can learn, grow, and reach their highest potential.
