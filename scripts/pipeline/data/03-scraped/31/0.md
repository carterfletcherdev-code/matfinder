### KIDS Brazilian Jiu Jitsu (BJJ) At Driftless Academy, the training that your child receives will remain a part of their life! Brazilian Jiu Jitsu helps increase confidence and discipline as they encounter new challenges in a fun safe environment. Facing these challenges will give children a head start in life, helping them be ready to deal with tough situations such as bullying and peer pressure.
 Kids who enroll in martial arts like Brazilian Jiu Jitsu tend to have higher self-esteem, better grades, learn to take personal responsibility, and develop goal setting skills. All of these benefits are taught through practice, participation, and most importantly through fun.
