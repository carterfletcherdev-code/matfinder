 # Fundamentals: Build Your Foundation ##### Our fundamentals class is the perfect starting point for anyone new to Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu. In this class, students learn the core concepts of BJJ, including body positioning, leverage, escapes, submissions, and transitions. You’ll work through essential techniques in a step-by-step format with plenty of time for questions and hands-on guidance. We emphasize situational drilling to help students understand how techniques apply in real-world scenarios. This class also introduces students to the self-defense aspect of Jiu-Jitsu, helping build not just physical skill, but also mental confidence and composure. If you’re new to grappling or returning after time off the mats, our fundamentals class offers the structure and support you need to succeed # Advanced Gi: Sharpen Strategy and Technique ##### The advanced gi class is designed for experienced practitioners looking to refine their technique and elevate their performance. This class moves at a faster pace and focuses on complex positional work, submissions, guard retention, and strategic transitions. Students are encouraged to approach the game with intention—learning how to think several steps ahead during both drilling and live sparring. We emphasize specific training situations and controlled sparring to help students develop timing, precision, and match awareness. Whether you’re preparing for competition or looking to improve your overall game, the advanced gi class delivers high-level instruction and a focused training environment. # No-Gi Grappling: Fast, Technical, Wrestling-Integrate ##### Our no-gi class blends traditional Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu with wrestling fundamentals to create a faster-paced and dynamic style of grappling. Without the grips and fabric of the gi, students rely more heavily on underhooks, body control, and real-time reactions. This approach is excellent for improving agility, cardio, and submission speed. In no-gi, students learn takedowns, clinch control, positional strategy, and submissions using body mechanics and timing. Whether you’re interested in MMA, submission grappling, or simply expanding your skills, this class offers valuable tools and a fresh perspective on grappling without the uniform. No-gi training is open to all levels and complements our gi program as part of a complete approach to Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu.
