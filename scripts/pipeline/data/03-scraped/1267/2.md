 ## GYM RULES

 ##### CLASSES ARE OFFERED FOR EVERY LEVEL

 Welcome all guest and visitors by introducing yourself.
 - Treat newcomers as you would like to be treated. - Rashguard is required for Gi and no Gi classes ( Kids and Adults) - Refrain from chatting while the instructor is talking - No gum chewing during class or on the mats - Tap EARLY tap OFTEN - Its OK to say NO - Respect the Mats - No shoes on the mats - White, blue or Black Gi – Alliance Gi - Blacks spats/long shorts in no gi class - Black rashguard or mainly Black. Please ensure your rashguard does not contain any inappropriate language - Keep fingers and toenails short and trimmed - Respect your partners and professor - Bow to enter and exit the mats - Greet teammates upon entering the mat. Shake everyone hands upon entering mat - Only apply as much force as needed. never intentionally harm anyone
 have fun and enjoy your times on the mats
