 but a community.
 Even though Brazilian jiu jitsu is an individual martial art you cannot accomplish your goals alone. We have a saying 'one team one dream' which indicates the meaning of Tawheed
 unification of one .

 Jiu Jitsu has long been a back bone in self defense and MMA as it has been proven in real world situations and combat.
 We can't wait for you to grow with us!
