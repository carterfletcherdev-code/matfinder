 ## FLEXIBLE TRAINING OPTIONS

 Our BJJ academy offers a wide range of classes designed to accommodate different skill levels and schedules. From introductory sessions to advanced training, we provide flexible options to help you achieve your grappling and fitness goals. Whether you're just starting or looking to refine your skills, there's a class for everyone.

 Intro to BJJ Class

 Discover the basics of Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu in our Intro to BJJ class. This 30-minute session covers essential techniques, gym etiquette, and what to expect in training. Whether you're looking to get fit, learn self-defense, or find a new hobby, our supportive community is here to welcome you. No experience necessary—just come ready to learn and have fun!

 BJJ Fundamentals Class

 Join our BJJ Fundamentals class, designed for all levels looking to deepen their skills. This one-hour session covers essential techniques and concepts, perfect for beginners or those new to BJJ. Learn in a supportive and engaging environment, with a focus on practical skills and physical fitness.

 Adult GI & No GI BJJ Class

 Explore our Adult BJJ class, featuring both GI and No GI sessions for those with some experience in Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu. These one-hour classes are ideal for practitioners aiming to refine skills, deepen technique understanding, and engage in live sparring. Our diverse community embraces those passionate about martial arts, fitness, and personal growth. Whether you're training for competition, self-defense, or simply to enhance your BJJ journey, our experienced instructors are here to support you every step of the way.

 FREE Womens BJJ Class

 Every Saturday, our free Ladies BJJ class offers a supportive and empowering environment exclusively for women. This one-hour session is perfect for those looking to learn Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, build confidence, and connect with like-minded women. Whether you're new to BJJ or enhancing your skills, this class provides a welcoming space to explore self-defense, fitness, and personal growth. Led by our experienced female instructor, you'll find a community that encourages and inspires. Experience the empowering benefits of BJJ with us.

 BJJ Community Open Mat:

 Our Community Open Mat is a welcoming space for practitioners of all skill levels to come together and train. Held every Saturday, this two-hour session invites BJJ enthusiasts from all backgrounds and schools to cross-train, share techniques, and build connections. Whether you're a beginner or an advanced practitioner, our open mat is a great opportunity to refine your skills in a friendly and inclusive environment. We encourage visitors from other schools to join us and experience the camaraderie and community spirit of BJJ. Come roll with us and expand your horizons!

 Kids Class

 Our Jiu-Jitsu Kids Class, designed for ages 6-15, offers a dynamic and fun environment for young martial artists. This one-hour session, led by our dedicated Kids Coach, focuses on building foundational Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu skills, including basic techniques, self-defense, and discipline. Perfect for both beginners and those with some experience, the class promotes physical fitness, confidence, and teamwork. Your child will learn valuable life skills while staying active and making friends. Join us to give your child a strong start in their BJJ journey in a supportive community!
