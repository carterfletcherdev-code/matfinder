 # Open Mat

 Join us Sunday mornings for jiujitsu church, a melting pot of jiujitsu disciplines from our expanding community, where open arms and arm bars wait to welcome you. With our philosophy of training with everyone and training for life our open mat provides a positive place for all those looking to learn, roll, laugh, and sweat no matter their level.
