 ### At ALLIANCE JIU JITSU SOUTH BAY, we’re here to help you reach your fitness goals through Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu. More than self-defense, our classes build confidence, a positive mindset, and life skills for men, women, and kids as young as three Years old.

 # Our PROGRAMS

 ### ADULT BJJ CLASS

 Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu teaches practical self-defense through technique and control, helping students grow physically and mentally—from fundamentals to advanced levels—while building confidence, discipline, and life skills on and off the mat.

 ### KIDS BJJ Class 7-15

 The children’s Jiu Jitsu program offers a safe, engaging space to build focus, confidence, and discipline. It supports physical, mental, and emotional growth—benefits that extend far beyond the mat.

 ### MINIs BJJ Class 3-6

 The program is designed for young children, focusing on motor development and the discovery of new physical and intellectual skills.

 #### We also offer private lessons, along with access to a fully equipped workout area featuring a lifting station, exercise equipment, airdyne bike, stationary bike, treadmill, and rowing machine.
