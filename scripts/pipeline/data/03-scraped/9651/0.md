 MMA Fight Team (7-16 years old) is composed of willing students who want a real challenge beyond baseball, swimming, and tennis. Taking martial arts training to the next level, students prepare for stepping into the ring with an intra-scholastic student (from within the Moore’s Company) to fight in a controlled environment with very conservative rules to ensure the safety of the student and yet bring them as close to the edge of real MMA fighting. This gives the students a real opportunity to develop their fighting skills, confidence, respect for self and others, sportsmanship, a sense of teamwork, and to channel/focus their energies positively.
