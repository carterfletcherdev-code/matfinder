 # Kids Striking

 ### Program Description

 At Ocean View Martial Arts, our Kids Striking classes are designed to help children build confidence, discipline, and athletic skills through structured martial arts training. We create a positive, high-energy environment where kids learn real striking fundamentals while developing respect, focus, and teamwork.

 Each class introduces age-appropriate techniques including stance, footwork, basic punches, and controlled kicking drills. Our coaches emphasize proper form, balance, and safe partner work so students build strong fundamentals from day one. Every session blends skill development with games and drills that keep kids engaged and excited to train.

 Beyond physical skills, our kids martial arts program helps students grow mentally and emotionally. Training improves listening skills, self-control, and perseverance—qualities that carry over into school, sports, and everyday life. As they progress, students gain confidence by earning milestones and seeing their hard work pay off.\n\nIf you’re looking for kids martial arts classes in Norfolk that are fun, structured, and focused on character development, Kids Striking at Ocean View Martial Arts is the perfect fit. Schedule a trial class today and give your child a place to grow stronger, more confident, and more focused.

 ##### Fundamentals First

 Kids learn proper stance, footwork, punches, and kicks with a strong emphasis on technique and safety. Building fundamentals early sets the stage for long-term success in martial arts.

 ##### Confidence Through Progress

 Our structured curriculum allows kids to see measurable improvement over time. Earning new skills and recognition boosts self-esteem and keeps them motivated.

 ##### Focus & Discipline

 Classes are designed to improve listening skills, respect, and self-control. Students practice following instructions and working with partners in a positive setting.

 ##### Fun, High-Energy Environment

 We combine skill-building drills with engaging activities to keep kids excited about training. Every class balances learning and fun to create an experience they look forward to.
