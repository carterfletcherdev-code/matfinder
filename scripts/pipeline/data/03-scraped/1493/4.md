 ## Why Train With Ocean View Martial Arts?

 Ocean View Martial Arts is a family-friendly martial arts academy in Norfolk helping kids, teens, and adults ages 6 and up build confidence, discipline, and real-world self-defense skills. Through expert-led training in Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, Muay Thai kickboxing, wrestling, sambo, and boxing, we offer structured classes for both youth and adults in a welcoming, supportive community. Family packages are available.
