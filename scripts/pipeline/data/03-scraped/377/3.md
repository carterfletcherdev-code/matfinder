# Private Lessons

 Unlock faster progress with private and small group training at DOJO Seal Beach. Whether you're a beginner or an advanced student, our world-class instructors offer individualized attention to help you reach your Jiu Jitsu goals.
 Private lessons are available to students of all ages and belt levels—both academy members and visitors. Sessions are tailored to your needs, whether you're preparing for competition, refining fundamentals, or focusing on specific techniques.
 You can train one-on-one or bring a friend or two for a small group (up to 3 students), maintaining a focused and personalized learning experience.
 Available Instructors:
 - Professor Jorge – Expert instruction with a sharp eye for detail and strong fundamentals. - Professor Victor – Dynamic, competition-tested techniques with high-level strategy. - Coach Rylee – Outstanding with kids, teens, and beginners, creating a supportive and technical learning environment.
 Ready to book a session or learn more? Contact us or speak to our front desk next time you’re in.
