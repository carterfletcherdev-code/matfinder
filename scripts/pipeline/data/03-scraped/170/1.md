# Workshop NYC 2018 Training Camp - Oct 11th - Oct 14th August 09, 2018 / Larry Hope

 Registration for the 2018 Workshop NYC camp is open.
 https://www.workshopbjj.com/nyc-camp-2018/
 Workshop student rate is $250 for whole camp, or $85/day.
 -----------
 This camp will be the fourth Workshop Jiu Jitsu Camp, and the first held in New York City. The primary goal is to have the head instructors from Workshop NYC, Workshop Honolulu, Workshop PDX and their teams coming together for a week of comprehensive instruction and training. A this camp we are happy to have guest instructor Eddie Cummings in attendance. Friends from other teams are always welcome to come train with us.
