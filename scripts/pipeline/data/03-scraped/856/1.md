 ## Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu (BJJ) is excellent for health and self-defense due to its holistic approach to fitness and personal safety. The rigorous training involved improves cardiovascular health, strength, and flexibility, promoting overall physical well-being. By combining physical conditioning with practical self-defense skills, BJJ offers a comprehensive path to improved health and personal safety.
