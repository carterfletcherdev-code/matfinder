 # World Class Training # In Rutland, VT

 ## Rutland American Jiu Jitsu Academy Our coaches work hard to give every student the best training they can. Our instructors have many years of experience in jiu jitsu and mixed martial arts, and they love teaching kids and adults. They also teach at seminars, train with other gyms, and keep learning so they can bring the best skills back to our students.
 We believe jiu jitsu should be fun, safe, and easy to start at any age. Our coaches take a hands-on approach and help every student learn in a way that feels comfortable and exciting.
 #### At Rutland American Jiu Jitsu Academy, you will find: • Great instruction
 • A friendly and welcoming place to train
 • A learning-focused culture
 • A clean, well-kept gym
 • Coaches who want to help you become your best
 Whether you want to learn self-defense, get in shape, or one day become an MMA fighter, RAJJA is here to help you grow and succeed.

 ## BRAZILIAN JIU-JITSU Rutland American Jiu Jitsu Academy offers high-level Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu and striking classes for all ages, sizes, and genders. Our academy is open 6 days a week and is known for providing top-quality martial arts training in a friendly, supportive environment.
 Whether you’re a complete beginner, a casual hobbyist, or an experienced athlete, you’ll find world-class instruction and effective self-defense training here. Our team is dedicated to helping every student feel welcome, improve their skills, and reach their goals.

 ## ADULTS & TEENS (13+) BOXING & MMA STRIKING Our BOXING & MMA Striking program for adults and teens 13+ is a fast-paced, high-energy class built to sharpen your stand-up game. You’ll learn the essential tools of modern MMA striking—footwork, angles, defense, punches, kicks, knees, and elbows—while building real strength, balance, coordination, and confidence.
 At Rutland American Jiu Jitsu Academy , we train MMA striking in a safe, supportive environment where beginners and experienced athletes can grow together. Whether your goal is fitness, self-defense, or developing well-rounded skills for MMA, this class gives you the technique and confidence you need to level up.

 ### KIDS CLASSES AT RULTAND AMERICAN JIU-JITSU ACADEMY
 Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu is a fun, low-impact activity that helps kids build strength, focus, and confidence. By learning how to move their bodies and stay in control, children develop better coordination, discipline, and problem-solving skills—all while having a great time.
 At Rutland American Jiu Jitsu Academy, kids learn real self-defense in a safe, supportive environment. The lessons they learn on the mat—respect, hard work, and perseverance—are skills they carry with them for life.
