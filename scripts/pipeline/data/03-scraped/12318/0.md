 ## Frequently Asked Questions

 Most frequent questions and answers about our Martial Arts Programs

 Will my child become a bully?
 We find that our students have more confidence to stand up to bullies, and show more humility and kindness to others through their martial arts lessons.

 Are parents allowed to watch?
 We encourage parents to watch their children! We love parents getting involved! And so do their kids. Even better? Join one of our martial arts classes for adults! Your kids will love that you have something in common. It will build a strong family bond!
 Or, if you just want some me-time? Simply drop the kids off, and go grab a coffee. They will greet you with a huge smile when you return, because they had SO much fun!

 How fit do I need to be to start Martial Arts?
 We find that people start at varying fitness levels, and you will find that you will get fit by joining our Houston martial arts classes. It’s a fun workout that gets results!

 How long until I earn my Black Belt?
 The journey to Black Belt is an individual one. Everyone progresses at a different pace and there is no judgement on the length of your journey. What we can tell you is that it is SO WORTHWHILE!
 The self defense skills you will master can truly be life-saving! And the other benefits of striving for Black Belt, such as perseverance, never giving up, commitment, and self discipline lead to lifelong success.
 You will find that your instructors at Meyerland Martial Arts Houston are dedicated to helping you on your journey to Black Belt or whatever your personal goals may be.

 How do I claim your limited-time offer?
 The fastest way to take advantage of this great offer is to fill out the online form.
 Or, call us at 713.777.KICK! We’d love to tell you about what we do and see if our martial arts classes are a great fit for you and your family.

 What do I need to bring to my first Martial Arts lesson?
 Wear comfortable clothes you can move freely in!
 Bring a water bottle for after class — it’s a great workout!

 I'm not very athletic. Can I even do this?
 Many students starting in our adult martial arts classes would have described themselves like that before they started. Martial arts is great for all fitness levels!
 Give yourself some time, and you will be astounded at how much you improve! You’ll find that our very experienced martial arts instructors are trained to keep their teaching clear and easily understood. Come see for yourself how quickly you develop your own “ninja-like” skills!
