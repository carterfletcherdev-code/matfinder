 ## Temple Martial Arts & Fitness Train with purpose.
 Belong to a community.
 Become your strongest self.
 Welcome to Temple Martial Arts & Fitness, home of The Buddha Brigade.
 Delray Beach’s homegrown martial arts club. Offering training in Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu , No-Gi Submission Grappling , and Muay Thai. We have classes for all ages and experience levels. Our members come for the training but stay for the culture — a supportive, ego-free environment where everyone grows together.
 Beyond the mats, our Mixed Methods Fitness program blends strength training, conditioning, and mobility work to help you move better, feel better, and thrive inside and outside the gym. Whether you’re here to learn, de-stress, get in shape, or push your limits, you’ll find your place at Temple.

 ## Our Programs

 ## Brazilian Jiu Jitsu Brazilian Jiu Jitsu is a grappling-based martial art focused on leverage, technique, and strategy. Training in the traditional gi allows athletes to develop precise grips, positional control, and high-level submissions. Whether you’re a beginner learning fundamentals or an advanced student sharpening your game, BJJ builds confidence, discipline, and real self-defense skills.

 ## No-Gi Submission Grappling No-Gi Submission Grappling delivers fast-paced, dynamic grappling designed to improve athleticism, transitions, and submission skills without the use of the gi. Classes blend wrestling, jiu jitsu, and modern grappling systems to create a fluid, competition-ready style. Perfect for students looking to move quicker, think faster, and develop an adaptable ground game.

 ## Muay Thai Muay Thai, the “Art of Eight Limbs,” is a striking-based martial art that uses punches, kicks, knees, and elbows in a powerful, efficient system. Students learn proper technique, conditioning, footwork, and timing in a safe, structured environment. Whether you're training for fitness, self-defense, or competition, Muay Thai delivers a complete full-body workout and rock-solid striking skillset.

 ## HPO Fitness HPO Fitness is a Mixed Methods Approach to Fitness. Our performance-driven training system combines strength work, conditioning, mobility, and functional athletic movement. Each session is designed to build a stronger, more durable, and well-rounded body that supports both martial arts and everyday life. Whether you want to improve endurance, gain strength, or simply move and feel better, this program delivers balanced, sustainable results for every fitness level.

 ## Kids - MMA and Jiu Jitsu Temple’s Kids MMA & Jiu Jitsu program teaches practical self-defense, discipline, confidence, and athletic skills in a fun, safe, and structured environment. Young athletes learn foundational Jiu Jitsu, grappling, basic striking, and movement while developing focus, respect, and perseverance. Our goal is to help every child build real skills, healthy habits, and the confidence to succeed—on the mats, in school, and in life.
