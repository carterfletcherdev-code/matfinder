 ## Meet the Instructor

 Andrew Eidson started practicing Judo in 1992 as a junior high student who wanted to learn martial arts after watching The Karate Kid. His interest in takedowns and ground techniques led him to Brazilian Jiu Jitsu in 1999, where he trained under 6th-degree black belt Paulo Fernando Agusto. After earning his black belt in both arts and testing in São Paulo, Brazil, Andrew now holds a 3rd-degree black belt in BJJ. He spent years as Paulo’s assistant instructor at one of San Diego County’s largest academies before opening his school. Andrew’s teaching philosophy has evolved from creating competitors to supporting families and building community connections. He finds coaching kids particularly rewarding, watching their confidence and behavior transform through consistent training.
