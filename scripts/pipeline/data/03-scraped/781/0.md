 ## Adult Jiu-Jitsu
 # Adult BJJ Classes in Cary, NC Our adult Brazilian Jiu Jitsu (BJJ) program in Cary, NC goes beyond tradition. Led by expert instructor Jason Culbreth , with over 25 years of experience, our school focuses on real-world self-defense — then equips you with the skills to thrive in advanced sport BJJ and competition if you choose to go further.

 Try A Free Class Class Schedule

 ## Why Choose Our Adult BJJ Program? - Self-defense first: Learn how to defend yourself in real-world situations — not just sport-based drills. - Advanced ready: Once the fundamentals are in place, you’ll train with intensity, precision, and purpose to level up for competition or mastery. - Expert instruction: Jason Culbreth has over 25 years of coaching and Brazilian Jiu Jitsu experience, training students at every level. - Modern facility: Spring-loaded mats for injury prevention and showers for those training before or after work.
 ## Our Curriculum: From Fundamentals to Elite ### Fundamentals Program Our fundamentals program teaches everything you need to be effective in a real confrontation — control, escapes, positional dominance, submissions, and awareness. The curriculum is structured, progressive, and intentionally designed for fast skill development.
 ### Advanced Program Once your foundation is solid, our advanced adult BJJ classes prepare you for sport-level training, high-paced situational drills, and competition-level rolling. You’ll sharpen techniques, expand your game, and train like a competitor — even if you’re not one (yet).
 ## Class Formats & Schedule We offer both Gi and No-Gi classes throughout the week, giving students the flexibility to train across disciplines. A 6:00 AM morning class is available for early risers, and Saturday sessions feature focused instruction designed to deepen your skillset.
 ## Specialized Training: Sparring & Specific Drills ### Sparring (“Roll”) Slap. Bump. Roll. Experience live sparring in a structured, supervised setting. Our sparring sessions help develop timing, reaction, and composure under pressure — all while minimizing risk.
 ### Specific Training These fast-paced sessions focus on “threads” — sequences of high-rep techniques to build real-time muscle memory. This is where you refine transitions, reactions, and strategy at speed.
 ## Who This Program Is For - Adults serious about learning real-world self-defense - Experienced grapplers or martial artists looking to sharpen and evolve - Professionals seeking structured training with morning flexibility - Competitors or those aspiring to compete
 ## What to Expect at Your First Class - Check-in 10–15 minutes early to meet our team and get oriented - Dress for Gi or No-Gi — or borrow gear your first day - Learn, drill, and roll — all at your level of comfort and readiness - Leave feeling accomplished, challenged, and supported
