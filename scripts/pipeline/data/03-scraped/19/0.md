 # About Us

 At our academy, you’ll find more than just world-class Brazilian Jiu Jitsu instruction—you’ll find a community .
 Led by brothers Pedro and Derrick Torres, both BJJ Black Belts and accomplished international competitors, our gym is built on the values of humility, growth, and respect. Pedro and Derrick bring years of high-level experience to the mats, including titles from IBJJF Worlds, Pan Jiu Jitsu, European Championships, the Abu Dhabi Grand Slam, and more. But beyond the accolades, they are passionate mentors who truly care about helping every student progress —whether you’re stepping onto the mats for the first time or preparing for your next tournament.
 Their approach combines elite-level technique with a welcoming, family-friendly atmosphere. Our academy is a space where kids, adults, hobbyists, and high-level competitors alike can thrive. Everyone is treated like family, and everyone is given the opportunity to grow—not just as martial artists, but as individuals.
 Come train with us and experience a culture where champions are built through hard work, support, and genuine connection.
