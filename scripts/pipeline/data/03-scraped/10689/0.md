 ## Frequently Asked Questions We welcome boxers of all fitness levels and are in your corner to make your first class experience easy and fun.

 #### I DON’T OWN A PAIR OF BOXING GLOVES. WHAT EQUIPMENT DO I NEED?
 Boxing gloves and hand wraps are required for class. Both can be purchased at all locations, and boxing gloves are available for rent at participating clubs.

 #### WILL I GET PUNCHED?
 Our classes are all about you and the heavy bag. We focus on fitness, technique, and fun, with no contact or sparring at any location.

 #### CAN I BRING A FRIEND?
 Of course! Classes are always better with a friend! Feel free to bring a buddy—just be sure they’re signed up for class ahead of time.

 #### WHAT IS TITLE BOXING CLUB? DO YOU PROVIDE CLASSES OR AN OPEN GYM?
 TITLE Boxing club is the best of all worlds. Our main offering is boxing fitness classes that are designed to maximize your workout and improve your boxing skill in a fun, inspiring environment. In addition, TITLE Boxing Club locations do offer private training and small group training in our training area.

 #### WHEN SHOULD I ARRIVE FOR MY FIRST CLASS
 For your first class, we ask that you arrive at least 30 minutes early. This gives us time to teach you the basic punches, get you set up with any necessary equipment, and show you around the club. Arriving early ensures you feel confident, prepared, and ready to get the most out of your first class.

 #### WHAT IS THE CLASS STRUCTURE?
 Every class is a full-body workout on your own heavy bag—targeting your arms, core, and legs. After a quick warmup, you’ll go through 8 high-energy rounds with active recovery in between. Each round builds your fitness and boxing skills through a mix of technique, conditioning, and power. Every class wraps up with a core and strength finisher, leaving you stronger, faster, and more energized.
