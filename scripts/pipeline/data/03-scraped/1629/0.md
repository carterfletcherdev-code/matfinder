# Devine Jiu-Jitsu Savannah & Five Star Wrestling Club
 We offer a variety of classes for both adults and children. We teach basic, fundamental, and advanced Jiu-Jitsu, Grappling, and Wrestling techniques. No-Gi classes and a Wrestling Club are offered as well for those who are interested. Open Mat sessions are held on Saturday. These are family day adult training sessions where visitors and kids are welcome. To learn more about specific classes and times, please visit our Classes or Hours page. Private lessons may be arranged through scheduling. We host a family-friendly environment for students of all ages and we welcome you to join our Jiu-Jitsu family!
