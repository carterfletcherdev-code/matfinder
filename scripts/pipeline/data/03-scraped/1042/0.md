 # Vickery Jiu-Jitsu & MMA is a gym where everyone is welcome, regardless of age, race, size or skill level.

 We are proud to offer a safe environment for everyone to train in. So, whether you’re a Pro MMA fighter or a stay-at-home mom, we encourage everyone to come and be a part of our team family. Our gym is a place where you can build your fitness and health as well as lasting friendships and community.

 For more than 20 years, Our head instructor, Carl Vickery, has trained relentlessly in the Fort Myers/Cape Coral area making memorable and high quality impacts within our team family.
 We offer Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu 6 days a week, and Muay Thai, MMA, and Boxing are offered 3 days a week.
