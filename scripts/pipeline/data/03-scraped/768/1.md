# Brazilian Jiu Jitsu / Police Jiu Jitsu
 Image

 Learn functional self defense while having fun! Our Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu program is an affiliate of Black Belt James Clingerman.
 Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu is a martial art, combat sport, and a self defense system that focuses on grappling and especially ground fighting. BJJ promotes the concept that regardless of size, a person can successfully defend against an assailant by using proper technique, leverage, and most notably, taking the fight to the ground, and then applying joint-locks and chokeholds to defeat the opponent. BJJ training can be used for sport grappling tournaments (gi and no-gi) and mixed martial arts (MMA) competition or self-defense.
