 #### Programs at ### Synthesis

 #### Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu (Gi and No Gi) At Synthesis Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, we offer both Gi and No Gi training , giving students a complete and well-rounded approach to grappling. Gi Jiu-Jitsu emphasizes technical grips, leverage, and strategy, while No Gi focuses on speed, fluidity, and adaptability. Whether you’re a beginner looking to learn self-defense or a competitor preparing for the highest level of competition, our BJJ program provides expert instruction in a supportive, high-energy environment . Train with us and develop the skills, confidence, and mindset needed to excel on and off the mats.

 #### Wrestling Wrestling is a fundamental discipline in martial arts, offering unmatched takedown ability, control, and endurance. At Synthesis, our wrestling program is designed for all levels , from beginners building a strong foundation to experienced athletes looking to sharpen their game. Our expert coaches focus on technique, strength, and mental toughness , helping students improve their balance, explosiveness, and positional awareness. Whether you’re a Jiu-Jitsu practitioner looking to enhance your takedowns or a competitor seeking high-level training, our wrestling classes will push you to the next level.

 #### MMA Our MMA program at Synthesis Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu is designed for students looking to develop a well-rounded skill set in all aspects of combat sports. Combining Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, Wrestling, Striking, and cage control , our classes focus on seamless transitions between disciplines, helping students become complete martial artists. Whether you’re an aspiring fighter or simply want to challenge yourself with high-intensity, full-spectrum training , our expert instructors will push you to reach your full potential.

 #### Striking Our striking program teaches students the art of stand-up combat, focusing on techniques from Muay Thai, Kickboxing, and traditional Boxing . With an emphasis on precision, power, and movement , students learn how to effectively attack, defend, and counter in real-world scenarios and competition. Whether you’re a beginner looking to build confidence or an experienced martial artist sharpening your skills, our structured and dynamic training will help you develop speed, technique, and endurance.
