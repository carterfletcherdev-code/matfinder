Elite Team Wisconsin is a small family orientated Brazilian Jiu Jitsu (BJJ) academy owned and operated by Professor Benjamin Price, an IBJJF certified Black Belt under Tom Knox. Our academy is part of a collective of Brazilian Jiu Jitsu academies that work to impower students with knowledge and confidence. Located in Stevens Point, WI we are in a perfect location to provide services to students throughout Central Wisconsin including Marshfield, Wausau, and Wisconsin Rapids.

 Brazilian Jiu Jitsu is the most practical form of self-defense. BJJ focuses on takedowns, positional control, joint submission, chokes, and disengagement. BJJ has no striking, making it more about using technique to neutralize stronger more athletic opponents. Our academy provides a welcoming, safe environment for all backgrounds to hone their skills.
