# Zenyu Judo Dojo Supporting Athletes' Dreams

 ## Zenyu's Priorities ##### Why choose our dojo?

 #### Community We are a family on and off the mat. Respect, inclusivity, and support are at the heart of our dojo culture. Whether you're new or experienced, young or old, you belong here.

 #### Foundation We emphasize strong fundamentals, thoughtful progression, and a deep respect for the principles of the sport. Every throw, grip, and transition is a chance to refine your skill.

 #### Growth Progress in judo mirrors growth in life. We support every student’s journey — building confidence, discipline, and resilience along the way. Our goal is to help you become the best version of yourself.

 Community Sports is a non-profit sports training facility with the mission of helping kids and adults learn about and train in Judo.
 Zenyu Judo Dojo’s mission is to provide competition and recreational judo opportunities for the youth community in King County
