# 10-Week Jiu Jitsu Basics Courses for Beginners in Lake Elsinore ## A Welcoming Martial Arts Program for Teens & Adults New to Jiu Jitsu Starting Jiu Jitsu shouldn’t feel intimidating. At Shimeru Judo & Jiu Jitsu Club in Lake Elsinore , our Basics program is built to make your first experience welcoming, structured, and confidence-building—training alongside other beginners in your local community.
 We offer two rotating 10-week Jiu Jitsu Basics curricula , scheduled on weekday evenings , allowing new students to start at any time and progress at their own comfortable pace:
 ### • Jiu Jitsu Overview Curriculum A 10-week overview of the most common Brazilian Jiu Jitsu positions, focusing on core principles, goals, and strategies used in real training and live sparring.
 ### • Guards Curriculum A dedicated 10-week study of effective and commonly used guards—the foundational positions where offense, control, and movement begin in Jiu Jitsu.
 ## Learning a martial art can be intimidating. Both courses are taught in a fun, low-stress environment designed specifically for beginners. Classes emphasize understanding why techniques work, not just how to perform them, helping students build real comprehension, confidence, and long-term skill. Courses repeat continuously, and students are encouraged to revisit them to deepen understanding and improve comfort on the mats.
