 # Auburn Martial Arts Center We’re proud to have nationally-certified instructors , a supportive and passionate community , and a safe training environment where kids and parents alike can enjoy learning Karate and other martial arts together! We encourage our students to remain physically fit, maintain self-discipline, and spend the majority of each class on technique — we provide high-quality instruction but also make sure that training is fun!
 At Auburn Martial Arts Center, our goal is to teach our students the intricacies of Karate while improving confidence and instilling a powerful sense of purpose. Our instructors strive to uplift, support on and off the mats, and to help our community become the best it can be.
 If you’re curious about Martial Arts training, we’re happy to sit down and talk about how focus, fitness, self-defense skills, unwavering confidence , and pure fun all come together to craft an incredible training experience.
