 ### Is There A Free Trial?
 Yes there is! Your first class is completely free to try. Simply show up 10 mins before class time to fill out a waiver.

 ### Is There Anything I Need To Bring?
 For your first class you should arrive 10 minutes early to fill out any paperwork. Wear athletic style clothing, and bring a water bottle. If you decide to join then a payment card is recommended. And don't forget the most important thing, a learners mindset that is ready to have fun!

 ### What Should I Expect For My First Class?
 Our general class format is as follows, basic line drill warm ups to get your body ready followed by approximately 40 minutes of jiu jitsu instruction. Expect to have fun, make new friends, get a workout in, and learn something new!

 ### What Does It Mean By Gi and NoGi?
 Gi is the traditional Brazilian Jiu Jitsu uniform that we wear. It consists of a mid weight pants, jacket, and belt set that we put on over our athletic clothes. This uniform is a vital tool in jiu jitsu, being used to control your opponent and apply submissions. No Gi is still the art of Brazilian Jiu Jitsu but is performed in our athletic clothing. It is a great tool that shows us how to rely on different points of control that don't include grabbing the gi.

 ### Do You Offer Family, Multi Kid, and Military Discounts?
 Yes we do! Our memberships include multiple kid and multiple family member pricing, with a discount given for each added member. We also offer a discount for single member first responders, active and former military.
