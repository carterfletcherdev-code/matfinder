# KIDS BJJ (AGES 7+) #### REAL SKILLS FOR KIDS We offer a BJJ class specifically for ages 7 and up. In this complete BJJ class, your child will learn discipline, fundamental body mechanics, and Brazilian Jiu- Jitsu techniques in order to help them pursue their BJJ goals.

 Our students will learn take-downs, forms of controls, fundamental and advanced BJJ concepts implemented in both structured drilling and during live grappling. Competition is not required for your child to participate in BJJ, however we do offer opportunities for our kids to test their BJJ skills in competition and the support needed for them to reach the top of the podium.
