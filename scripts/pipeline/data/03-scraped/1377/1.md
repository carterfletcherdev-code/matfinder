# KIDS BJJ (AGES 4-6) #### Build Skills. Build Confidence. We are proud to offer a kids class for our tiny BJJ friends. In this class, your child will learn discipline, fundamental body mechanics, and cooperation in order to help grow a love for fitness in a social setting.

 Your child will learn basic takedown concepts, self-defense, how to deal with bullies, forms of control, and basic BJJ techniques. We implement BJJ skills, games, and drills in order to promote body awareness in a structured and caring environment.
