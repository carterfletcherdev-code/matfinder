 ### WHY UNIFIED BRAZILIAN JIU-JITSU
 Over 20 years in business training students in Brazilian Jiu Jitsu - Unified Brazilian Jiu Jitsu Academy opened in 2006 with a group of five Pedro Sauer black belts who were among the early pioneers of the Brazilian Jiu Jitsu scene in Salt Lake City.
