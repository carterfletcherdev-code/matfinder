# ADULT JIU JITSU

 Jiu Jitsu has gained a lot of attention in recent years, and it’s pretty obvious why.
 Made for the real world.
 As a sport, Jiu Jitsu has become the fastest growing martial art in the world due to its addictive nature, competitiveness and camaraderie. As a martial art itself, Jiu Jitsu is a form of self defense that every person can benefit from. Focusing on ground level altercations – utilizing holds, throws, blows, and a variety of submission techniques, Jiu Jitsu grants you the ability to fight wherever the battle takes you.
