 Class Programs
 1

 ### General Classes
 General Classes are designed for students who are eight years of age and older, and they are divided into nine levels and sixteen steps following the Shaolin Kungfu Zen teaching principles. Through the course of their studies, students learn basic Kung Fu stances, kicks, fighting techniques, traditional hand forms, and traditional weapon forms. In the past dozen years, the Shaolin Kungfu Zen curriculum has trained various talented individuals and has been a significant guide in their preparation for international martial arts.

 ### Kids Kungfu
 Kids Kung Fu is primarily for kids between four and seven years old. The program revolves around developing and honing concentration, focus, flexibility, and agility. While working with kids, we consider every child's cognitive skills and attention span and help them grow at their own pace. Naturally, belt level advancements depend on each child’s age and cognitive abilities. The Kids Kung Fu Program gives a child a martial arts foundation and a deep understanding of Kungfu and its philosophy.

 ### Taichi & Qigong
 The Taichi & Qigong program is for students eight years old and up, and it caters for their fitness and health needs. It helps them acquire skills to maintain good health and wellbeing. The course content is broad but representative and relevant. The objective is achieved through the Shaolin Kungfu Zen three steps: maintaining the body, controlling breathing, and adjusting the mind to achieve mental and physical well-being and longevity.
