# KIDS PROGRAM

 Programs Kids Program

 # Begin Your Jiu-Jitsu Journey. Our kids program is built to empower young students both on and off the mats. Through structured classes, technical instruction, and age-appropriate training, we teach children the art of Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu in a safe, supportive, and motivating environment.
 Our core values— confidence, discipline, resilience, perseverance , and respect —are at the heart of everything we do. We help kids develop strong character, learn how to handle challenges with a positive mindset, and grow into confident, respectful individuals.
 Our students learn that challenges are meant to be faced , not avoided , and that progress comes through effort, consistency, and a willingness to keep going, even when it gets tough.

 # Our Kids Programs Structure. # 3 - 5 PROGRAM
 Our Kids 3 to 5 Program introduces our youngest practitioners to the world of Jiu-Jitsu through play-based learning and fundamental movement patterns.

 # 6 - 8 PROGRAM
 As children enter school age, our Kids 6 to 8 Program introduces more structured learning while maintaining a playful approach.

 # 9 - 14 PROGRAM
 Our Kids 9 to 14 Program prepares older children and pre-teens for more advanced Jiu-Jitsu training while addressing the unique physical and emotional challenges of this developmental stage.

 # ELITE PROGRAM
 Our Kids Elite Program is designed for advanced young practitioners who have demonstrated exceptional skill and focus in their regular classes.

 Your child will have fun and get strong while learning how to protect themselves! Our jiu jitsu program for 3- to 5-year olds will help your little one learn confidence, discipline, and how to defend themselves.

 Our kids jiu-jitsu program for 6 to 8 year olds build their confidence, and teaches them how to stand up for themselves. The emphasis is on the development of fundamental movement and awareness through fun games, cooperative learning and strategic problem solving.

 Our kids jiu jitsu program teaches children 9 to 14 years old the tools they need to reduce bullying and social exclusion, develop self-confidence, and help them set and achieve their goals while also providing a fun environment where they learn to exercise.
