 ## About The Mount BJJ
 #### BJJ and MMA in Mount Airy and Urbana
 The Mount BJJ is a family-owned and operated Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu (BJJ) and Mixed Martial Arts (MMA) school, whose mission is to provide a safe environment for individuals and families to learn and train BJJ and MMA, right here in Mount Airy and Urbana.
 At The Mount, we believe that anyone is capable of success. The art of Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu is not just amazing for the body, but it cultivates the mind too. The Mount’s Jiu-Jitsu program will help you master self-defense, while also instilling a lifelong sense of confidence and empowerment.
