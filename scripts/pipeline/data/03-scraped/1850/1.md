 ## Our Story

 AE UNLTD JIU JITSU traces its roots to 2019, when Armando Esquer—a 2x No-Gi World Champion, professional MMA fighter (5–1 record), and black belt since 2019—launched AE Jiu Jitsu under the respected Cascão lineage. With deep technical expertise and a welcoming, family-friendly approach, Armando’s leadership has shaped students of all ages, focusing on confidence, skill, and discipline at every level.
 Joe Guarino’s vision for innovation and community grew through Unlimited Jiu Jitsu, building a thriving academy with a culture of progress and support. In 2025, their paths converged, and together they unified their expertise and teams—creating a powerhouse school at the intersection of deep tradition and modern leadership.
 Both Armando and Joe have honed their methods through years of dedication to Jiu Jitsu, blending championship-level instruction with an unwavering commitment to student growth. Our academy stands as a testament to collaboration, built on trust, respect, and the desire to lift every member to their highest potential. Whether you’re stepping onto the mats for the first time or pursuing big goals as a seasoned competitor, AE UNLTD Jiu Jitsu provides a home to grow, connect, and excel. With a family-oriented culture, top-tier training, and innovative thinking, we’ve created a unique environment where every student is valued and the pursuit of mastery never ends.
