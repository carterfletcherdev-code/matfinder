 ## Training for Every Schedule 🌅 Start Your Day Strong: Early birds can get their training in with 6:00 AM BJJ Gi sessions on Tuesdays and Thursdays . If you're nearby during lunch, our 11:30 AM–12:30 PM BJJ Gi classes on Mondays and Wednesdays are perfect for a midday break.
 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Kids Train Too: Our Kids Program runs Monday through Thursday from 5:00–6:00 PM with variety each day—BJJ Gi on Monday, BJJ No-Gi on Tuesday, Muay Thai/Wrestling on Wednesday, and back to BJJ Gi on Thursday.
 🎯 Perfect Progression for Adults: Every weeknight follows the same smart progression. Fundamentals at 6:00–6:45 PM (perfect for beginners), immediately followed by Advanced BJJ at 6:45–7:30 PM . Monday and Wednesday focus on Gi training , while Tuesday and Thursday are No-Gi days .
 🥊 Strike Training: Muay Thai happens Monday, Tuesday, and Thursday at 7:30–8:30 PM —giving you three chances per week to work on your striking game.
 🤼 Wrestling: Sunday mornings at 10:00–11:00 AM —build your takedown game and grappling fundamentals with dedicated wrestling training.
 🤝 Open Training: Round out your week with Open Mat sessions —Friday evening (6:00–7:00 PM) and weekend mornings (Saturday/Sunday 11:00 AM–12:00 PM). These are your time to drill techniques, roll with training partners, and polish everything you've learned during the week.
