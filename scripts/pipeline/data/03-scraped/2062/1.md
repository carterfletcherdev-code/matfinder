 ## What to Expect in Your First Class No experience required! Our structured approach makes learning BJJ accessible and fun for everyone.

 1️⃣
 #### Warm-Up (5 mins): Functional movement, mobility, and BJJ-specific movement drills to prepare your body.

 2️⃣
 #### Drilling (10 mins): Partner repetitions and situational work to build muscle memory and timing.

 3️⃣
 #### Techniques (15 mins): Step-by-step instruction with clear progressions. No rushing—we make sure everyone understands.

 4️⃣
 #### Live Training (15 mins): Controlled rolling/sparring to apply skills (beginners watch first few classes).

 5️⃣
 #### Closing the Class: Time to discuss philosophy, theory, and the deeper aspects of BJJ. Questions and reflection welcomed!
