# Our Classes

 ## No Gi JiuJitsu

 No Gi Jiujitsu sometimes called submission grappling is a faster paced sport. Without the gi to grab onto, the control of this Jiujitsu is different than the traditional uniform. Shorts and a form fitting shirt is all you need to get started in no gi.

 ## Traditional Brazilian JiuJitsu

 Our traditional BJJ is taught in a Gi. Utilizing the gi lapels, sleeves and pants play an important roll in mastering Brazilian JiuJitsu. The game we work is a pressure game with a focus on control ahead of submission, ultimately leading to the tap.

 ## Kids JiuJitsu

 Kids JiuJitsu is a safe place for you child to make friends, stay active, have fun, get exercise and learn a self defense based martial art. With a warm up, technique, live training and fun games the kids learn to focus and challenge themselves mentally and physically.
