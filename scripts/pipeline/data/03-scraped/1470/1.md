 ## About Elite

 We strive to provide the means of improvement for ourselves and our student. This includes seminars, special training and guest lectures. In other words, we invest our time in you.
 At Elite BJJ our goal is to provide a safe, clean, enjoyable environment. Our students learn the “ring tested” techniques of Master Robson Moura. In conclusion, we view all of our members as part of the Elite BJJ team. This encourages members to support each other in class and outside the academy.
