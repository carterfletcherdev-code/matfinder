## Six Blades Jiu-Jitsu Yorktown Youth Programs Ages (4- 12)
 #### Youth Overview Our Youth Martial Arts Program at Six Blades Jiu-Jitsu Yorktown is dedicated to empowering children through the art of Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu. Designed specifically for different age groups, our program tailors instruction to match each child’s developmental stage. Through our Six Blades values—Family, Respect, Honor, Loyalty, Attitude, and Discipline—students not only develop physical skills like coordination, focus, and self-defense but also build character, confidence, and social awareness. Whether your child is in our Little Samurai class (ages 4-6) or Jr Samurai class (ages 7-12), they will gain valuable life skills, all while having fun in a supportive and positive environment.

 #### Little Samurai (4-6 years) - Develop basic body awareness and coordination. - Be able to follow basic instructions. - Improved focus. - Build social skills, like sharing and listening. - Be more aware of emotions. - Learn Self-Defense. - Build Confidence.

 #### Jr Samurai (7-12 years) - Improve Focus. - Enhance Fitness. - Learns Manners & Respect. - Pay more attention to teamwork and friendships. - Show more independence. - Follow rules of a game or sport. - Competition Mindset. - Self-Defense. - Build Confidence.
