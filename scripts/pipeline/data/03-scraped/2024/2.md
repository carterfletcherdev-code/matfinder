Red Pandas Class Overview

 - Our younger pandas will learn basic concepts, movements, and the principles of balance, control, and leverage. Instead of just memorizing moves, this class focuses on teaching them the importance of teams work, being a good partner and student, training safely and learning how to use their bodies.

 Junior Pandas Class Overview

 - Our junior pandas will learn more advanced concepts and techniques building on a solid foundation. Since these kids are older, we are able to go deeper into details and teach more complex positions and techniques. Instead of just memorizing moves, this class focuses on teaching the "why" behind Jiu-Jitsu techniques so kids learn how to solve problems on the mat and move smoothly from one position to the next.
