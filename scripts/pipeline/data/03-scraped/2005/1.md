# Our Programs #### Kids (4-9) Our kids program builds coordination, motor skills, and body awareness through fun, structured training. Classes emphasize respect, discipline, physical fitness, and teamwork in a positive community focused environment.

 #### Juniors (9-14) The juniors program teaches real jiu jitsu fundamentals while developing strength, focus, and self discipline. Students learn teamwork and confidence, with the option to explore competitive jiu jitsu.

 #### Adults (15+) Our adult program focuses on physical fitness, technical skill, and confidence. Students challenge themselves, learn difficult skills, and train in a supportive and motivating community.

 #### Competition (All Ages) The competition program is designed for athletes seeking high level training. Classes emphasize technique, conditioning, strategy, and mental toughness to prepare for tournaments.
