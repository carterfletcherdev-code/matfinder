 Welcome to Finishers MMA HQ in the heart of Lehigh Valley, your ultimate destination for world-class training in no-gi Jiu-Jitsu, MMA, and kickboxing. Step onto the mat and embark on a journey of skill development, fitness, and self-discovery. Our experienced instructors are dedicated to helping you reach your full potential in the exciting world of martial arts. Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned athlete, we're here to guide you on your path to greatness.

 ### no-gi JIU-JITSU
 The coaches at Finishers MMA teach techniques that are proven in competition and in self defense. Jiu-Jitsu classes can give you confidence by building up your body along with your mind. Come try a class, join the team and see the benefits of what a healthy lifestyle can do for you. Jiu-Jitsu can change your life.

 ### MIXED-MARTIAL ARTS
 We rely on our experience at every level and want to provide the Lehigh Valley and Bethlehem area with high level training for beginners and experienced fighters. The squad will take you from learning how to punch and kick effectively, defend strikes, learn takedowns, in addition to learning jiu-jitsu in one of the best no-gi programs in the world.

 ### KICKBOXING
 Striking classes at Finishers MMA will incorporate all effective styles. our instructors on staff are professionals in Dutch Style, Muay Thai and Boxing. We plan to offer bag training classes and fundamental classes for beginners along with more fast paced classes only if you want to dive deeper into the game
