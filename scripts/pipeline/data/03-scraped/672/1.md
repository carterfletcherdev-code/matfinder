 # Kids Jiu-Jitsu

 Location

 Finishers HQ | Bethlehem, PA

 Class Type

 Kids class

 Our jiu-jitsu program at Finishers MMA helps kids build confidence through learning self defense techniques in a safe environment. So many things are cancelled these days like school activities and sports. Martial arts isn't going anywhere and it's showing the kids how to live a healthy life by taking care of their body and expanding their mind.
 Physically kids will get stronger over time by training on average 2-3 times a week in addition to building a strong mind and developing the confidence they need to have a successful life. Many parents have told us how over the years jiu-jitsu has had a very positive influence on their kids.
