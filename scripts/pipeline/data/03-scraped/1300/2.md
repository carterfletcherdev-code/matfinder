# About us

 #### BASE Brazilian Jiu Jitsu

 Learn more about our BJJ Instructors below.

 ## Instructors

 At BASE BJJ, our remarkable BJJ instructors offer the finest instruction money can buy. Through Brazilian Jiu Jitsu (BJJ), they will teach you focus, discipline, goal setting, respect, confidence, and what it takes to become an excellent BJJ athlete.
 Our instructors are Background Checked, IBJJF Registered & Certified Black Belts.

 #### Over 35 Years of Experience We deliver unparalleled training under the guidance of seasoned BJJ Instructors dedicated to your growth. With a steadfast commitment to excellence, we provide a safe, professional environment where practitioners of all levels thrive.

 #### Excellent Training Facility Our BJJ training facility offers a top-notch environment with expertly maintained, clean mats and equipment, ensuring a safe and hygienic space for all practitioners. It’s the perfect place to hone your BJJ skills under world-class instruction.

 #### Family-Oriented Culture We foster a culture where members of all ages and skill levels support and uplift one another in a welcoming, inclusive environment. The tight-knit community encourages camaraderie, mutual respect, and shared growth between all althletes.

 #### Greg Hernandez Black Belt Professor
 IBJJF Registered & Certified 1st Degree Black Belt
 As an overweight teenager with a learning disability, Greg’s options seemed pretty limited twenty years ago. Today, thirty-three pounds lighter and no stranger to the podium. Greg is seen as a force to be reckoned with as a Black Belt in Brazilian Jiu Jitsu with numerous podium medals in the prestigious IBJJF.
 Greg can trace the beginnings of his transformation to the day in 2012 when, at the age of twelve, he started working with World-Renowned trainer Bruno Bastos.
 Greg currently competes in the Adult Black Belt Division and is our Assistant BJJ Instructor at BASE BJJ which he and his black belt father, Gabe Hernandez, founded and operate in Lubbock, Texas.
