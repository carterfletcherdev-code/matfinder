 # DISCIPLINES

 #### Kid’s Self-Defense Instill confidence, discipline, and respect in your child while equipping them with essential self-defense skills. Our specially designed program blends fun with effective techniques to ensure your child develops both physically and emotionally.

 #### Adult Self-Defense Whether you’re a complete beginner or an experienced practitioner, our adult self-defense classes cater to all skill levels. Learn practical techniques that not only keep you safe but also enhance your overall fitness and mental focus.

 #### Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu Discover the beauty and effectiveness of ground fighting with our Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu classes. Develop your ground game, improve your grappling skills, and learn the art of submission in a supportive and challenging environment.

 #### Muay Thai Kickboxing Unleash your inner warrior with our Muay Thai Kickboxing classes. Perfect your striking techniques, enhance your cardiovascular fitness, and gain a deep understanding of the art of eight limbs.

 #### Shorei-Ryu Karate Immerse yourself in the traditional martial art of Shorei-Ryu Karate. Our instructors guide you through katas, drills, and sparring, fostering a strong foundation in discipline, respect, and self-control.

 #### Judo Experience the dynamic art of Judo, focusing on throws, takedowns, and ground control. Develop balance, leverage, and mental resilience as you progress through our Judo program.

 #### Sambo Explore the Russian martial art of Sambo, emphasizing throws, ground control, and submissions. Our Sambo classes provide a unique blend of techniques suitable for both self-defense and sport.
