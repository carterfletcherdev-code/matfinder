# Kids BJJ Classes Ages 4 to 7 Confidence, Coordination, and Fun -- in a Safe, Positive Environment. Gi & No-Gi Classes Available.

 We believe that for a child to learn, they first have to love what they are doing. That's why our program for ages 4-7 focuses on building coordination and discipline through fun, high-energy classes. We use a game-based curriculum to teach fundamental movements without the pressure, allowing kids to learn respect and focus while burning off energy. Whether they are training in the Gi or No-Gi, your child will experience a positive, structured atmosphere where progress is celebrated and learning is genuinely enjoyable.

 ## What Class Looks Like (Ages 4-7) Our curriculum is specifically structured to keep young students engaged from start to finish. We balance discipline with fun by using high-energy games to teach real martial arts skills. Instead of long lectures, we focus on playful warmups, simple techniques, and supervised partner drills that keep them moving. The goal is to create a positive association with training, so we prioritize encouragement and connection. Your child will work hard and learn respect, but the atmosphere is always lighthearted, safe, and filled with high-fives.

 ## A Safe Place for Kids to Grow Our priority is to create a space where your child feels safe to try new things. We focus on gentle, controlled training that allows for learning without fear or intimidation. The culture on our mats is built on kindness and teamwork, ensuring that every student is supported by coaches who care. We do not allow bullying or aggressive behavior; instead, we offer a supportive family atmosphere designed to help your child grow into a confident and respectful individual.

 ## Perfect for Shy Kids and Wild Ones We have seen firsthand how BJJ transforms children across the personality spectrum. For the shy child, the mats provide a structured way to build self-belief and make friends naturally. For the high-energy child, the training offers an outlet that teaches discipline and self-regulation. It helps anxious children feel capable and secure, while providing natural athletes with a challenging new edge. Ultimately, every student learns resilience -- the ability to try hard and stay calm under pressure -- which serves them well beyond the gym.

 ## FAQ (Ages 4-7) ### Does my child need experience? No. Most kids start with zero experience.

 ### Is it safe? Yes. There is no striking, and training is supervised and gentle.

 ### What should they wear? For trial class: comfy sports clothes. We'll guide you on a Gi after.
