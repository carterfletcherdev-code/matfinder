 ## Kelley Hutta

 Owner & Chief Instructor – Chandler Martial Arts

 Kelley Hutta brings over 35 years of martial arts experience to Chandler Martial Arts, where she serves as Owner and Chief Instructor. A 4th Degree Black Belt in Wado-Ryu Karate, certified through USA Karate (NKF), Kelley has dedicated her life to training, teaching, and growing the karate community.
 She is a multiple-time US Open Advanced Kumite Gold Medalist and has earned recognition on the national and international stage as a USA Karate National Referee of the Year (2025) and a PKF International Referee . Kelley is also a Certified National Referee and Regional Judge for both Kata and Kumite, with deep expertise in officiating and competition.
 Her martial arts journey began in 1990, with primary focus in Wado-Ryu and Wado-Kai Karate, along with additional training in Shito-Ryu, Goju-Ryu, fencing, and other competitive sports. Beyond karate, Kelley is a Certified Personal Trainer and Certified Nutritionist, bringing a holistic approach to her students’ fitness, discipline, and well-being.
 Outside the dojo, Kelley is a proud mom of four amazing kids and is happily married. She is passionate about lifelong learning and excited to continue inspiring her students and building the Chandler Martial Arts community.
