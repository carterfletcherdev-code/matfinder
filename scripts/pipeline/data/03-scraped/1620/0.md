 ##### Step on to the Mat
 # Our Classes
 Kickboxing/Sparring Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu Open Mat Fitness Training
 ## Kickboxing: Strike, Sweat, Succeed
 Hone your striking skills and elevate your fitness with Elite Performance’s kickboxing classes. Whether you’re aiming for a high-energy cardio workout or full-on competitive training, kickboxing challenges your speed, power, and precision while sculpting your whole body. Learn real techniques to defend yourself at a high level, push your limits, and get your body into world-class shape — all while having a blast on the mats.

 The Intro Class is a great way to get fit and learn MMA skills with bags. The Kickboxing also takes it up a notch from the beginner level. Then, when you’re ready to work your advanced skills, come to the Sparring class and test your skills against a partner.

 ## Roll With the Best in Our BJJ Classes
 Our Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu classes focus on grappling techniques and ground fighting. This art incorporates wrestling moves and emphasizes skill over strength, making it accessible to all body types. In a friendly atmosphere, learn how to defend yourself effectively while improving your fitness and flexibility.

 Traditional BJJ Classes: Join us on Wednesdays and Saturdays for Gi classes that dive deep into classic Jiu-Jitsu techniques. Bring your Gi and get ready to master the fundamentals the right way.

 ## Don’t Just Watch MMA — Live It.
 Open Mat is your chance to come into the gym and work on whatever skills you want—no structured class, just pure training. Whether you’re into submission grappling, Jiu-Jitsu, boxing, Muay Thai, or wrestling, this is the time to focus on your game. Jeremy Horn heads up the gym and teaches nearly every day. There’s truly no better place in Utah to sharpen your skills, push your limits, and train like a Mixed Martial Artist.

 ## Personalized Fitness Training with Jeremy Horn
 Get fit your way. Jeremy Horn will help you design a fitness plan tailored to your goals, whether that’s crushing it in our Kickboxing classes, adding extra cardio in the weight room, or a mix of both. Our program welcomes all levels, helping you build strength, boost endurance, and improve overall health. Workouts are guided by experienced instructors in a supportive environment because getting stronger shouldn’t be intimidating. Join us and train smarter, reach your goals, and feel your absolute best.
