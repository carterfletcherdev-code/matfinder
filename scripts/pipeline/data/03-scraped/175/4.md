 ### How do I find the jiu-jitsu academy?
 We are located at 98-390 Kamehameha Highway , right between Dixie Grill and Da Seafood Cartel in Aiea. Look for the shared parking lot with Dixie Grill —you can park there and find us just a few steps away. If you're coming from the main road, keep an eye out for the Dixie Grill sign—we're in the same lot!
