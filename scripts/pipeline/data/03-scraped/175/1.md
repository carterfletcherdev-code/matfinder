 # Little Champions (4-6 yrs.) In our Little Champions class, we focus on developing essential motor skills that will support each child's growth on and off the mat. Through fun games and exercises, students practice basic takedowns, entries, and defense techniques, helping them overcome physical barriers and build confidence in unfamiliar situations. These activities set a foundation for self-discipline and a love for Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu.
