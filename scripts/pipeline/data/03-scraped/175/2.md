 # Kids NoGi (Invite Only) KIDS NOGI (INVITE ONLY)
 The Kids NoGi class is an advanced, invite-only program designed for young athletes who have mastered the fundamentals of Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu and are ready to take their skills to the next level. In this class, students focus on refining their technique without the traditional gi (uniform), learning how to use wrestling-style grips, transitions, and submissions in a dynamic, fast-paced environment. The class emphasizes speed, agility, and strategic thinking as students apply their Jiu-Jitsu knowledge to live sparring and specific situational drills. The Kids NoGi class is for dedicated students who are committed to pushing their limits, improving their overall grappling game, and preparing for future competition opportunities.
