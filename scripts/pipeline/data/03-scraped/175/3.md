 # Adult Gi (White Belt Only) The BJJ White Belt Only class is a dedicated space for new students to grow and progress through their early Jiu-Jitsu journey. This class focuses on the foundational techniques that every white belt needs to build confidence on the mat, including basic positions, escapes, submissions, and defenses. With a focus on technique over speed, students will gain a deeper understanding of how Jiu-Jitsu works and develop the skills needed to progress to the next level. The class is structured to foster a positive and supportive environment where new students can ask questions, make mistakes, and build a strong base in a stress-free setting.
