## Frequently Asked Questions ## Will I get hurt? - The injury rate IN Jiu Jitsu is extremely low, especially compared to Basketball, Track, Football, or other traditional sports. - We want you to go at your speed, which is why we have more fundamental classes than any other
 ## Will this be really hard on my body? Is it violent? - Any physical activity is taxing to the body, especially if you haven't been physically active. everyone is different but 95% of our students start of with absolutely no experience and are generally out of shape when starting. We will build you up properly.
 ## Do I have to be athletic, flexible, or in good shape? - Nope! People always say "I want to get in shape before I try BJJ" This is completely WRONG. You can't get in shape for jiu-jitsu, without doing jiu jitsu. the problem-solving aspect brings an element to the sport that can’t be replicated without training partners.
 ## What should I bring to my first class? - Flip flops, and a towel if you plan to shower after class.
 ## Is sparring (rolling) required? - The simple answer is, NO! You can begin live training whenever you feel comfortable.
