 ## Kids Competition jiu Jitsu

 At JM Modern Jiu Jitsu, we believe that competition is a powerful tool for personal growth, especially for children. While training in class builds skills and confidence, stepping onto the competition mat takes their development to the next level. Competing teaches kids how to set goals, manage nerves, and push past their limits in a way that regular training alone cannot. Win or lose, every tournament is an opportunity for them to learn, adapt, and grow—both as athletes and individuals. We emphasize that competition isn’t just about victory; it’s about testing yourself, embracing challenges, and improving with each experience.
 Because of the benefits competition provides, we strongly encourage all of our kids to compete. It reinforces discipline, accountability, and perseverance, all of which translate to success both on and off the mats. Our coaches work closely with students to prepare them for tournaments, ensuring they feel confident and ready to perform at their best. By fostering a competitive mindset in a supportive environment, we help our young athletes develop resilience, determination, and a lifelong love for Jiu Jitsu.

 Brazilian Jiu Jitsu is especially well-suited as a youth martial art. Every child is unique in their stature, learning ability and physical characteristics. Brazilian Jiu Jitsu (BJJ) provides such a broad scope of techniques and movements that each child adopts only that which is effective for him/her without diminishing the effectiveness of their training. And with any martial arts discipline, common benefits of consistent training include:
 - Increased concentration and focus - Increased balance and coordination - Increased conditioning, better health

 Training also provides mental and emotional empowerment. Some of our kids challenge themselves in the arena of competition, tackling head-on the fear and anxiety leading up to the challenge and the challenge itself, making not only parents and coaches proud but also themselves. This is also true in today’s society where bullying has become pandemic. Some of these added benefits also include:
 - Self-awareness - Self-confidence - Respect - Teamwork - Camaraderie
 Our goal is to empower your children with the attributes and technical abilities of a Brazilian Jiu Jitsu practitioner, while at the same time instilling the respect and responsibility that comes with acquiring such skills.
