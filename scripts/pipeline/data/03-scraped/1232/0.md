 ## Common Questions What is taught in your kids classes? Our kids program offers a dynamic blend of Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu—both Gi and No-Gi—widely regarded as one of the most effective martial arts in the world. To help young students become well-rounded martial artists, we also incorporate striking techniques a few days each week, including fundamentals of Western boxing and Muay Thai kickboxing.

 Is Jiujitsu safe for kids? Yes, Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu is safe for kids when taught in a structured, supportive environment. With five instructors in each class, we ensure close supervision and personalized guidance. Our team focuses on proper technique, safety, and respect—helping kids build confidence, coordination, and discipline while having fun.
