 ## Ground Zero

 Ground Zero Martial Arts is a place to train, grow, and reset—on and off the mat. We offer Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu (Gi & No-Gi) and Combat Fitness classes for kids and adults, right here in Henderson, Nevada. Whether you’re stepping onto the mat for the first time or returning with experience, this is where your journey begins—or begins again. With a focus on discipline, technique, and community, we train hard, stay humble, and grow together.
