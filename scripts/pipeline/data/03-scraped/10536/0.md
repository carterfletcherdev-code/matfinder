 ## Frequently Asked Questions

 Most frequent questions and answers about our programs

 Will My Child Become A Bully?
 It’s one of the most common concerns parents bring to us — and the answer might surprise you. Students at Coffrin’s ATA consistently grow into kids who stand up against bullying, not become one. The confidence they build through training naturally comes with humility and genuine kindness toward others.

 Are Parents Allowed To Watch?
 Absolutely — we love having you here. Watching your child in their kids martial arts classes is something we actively encourage, and honestly? Your kids love it too. If you’re feeling inspired, jump into one of our adult classes and give your child something to brag about. There’s something special about a family that trains together.
 And if you need a little time to yourself, simply drop them off and go grab that coffee. You’ll come back to a big smile — we can almost guarantee it.

 Do I Need To Be Fit For This?
 Not even close. Students walk through our doors at every fitness level — and that’s exactly how it should be. The truth is, our Bloomington classes are what get you fit. Think of it as a workout that never feels like one: you’re too busy having fun to notice how hard you’re working

 How Long Until I Get My Black Belt?
 Honestly, there’s no single answer — and that’s not a bad thing. Every student moves at their own pace, and no one here is keeping score. What we can tell you is that the journey itself is where the real value lives: the perseverance, the self-discipline, the commitment to showing up even when it’s hard. Those are the things that follow you off the mat and into the rest of your life.
 The self-defense skills you’ll build along the way are genuinely life-changing. And your instructors at Coffrin’s ATA Martial Arts in Bloomington will be right there with you — whether your goal is black belt or something entirely your own.

 How Do I Claim Your Limited Time Offer?
 Space in our training area is limited, so the quickest way to lock in your spot is to fill out the form on this page. You can also give us a call — we genuinely enjoy talking with families about what we do and whether our Bloomington programs are the right fit for you.

 What Do I Need To Bring To My First Martial Arts Lesson?
 Keep it simple: wear clothes you’re comfortable moving in and bring a water bottle. That’s really all you need. From there, our kids and adult classes in Bloomington take care of everything else — including making sure you leave with a grin on your face. Most students do.

 I'm Not Very Sporty or Coordinated, Will That Be A Problem?
 Not at all — you’re actually in good company. A lot of students who walk into our martial arts classes for the first time would have said the exact same thing about themselves. Within a few weeks, those same students are moving with a confidence they didn’t know they had.
 Our instructors are trained to teach in a way that actually makes sense — breaking things down so that anyone, regardless of experience, can follow along and improve. Give it a little time, and you might just surprise yourself.
