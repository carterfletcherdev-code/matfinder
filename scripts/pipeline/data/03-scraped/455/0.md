 ## Honoring the Legacy of Relson Gracie At Gracie Arizona , we are committed to preserving the time-tested techniques and philosophy of Relson Gracie Jiu Jitsu . Led by Professor Nathan Zigler , our academy is built on the foundation of one of the most respected lineages in Brazilian Jiu Jitsu.
 As a Relson Gracie black belt, Nathan carries forward the teachings of Grandmaster Relson Gracie , a pioneer in self-defense and a key figure in the evolution of Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu. Under Relson's mentorship, Nathan has developed world class technique and a deep understanding of Jiu Jitsu as a way of life blending discipline, resilience, and self-mastery.
 More than just a training facility, Gracie Arizona is a community. Whether you're a beginner, competitor, or lifelong practitioner, our academy is a place to grow, challenge yourself, and evolve. Whether your goal is self defense, high level competition, or overall physical and mental well being, we invite you to become part of the legacy.
 Train with us in a system that has been battle-tested for generations. Your journey starts here.

 ## Our Jiu-Jitsu Classes Explore a variety of classes tailored for all experience levels—from beginners to advanced competitors. Our comprehensive curriculum ensures every student receives proper instruction and guidance on their martial arts journey.

 #### Fundamentals Class 60 min | No sparring | Essential techniques for self-defense & sport. Perfect for beginners to build a solid foundation.

 #### Beginners Class 60 min | Drilling, warm-ups, & controlled sparring. Introduction to live training in a safe environment.

 #### Mixed Class 60 min | Technique focus varies by student level. All belt levels training together with personalized instruction.

 #### Advanced Class 60 min | Blue belts & above | High-level strategy and complex techniques for experienced practitioners.

 #### Competition Class 60 min | Tournament-focused | High-intensity sparring and competition preparation.

 #### Women's Only Class 60 min | Taught by women | Self-defense & sport techniques in a supportive environment.

 #### No-Gi Class 60 min | Submission grappling without the gi. Focus on wrestling and modern grappling techniques.

 #### Mighty Mights Class 30 min | Ages 5-8 | Coordination, balance, & fun drills designed for young children.

 #### Private Lessons One-on-one training for personalized skill development. Perfect for accelerated learning and specific goals.

 #### Juniors Class 60 min | Ages 8-14 | Self-defense & competition techniques with character development focus.
