 ##### About Cross Collar Jiu Jitsu Clear Lake ##### At Cross Collar Jiu Jitsu, we believe great training starts with a great culture. Our academy is home to people of all ages, backgrounds, and experience levels—from first-day beginners to dedicated competitors—who all share the same mat and the same respect for the journey.
 Whether your goal is to compete at a high level, get in shape, learn real self-defense, or simply enjoy training as a hobby, you’ll find your place here. We are proud to have a strong team that trains with purpose and intensity, while also offering a welcoming, pressure-free environment for hobbyists and newcomers who want to progress at their own pace.
 Our Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu program is the foundation of everything we do, but we go beyond BJJ alone. We offer high-level Vici Wrestling instruction for kids, helping young athletes build confidence, discipline, and athletic fundamentals, as well as MMA training for adults looking for a complete, well-rounded combat sports experience.
 One of the things that truly sets Cross Collar apart is our family-oriented environment. Many parents and children train together, creating stronger bonds and growing side by side through shared challenges. It’s common to see entire families on the mats, supporting one another and building lifelong habits of resilience and respect.
 More than just a gym, Cross Collar Jiu Jitsu is a community. Our members support each other, celebrate each other’s wins, and show up consistently—on and off the mats. It’s why so many people call Cross Collar their second home here in the Seabrook/Clear Lake area.
 As a proud affiliate of the BJJ Revolution Team, our students are also welcomed to train at other affiliated academies, giving them access to a broader network and expanded learning opportunities.
 Whether you’re stepping onto the mats for the first time, returning after time away, or chasing the next level of competition, you’ll find a team here that pushes you, supports you, and genuinely cares about your growth.
