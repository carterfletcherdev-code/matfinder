 We’re having a fantastic month. Meeting lots of new people while building a strong and friendly culture in the new digs. In an effort to help everyone stay active through the holiday season, or to take advantage of some spare time to try out a class, we will be keeping a normal schedule (weather permitting of course).
 If you’d like to try a class, just show up with comfortable workout clothes, no zippers or buttons. If you want to ask some questions or talk to an instructor, show up a few minutes early.
