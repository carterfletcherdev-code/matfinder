 ## Service Description BRAZILIAN JIU-JITSU IS A SELF-DEFENSE MARTIAL ART AND A COMBAT SPORT THAT ORIGINATED FROM THE FAMOUS GRACIE FAMILY IN BRAZIL. BRAZILIAN JIU-JITSU, OR BJJ AS IT IS COMMONLY KNOWN AS, EMPHASIZES TAKING AN OPPONENT TO THE GROUND, GAINING A DOMINANT POSITION, AND USING A NUMBER OF TECHNIQUES TO FORCE THEM INTO SUBMISSION VIA JOINT LOCKS OR CHOKEHOLDS. SPARRING, COMMONLY REFERRED TO AS “ROLLING” WITHIN THE BJJ COMMUNITY, PLAYS A MAJOR ROLE IN TRAINING AND THE DEVELOPMENT OF THE STUDENT AND/OR ATHLETE.
