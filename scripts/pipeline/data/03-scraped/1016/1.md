 POWERLIFTING

 Powerlifting is a strength-based sport where athletes compete in three main lifts: the squat, bench press, and deadlift. The objective in powerlifting is to lift the maximum weight possible for one repetition in each of these exercises.

 JIU JITSU

 Jiu Jitsu is a martial art and combat sport that focuses on grappling, ground fighting, and submission techniques. It emphasizes technique, leverage, and strategy over brute strength, making it effective for people of all sizes.

 MMA

 Mixed Martial Arts (MMA) is a full-contact combat sport that combines techniques from various martial arts disciplines, including striking, grappling, and submission fighting. Fighters in MMA use skills from disciplines such as boxing, Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, Muay Thai, wrestling, judo, and karate, among others.
