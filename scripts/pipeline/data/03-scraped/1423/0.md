## Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu

 Adult Gi

 Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu is a martial art and combat sport that is the culmination of techniques that originated from kodokan judo in Japan, were later innovated in Brazil and is now practiced all over the world. It has a particular emphasis on ground fighting, positioning and submissions, but also includes wrestling and judo takedowns.

 Adult No-Gi
 No-Gi Jiu-Jitsu is more akin to submission wrestling, wherein the goal is much the same as traditional Gi Jiu-Jitsu, out position or submit your opponent, however you do not wear a Gi or have the belt rank system.

 Kids Gi

 In the kids Gi Jiu-Jitsu program students will be taught the basics and fundamentals of Gi Jiu-Jitsu including takedowns, Gi grips, positioning and submissions as well as discipline and respect in a safe, controlled environment.

 Kids No-Gi
 In the kids No-Gi Jiu-Jitsu program students will learn similar techniques to the Gi program adapted for No-Gi. It also makes for a great preparatory class for scholastic wrestling.
