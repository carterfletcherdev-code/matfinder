 ## PROFESSOR ANDRE GOMES

 Head Coach – Carlson Gracie Lewisville
 Born in São Paulo, Brazil, Andre Gomes began his Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu journey in 2002 at the age of 14. With over 20 years of experience on the mats, he has built a career defined by technical excellence, leadership, and international recognition.

 ### MORE Andre spent four years living in Dubai, where he coached the United Arab Emirates Army. During this time, he gained high-level experience training military personnel and refined a disciplined, results-driven coaching methodology.
 In 2018, he moved to the United States to continue expanding his career as both a competitor and instructor. In 2021, Andre opened his own academy in Texas alongside his partner, Gary.
 Today, he serves as the Head Coach at Carlson Gracie Lewisville, where he continues to earn titles as an active competitor while building a strong, disciplined, and competitive Jiu-Jitsu team.
 Andre Gomes is recognized not only for his achievements on the mat, but for his commitment to developing confident students, high-level competitors, and a culture rooted in respect, consistency, and excellence.
