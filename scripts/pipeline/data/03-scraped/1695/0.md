 Fundamentals of Jiu-Jitsu Class
 In our Fundamentals of Jiu-Jitsu course we use the teaching methods based on the “games” that have been used for generations in the Gracie family to teach the foundations of movement and body control. In this 20 week course you and your 4-6 year old child will learn basic self-defense techniques that are based on leverage and control in a fun and safe environment. This course is designed to include a parent or relative in the class to make learning Jiu Jitsu truly a family event. We believe that the bond that is created between parent and child while learning how to “play” Jiu Jitsu is one of the most rewarding aspects of this program. In certain circumstances a parent or guardian will not be required to participate. This decision will be based on the age and ability of the student. However, we encourage all parents to join us on the the mat and be a part of their child’s development.
 Kids Class - Gi - All Levels
 Our Kids Jiu-Jitsu classes will teach your children a way to protect themselves and a fun new sport. The practice of Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu is seen as a means to individual development and personal growth. Self-awareness, self-confidence, respect, discipline, determination and responsibility, which are common core values taught within all of our Kids Jiu-jitsu classes.
 Fundamentals Class - Gi - All Levels
 Fundamentals Gi Class is structured in a way to provide a concise and easy-to-learn system to mastering Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu Fundamentals and Basic Self-Defense regardless of previous experience. The concepts learned in this class will serve as a foundation to the more advanced systems and concepts taught in the Advanced class.
 Advanced Class - Gi - Colored Belts Only
 Advanced Gi Class is designed to take the principles, concepts and techniques learned in the beginner's course to a several levels higher. This class takes a deep dive into Kroyler's systematic and detail-oriented instruction. The purpose of this class is to help participants achieve mastery of all aspects of Jiu-Jitsu.
 NoGi - All Levels
 This class serves as a focused enhancer to our Gi-based classes ( Beginner and Advanced ), where we add-on No-Gi specific techniques and systems to help ensure that our students' Jiu-Jitsu is well-rounded and properly developed. This class moves at a faster pace, but very much like our other classes, it is designed in a way that allows for the knowledge to be absorbed and recalled by the students in practical situations.
 Open Mat - All Levels
 Open mat is just that: the mats are open and available for use. All students are welcome to use them. Therefore, it is encouraged that students take advantage of this time, using it wisely to review, drill, roll/spar, ask questions, etc...
 Competition Class - Gi - All Levels
 This class is designed for those that want to turbo charge their Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu ability. While designed with competitors in mind, this class is open to all students and competitors alike. It is a fast-paced class that will take your Jiu-Jitsu to new levels.
 Positional Rolling - All Levels
 The positional rolling class will focus on the techniques that our advanced students are learning. Rolls will begin from the position which will give you more experience defending and executing the techniques that are being developed in the advanced curriculum class. This class is designed to take the advanced concepts from the curriculum and translate them to live grappling.
 Judo - All Levels
 This is an accredited Judo program that will allow for certified ranking through IJF and will provide technical instruction that will help all participants to grow and improve their skillset. This is valuable to everyone, especially competitors.
