 # Kroyler Gracie Jiu-Jitsu – Sturgis ## The Same Standards. The Same Team. Serving the Sturgis Community. ## Kroyler Gracie Jiu-Jitsu – Sturgis is an official KGJJ satellite school, owned and operated as part of the Kroyler Gracie Jiu-Jitsu system. ## ## This location follows the same curriculum, technical standards, and teaching methodology as the main academy, providing students in the Sturgis area access to high-quality Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu instruction within a structured, professional environment. ## ## While rooted in the Sturgis community, this school is fully integrated into the KGJJ team. ## ## Instruction and Curriculum ## ## Training at KGJJ Sturgis follows the same curriculum and instructional framework used across all KGJJ locations. ## ## Students develop skills across the full scope of Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, including positional control, escapes, submissions, transitions, and standing engagements. Instruction emphasizes fundamentals, sound mechanics, and long-term development rather than shortcuts or trends. ## ## Regardless of the KGJJ location, our students benefit from a uniform standard of instruction and a seamless path to advancement. ## ## Led by Jesus Garcia ## ## KGJJ Sturgis is led by Jesus Garcia, a handpicked instructor who received his instruction and mentorship directly under Kroyler Gracie. ## ## Jesus teaches with the same attention to detail, structure, and professionalism that define KGJJ. His leadership reflects a commitment to technical quality, responsible instruction, and maintaining a positive, focused training environment. ## ## Students at the Sturgis location benefit from hands-on coaching within a system that values consistency, accountability, and long-term growth. ## ## One Team, Full Access ## ## Students training at KGJJ Sturgis are part of the same KGJJ team. ## ## Membership at the Sturgis location allows students to attend classes at any KGJJ school within the association. This flexibility supports consistent training, travel needs, and deeper connection across locations. ## ## No matter where you train, you are part of the same system and community. ## ## Culture and Community ## ## The culture at KGJJ Sturgis reflects the same expectations found throughout the academy. ## ## Training is respectful, focused, and supportive. Students are encouraged to train with intention, take responsibility for their progress, and contribute positively to the environment. Egos are checked, safety is prioritized, and training partners support one another. ## ## This creates a welcoming space for beginners and a productive environment for experienced students. ## ## Training Opportunities ## ## KGJJ Sturgis offers Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu programs aligned with the broader KGJJ system, including youth and adult training options. ## ## Classes are structured, supervised, and designed to support steady progression while maintaining safety and quality instruction. Students are encouraged to train consistently and build skills over time. ## ## For specific class schedule and offerings, please visit the schedule page or contact the academy directly. ## ## Getting Started ## ## Starting Jiu-Jitsu should feel clear and approachable. ## ## Your first class at KGJJ Sturgis is guided and pressure-free.
