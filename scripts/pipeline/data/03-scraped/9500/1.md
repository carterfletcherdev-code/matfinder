# Schedule Update: NEW Judo Classes by Thiago Azeredo | Dec 28, 2015 | Uncategorized | 0 comments

 Due to popular demand, Sitan Gym AZ is adding several Judo classes to our schedule and separating kids’ and adult training sessions.
 As of Monday, January 4th, the weekly Judo schedule will be as follows:
 Monday:
 12:15-1:15pm- Adult Judo
 1:15-2:15pm- Kids’ Judo
 7:30-8:30pm- Adult Judo
 Tuesday:
 7:30-8:30pm- Kids’ Judo
 Wednesday:
 12:15-1:15pm- Adult Judo
 1:15-2:15pm- Kids’ Judo
 7:30-8:30pm- Adult Judo
 Thursday:
 7:30-8:30pm- Kids’ Judo
 Friday:
 No Judo Classes
 Saturday:
 12:00-1:30pm- All Ages Judo
 Sunday:
 No Judo Classes
 It’s our hope that this new schedule will allow us to better meet all students’ needs. Please don’t hesitate to send comments or suggestions our way. See you in class!
