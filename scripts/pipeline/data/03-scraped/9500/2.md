# Schedule Update: Changes to Our New Judo Classes by Thiago Azeredo | Jan 5, 2016 | Uncategorized | 0 comments

 After listening to your suggestions, we will be switching the adult and kids’ weeknight Judo classes.
 Beginning on Monday, January 11th, the kids’ Judo classes will take place on Mondays and Wednesdays from 7:30-8:30pm .
 Adult Judo will take place on Tuesdays and Thursdays from 7:30-8:30pm .
 We began this week with the opposite schedule (adults on M-W, kids on T-Th), but we’ve heard from many of you that this will be a better fit. Please don’t hesitate to come forward with further comments or suggestions. We greatly appreciate your feedback.
 Thank you. We look forward to seeing you in Judo!
