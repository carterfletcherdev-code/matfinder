 ## Class Format Our Adult Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu Fundamentals classes in San Jose are structured to support both beginners and experienced practitioners refining their base skills.
 Each class begins with conditioning and solo movement drills designed to improve coordination, balance, and mat awareness. After warming up, instructors review key concepts before introducing the technical focus of the day.
 Students then drill the technique in controlled repetitions, followed by low-resistance situational sparring from the featured position to build confidence and application. Classes conclude with live Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu sparring rounds to reinforce timing, control, and decision-making under pressure.

 ## Curriculum Our Adult All Levels Jiu-Jitsu curriculum is organized into structured, lesson-planned training blocks rather than isolated techniques. Each block focuses on a specific positional theme, connecting takedowns, control, transitions, and submissions into a cohesive system.
 This approach allows students to build depth of understanding within key areas of Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu while refining timing, strategy, and application through progressive drilling and live training. By training within focused blocks, students develop a more complete and functional skill set over time.
