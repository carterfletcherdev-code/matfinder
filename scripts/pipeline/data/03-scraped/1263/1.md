 ## About Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu

 At Unita, we teach Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu (BJJ), which is a martial art and combat sport that focuses on ground fighting and submission holds.
 BJJ is widely recognized to be the most effective foundation for self defense and is a key part of Mixed Martial Arts.
 Whether you have never trained before or have years of experience, you will find Unita BJJ a friendly and welcoming environment.
 Our classes are led by experienced black belt instructors, whose focus is making every class a challenging workout and a great time!
 In addition, Unita partners with Baan Muay Thai, and many students train in both BJJ and Muay Thai. Find out more about Baan here .

 ## Head Coach Dax Mitchell

 Starting BJJ a bit later in life, Coach Dax began his journey at the age of 35. After training for 20 years, Dax still finds BJJ endlessly fascinating and loves sharing this enthusiasm with others. He hopes to keep training and teaching for decades to come!

 Dax’s journey started in Texas where his main instructor, Tom Dinklage, gave Dax his blackbelt after 10 years of training. Dax moved to the Upper Valley the same year (2016) and has been teaching BJJ in Lebanon for over 8 years. Dax received his 3rd degree from Dedeco Almeida in 2025 (Dax in the white gi in photo). BJJ lineage:
 Carlos Gracie > Carlson Gracie > Ricardo Liborio > Dedeco Almeida > Dax Mitchell
