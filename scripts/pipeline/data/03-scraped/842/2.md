# Intro Classes

 ### INTRO TO JIU-JITSU This introduction to Jiu Jitsu class is designed to welcome the beginning student into the world of grappling. Its primary focus is on teaching the fundamental movements and positions so new students can transition confidently into the Basic class.
 The intro class runs for 45 mins and does not include sparring. Contact us today to schedule.
