 ## Adult Jiu-Jitsu Our Fundamentals class focuses on postures, offensive and defensive positions and techniques. There is also a continual aspect of physical and mental conditioning. This adult Brazilian Jiu Jitsu class is suitable for beginners but recommended for all belt levels.
 ### GI Instruction Using the Gracie Allegiance curriculum, the intermediate class is designed to create a seamless transition between the fundamental techniques, while integrating concepts and strategies of Jiu Jitsu lessons.
 ### Organized Sparring / Specific Training By applying the fundamental techniques with concepts and strategies of Jiu Jitsu, the advanced BJJ class concentrates on situational training, to include sparring for tournaments, or real life self defense.
