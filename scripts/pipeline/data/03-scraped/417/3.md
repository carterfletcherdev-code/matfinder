Our No-Gi program for ages 6 to 15 offers a fast-paced and exciting approach to grappling. Training without the Gi helps students improve agility, reaction time, and real-life movement skills.
 This class connects elements of Jiu-Jitsu and wrestling, giving kids and teens a fun way to build athleticism and confidence while learning self-defense fundamentals.
 Benefits of No-Gi for Youth:
 · Faster transitions and reaction time
 · Improved conditioning and athletic performance
 · Awareness and practical self-defense
 · Strength, mobility, and coordination
 · Confidence and focus
 Included in Membership
 Students ages 6–15 have full access to the No-Gi program and may attend as many weekly classes as they wish.
 No-Gi perfectly complements Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu and Judo, and students can choose to mix or focus on their favorite discipline.
 No-Gi Class Schedule (Ages 6–15)
 · Tuesday – 6:25 PM to 7:25 PM
 · Thursday – 6:25 PM to 7:25 PM
 Students may train one or both days and may freely combine No-Gi with Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu and Judo based on their goals.
