 #### About Our School At our school, martial arts is more than just training, it’s a path to building stronger people, inside and outside the gym. We provide a place where students of all ages and skill levels can pursue Jiu-Jitsu, Boxing, and Muay Thai in a focused, motivating environment. Our goal is to help every student stay consistent, push their limits, and become their best selves.
 Our culture is rooted in safety, respect, discipline, and hard work. This shows in every part of our training, especially in our emphasis on drilling with purpose. We keep the main thing the main thing by focusing on what truly matters: learning, growth, and progress, with no drama and no ego. Through consistent practice, students develop a strong foundation in Jiu-Jitsu and other martial arts that prepares them to perform at their highest potential.

 #### History The story of Coast to Coast Athletic Club & Resilience Jiu-Jitsu is one built on two very different paths that ultimately led to the same mats. Co-owners PJ Ross, a former professional MMA fighter, and Albert Ross, a 20-year Marine Corps veteran, share a vision of using martial arts to build something lasting in the Topsail community.
 Over the years, martial arts became the foundation of their family life. Training alongside their children on bases across the country and overseas, they gained experience and perspective from a variety of coaches and teams. These lessons, gathered from coast to coast, inspired not only the name of the academy but also its philosophy: resilience, respect, and growth through hard work.
 In June 2022, the Ross family began offering training out of their garage. Within months, the program grew into its own facility in Holly Ridge, where it quickly became recognized as a school dedicated to high standards and authentic instruction. Having retired to North Carolina, the family is finally ready to grow roots and invest deeply in the place they now call home.
