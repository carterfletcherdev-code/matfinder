 No-Gi Brazillian Jiu Jitsu at Underdog Martial Arts is what happens when chess, wrestling, and a low-key existential crisis meet on the mats. No pajamas, no belts to grab - just sweat, leverage, and the humbling realization that someone half your size can fold you like a beach chair. It’s fast, technical, and oddly addictive.  Gi Jiu-Jitsu at Underdog Martial Arts is the art of patience, precision, and occasionally getting choked with your own jacket. The uniform (“Gi”) gives you grips, handles, and about a thousand more ways to get tangled up. It’s slower, more technical, and feels a bit like human Rubik’s Cube fighting back. You’ll learn control, defense, and how to strangle and submit opponents using leverage, timing, and a humble dose of audacity.. Whether your goal is self-defense, sport competition, or just learning how to stay calm while someone’s trying to tie you in a knot, this is where it starts..
 For those of you unimpressed by our humor, Brazilian Jiu Jitsu is a grappling-based martial art (sometimes called “the gentle or yielding art”) that focuses on manipulating your partner’s energy to gain dominant position and submissions. It is widely considered one of the best arts to study for self defense, and is a brilliant sport for players of all ages.

 Kickboxing at Underdog Martial Arts pulls from Muay Thai, Dutch kickboxing, Glory and K1 Kickboxing, boxing, and more - because taking the best from everything seems to work pretty well. The fundamentals class covers striking basics: movement, punches, kicks, and how to shell up at the perfect time. Advanced classes connect those dots in sport and self defense scenarios.  As class intensity progresses, we use heavy bags, Thai pads, focus mitts, partner work, controlled sparring drills, and light sparring to build real skill through smart, progressive training.
