 ## CLASS DESCRIPTIONS

 - Brazilian Jiu Jitsu No-Gi Jiu Jitsu at Underdog Martial Arts is what happens when chess, wrestling, and a low-key existential crisis meet on the mats. No pajamas, no belts to grab - just sweat, leverage, and the humbling realization that someone half your size can fold you like a beach chair. It’s fast, technical, and oddly addictive. Whether you’re training for self defense, competition, or just want a socially acceptable reason to aggressively hug strangers, we’ve got you. First class is free. Regret is minimalGi Jiu-Jitsu at Underdog Martial Arts is the art of patience, precision, and occasionally getting choked with your own jacket. The uniform (“Gi”) gives you grips, handles, and about a thousand more ways to get tangled up. It’s slower, more technical, and feels a bit like human Rubik’s Cube fighting back. You’ll learn control, defense, and how to strangle and submit opponents using leverage, timing, and a humble dose of audacity.. Whether your goal is self-defense, sport competition, or just learning how to stay calm while someone’s trying to tie you in a knot, this is where it starts..For those of you unimpressed by our humor, Jiu Jitsu is a grappling-based martial art (sometimes called “the gentle art”) that focuses on manipulating your partner’s energy to gain dominant position and submissions. It is widely considered one of the best arts to study for self defense, and is a brilliant sport for players of all ages. - Kickboxing Kickboxing at Underdog Martial Arts pulls from Muay Thai, Dutch kickboxing, Glory and K1 Kickboxing, boxing, and more - because taking the best from everything seems to work pretty well. The fundamentals class covers striking basics: movement, punches, kicks, and how to shell up at the perfect time. Advanced classes connect those dots in sport and self defense scenarios. Classes are highly technical, partner-based, and are known to…accelarate the heart rate. Expect light &controlled contact (we don’t love CTE), thoughtful instruction, and the occasional moment where you genuinely surprise yourself. As class intensity progresses, we use heavy bags, Thai pads, focus mitts, partner work, controlled sparring drills, and light sparring to build real skill through smart, progressive training. - Kid's Martial Arts Classes Want your kid to be the kid who stands a little taller, thinks a little sharper, and handles whatever life throws their way? Brazilian Jiu Jitsu and kickboxing do exactly that. Here’s the deal: 81% of parents noticed their kids got noticeably more respectful after starting martial arts (Martial Arts Industry Association, 2020). In just three months, over 80% of kids gained real confidence — the kind that sticks (Youth Martial Arts Association, 2022). After eight weeks, 64% of kids felt safer at school, like they actually know how to handle trouble if it shows up (Journal of Applied Psychology, 2019). Martial arts training also slashes bullying by 68% — not by teaching kids to fight, but to outsmart and outmaneuver (BulliesOut, 2020). Focus and emotional control? Those jump by 52%, especially for kids who struggle to sit still in class (Psychology of Sport and Exercise, 2016). Plus, self-regulation and conflict skills can improve by up to 63% (Children & Schools Journal, NASW, 2017).This isn’t just a sport — it’s an opportunity for your kid to surprise themselves, and to internalize that they’re stronger than they think.Why us? Our kids’ program is run by parents, people who GET IT. Our program is intentionally small, allowing for lots of personalized attention. We’ve mastered the perfect blend of honing technique and play, making for a class that keeps your kid’s attention, challenges their brains AND bodies, all while building strong friendships and a sense of community. Best part?
