 ## FAQs
 Explore our FAQ for any questions you have about our jiu-jitsu programs at Odyssey.

 View All FAQs
 - What makes Odyssey Jiu-Jitsu different? At Odyssey Jiu-Jitsu, we believe our culture is key. Our experienced instructors focus on not only developing your technical skills but also providing a supportive and inclusive community. We believe in the transformative power of Jiu-Jitsu, both on and off the mat. - Is Jiu-Jitsu suitable for children, and what are the benefits for kids? Absolutely! Jiu-Jitsu is a fantastic martial art for children. At Odyssey Jiu-Jitsu, we offer specialized classes tailored to the unique needs and energy of young practitioners. Jiu-Jitsu for kids not only provides a fun and engaging physical activity but also instills important life skills such as discipline, respect, and teamwork. - Who can participate in Odyssey Jiu-Jitsu classes? Our classes are open to everyone, regardless of age, fitness level, or experience. Whether you're a seasoned martial artist or stepping onto the mat for the first time, there's a place for you at Odyssey Jiu-Jitsu. - What do I need for my first class? For your first class, wear comfortable athletic clothing, such as a rash guard or T-shirt and athletic shorts. We recommend bringing a water bottle and a pair of shoes you can slip off and on easily for getting on and off the mats.
