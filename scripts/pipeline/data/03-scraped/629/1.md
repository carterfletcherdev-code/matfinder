 ### Program Description
 For children ages 4-7 (Mini Grapplers) and ages 8-13 (Junior Grapplers).

 This class helps students achieve a strong understanding of the key components of jiu-jitsu and self-defense, while building confidence and self-discipline in a fun, social setting.

 ##### Boost Confidence: learn to overcome challenges, set goals, and achieve success on and off the mats

 ##### Discipline and Focus: Jiu Jitsu teaches kids the value of discipline and concentration, valuable skills for school and beyond

 ##### Anti-Bullying Skills: practical self-defense techniques to deal with bullying, while promoting non-violent conflict resolution

 ##### Social Skills: Kids develop social skills and learn teamwork through partner drills
