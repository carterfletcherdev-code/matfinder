 ## Paddy Dodd Owner & Head Instructor
 Paddy Dodd is a Brazilian Jiu Jitsu black belt under Master Shaun Dillon, a 3rd-degree black belt from Ricardo Liborio of American Top Team. Paddy began his Jiu Jitsu journey in 2011 in Memphis, Tennessee, under Chad Chilcutt, a black belt under Royce Gracie, and has been training continuously ever since.
 In 2014, Paddy and his family relocated to the beautiful mountains of Asheville, North Carolina, where he began training at American Top Team Asheville. By 2017, his dedication and skill led him to begin teaching there, and he quickly became known for his creative, effective, and engaging teaching style.
 After nearly a decade of training and teaching at American Top Team, Paddy opened Asheville’s Northside Jiu Jitsu in December 2024 to bring his passion for Jiu Jitsu to a new community.

 Paddy has competed at every rank and achieved the impressive accomplishment of being ranked 5th in the world in the IBJJF Heavyweight Brown Belt division. Known for his pressure-heavy game and focus on fundamentals, Paddy creates engaging, fun, and accessible classes for all skill levels.
 At Asheville’s Northside Jiu Jitsu, Paddy combines his extensive competition experience with over a decade of training to help students build a strong foundation and achieve their personal goals in Jiu Jitsu. Whether you’re a beginner or an experienced grappler, Paddy’s classes promise to challenge, inspire, and elevate your Jiu Jitsu skills.
