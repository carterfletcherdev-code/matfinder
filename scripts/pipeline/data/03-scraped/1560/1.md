# Schedule

 Classes every day. Programs for every level. Whether you’re just starting or preparing to compete, there’s a spot on the mat for you.

 ## Black Flag Jiu-Jitsu Schedule | Rockford, IL

 If you’re looking for Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu in Rockford, Illinois , Black Flag Jiu-Jitsu offers a complete schedule designed for all ages, skill levels, and training goals. Whether you’re a beginner seeking your first class, an experienced grappler wanting to refine your skills, or a parent looking for quality martial arts training for your kids, our weekly schedule has something for everyone.

 ## Morning Training

 Start your day with purpose. Our morning sessions are built for people who want to train before work or school.
 Strength & Conditioning runs Monday, Wednesday, and Friday, followed immediately by All Levels Jiu-Jitsu. This pairing gives you the opportunity to build strength and apply it directly on the mats.
 ###

 ## Midday Classes

 For those with flexible schedules or lunch-hour availability:
 - All-Levels Jiu-Jitsu: Monday–Thursday at 12:00 PM – Perfect for anyone who wants to get in extra training or prefers smaller class sizes.

 ## Kids & Teens | Building Skills and Confidence

 Our youth program develops discipline, self-defense, and athleticism in a safe, encouraging environment:
 - Youth I Jiu-Jitsu (Ages 5–9): Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday at 4:30–5:15 PM – Foundational skills, fun games, and a focus on movement and coordination. - Youth II Jiu-Jitsu (Ages 9–17): Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday at 5:15–6:00 PM – A more advanced class that introduces strategy, positional sparring, and competition preparation.

 ## Evening Training | For Adults of All Levels

 Our evening sessions are where Rockford’s grappling community comes together:
 - Grappling Foundations: Monday, Wednesday, Friday at 5:00 PM – Ideal for beginners or anyone refining the fundamentals of no-gi grappling. - All-Levels Jiu-Jitsu (No-Gi): Monday & Wednesday at 6:00 PM – A complete CLA (Constraint-Led Approach) class focused on skill development, active participation, and competition training. - All-Levels Jiu-Jitsu (Gi): Tuesday & Thursday at 6:00 PM – A complete CLA (Constraint-Led Approach) class focused on skill development, active participation, and competition training.

 ## Wrestling Training

 We integrate wrestling into our program for better takedowns and mat control:
 - Wrestling: M/T/TH 7:00 PM – Great for BJJ competitors or anyone wanting to improve stand-up grappling. - Wrestling Open Mat: Sunday at 4:00 PM – A time to drill, spar, and practice takedown techniques with partners.

 ## Open Mats

 Open mats are unstructured sessions where you can spar, review techniques, or get one-on-one help from teammates:
 No-Gi Open Mat : Friday at 6:00 PM – Bring your no-gi gear and work on any part of your game.

 ## Private Lessons

 In addition to group classes, private lessons are available with our coaches for both adults and kids . These sessions are perfect for sharpening your skills, preparing for competition, or getting extra guidance outside of regular classes.

 ## Why Choose

 ## Black Flag Jiu-Jitsu?

 At Black Flag Jiu-Jitsu, we use a constraint-led approach (CLA) to teaching, which means every class emphasizes live resistance and realistic training situations. We believe in building skill through problem-solving and adaptation, not just memorizing moves.
 Whether you want to compete at the highest levels , learn self-defense, get in shape, or simply join a supportive martial arts community, our Rockford BJJ program offers a schedule that fits your lifestyle.
 With morning, midday, and evening classes available six days a week, plus wrestling, open mats, and youth programs, there’s always an opportunity to train.
