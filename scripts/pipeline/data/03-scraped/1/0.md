# BRAZILIAN JIU JITSU TAUGHT BY DAVID VAVA , AND COREY JOHNSON

 Brazilian Jiu Jitsu is a martial art primarily consisting of ground fighting; it is similar to wrestling, but includes submissions (like chokes) and joint locks (like arm bars and foot locks). Learn from our experienced instructors, alongside dedicated training partners. Bodyfit offers a special beginner class welcoming any experience level, (including no experience!), regular Brazilian Jiu Jitsu with a diverse mix of all experience levels, and advanced sessions that will include plenty of live training.
