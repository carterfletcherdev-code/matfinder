 ## Welcome to Agan Jiu-Jitsu #### Our Philosophy #### Our Philosophy #### Our Philosophy
 At Agan Jiu-Jitsu, we believe that Jiu-Jitsu is not just a martial art, but a way of life. We strive to help our students develop not only their physical abilities, but also their mental and emotional strength, discipline, and respect.

 #### Our Training #### Our Philosophy #### Our Philosophy
 Our training program is designed to be challenging, but also fun and rewarding. We use a variety of teaching methods to help students learn and progress at their own pace, including partner drills, sparring.

 #### Our Community #### Our Philosophy #### Our Community
 We are proud to have a welcoming and supportive community at Agan Jiu-Jitsu. Our students come from all walks of life, but share a common passion for Jiu-Jitsu and a desire to improve themselves.
