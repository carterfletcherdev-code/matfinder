 ## What Is 10th Planet Jiu-Jitsu?

 10th Planet Jiu-Jitsu is a modern evolution of Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu (BJJ) that focuses entirely on No-Gi grappling, meaning students train without the traditional kimono or Gi. Created by Eddie Bravo in the early 2000s, this system transformed traditional Jiu-Jitsu into a more adaptable and practical style of training that fits both self-defense and competitive settings. At 10th Planet Miami in Miramar, students learn innovative techniques that emphasize flexibility, leverage, and strategic movement. Every class develops your ability to adapt, think critically, and perform under pressure, all while improving strength, coordination, and confidence.

 ## The History of 10th Planet Jiu-Jitsu

 The 10th Planet system was founded by Eddie Bravo, a black belt under Jean Jacques Machado, one of the most respected instructors in the Gracie lineage. In 2003, Eddie gained international recognition by submitting Royler Gracie at the ADCC (Abu Dhabi Combat Club) Submission Wrestling Championship. That moment demonstrated the effectiveness of No-Gi Jiu-Jitsu and marked the birth of the 10th Planet system. From there, Bravo developed a complete curriculum built around innovative positions such as the Rubber Guard, Lockdown, Truck, and Twister. These movements were created for real-world application, helping practitioners control opponents in mixed martial arts, self-defense, and competitive grappling. Today, 10th Planet Jiu-Jitsu has grown into a global network of schools united by creativity, adaptability, and a shared commitment to pushing the art forward. 10th Planet Miami in Miramar proudly carries this legacy, providing world-class instruction and a welcoming community for students of all experience levels.
