## PRIME BJJ: CARLSON GRACIE LINEAGE AND GRACIE JIU-JITSU August 21, 2016
 Prime Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu was founded in 2012. Head Professor, Marcelo Motta, is a 4th Degree black belt and his lineage is Carlson Gracie style BJJ.
 Most people know what “Gracie Jiu-Jitsu” is, but they don’t understand the different lineages. Watch this video for a complete history on Carlson Gracie Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu.
 In Colorado Springs, Prime BJJ is the only academy that teaches Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu as it was originally taught in its native country of Brazil.
