 ## Benefits of Training Jiu-Jitsu
 At the fundamental core of BJJ the most important thing is defense, where the skills you learn on the mat will allow you to protect yourself and love ones. You'll build stregnth, flexibility and a knowledge of positioning to where to size and weight don't matter.

 - Helps build physical fitness and self-defense skills - Improves mental health - Promotes weight loss and enhances flexibility - Improves attention span and critical thinking skills - Builds discipline and self-confidence - Allows you to meet new friends and teammates - Helps develop a better range of motion - Improves your ability to focus
