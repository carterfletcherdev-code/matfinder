 # Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu

 ### Program: Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu Description

 Our Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu program provides our students the opportunity to take their game to the next level through in-depth instruction from multiple high-level black belts and brown belts.

 With this program, our students are able to increase their jiu-jitsu knowledge, improve their movement and coordination, and develop a solid foundation while being introduced to more advanced jiu-jitsu techniques.

 Goals

 - Improve movement, coordination, and strength - Boost self-confidence - Increase overall physical fitness - Create a strong jiu-jitsu foundation - Improve your understanding of jiu-jitsu
