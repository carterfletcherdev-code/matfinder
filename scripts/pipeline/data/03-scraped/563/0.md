 ## Frequently Asked Questions Please contact us if you cannot find an answer to your question.

 What is Jiu Jitsu? Jiu Jitsu is a Grappling Art that entails Takedowns, Chokes, Joint Locks, and Pins to achieve control and submission over ones opponent. Jiu Jitsu is a predominantly ground based Martial Art with its roots stemming from Japan and Brazil.

 Is Jiu Jitsu safe? As with any Combat Sport Jiu Jitsu does carry the possibility of injury. We take great steps to ensure that everyone trains safe and are aware of the risks and how to mitigate them.

 Why choose Heel Hooks Jiu Jitsu? We provide top quality training and at a reasonable price. We are a firm believer that you should not have to break the bank to train Jiu Jitsu. We want you to better yourself both on the mats and off.

 What makes us different? We are a No-Gi Jiu Jitsu Academy. No-Gi is typically a faster style with more of an emphasis on Attacking and ensuring that your controls are solid and your defenses allow you to be able to counter attack efficiently and effectively.
