## About our Culture
 10th Planet Jiu Jitsu Chino Hills is a thriving community built on 25 years of experience in Brazilian Jiu Jitsu, Nogi Jiu Jitsu, and mixed martial arts. Our instructor began his journey with the esteemed 2nd American Brazilian Jiu Jitsu Black Belt, Ken Gabriel, in 2000 and has since achieved a 2nd degree Black Belt in Gracie Jiu Jitsu under Pedro Sauer, as well as a 1st degree Black Belt in 10th Planet Jiu Jitsu under Eddie Bravo Black Belt Jeremy Fields. While our students have celebrated victories in World Championships and numerous competitions, many of our members train as hobbyists, pursuing goals like weight loss, muscle building, self-confidence, self-defense, and a passion for the art. By mastering the 10th Planet Jiu Jitsu system, you'll gain a profound sense of as you progress through various techniques and movements. We can't wait to welcome you!
