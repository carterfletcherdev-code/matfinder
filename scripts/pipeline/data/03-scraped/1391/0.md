# Red Schafer Mixed Martial Arts

 ## About

 Red Schafer Mixed Martial Arts is Milwaukee, Wisconsin’s finest Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu and Mixed Martial Arts training facility. Owner and head coach Eric “Red” Schafer is a 9-time Ultimate Fighting Championship Veteran and a Gracie Jiu-Jitsu Blackbelt under Professor Pedro Sauer. The gym officially opened in December of 2010, and has continued to improve and expand.
 Red Schafer MMA offers a full schedule of classes including Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu (in the gi), no-gi submission grappling, mixed martial arts (UFC-style), boxing, and kickboxing. The classes are UNLIMITED and all-inclusive to every member! The gym offers the best rates around including discounts to police, firefighters, and military veterans.
