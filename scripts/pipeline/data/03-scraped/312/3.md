 ## Frequently Asked Questions
 ### Do I need experience to join? Not at all. Our classes are designed for everyone, including complete beginners.
 ### What should I wear to my first class? Athletic clothing works great for your first class. We’ll provide a loaner Gi if needed.
 ### Can parents watch kids’ classes? Absolutely. We encourage parents to stay and see how their kids are learning and progressing.
 ### How often can I train? Our memberships are unlimited, so you can attend as many classes as you’d like each month.
 ### Is Jiu Jitsu safe for kids? Yes. We carefully match training partners, monitor sparring closely, and emphasize safety and control in all movements.
 ### Are there classes specifically for women? No separate classes—but our academy fosters a respectful, inclusive environment where women and men train together safely and successfully.
 ### What’s the best way to start? Reach out to us and schedule your free trial. We’ll guide you through everything from your first step on the mat to your first class.
