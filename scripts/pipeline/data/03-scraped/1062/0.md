 ## What You’ll Learn in Our BJJ Classes Brazilian Jiu Jitsu is grappling-focused training built around leverage, timing, and positional control. You learn how to stay balanced, manage distance, and solve problems calmly through technique, not brute force.

 - Movement fundamentals such as base, posture, frames, and hip control - Positional awareness from common scenarios like guard, side control, mount, and back control - Escapes that help you stay composed and safe - Sweeps and transitions that teach you how positions connect - Submissions introduced with a “control first” mindset

 ## Gi vs No-Gi: Key Differences Explained New students often ask whether they should train in a gi or not. Both are valuable, and both can be beginner-friendly with good coaching. These are the key differences between both:
 Gi training
 Uses grips on sleeves, collar, and pants. It often feels more methodical and teaches control through grip fighting and pressure.
 No-gi training
 It’s typically done in athletic apparel. It can feel faster because there are fewer grips, so movement and positioning become even more important.
 For your first visit, comfortable workout clothing is usually enough . Once you decide what schedule and format you prefer, we can guide you on gear.
