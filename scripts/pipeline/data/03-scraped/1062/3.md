# Private Lessons for MMA & BJJ in Queens, NY

 Home » Programs » Private Lessons for MMA & BJJ in Queens, NY

 Private lessons are the fastest way to build confidence, sharpen technique, and get coaching tailored to your exact goals. If you want more attention than a group class can provide, or prefer a structured plan that moves at your pace, private training is a strong option.
 These sessions help beginners get comfortable quickly and give experienced students the focused feedback needed to break through plateaus. They’re also ideal for students preparing to compete, with targeted work on strategy, timing, and specific game plans.
 Private training is also a great fit for busy schedules. With flexible session times, you can train consistently without being tied to a fixed class schedule. Whether your goal is skill development, conditioning, or practical self-defense, each session is designed so you leave knowing exactly what to focus on next
 ## Who Benefits Most From Private Lessons? Private coaching is not only for advanced students. Many people start with private sessions because it reduces anxiety and makes the learning process easier. Others add private training later to improve faster or focus on specific areas.
 Private lessons are a great fit if you are:
 - A total beginner who wants a clear, comfortable starting point - Returning to training after time away and rebuilding confidence - Busy and need flexible scheduling that fits your week - Stuck at a plateau and want more detailed corrections - Working toward a specific goal (like competition) and want a structured plan - Looking for a more focused self-defense pathway with coaching guidance
 MMA and BJJ training work best when you can stay consistent, and private sessions can make that consistency easier to maintain.
 ## Why Training Feels Different with One-on-One Coaching Many people want self defense training, but the most important part is how it is taught. A good class should be structured, calm, and based on real fundamentals, not reckless intensity. In private lessons, coaching becomes even more personal and supportive, which helps students learn faster and feel more confident.
 These are the main difference sbetween private sessions and group classes:
 | Private Lessons | Group Classes Coaching Style | 100% personalized, tailored to your goals | Structured for groups with shared instruction Pace of Learning | Faster, adjusted to your level and progress | Moderate, follows class curriculum Attention Level | Full one-on-one attention | While everyone receives attention, it is shared across the group Technique Focus | Highly specific (your weaknesses & goals) | General techniques for all levels Flexibility | Flexible scheduling | Fixed class schedule Competition Prep | Targeted strategy, drilling, and feedback | Live rounds and general preparation Training Partners | Coach-led or limited partners | Multiple partners with different styles Best For | Fast progress, busy schedules, specific goals | Routine, consistency, and community
 ## How Our Private Lessons Are Structured Private lessons are organized with a clear flow so you progress with purpose. Each session is built around your goals, your experience level, and what you need most right now.
 A typical private session includes:
 - A quick check-in about goals, progress, and what you want to focus on - A warm-up that supports mobility, coordination, and performance - Technique instruction broken into clear steps - Drilling and repetition with coaching corrections in real time - Scenario or positional work to build confidence and comfort - A simple plan for what to practice next and how to progress
