 #### Who we are at ### Blue Ridge Jiu Jitsu

 #### Our School Our culture is built around one principle: rising to the occasion in Jiu Jitsu and in life.
 At Blue Ridge Jiu Jitsu, we are a local Brazilian Jiu Jitsu and self-defense academy in Mills River, NC. Our students train a structured system that develops real skill under pressure.
 We offer Jiu Jitsu classes for kids, adults, and families, including self-defense, takedowns, grappling, and competition-focused training.

 Our approach is guided by the 753 Warrior Code, emphasizing discipline, respect, and personal accountability on and off the mats.
 Adult Jiu Jitsu training develops proven self-defense ability and the ability to stay calm and effective under pressure. For students interested in competition, we provide high-level Jiu Jitsu instruction and a clear path for progression in sport BJJ.
 Kids Jiu Jitsu classes focus on confidence, using their voice, and practical anti-bullying self-defense skills.
 Every student has a different goal, but the standard is the same: train with focus, improve consistently, and grow stronger in every way.
 We combine structured Brazilian Jiu Jitsu instruction with a welcoming training environment, serving Mills River, Brevard, Hendersonville, Fletcher, and the surrounding Western North Carolina area.

 For Samantha, her Jiu Jitsu journey began in 2015. She quickly learned how beneficial the art was in regards to women and self-defense. Together, Josh and Samantha strive to help each student achieve their highest potential on and off the mat – mentally, physically, and psychologically.

 Josh started his journey in 2009 when shortly after graduating high school he decided to enter a Toughman contest. After finding Young Guns boxing academy to help him prepare, he soon began boxing under the tutelage of Chris Sawtelle where he successfully competed under Chris’ Young Guns banner until 2012. In January of 2013, Josh’s focus would shift when he found Jiu Jitsu at BlackEye Fitness in Arden, NC.
 Initially, Josh began training Jiu Jitsu to supplement his boxing solely to try his hand at mixed martial arts however he quickly fell in love with the lifestyle and sport of Jiu Jitsu. After training exclusively in Jiu Jitsu for a year, he earned his blue belt from legendary Royce Gracie black belt, Steve Hall, and shortly after began competing in mixed martial arts.

 #### ### Striking
 Our striking program teaches practical fundamentals. Focusing on stand-up skills designed to work in real-life fighting.
 The goal is simple: have confidence when standing. Stay safe, stay calm under pressure, and stop the threat.
 Students learn how to manage distance, protect themselves from common attacks, and deliver simple, effective strikes when needed.
 The program emphasizes awareness, control, and decision-making just as much as physical techniques.
 Rather than flashy combinations or competition-style striking, the training focuses on high-percentage movements such as basic punches, elbows, knees, defensive positioning, and clinch control.
 Students also learn how to transition safely between striking and grappling, which is a key principle of the our self-defense system.
 The program is designed to build confidence and resilience while reinforcing discipline and respect. Classes are structured to be safe, approachable for everyone.
 Above all, the goal is to give students the tools to avoid conflict when possible and protect themselves when necessary.
