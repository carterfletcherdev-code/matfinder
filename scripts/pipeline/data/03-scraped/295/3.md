 #### The Perfect Non-Violent Self-Defense for Children We use Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu as a tool to incorporate useful life-skills in your child's life. We start with physical self-defense , move on to mental and verbal self-defense , and tie it all together with character development . By utilizing this multi-layer approach, we guarantee that you will see a difference in your child as they progress through our classes!
 ##### With Kids Martial Arts, your child can develop: - Greater confidence and self-respect - Helpful physical techniques used in self-defense - Coached and effective responses to verbal abuse - Increased responsibility and manners
 #### Martial Arts For All Ages We know that as your child grows, there are different things that they can handle and that they will encounter. That's why we've set up a few different programs that are either age-specific or ability specific so that way your child can learn in a group of their peers and grow at a speed that is comfortable for them.
 We offer 2 different Kid's Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu Classes:
 ##### Little Warriors Jiu Jitsu: Building Strong Foundations (Ages 4–6) Our Little Warriors program is all about helping kids build strong foundations in a fun, engaging way. Through games and playful drills, children develop balance, coordination, strength, and focus — all while learning to listen, follow directions, and gain confidence. Every activity is carefully designed to prepare them for more advanced jiu jitsu skills as they grow, while also teaching important life skills like teamwork, respect, and self-control. Most importantly, we want kids to fall in love with movement, learning, and martial arts — setting them on the path to becoming strong, focused, and respectful martial artists.
 ##### Ages 7 and Up In our 7 and up Kids BJJ class, we teach nearly all of the techniques taught in the Adult Jiu-Jitsu Class and focus on teaching your child how to defend themselves from any type of physical attack. This instills a great deal of confidence and self-respect as a fun by-product!
