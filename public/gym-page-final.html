About this gym

10th Planet Austin is the city's longest-running No-Gi-only academy. Founded in 2014, the gym focuses on the 10th Planet system pioneered by Eddie Bravo, with a strong competitor pipeline and a beginner-friendly fundamentals track. Saturday open mats are free for visiting practitioners with a rashguard.

Research findings — what other directories do that we should consider

  • Map embed in sidebar — every major directory (Yelp, Google, ClassPass, MindBody) shows one. We added a mini preview ✓
  • Photo lightbox — universal pattern, modal with 4-up grid + arrow navigation. Added ✓
  • Sticky check-in CTA on mobile scroll — Yelp pins a "Reserve" button to the bottom on mobile after you scroll past the hero. Worth adding when we ship the iOS app.
  • "Walk-in policy" line — first thing visitors want to know. Captured in our "Drop-in fee" + "Best day to visit" rows.
  • Coach/instructor profiles — important for BJJ where lineage matters. Future addition once we have a `coaches` table.
  • Required gear / dress code — captured in "Bring" row.
  • Pricing transparency — most gyms hide pricing. The "Drop-in fee" line is the single most useful thing we can show because it's the only price most travelers care about.
  • FAQ / "What to expect on your first visit" — adds a lot. Could go between About and Reviews. Skip for v1, add once owner portal can populate it.
