Notes
  • The Instagram pill renders only when gym.instagram is set on the gym data. Most current gyms don’t have one yet — that’s why you may not see it on the live site even after the wrap fix.
  • Instagram glyph color: #E1306C (Instagram brand pink) with a matching pink outline. Glyph-only across every viewport for consistency.
  • The wrap break point depends on container width — popup overlay (560px max) stays single-line, sidebar list cards (~280–300px) wrap to two rows, portrait/landscape behave per their own widths.
  • If you want to confirm the Instagram pill is actually rendering in production: pick a gym that has instagram in its data (Corsair BJJ has @corsair.bjj on its Add Gym entries). Or temporarily add an Instagram via the Help Confirm form’s new Instagram input.
