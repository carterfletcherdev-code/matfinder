Your Gym pill — owner-only swap

When the current user is a verified owner of a gym, the gold Claim pill on that gym's card is replaced with a gold Your Gym pill that links to /owner/[gymId] instead of /claim/[gymId]. Same geometry, same gold colour, so the row's silhouette doesn't shift. Click-throughs to Manage Schedule / Listing / Analytics. All other gyms still show the regular Claim pill.

Behaviour
  • Pill text changes only on the cards belonging to gyms in public.gym_owners for the current user (status = verified). Every other gym still shows the regular Claim pill.
  • The useOwnedGyms() hook returns the array of owned gym IDs once on sign-in, then GymCard checks ownedGymIds.includes(gym.id) per render.
  • Same gold geometry on both pills so the action row's overall layout is identical — no shifting when ownership changes.
  • Click-through: Claim → /claim/[gymId] · Your Gym → /owner/[gymId].
