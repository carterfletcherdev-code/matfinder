About this gym

10th Planet Austin is the city's longest-running No-Gi-only academy. Founded in 2014, the gym focuses on the 10th Planet system pioneered by Eddie Bravo, with a strong competitor pipeline and a beginner-friendly fundamentals track. Saturday open mats are free for visiting practitioners with a rashguard.

What's on the gym page (and why)

  • Hero photo + 2 thumbnails. We have photos for 96% of gyms. The full row hints at a gallery (click "8 photos" → expands to lightbox).
  • Header with Featured pill. Name is a website link (same external-arrow pattern as the card). Featured pill renders only on paid Featured/Sponsor tier gyms.
  • Two right-side actions: Save (secondary) + Check in (big primary). Same hierarchy as the card — one big CTA wins.
  • NEXT OPEN MAT hero panel. Same component as on the card, just bigger. Pulses green dot. RSVP becomes a full-size CTA here since space allows.
  • Day strip with full day names + class counts. This is where the day-strip pattern lives — each day shows "2 classes" / "1 open mat" / "closed" so you can scan the week at a glance. Tap → full schedule list updates below.
  • Schedule list. Per-day list with verified ✓ citation tooltips (clicking shows the source quote pulled from the gym's website). Per-class RSVP for open mats. The "verified" tag is your trust moat.
  • About section. Only renders when the gym owner has filled it in via the owner portal. Empty by default.
  • Reviews with tag pills. Reviews can be auto-categorized (beginner-friendly / hard sparring / drop-in welcome / competition team) — the pills give scanability without making people read every review.
  • At-a-glance stats sidebar. 6 numbers that answer "is this a real, active gym?" instantly.
  • Contact card with full info. Address (tap → Maps), phone (tap → dialer), website (tap → opens), Instagram (tap → opens). Replaces the 3-button action grid since this page has the room.
  • Upcoming events. Tournaments, seminars, camps — gold accent ties to the Featured/paid pattern. Future revenue stream when we add ticketing.
  • Trained here avatars. Community signal. Connects to the Passport channel from the charter.
  • Wrong info? at the bottom. Same Phase A treatment as the card — visible, low-weight, opens the existing correction modal.
