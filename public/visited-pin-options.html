Visited pin — design options

Eight variants. Each card shows the standard (not-visited) pin next to the new visited variant on both a dark and light map style, so you can gauge contrast at a glance. All variants follow the rules: bone outlines only, no emojis, no arrows, no black accents. Pick the letter you like and I’ll wire it into the live map.

Option A

Inverted stamp · bone fill, colored core

Pin is mostly bone with a small discipline-colored dot at the center and a thin colored outline. Reads like a “completed” stamp. High contrast against any map style — easily the most distinct.

How to pick: tell me a letter (A–H) and I’ll wire it into components/Map.tsx for the visited pin. The current implementation is a thin 1.5px bone stroke directly on the pin — too close to invisible — so any of these will be a meaningful upgrade. My personal pick: Option A (inverted stamp) — most distinct at small map sizes, can’t be confused with anything else. Runner-up: Option G (reversed sticker).
